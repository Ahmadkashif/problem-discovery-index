## Why These Niches

Delivery and service fleets represent the two largest segments with fundamentally different operational patterns — delivery optimizes for route density while service optimizes for response time. Municipal and small owner-operator fleets are digitally neglected due to budget constraints and low IT support. EV transition and aging fleet management represent emerging underserved needs that existing fleet tools don't address. DOT compliance and preventive maintenance are the two most rule-based, automatable workflows consuming disproportionate manual labor.
