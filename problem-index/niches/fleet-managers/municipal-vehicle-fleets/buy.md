# Government-Ready Cloud Fleet Management with FedRAMP/StateRAMP Compliance

**Niche:** [[niches/fleet-managers/municipal-vehicle-fleets/profile|Municipal Vehicle Fleets]]
**Industry:** [[industries/fleet-managers|Fleet Managers]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Fleetio and RTA Fleet Management are excellent cloud fleet platforms, but they lack government IT security certifications (FedRAMP, StateRAMP, CJIS) that municipalities require for procurement approval.
**Tags:** #compliance #data-integration #automation

## The Problem
A city fleet superintendent identifies Fleetio as the ideal replacement for their 15-year-old AssetWorks installation. They submit a procurement request and the city IT department rejects it: "The vendor isn't FedRAMP or StateRAMP certified, can't demonstrate CJIS compliance for police vehicle data, and doesn't support the city's SSO infrastructure." The fleet superintendent is stuck with legacy software not because better tools don't exist, but because those tools haven't invested in government compliance certifications.

## What Already Exists
Fleetio, RTA Fleet Management, and Fleet Complete provide modern cloud-based fleet management with excellent UX, mobile apps, and API integrations. AssetWorks FleetFocus and Faster Asset Solutions are government-focused but legacy (client-server, outdated UI, limited mobile). No vendor offers both modern cloud UX and government IT compliance.

## The Customization Gap
The missing piece is government compliance packaging: FedRAMP Moderate or StateRAMP certification, CJIS compliance for law enforcement vehicle data, SOC 2 Type II, integration with government SSO (Azure AD, Okta for Government), data residency guarantees (data stays in US), and a procurement package (GSA Schedule, cooperative purchasing agreements) that bypasses the 12-18 month RFP process. The underlying product can be an existing modern fleet platform — the gap is the compliance and procurement wrapper.

## Target Customer
Municipal fleet superintendents whose IT departments have rejected modern fleet tools on security/compliance grounds, forcing continued use of legacy systems.

## Impact If Solved
Unlocks modern fleet management for 5,000+ municipalities currently locked into legacy systems, improving fleet utilization by 15-20% and reducing administrative overhead by 25-30% through mobile-first workflows and automated maintenance scheduling.
