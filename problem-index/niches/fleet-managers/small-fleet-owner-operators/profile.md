# Small Fleet Owner-Operators

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/fleet-managers|Fleet Managers]]
**Category:** Low Digitized

## Profile
**Market Size:** $6B
**Share of Parent Industry:** 8%
**Digital Adoption:** Low — Most small fleet owners (5-25 vehicles) track maintenance in spreadsheets, manage fuel with personal credit card statements, and handle compliance documents in filing cabinets.
**Target Buyer:** Business owner managing 5-25 vehicles as part of a larger operation (not a dedicated fleet manager)
**Automation Potential:** High — The workflows are identical to enterprise fleet management but done manually at a fraction of the scale — perfect for lightweight automation.

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Small fleet owner-operators (pest control companies, landscapers, small delivery services, cleaning companies) manage 5-25 vehicles as a secondary responsibility — there's no dedicated fleet manager. The owner or office manager tracks oil changes on a whiteboard, keeps registration renewals in a desk drawer, and reconciles fuel cards against bank statements once a month. Enterprise fleet platforms (Samsara, Verizon Connect) cost $25-$50 per vehicle per month and require dedicated admin time to configure and maintain — overkill for a 10-truck landscaping company. These operators need "set it and forget it" fleet basics, not enterprise features.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Spreadsheets and calendar reminders are the standard. Some use Fleetio's free tier (limited to 1 vehicle) or Azuga's basic GPS tracking. Enterprise platforms require more setup and ongoing management than these operators can justify. The gap is an ultra-simplified fleet management tool that handles the 5 things small fleet owners actually need: maintenance reminders, fuel tracking, registration/insurance expiration alerts, basic GPS location, and total cost of ownership per vehicle.
