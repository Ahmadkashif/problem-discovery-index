# Set-and-Forget Fleet Basics for 5-25 Vehicle Operations

**Niche:** [[niches/fleet-managers/small-fleet-owner-operators/profile|Small Fleet Owner-Operators]]
**Industry:** [[industries/fleet-managers|Fleet Managers]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No fleet management tool is designed for operators who spend zero hours per week on fleet management — the smallest useful product for the smallest viable fleet.
**Tags:** #quick-win #automation #worker-facing #revenue-impact

## The Problem
A pest control company owner with 12 trucks doesn't want to "manage a fleet" — they want to never get surprised by an expired registration, a missed oil change, or a breakdown that could have been prevented. They need 5 things: (1) automatic maintenance reminders based on time and mileage, (2) registration and insurance expiration alerts, (3) fuel spend per vehicle, (4) total cost per vehicle per year, and (5) location of each vehicle right now. Every existing fleet platform bundles these basics with dozens of features (driver scoring, route optimization, ELD compliance, IFTA reporting) that this operator will never use, creating configuration overhead that prevents adoption.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
Fleet management vendors build for the enterprise buyer who pays $25-$50 per vehicle per month. A 10-vehicle small operator paying $8/vehicle/month represents $80/month in revenue — not enough to justify a sales rep, onboarding call, or dedicated support. The market is large in aggregate (500,000+ small fleet operators in the US) but each account is too small for enterprise SaaS economics. The product needs to be self-serve, zero-configuration, and priced for impulse purchase.

## What to Build
A mobile-first fleet app where the owner adds vehicles by scanning the registration card (OCR extracts VIN, registration date, insurance expiration), connects an OBD-II plug for mileage tracking (or enters mileage manually monthly), and sets maintenance intervals. The app runs silently and sends push notifications: "Truck #7 is due for oil change in 200 miles," "Van #3 registration expires in 30 days," "Your fleet fuel spend is up 15% this month." No dashboard to monitor. No reports to run. Just alerts when action is needed.

## Target Customer
Business owners managing 5-25 vehicles who currently track fleet maintenance in their head and discover problems reactively.

## Impact If Built
Prevents 3-5 missed maintenance events per vehicle per year, eliminates expired registration/insurance incidents, and reduces unplanned breakdowns by 30-40%. At $5-$10 per vehicle per month, the tool pays for itself with the first prevented breakdown ($500-$3,000 savings).
