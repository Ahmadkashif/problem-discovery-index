## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Fire and smoke restoration is the most complex restoration category — a single fire loss may require emergency board-up, water extraction (from fire suppression), smoke and soot cleaning, structural demolition and rebuild, contents pack-out and cleaning, and odor elimination. Jobs range from $20K smoke-only losses to $500K+ full-structure fires. The niche requires both mitigation skills (emergency response, board-up, water extraction) and general contracting skills (structural rebuild). The insurance documentation burden is extreme — every item demolished, cleaned, or replaced must be individually scoped in Xactimate with supporting photos.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Xactimate handles structural estimates. Contents inventory tools (Encircle, ContentsTrack) manage pack-out documentation. Job management platforms (Next Gear, PSA) coordinate workflows. The gap is in smoke damage assessment — determining which surfaces and contents can be cleaned versus replaced is a judgment call that experienced restorers make by evaluating soot type (wet, dry, protein), penetration depth, surface material, and item value. No tool assists with this assessment or documents the rationale for carrier review.
