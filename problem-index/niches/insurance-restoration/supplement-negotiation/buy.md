# Supplement Tracking and Analytics Dashboard

**Niche:** [[niches/insurance-restoration/supplement-negotiation/profile|Xactimate Supplement Negotiation]]
**Industry:** [[industries/insurance-restoration|Insurance Restoration]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** CRM and project management tools track task completion, but none model the multi-round supplement negotiation lifecycle with carrier-specific analytics showing which items are worth fighting for and which to concede.
**Tags:** #gradient-boosting #binary-classification #tabular-ml #automation #revenue-impact

## The Problem
A restoration company has 150 active jobs, each with 1-4 pending supplements at various stages (drafted, submitted, carrier reviewing, carrier responded, counter-supplement in preparation). The office manager tracks all of this in a spreadsheet or whiteboard, manually checking carrier portals for responses and emailing supplement writers about deadlines. With no analytics, the company can't answer basic questions: "What's our supplement success rate with State Farm?" "Which line items do carriers consistently deny?" "How much revenue is sitting in unsubmitted supplements?"

## What Already Exists
Job management platforms (Next Gear, PSA) track job status at a high level. CRMs track customer interactions. Financial platforms track invoicing and payments. None model the supplement lifecycle as a distinct workflow stage with its own pipeline metrics (supplements in progress, submitted, pending response, recovered amount), carrier-specific performance analytics, and item-level outcome tracking.

## The Customization Gap
The gap is a supplement pipeline manager with analytics: a kanban-style board showing all supplements by stage, with carrier-specific response time benchmarks, automated follow-up triggers, and outcome tracking that rolls up to analytics — supplement success rate by carrier, by adjuster, by line item category, and by justification type. Over time, the data reveals which battles are worth fighting (90% success rate on O&P disputes with State Farm) and which are not (5% success rate on waste factor disputes with USAA).

## Target Customer
Restoration companies processing 200+ supplements per year that need visibility into their supplement pipeline and data-driven negotiation strategy.

## Impact If Solved
Improves supplement ROI by focusing effort on high-success-rate disputes. Reduces supplement cycle time by 20-30% through automated follow-up. Provides management visibility into $500K-$2M in supplement revenue pipeline. Enables data-driven staffing decisions for the supplement team.
