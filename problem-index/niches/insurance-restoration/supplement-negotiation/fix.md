# Revenue Leakage from Unsupplemented Carrier Reductions

**Niche:** [[niches/insurance-restoration/supplement-negotiation/profile|Xactimate Supplement Negotiation]]
**Industry:** [[industries/insurance-restoration|Insurance Restoration]]
**Type:** Fix (Pain Point)
**One-liner:** Restoration companies accept 30-50% of carrier estimate reductions without supplementing because the cost and time of writing supplements for small-dollar items exceeds the expected recovery — leaving $100K-$500K per year on the table.
**Tags:** #automation #revenue-impact

## The Problem
Carriers reduce restoration estimates by an average of 25-35%. Some reductions are legitimate (scope disagreements, pricing adjustments). Many are recoverable through supplements — but the supplement writing process takes 4-8 hours per item group, and if the disputed amount is under $2,000, the labor cost of writing and negotiating the supplement exceeds the expected recovery (given that not all supplements succeed). Restoration companies make a rational choice to write off small reductions — but across 200 jobs per year, these "not worth supplementing" reductions total $100K-$500K in unrecovered revenue.

## Why It's Still Broken
The supplement writing process is too labor-intensive for small-dollar disputes. A $500 disputed item requires the same process as a $5,000 disputed item: import the carrier's estimate, compare line items, identify the discrepancy, write a justification narrative, format the supplement, submit to the carrier, and track the response. There's no way to batch small disputes into efficient submissions or auto-generate justifications for common low-dollar reductions.

## What a Fix Looks Like
A micro-supplement system that batches all carrier reductions below a threshold ($2,000) into a single automated submission per job. For each reduced item, the system auto-generates a one-paragraph justification from a library of pre-written arguments indexed by Xactimate selector code and reduction type. The batch supplement is submitted as a single document with item-level justifications, reducing the per-item effort from 2 hours to 5 minutes. Even a 30% success rate on micro-supplements recovers $30K-$150K annually for a mid-size restoration company.

## Who Feels the Pain
Restoration company owners who know they're leaving money on the table but can't justify the labor cost of supplementing every carrier reduction. Supplement writers who triage their workload by focusing only on large-dollar disputes.

## Impact If Fixed
Recovers $30K-$150K annually in previously written-off carrier reductions. Improves overall estimate recovery rate from 70-75% to 80-85% of submitted amounts. Creates revenue from activity that currently produces zero return because it's below the economic threshold for manual supplement writing.
