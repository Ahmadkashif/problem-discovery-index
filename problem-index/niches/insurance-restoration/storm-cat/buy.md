# Carrier Program Compliance Manager

**Niche:** [[niches/insurance-restoration/storm-cat/profile|Storm & Catastrophe Restoration]]
**Industry:** [[industries/insurance-restoration|Insurance Restoration]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Compliance management tools exist for regulatory requirements, but none track the carrier-specific program requirements that restoration companies must follow to remain in good standing with their insurance company referral programs.
**Tags:** #automation #compliance #data-integration

## The Problem
Restoration companies derive 50-80% of their revenue from carrier referral programs (State Farm, USAA, Allstate, Farmers). Each program has specific requirements: response time SLAs (arrive within 4 hours), documentation standards (photos at specific intervals), pricing constraints (must use Xactimate pricing without markup), communication protocols (update the carrier portal within 24 hours of each milestone), and quality metrics (customer satisfaction scores above threshold). A company participating in 5-8 carrier programs must track 5-8 different sets of SLAs, documentation requirements, and pricing rules simultaneously. Non-compliance results in program suspension — losing the primary revenue source.

## What Already Exists
Quality management systems (ISO 9001 platforms), SLA tracking tools, and carrier-specific portals exist. Carrier portals (USAA Contractor Connection, State Farm Select Service) track jobs within their program. No tool aggregates requirements across all carrier programs into a unified compliance view for the restoration company.

## The Customization Gap
The gap is a multi-carrier compliance dashboard that tracks the company's performance against each carrier program's SLAs, documentation requirements, and quality metrics in real-time. When a job is assigned from a specific carrier, the system auto-populates the carrier's requirements for that job: "State Farm requires initial contact within 2 hours, photos at 0/24/48 hours, daily moisture readings uploaded to their portal, and Xactimate estimate submitted within 5 business days." Flags compliance risks before they become violations.

## Target Customer
Restoration companies participating in 4+ carrier referral programs that generate $5M+ in program revenue and employ a dedicated program compliance manager.

## Impact If Solved
Reduces carrier program compliance violations by 60-70%, preventing program suspensions that cost $500K-$2M in annual revenue. Eliminates the need for a dedicated compliance tracking FTE ($60K-$80K) by automating monitoring and alerting.
