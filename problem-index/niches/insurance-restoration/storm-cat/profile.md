## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Storm and catastrophe restoration is the highest-revenue, most operationally chaotic segment. After a major hurricane or tornado, restoration companies deploy to disaster zones, managing 50-200 simultaneous emergency jobs (tarping, board-up, water extraction) followed by months of full restoration. The operational challenges are unique: supply chain collapse (materials are scarce in disaster zones), labor surge (companies fly in workers from other states), carrier program requirements (each carrier has different emergency service protocols and pricing), and regulatory complexity (state emergency licensing, price gouging laws, FEMA coordination).

## Current Tools & Gaps
Next Gear Solutions and PSA provide basic job management. Xactimate handles estimates. Communication relies on cell phones and group texts. No platform supports the unique operational demands of CAT restoration — mass dispatching of emergency services across a geographic zone, real-time capacity management (which crews are available, where are materials staged), carrier program compliance (different carriers have different emergency service authorization requirements), and financial management for the revenue surge and subsequent collection challenges.
