## Why These Niches

Water damage and fire restoration are the two largest revenue segments, together representing over 40% of the industry. Mold remediation and storm response are surprisingly under-digitized — mold protocols rely on manual moisture readings and paper inspection forms, while storm response shares the same offline-deployment challenges as public adjusting. Multifamily properties and commercial accounts are underserved because most restoration companies optimize for single-family residential work and lack the project management infrastructure for multi-unit or large-commercial jobs. Xactimate supplement negotiation and moisture documentation are high-frequency, rule-intensive workflows where automation would have immediate ROI.
