# Mold Remediation Protocol Management Platform

**Niche:** [[niches/insurance-restoration/mold-remediation/profile|Mold Remediation]]
**Industry:** [[industries/insurance-restoration|Insurance Restoration]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No software platform manages the end-to-end mold remediation workflow — from initial assessment through containment, remediation, clearance testing, and post-remediation documentation — in compliance with IICRC S520 standards.
**Tags:** #workflow-orchestration #automation #compliance

## The Problem
Mold remediation follows a strict protocol: assessment (identify extent and source), scope of work creation, containment construction, HEPA filtration setup, material removal, antimicrobial treatment, clearance testing, and documentation. Each step has specific IICRC S520 requirements — containment must maintain negative air pressure, clearance testing must follow specific sampling protocols, and documentation must prove each step was completed correctly. Remediators manage this on paper checklists, whiteboards, and memory. When a carrier or industrial hygienist questions the remediation, the company scrambles to reconstruct documentation from photos, texts, and crew recollections.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
The mold remediation market is fragmented — thousands of small companies (2-15 employees) doing $500K-$5M in annual revenue. No single company is large enough to justify custom software development, and the technical workflows are too specialized for generic construction or restoration platforms. The compliance requirements vary by state (Texas requires licensed mold assessors, California does not, Florida requires mold-related licenses), adding regulatory complexity.

## What to Build
A mobile-first platform that guides remediation crews through the IICRC S520 protocol step by step: containment verification (negative pressure readings), HEPA filtration logs, material removal documentation, antimicrobial treatment records, and clearance testing coordination. Generates compliance-ready documentation packages automatically as crews complete each step. Includes state-specific regulatory checklists and industrial hygienist coordination (scheduling, sample tracking, results integration).

## Target Customer
Mold remediation companies handling 50-200 mold jobs per year, with 2-10 field crews, currently managing protocols on paper.

## Impact If Built
Reduces documentation time by 60-70% per job. Eliminates compliance gaps that result in failed clearance tests (average rework cost: $3,000-$8,000). Provides audit-ready documentation that withstands carrier and industrial hygienist scrutiny.
