# Ready-for-Pickup Notification System

**Niche:** [[niches/alterations-tailoring/everyday-garment-alterations/profile|Everyday Garment Alterations]]
**Industry:** [[industries/alterations-tailoring|Alterations & Tailoring]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** SMS notification tools like Twilio and SimpleTexting exist but aren't packaged for alterations shops that need to trigger "your garment is ready" messages when work is completed, not on a schedule.
**Tags:** #automation #quick-win #workflow-orchestration

## The Problem
Alterations shops spend 30-60 minutes per day calling customers to say "your garment is ready for pickup." Many calls go to voicemail, requiring follow-up. Customers who don't hear back check in by calling or visiting the shop, interrupting the tailor's work. Some shops just wait for the customer to come in — resulting in garments sitting uncollected for weeks. The communication friction between "work is done" and "customer picks up" is a disproportionate time sink for a process that should be a one-way notification.

## What Already Exists
Twilio, SimpleTexting, and EZTexting provide SMS notification services. Square and Toast offer order-ready notifications for restaurants. Cleanly and SPOT (dry cleaning software) include ready-for-pickup SMS. But SMS platforms require technical setup beyond most alteration shop owners' capabilities, and dry cleaning software bundles features (route delivery, wash tracking) that alterations shops don't need.

## The Customization Gap
The notification system needs to be dead simple: the tailor finishes a garment, scans the ticket barcode (or taps the order on a tablet), and an SMS is automatically sent to the customer's phone number: "Your alteration at [Shop Name] is ready for pickup. Open [hours]. Ticket #[number]." No app download, no account creation for the customer. Optional: multilingual messages matching the customer's preferred language. The system should also auto-send a reminder if the garment isn't picked up within 7 days.

## Target Customer
Any alterations shop processing 15+ garments per day that currently relies on phone calls for pickup notification — spending 30-60 minutes daily on a task that could be automated for $20-40/month.

## Impact If Solved
Saves 30-60 minutes of daily phone call time, reduces garment dwell time (days between completion and pickup) by 40-50%, and decreases interrupting customer inquiries by 60-70%.
