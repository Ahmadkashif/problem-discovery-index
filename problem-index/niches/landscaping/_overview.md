## Why These Niches

Landscaping fragments along service type (maintenance vs. install vs. design), customer type (residential vs. commercial), seasonality (year-round vs. snow overlay), and business scale (solo operator vs. multi-crew operation). These 8 niches cover the full span: the two largest revenue segments (residential maintenance and commercial grounds), the two most digitally neglected service specialties (landscape design-install and irrigation), the two most underserved operator profiles (solo mow-and-blow and seasonal snow removal), and the two highest-ROI automation targets that cut across all landscaping companies (crew routing and estimating). Excluded: tree care / arboriculture (distinct licensing, equipment, and insurance profile), hardscape-only contractors (closer to masonry/concrete trades), and lawn chemical application franchises (Scotts/TruGreen franchise model with different economics).
