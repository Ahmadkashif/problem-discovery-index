# Pre-Arrival Property Preparation Checklist Automation

**Niche:** [[niches/short-term-rentals/luxury-vacation-home-managers/profile|Luxury Vacation Home Managers]]
**Industry:** [[industries/short-term-rentals|Short-Term Rentals]]
**Type:** Fix (Pain Point)
**One-liner:** Preparing a luxury vacation home for guest arrival involves 40-80 item checklists specific to each property — pool heated, hot tub chemicals checked, smart home scenes programmed, welcome amenities placed, specialty linens set, wine fridge stocked — managed through paper checklists and text message confirmations that produce 5-10% error rates visible to guests paying $500+/night.
**Tags:** #automation #workflow-orchestration #worker-facing #quick-win

## The Problem
Each luxury property has a unique pre-arrival preparation checklist: some have pools, others have wine cellars, some have smart home systems requiring scene configuration, others have firewood delivery in winter. A property with a pool, hot tub, smart home, and wine cellar might have a 60-item pre-arrival checklist involving 3-4 different vendors (pool service, smart home tech, provisioning company, cleaning team). The operations manager currently tracks completion through a combination of paper checklists left at the property, text confirmations from vendors, and a personal site visit for high-profile guests. One missed item — the hot tub isn't heated, the WiFi isn't working, the welcome basket is missing — generates a complaint from a $500+/night guest that damages reviews and owner confidence.

## Why It's Still Broken
Each property's checklist is different, and checklists change seasonally (pool items removed in winter, fireplace items added). Breezeway and Properly handle cleaning inspection checklists but don't coordinate multi-vendor pre-arrival preparation. No platform models the per-property, seasonally-adjusted preparation workflow with multi-vendor task assignment and completion verification.

## What a Fix Looks Like
A per-property preparation management tool that: maintains property-specific preparation checklists with seasonal adjustments; assigns tasks to the correct vendor based on task category (pool → pool vendor, smart home → tech vendor, provisions → provisioning company); tracks vendor completion with photo verification; sends a "property ready" confirmation to the operations manager only when all tasks are verified complete; and alerts on overdue tasks 4 hours before guest arrival.

## Who Feels the Pain
Operations managers at luxury STR companies who personally verify property readiness for 10-20 turnovers per week — often driving to properties to confirm what text messages claim is done.

## Impact If Fixed
Pre-arrival preparation errors drop from 5-10% to under 1%. Operations manager site visits drop from daily to exception-only (only when task verification flags an issue). Each prevented guest complaint protects $2,000-$10,000 in future booking revenue per incident (review impact on luxury bookings is amplified because guests have more alternatives and lower tolerance).
