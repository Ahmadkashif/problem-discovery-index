# Urban Multi-Property Managers

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/short-term-rentals|Short-Term Rentals]]
**Category:** High Market Share

## Profile
**Market Size:** $7-9B
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~35-40%
**Digital Adoption:** Medium-High — Guesty or Hostaway for PMS, PriceLabs for pricing, but operations coordination (cleaning, maintenance, guest issues) remains fragmented across WhatsApp groups and phone calls
**Target Buyer:** Professional STR management company owner operating 20-200 urban listings across apartments, condos, and townhomes
**Automation Potential:** High — guest communication, cleaning dispatch, pricing overrides, and review management are automatable

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Urban multi-property STR managers handle high turnover density in geographically concentrated portfolios: 20-200 units within a single metro area, with average stays of 2-4 nights and back-to-back bookings requiring military-precision cleaning coordination. The economics differ from vacation rental management: urban properties compete on location and price (not amenities), average nightly rates are lower ($100-$250 vs. $300-$800 for vacation homes), and the margin depends on minimizing operational cost per turnover. Guest demographics skew business travelers and event attendees — less damage risk but higher expectations for consistency and responsiveness.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Guesty and Hostaway provide channel management and booking automation. PriceLabs and Beyond Pricing handle dynamic pricing. Breezeway handles cleaning task management. No platform integrates all three workflows — pricing, guest communication, and operations — into a single urban-optimized dashboard that accounts for same-day turnovers, proximity-based cleaning routing, and real-time occupancy across a dense portfolio.
