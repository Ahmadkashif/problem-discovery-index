# Regulatory Compliance Tracking

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/short-term-rentals|Short-Term Rentals]]
**Category:** Highly Automatable

## Profile
**Market Size:** $1-2B (embedded)
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~5-8% (embedded operational cost and risk)
**Digital Adoption:** Low — most operators track STR permits, occupancy tax filings, and municipal regulations manually or don't track them at all until they receive a violation notice
**Target Buyer:** Multi-market STR operator, compliance manager at a property management company, or STR host in a heavily regulated municipality
**Automation Potential:** High — permit renewal tracking, occupancy tax calculation and filing, regulatory change monitoring, and violation response are rule-based and highly automatable

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
STR regulation is the fastest-changing compliance landscape in real estate: cities and counties enact new STR ordinances continuously, existing regulations are amended, enforcement mechanisms vary from complaint-driven to proactive inspection, and penalties range from $500 fines to criminal misdemeanor charges and forced delisting. An operator with properties in 3 municipalities may face 3 completely different regulatory frameworks: one requires a business license and quarterly occupancy tax filing, another requires a conditional use permit with annual renewal and neighbor notification, and the third bans STR entirely except in specific zones. Keeping track of these requirements — and knowing when they change — is a full-time compliance job at scale.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Avalara and Lodging Tax automate occupancy tax calculation for some jurisdictions. Granicus Host Compliance provides regulatory monitoring for municipalities (not operators). STR advocacy groups (VRMA, local host organizations) share regulatory updates informally. No platform provides operators with a consolidated view of their regulatory obligations across all jurisdictions where they operate, with automatic change detection and compliance deadline tracking.
