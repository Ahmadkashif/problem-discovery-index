# Pet Amenity Provisioning and Inventory Management

**Niche:** [[niches/short-term-rentals/pet-friendly-str-specialists/profile|Pet-Friendly STR Specialists]]
**Industry:** [[industries/short-term-rentals|Short-Term Rentals]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** STR inventory management tools (Breezeway, Turno) track linens, toiletries, and cleaning supplies, but pet-friendly properties also stock pet bowls, beds, waste bags, treats, and pet-specific cleaning supplies — items that need restocking based on pet booking frequency, not general occupancy, and that no inventory system tracks separately.
**Tags:** #automation #workflow-orchestration #quick-win

## The Problem
A pet-friendly property needs stocked: food and water bowls, a pet bed, waste bags, welcome treats, a lint roller, pet-safe cleaning spray, and sometimes a pet gate. These items wear out faster at properties with frequent pet bookings — the pet bed needs replacement every 3-4 months, waste bags restock bi-weekly during high season, and treats must be fresh. Currently, the host or cleaner visually checks pet supplies during turnover and texts the host when something runs low. Items are missed, and the next pet-owning guest arrives to find no waste bags or a chewed-up pet bed — undermining the "pet-friendly" positioning that generates the premium.

## What Already Exists
Breezeway and Turno manage cleaning supply inventory and restocking for STR operations. These systems track standard consumables (toilet paper, soap, coffee pods) but don't have pet supply categories, don't trigger restocking based on pet booking frequency, and don't account for pet supply degradation rates.

## The Customization Gap
A pet amenity management tool needs: (1) pet-specific supply categories with per-item par levels and replacement schedules; (2) restocking triggers based on pet booking density (not general occupancy — a property with 10 pet bookings per month depletes supplies 3x faster than one with 3 pet bookings); (3) per-property pet amenity checklists included in the turnover inspection workflow; (4) pet supply ordering integration with bulk suppliers (wholesale pet supply accounts).

## Target Customer
Pet-friendly STR operators managing 10+ pet-accepting properties — approximately 10,000-20,000 operators nationally.

## Impact If Solved
Pet supply stockouts drop from 15-20% of pet bookings to under 3%. Guest satisfaction for pet-owning guests improves (pet amenity quality is the #1 factor in pet-specific review mentions). Supply cost per property drops 10-15% through bulk ordering triggered by consumption-based restock alerts instead of over-ordering from uncertainty.
