# Pet Damage Assessment and Fee Recovery Workflow

**Niche:** [[niches/short-term-rentals/pet-friendly-str-specialists/profile|Pet-Friendly STR Specialists]]
**Industry:** [[industries/short-term-rentals|Short-Term Rentals]]
**Type:** Fix (Pain Point)
**One-liner:** When a pet damages a property, the host must document the damage within Airbnb's 14-day window, obtain repair estimates, submit a claim through the Resolution Center, and often dispute the platform's reduced payout — a 3-5 hour process per incident that recovers only 40-60% of actual damage costs, discouraging hosts from accepting pets at all.
**Tags:** #automation #compliance #revenue-impact #quick-win

## The Problem
A dog scratches hardwood floors during a 3-night stay. The host discovers it during turnover cleaning (often 4+ hours after checkout), must photograph the damage, compare to pre-stay photos, obtain a repair estimate from a flooring contractor, file an Airbnb Resolution Center claim within 14 days, and then negotiate with Airbnb's claims team — which routinely applies "wear and tear" deductions that reduce the $800 repair cost to a $350 payout. The entire process takes 3-5 hours of the host's time for partial recovery. Many pet-friendly hosts absorb $2,000-$5,000/year in unrecovered pet damage because the claim process isn't worth the time.

## Why It's Still Broken
Airbnb's Resolution Center is designed for general damage claims, not pet-specific damage. Pet damage has specific characteristics (carpet stains, furniture scratches, yard digging, odor remediation) that require pet-specific documentation standards and repair cost benchmarks. Airbnb's claims adjusters don't specialize in pet damage and apply inconsistent "wear and tear" deductions. No tool helps the host build a claim-ready documentation package specifically for pet damage with pre-stay baseline photos, pet-specific damage categorization, and industry-standard repair cost references.

## What a Fix Looks Like
A pet damage claim management tool that: (1) prompts pre-stay baseline photos of high-risk areas (carpets, furniture, floors, yard) before each pet booking; (2) provides a post-stay damage assessment checklist by damage category (scratches, stains, odor, chewing, digging); (3) auto-generates a claim package with before/after photo comparison, damage category classification, and repair cost estimates from a pet damage cost database; (4) submits the claim to Airbnb/VRBO Resolution Center in the format that maximizes recovery. Learning from claim outcomes to improve documentation quality over time.

## Who Feels the Pain
Pet-friendly STR hosts who process 5-20 pet damage claims per year — approximately 30,000-50,000 operators nationally who accept pets and have experienced property damage.

## Impact If Fixed
Claim recovery rate improves from 40-60% to 70-85% of actual damage costs. Claim preparation time drops from 3-5 hours to 30-45 minutes per incident. Hosts who stopped accepting pets due to unrecovered damage re-enter the pet-friendly market — each returning property adds $5,000-$15,000 in annual revenue from the pet booking premium.
