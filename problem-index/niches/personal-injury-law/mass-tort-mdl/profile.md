**Share of Parent Industry:** ~11%

**Target Buyer:** Mass tort firm partner managing 500-10,000+ plaintiff cases in a single litigation
**Automation Potential:** Very High — plaintiff intake screening, census sheet completion, document collection tracking, and settlement fund allocation are high-volume repetitive tasks operating at scales that single-plaintiff PI tools cannot handle

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Mass tort and MDL litigation operates at a completely different scale than single-plaintiff PI. A firm handling Camp Lejeune water contamination, 3M earplugs, or Roundup cases may have 2,000-10,000 individual plaintiffs, each requiring a signed retainer, completed plaintiff fact sheet, medical records proving exposure and injury, and ongoing communication about case status. The operational challenge is not legal strategy — it's industrial-scale plaintiff management. Census sheets (standardized forms required by the MDL court) must be completed for every plaintiff. Medical records must be obtained, reviewed, and matched to qualifying diagnoses. Unqualified plaintiffs (signed up through mass advertising but don't meet case criteria) must be identified and released quickly to avoid wasting resources. The economics are entirely different: individual case values may be $20K-$200K, but volume makes the aggregate worth billions.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Filevine has a mass tort module with intake workflows and census tracking, but plaintiff qualification logic is weak — it cannot automatically screen medical records against qualifying diagnosis criteria. Salesforce-based custom builds handle the CRM aspects but require expensive consultants to maintain. LegalMation and CaseGlide focus on defense-side mass tort. No existing tool handles the full lifecycle: lead intake, qualification screening, census completion, medical record collection tracking, settlement fund allocation modeling, and plaintiff communication at scale.
