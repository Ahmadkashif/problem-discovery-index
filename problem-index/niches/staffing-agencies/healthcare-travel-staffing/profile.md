**Automation Potential:** Medium — credential verification and compliance tracking are highly automatable, but relationship-driven placement for travel assignments requires human judgment

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Healthcare staffing operates under regulatory requirements that do not exist in other staffing verticals: every placed worker must have verified licenses (state-specific nursing licenses, certifications), current immunization records, background checks, drug screens, and facility-specific competency assessments. A single credential lapse can trigger a CMS violation for the client facility. Travel assignments add complexity — nurses work 13-week contracts in states where they may need temporary licenses, and housing/travel stipend compliance must follow IRS rules to maintain tax-free status. The compliance documentation burden is 5-10x heavier than general staffing, and the cost of failure (regulatory fines, patient safety) is orders of magnitude higher.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Aya Healthcare, Cross Country, and AMN Healthcare dominate travel nursing with proprietary tech stacks. ShiftMed and ShiftKey are marketplace platforms for per diem. Bullhorn and BlueSky serve mid-market healthcare staffing. Credential management is handled through bolt-on tools (Modio, Complio) or manual spreadsheet tracking. The gap is real-time credential monitoring across hundreds of placed clinicians — tracking license renewals, certification expirations, and facility-specific requirements that change with each placement.
