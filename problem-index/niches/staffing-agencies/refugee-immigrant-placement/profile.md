# Refugee & Immigrant Workforce Placement

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/staffing-agencies|Staffing Agencies]]
**Category:** Underserved Audience

## Profile
**Market Size:** $3-5B
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~2%
**Digital Adoption:** Low — most placement happens through community organizations and resettlement agencies using paper files and personal relationships; mainstream staffing tech does not accommodate the unique needs
**Target Buyer:** Community-based staffing coordinator, refugee resettlement agency workforce director, or social enterprise operator
**Automation Potential:** Low-Medium — the human services component is essential, but document management and skill translation can be significantly automated

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Refugee and immigrant workforce placement involves challenges that mainstream staffing agencies are not equipped to handle: work authorization documentation (EADs, refugee travel documents, TPS designations) with complex eligibility rules and expiration timelines; skill translation from foreign credentials and work experience that do not map to US occupational categories; limited English proficiency requiring multilingual communication; cultural orientation to US workplace norms; and often, coordination with case managers, social workers, and government agencies. The workers in this population are frequently highly skilled (engineers, doctors, skilled tradespeople in their home countries) but cannot access roles matching their experience because their credentials are not recognized in the US.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Resettlement agencies use case management platforms (Apricot, ETO by Bonterra) for social services tracking, but these are not staffing tools — they do not manage job orders, candidate matching, or employer relationships. Mainstream staffing ATS platforms (Bullhorn, Avionté) lack fields for work authorization types, do not understand EAD categories, and cannot track the complex timelines of asylum, TPS, and refugee status adjustments. There is no tool that bridges the case management and staffing workflows for this population.
