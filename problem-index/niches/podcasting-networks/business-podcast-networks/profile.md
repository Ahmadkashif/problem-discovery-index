**Target Buyer:** CEO / Sales Director / Content Director

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Business podcast networks (Hubspot Podcast Network, Ramsey Network, Kast Media) produce content targeting entrepreneurs, executives, and professionals. Their revenue model differs from entertainment networks: alongside advertising ($20–$50 CPM), they monetize through sponsored content partnerships, lead generation for the host's business, course/product sales, and speaking engagements. Guest booking is a core operational function — business shows feature 100–200 guests per year across the network, requiring coordination, research, and relationship management. Content repurposing (podcast → YouTube → clips → newsletter → social) is essential for discoverability but creates a multiplication of production work.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Megaphone and Simplecast handle hosting and dynamic ad insertion. Chartable (now Spotify) provides attribution analytics. Calendly handles guest scheduling. But the guest pipeline — sourcing, vetting, scheduling, prep materials, post-interview follow-up — runs on email and personal relationships. Content repurposing is done manually: an editor creates YouTube clips, a social media manager writes post captions, a VA creates show notes. The entire downstream content workflow from a single episode recording generates 10–15 separate deliverables, all coordinated by hand.
