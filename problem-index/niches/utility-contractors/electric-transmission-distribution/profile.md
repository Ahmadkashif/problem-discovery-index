**Parent Industry:** [[industries/utility-contractors|Utility Contractors]]
**Category:** High Market Share

## Profile
**Market Size:** $55-60B
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~38%
**Digital Adoption:** Medium — Procore and Viewpoint Vista handle project management. GIS platforms (ESRI ArcGIS) manage asset mapping. SCADA monitors real-time grid operations. But vegetation management, right-of-way encroachment detection, and outage restoration dispatch are still heavily manual processes.
**Target Buyer:** Electric utility contractor owner, project manager, or operations director
**Automation Potential:** High — vegetation encroachment is a visual detection task, outage restoration dispatch is an optimization problem, and line inspection from drone/aerial imagery is a CV opportunity

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Electric T&D contracting covers the full lifecycle of power delivery infrastructure: new construction (transmission lines, substations, distribution feeders), maintenance (vegetation management, pole replacement, transformer upgrades), and emergency restoration (storm response, mutual aid). The work is safety-critical — contact with energized conductors is immediately fatal, and improper construction can cause wildfires (as demonstrated by PG&E's catastrophic failures). Vegetation management alone is a $10B+ annual spend: utilities must keep trees and vegetation clear of power lines to prevent outages and fires. Currently, vegetation management is assessed by line patrol — crews driving or flying routes to visually identify encroachment, then scheduling trim crews. The assessment is subjective, inconsistent, and produces a one-time snapshot rather than continuous monitoring. Outage restoration during storms requires dispatching dozens of crews to hundreds of outage locations, prioritizing by public safety impact and customer count — a complex optimization that dispatch managers currently perform using paper maps and radio communication.

## Current Tools & Gaps
ESRI ArcGIS maps utility assets (poles, conductors, transformers, substations) but doesn't detect changes or encroachments. SCADA monitors real-time electrical conditions but doesn't optimize field crew dispatch. Procore handles construction project management but not utility-specific workflows. No integrated platform connects asset mapping, vegetation encroachment detection, outage identification, and crew dispatch into a single operational system.
