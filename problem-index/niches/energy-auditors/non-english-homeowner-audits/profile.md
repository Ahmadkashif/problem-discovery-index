# Non-English Homeowner Audits

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/energy-auditors|Energy Auditors]]
**Category:** Underserved Audience

## Profile
**Market Size:** $300-500M
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~6-10%
**Digital Adoption:** Low — Audit reports and program materials are available almost exclusively in English; bilingual auditors are scarce and command premium rates
**Target Buyer:** Bilingual auditor / community-based organization program manager / utility program equity officer
**Automation Potential:** Medium — Report translation and multilingual communication are high-value automation targets, but the in-home consultation requires language skills that technology can augment but not replace

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
An estimated 25 million US households have limited English proficiency (LEP), representing 15-20% of the residential market. These households are disproportionately low-income, live in older, less efficient housing, and would benefit most from energy efficiency programs — but they are severely underserved. Energy audit reports, utility program applications, retrofit proposals, and financing documents are almost exclusively in English. Bilingual auditors are scarce, and even when available, the technical vocabulary of building science (blower door, R-value, air changes per hour) has no standard translation in most languages. The result: LEP households either do not apply for programs they qualify for, do not understand audit findings, or do not authorize recommended work because they cannot evaluate the proposal.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Google Translate and generic translation services cannot handle technical building science terminology accurately. Utility programs produce Spanish-language program descriptions in some markets, but audit reports and proposals remain English-only. No energy audit software generates reports or proposals in languages other than English. Community-based organizations (CBOs) serving immigrant communities provide cultural bridges but lack technical energy audit expertise.
