# Sales-Ready Energy Audit Report Generator

**Niche:** [[niches/energy-auditors/home-performance-contractors/profile|Home Performance Contractors]]
**Industry:** [[industries/energy-auditors|Energy Auditors]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Energy modeling software generates technical reports that building scientists understand, but homeowners need visual, plain-language presentations that show why improvements are worth the investment.
**Tags:** #llm #text-generation #automation #revenue-impact

## The Problem
TREAT and Ekotrope produce technically accurate audit reports: tables of R-values, CFM50 readings, AFUE ratings, and modeled savings in therms and kWh. A homeowner receiving this report sees incomprehensible technical jargon and makes no purchasing decision. The home performance contractor must manually translate the technical output into a sales-ready presentation: annotated thermal images showing heat loss, before/after comfort descriptions, dollar savings (not energy unit savings), ROI calculations, and rebate/financing information. This translation is done in PowerPoint, Word, or Canva — tools that have no connection to the energy model.

## What Already Exists
TREAT and Ekotrope generate PDF reports with technical data. Proposal generation tools (PandaDoc, Proposify, Better Proposals) create professional-looking sales documents. Thermal imaging software (FLIR Tools) annotates infrared images. Home improvement visualization tools (Hover, EagleView) show exterior improvements. None of these pull from energy model outputs to generate homeowner-facing content with savings translations and rebate information.

## The Customization Gap
HPCs need a report generator that takes energy model outputs (savings by measure in energy units), translates them into homeowner-friendly formats (dollar savings, comfort improvements, environmental impact, monthly payment comparisons), incorporates thermal images with automated annotations ("this blue area shows heat escaping through your attic hatch"), includes locally available rebates and financing options with estimated out-of-pocket costs, and produces a branded, professional presentation that the contractor delivers in person or via email. The tool must handle the 50+ measure types that HPCs recommend, with plain-language explanations for each.

## Target Customer
Home performance contractors who audit 50+ homes per year and lose 20-30% of potential retrofits because technical audit reports fail to motivate homeowner action.

## Impact If Solved
Increases retrofit close rates by 15-25% by presenting audit findings in a format that homeowners understand and find compelling. For an HPC closing $500K in annual retrofit work at current rates, a 20% improvement in close rates adds $100K in revenue.
