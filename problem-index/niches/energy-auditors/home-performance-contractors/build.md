# Audit-to-Proposal-to-Install Workflow Platform

**Niche:** [[niches/energy-auditors/home-performance-contractors/profile|Home Performance Contractors]]
**Industry:** [[industries/energy-auditors|Energy Auditors]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No single tool connects a home performance contractor's field audit data through energy modeling to a customer-facing sales proposal and installation project plan.
**Tags:** #workflow-orchestration #automation #data-integration #revenue-impact

## The Problem
A home performance contractor audits a home, identifies $12,000 in recommended improvements (attic insulation, air sealing, duct sealing, furnace upgrade), and must convert these findings into a compelling homeowner proposal. The current workflow: take field notes on paper, enter data into TREAT or Ekotrope for modeling, generate a technical audit report (which homeowners do not understand), manually build a sales proposal in Word or PandaDoc (with before/after photos, savings estimates, financing options, and rebate information), then create a separate installation project plan (materials list, crew scheduling, permit requirements). Each step is a separate tool and a separate data entry process. The contractor spends 4-8 hours between the audit and the proposal delivery — time during which the homeowner's urgency cools and competing contractors submit their own bids.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
Home performance contracting sits between energy auditing (building science) and home improvement (construction), requiring a platform that spans both domains. Energy modeling tools are built for engineers; proposal tools are built for salespeople; project management tools are built for contractors. Building a platform that integrates all three requires deep domain knowledge in building science, residential sales, and construction project management — an unusual combination. The market is also small: an estimated 5,000-10,000 active HPCs in the US, most generating $200K-$2M in annual revenue.

## What to Build
An integrated platform where the HPC captures field data on a tablet, runs energy modeling in the background, and generates a homeowner-facing proposal with: before/after photos, plain-language problem descriptions, projected savings by measure, available rebates auto-matched to the home's location and utility, financing options with monthly payment estimates, and a project timeline. When the homeowner approves, the proposal converts to a project plan with materials lists, crew assignments, and permit requirements. One data entry drives the entire workflow.

## Target Customer
Home performance contractors doing 50+ residential audits per year who currently spend 4-8 hours between audit and proposal delivery, losing 20-30% of leads to delayed follow-up.

## Impact If Built
Reduces audit-to-proposal time from 4-8 hours to under 1 hour, increasing proposal delivery speed by 4-8x. Faster proposals improve close rates by an estimated 20-30%, translating to $100K-$300K in additional annual revenue for a typical HPC.
