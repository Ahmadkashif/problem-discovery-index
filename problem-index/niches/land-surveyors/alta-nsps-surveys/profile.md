## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
ALTA/NSPS surveys are the standard survey product required for commercial real estate transactions — any commercial property purchase, refinance, or development project requires one. The survey follows the ALTA/NSPS 2021 Minimum Standard Detail Requirements, which specify exactly what must be shown, how it must be measured, and what accuracy standards apply. The client (typically a title company or lender) selects from "Table A" optional items (20 options ranging from flood zone determination to underground utility location). This standardization makes ALTA surveys the most commoditized and highest-volume commercial survey product, but the compliance requirements are exacting — a survey that fails to meet the standard is rejected by the title company.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Survey firms use CAD (AutoCAD, Carlson) with ALTA-specific templates and layering standards. Some firms have developed proprietary checklists for Table A compliance. Title companies use SoftPro and RamQuest for ordering and tracking surveys. But no tool automates the compliance verification process — checking whether every Table A item selected by the client has been addressed on the plat, whether the certification language matches the 2021 standard, whether boundary tolerances meet precision requirements. This is done by a senior surveyor reviewing the plat manually, often catching omissions that would cause title company rejection.
