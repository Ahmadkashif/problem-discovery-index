## Why These Niches

Dog walking and boarding/daycare together represent over 75% of pet services revenue, making them essential high-market-share segments — but they face very different operational challenges (solo mobile workers vs. facility-based businesses). Medical/senior pet sitting and mobile grooming are fast-growing specializations where operators use generic tools poorly adapted to their workflows. Rural pet owners and exotic pet owners are systematically underserved by platforms built for urban dog owners. Client communication and health documentation were selected as automation targets because pet service providers spend 1-3 hours daily on texts, updates, and care logs that follow predictable patterns.
