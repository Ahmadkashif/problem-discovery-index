# Off-Platform Client Migration Tool

**Niche:** [[niches/pet-services/dog-walking-daily-care/profile|Dog Walking & Daily Pet Care]]
**Industry:** [[industries/pet-services|Pet Services]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** CRM and booking tools exist but none help dog walkers professionally transition clients from Rover/Wag to direct booking — the single most impactful revenue move for an independent walker.
**Tags:** #automation #revenue-impact #workflow-orchestration #quick-win

## The Problem
Independent dog walkers acquire clients through Rover or Wag, then want to move them off-platform to avoid the 15-20% commission ($3-5 per walk, $60-100/month per recurring client). But the transition is awkward: the walker must convince the client to switch without violating platform terms, provide a professional booking alternative (not just "text me"), and maintain the service quality indicators (GPS tracking, walk reports, photos) that clients have come to expect from the platform. Many walkers lose clients during the transition because their "direct booking" experience feels less professional.

## What Already Exists
Time To Pet, PetPocketbook, and Precise Petcare offer independent pet service management with client portals, scheduling, invoicing, and GPS tracking. These tools provide the infrastructure for off-platform operation. But they don't address the migration itself — the process of transitioning a client relationship from platform-mediated to direct while maintaining trust and professionalism.

## The Customization Gap
The tool needs a client migration workflow: a professional "welcome to direct booking" onboarding package (personal booking link, service agreement, payment setup), feature parity with platform experience (GPS walk tracking, real-time photo updates, end-of-walk report cards), and a savings calculator showing the client that direct booking means lower prices (the walker can split the saved commission). The transition should feel like an upgrade, not a downgrade.

## Target Customer
Independent walkers doing 20+ walks per week on Rover/Wag who are paying $300-600/month in platform commissions and have built enough client trust to transition to direct booking.

## Impact If Solved
Saves the walker $300-600/month in platform commissions while potentially lowering client prices by 5-10%, creating a win-win that strengthens client retention and increases walker take-home pay by 15-20%.
