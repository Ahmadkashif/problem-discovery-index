# Unified Booking Inbox

**Niche:** [[niches/pet-services/client-communication-booking/profile|Client Communication & Booking]]
**Industry:** [[industries/pet-services|Pet Services]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Unified inbox tools like Front and Intercom exist for businesses but aren't adapted for pet service providers who receive booking requests across text, Instagram DM, Facebook Messenger, and email — each requiring different response formats.
**Tags:** #automation #workflow-orchestration #data-integration #quick-win

## The Problem
A pet sitter receives booking requests via text (40%), Instagram DM (25%), Facebook Messenger (20%), email (10%), and phone calls (5%). Each channel requires a different response format and none are connected. The sitter checks 4+ apps throughout the day, responds to requests while walking dogs, and inevitably misses messages — losing bookings and frustrating clients who don't hear back for hours. There's no single view of "all incoming requests" and no way to convert a DM conversation into a booked appointment without manual transcription.

## What Already Exists
Front, Intercom, and Zendesk unify multi-channel communication for businesses. Time To Pet offers a booking portal for pet services. But unified inbox tools are designed for teams at companies, not solo operators on their phones, and they cost $19-99/user/month. Pet service booking tools require clients to use a specific portal rather than their preferred communication channel. Neither category solves the solo provider's problem of managing bookings across the channels clients actually use.

## The Customization Gap
The tool needs to aggregate messages from text, Instagram, Facebook, and email into one mobile-first inbox, with booking detection ("Can you walk Bella on Thursday?") that auto-suggests a booking response with availability from the provider's calendar. Confirmed bookings are auto-added to the calendar with client and pet details. Automated responses handle common queries ("What are your rates?" → rate card; "Are you available next week?" → availability check) while flagging complex requests for personal response.

## Target Customer
Independent pet service providers handling 10+ booking inquiries per week across 3+ communication channels who miss 2-3 bookings per week from delayed responses.

## Impact If Solved
Reduces response time from hours to minutes, recovers 2-3 lost bookings per week worth $100-300, and consolidates communication management from 4+ apps to 1 — saving 30-60 minutes daily.
