# Trust & Verification Layer for Informal Sitters

**Niche:** [[niches/pet-services/rural-suburban-services/profile|Rural & Suburban Pet Services]]
**Industry:** [[industries/pet-services|Pet Services]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Background check and trust verification services exist (Checkr, Sterling) but aren't packaged for informal pet sitters in low-density areas who need affordable, community-appropriate vetting.
**Tags:** #data-integration #quick-win #automation #compliance

## The Problem
When a rural pet owner finds a potential sitter through Facebook or a community referral, they have no way to verify the person's reliability beyond gut feel and mutual acquaintances. They're handing over house keys and entrusting a living animal to someone whose qualifications are "seems nice on Facebook." Urban platforms (Rover, Wag) provide background checks and reviews as trust infrastructure — but these platforms don't serve rural areas, and their per-sitter verification costs don't scale to someone who sits for 2-3 people informally.

## What Already Exists
Checkr and Sterling offer background check APIs. Rover and Wag include background checks for their sitters. Care.com provides caregiver background checks. But these are built for professional caregivers on commercial platforms — the cost ($25-75 per check) and process (SSN-based, corporate interface) feel heavy for a neighbor who watches your dog twice a year.

## The Customization Gap
The verification needs to be: affordable ($10-15 per check, funded by the sitter or split with the pet owner), simple (no corporate onboarding flow — phone-based identity verification), community-visible (a "verified" badge that the sitter can share in local Facebook groups), and pet-relevant (includes animal cruelty record check, not just generic criminal background). Add a lightweight pet care certification (free online module covering basic pet first aid, feeding, medication administration) to build competence alongside trust.

## Target Customer
Pet owners in low-density areas who use informal sitters found through community networks and want a lightweight, affordable way to verify trustworthiness before handing over house keys and pets.

## Impact If Solved
Enables confident informal pet care arrangements in communities without professional sitters, reduces the anxiety that prevents pet owners from using community networks, and provides informal sitters with a credential that increases their booking rate.
