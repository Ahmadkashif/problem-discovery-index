## Why These Niches

Commercial real estate fragments along asset type (office vs. industrial vs. retail), geography (metro vs. rural), buyer demographic (institutional vs. immigrant investor vs. minority entrepreneur), and operational function (deal-making vs. back-office administration). These 8 niches cover the two largest revenue-generating asset specializations (office leasing and industrial), the two most digitally neglected segments (rural brokers operating without CoStar and owner-occupied deal brokers ignored by institutional tech), two underserved buyer populations (minority-owned firms lacking network access and immigrant investors navigating CRE without culturally-aligned services), and two high-volume back-office workflows ripe for automation (lease administration and commission tracking). Excluded: institutional REIT asset management (distinct industry), residential-to-commercial crossover agents (too diffuse), and national platform brokerages (already well-tooled).
