# SBA Loan Document Assembly and Tracking

**Niche:** [[niches/commercial-real-estate/owner-occupied-small-business-properties/profile|Owner-Occupied Small Business Properties]]
**Industry:** [[industries/commercial-real-estate|Commercial Real Estate]]
**Type:** Fix (Pain Point)
**One-liner:** SBA commercial real estate loan packages require 15-25 distinct documents from the borrower, the property, and the business — assembled manually by the broker and borrower over 4-8 weeks of back-and-forth emails, with no standardized checklist or tracking system, causing 30-40% of deals to miss their projected closing date.
**Tags:** #automation #workflow-orchestration #compliance #quick-win

## The Problem
An SBA 504 loan package requires: business tax returns (3 years), personal tax returns (3 years), personal financial statement, business plan or P&L, property appraisal, environmental report (Phase I ESA), title report, purchase agreement, entity documents, insurance quotes, and more — varying by lender and SBA district. The borrower's broker, the SBA lender, the CDC (Certified Development Company), and the borrower each contribute documents on different timelines. The broker tracks completion status in email threads and spreadsheets, chasing missing documents manually. One missing document delays the entire loan package by 1-2 weeks.

## Why It's Still Broken
SBA loan document requirements vary by lender, CDC, and SBA district office. No standardized digital checklist exists because each lender customizes requirements. SBA lender portals accept document uploads but don't provide collaborative tracking between broker, borrower, and lender. The SBA lending market is fragmented across 2,500+ preferred lenders with no common technology platform.

## What a Fix Looks Like
A collaborative document checklist and tracking tool that generates a lender-specific document requirement list, assigns each document to the responsible party (borrower, broker, appraiser, environmental consultant), tracks completion status with automated reminders, and provides a single upload portal accessible to all parties. Pre-populated templates for common documents (personal financial statement, business plan outline) reduce borrower friction.

## Who Feels the Pain
Buyer's brokers handling 5-15 SBA transactions per year, where each transaction requires 10-20 hours of document chase — and SBA loan officers who process the incomplete packages.

## Impact If Fixed
Document assembly drops from 4-8 weeks to 2-3 weeks. Closing date accuracy improves from 60% to 85%+. Each week of accelerated closing saves the borrower $2,000-$5,000 in opportunity cost (rent payments on their current space while waiting to occupy the purchased building).
