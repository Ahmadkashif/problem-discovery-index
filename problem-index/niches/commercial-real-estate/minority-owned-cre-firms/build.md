# Minority CRE Deal Sourcing and Investor Matching Platform

**Niche:** [[niches/commercial-real-estate/minority-owned-cre-firms/profile|Minority-Owned CRE Firms]]
**Industry:** [[industries/commercial-real-estate|Commercial Real Estate]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No platform connects minority CRE developers with mission-aligned equity investors — CDFIs, impact funds, and community-focused family offices — who specifically seek diverse developer partnerships for projects in underserved markets.
**Tags:** #automation #data-integration #revenue-impact #ai-platform

## The Problem
A minority developer with a $5M mixed-use project in a majority-minority neighborhood needs $1.5M in equity to close their capital stack. Institutional equity sources (private equity real estate funds) focus on $20M+ deals and source through established broker relationships the minority developer doesn't have. CDFIs (Community Development Financial Institutions), impact investment funds, and community-focused family offices exist and actively seek minority-developer partnerships — but there's no marketplace connecting them. The developer spends 6-12 months networking at conferences, cold-emailing impact investors, and navigating CDFI application processes manually.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
The intersection of CRE technology and diversity-focused capital is too niche for mainstream CRE platforms (CoStar, CREXi). Impact investing platforms (ImpactAssets, Calvert) focus on fund-level investments, not direct project-level matchmaking. CDFI directories exist but aren't integrated into deal flow. Building this requires relationships with both minority developer organizations (NAIOP diversity initiatives, local real estate boards) and impact capital sources — communities that CRE tech companies don't typically engage.

## What to Build
A deal-capital matching platform where minority developers post project summaries (location, project type, capital needed, community impact metrics) and mission-aligned investors browse, filter by geography and project type, and express interest. Include CDFI and Opportunity Zone eligibility screening, NMTC allocation tracking, and community impact scoring (jobs created, affordable units, minority contractor spend). Revenue model: success-based fee (0.5-1% of equity raised) plus investor subscription for deal flow access.

## Target Customer
Minority-owned CRE development firms pursuing projects in the $2-20M range — approximately 3,000-5,000 active firms nationally. Investor side: 200+ CDFIs, 50+ impact real estate funds, and 500+ community-focused family offices.

## Impact If Built
Minority developers access equity capital 3-6 months faster, reducing carrying costs and project risk. Impact investors get curated deal flow from diverse developers they struggle to source through traditional channels. Each successful $5M project match generates $25,000-$50,000 in platform revenue and creates measurable community impact.
