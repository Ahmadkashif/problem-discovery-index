# EB-5 Direct Investment Property Compliance Platform

**Niche:** [[niches/commercial-real-estate/immigrant-investor-cre-services/profile|Immigrant Investor CRE Services]]
**Industry:** [[industries/commercial-real-estate|Commercial Real Estate]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No platform tracks EB-5 direct investment compliance for individual CRE purchases — job creation documentation, capital-at-risk verification, and I-526/I-829 petition evidence assembly — leaving immigration attorneys to manage compliance through spreadsheets and document folders over a 5-7 year conditional residency period.
**Tags:** #automation #compliance #workflow-orchestration #data-integration

## The Problem
An EB-5 direct investor purchasing a commercial property (hotel, restaurant, commercial building with tenants) must demonstrate that their investment created 10+ full-time jobs, that capital remained at risk throughout the conditional residency period, and that the investment was made with lawfully obtained funds. The compliance documentation spans 5-7 years: quarterly payroll reports proving job creation, tax returns showing continued investment, and evidence that the business is operating. Immigration attorneys managing 10-50 EB-5 clients track this compliance manually — a single missed filing or documentation gap can jeopardize the investor's permanent residency petition.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
The EB-5 direct investment market is small relative to regional center investments (which pool multiple investors into a single project with centralized compliance). Direct investors are geographically scattered, each with a unique property and business, making centralized compliance management harder. Immigration law firms are small (2-10 attorneys) and don't budget for enterprise software. The 2019-2024 EB-5 program uncertainty (lapsed authorization, rule changes) further deterred investment in purpose-built tools.

## What to Build
A compliance management platform for EB-5 direct investors and their immigration attorneys: tracks job creation against the 10-job requirement using payroll data integration, monitors capital-at-risk status from financial statements, generates I-829 petition evidence packages, and provides a compliance dashboard showing petition readiness across the attorney's client portfolio. Automated alerts for quarterly filing deadlines and documentation gaps.

## Target Customer
Immigration attorneys managing EB-5 direct investment cases — approximately 500-800 firms nationally with active EB-5 practices. Average contract value: $200-$500/month per firm, covering 10-50 active client cases.

## Impact If Built
Attorneys managing 20+ EB-5 cases reduce compliance tracking from 5-10 hours per client per quarter to 1-2 hours. I-829 petition evidence packages assemble in hours instead of weeks. Most importantly, documentation gaps that could jeopardize permanent residency are caught automatically — protecting both the investor and the attorney.
