# Peak Week Protocol Planner

**Niche:** [[niches/personal-trainers/contest-prep-coaches/profile|Contest Prep Coaches]]
**Industry:** [[industries/personal-trainers|Personal Trainers]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Nutrition planning apps like MacroFactor handle daily macro targets, but none support peak week — the final 5-7 day protocol of water manipulation, sodium cycling, carb loading, and depletion that determines whether a competitor looks their best on stage day.
**Tags:** #automation #workflow-orchestration #tabular-ml #quick-win

## The Problem
Peak week is the most high-stakes phase of contest prep: over 5-7 days, the coach manipulates water intake (from 2+ gallons to near-zero), sodium (loading then depleting), carbohydrates (depleting then loading), and training volume to achieve a specific visual effect (dry, full, vascular appearance) for a specific hour on stage day. The protocol varies by competitor (body type, metabolism, response to prior peaks), competition timing, and category. Coaches design these protocols on paper or in spreadsheets, calculating hour-by-hour water targets, meal-by-meal carb loads, and sodium amounts — a complex multi-variable schedule with no room for error. One timing mistake (peaking 12 hours early, for example) ruins 16-24 weeks of preparation.

## What Already Exists
MacroFactor and MyFitnessPal handle daily nutrition tracking. Google Sheets handles protocol planning. Some coaches use custom Excel templates shared within coaching communities. No commercial nutrition or training platform includes peak week-specific features: hour-by-hour water targets, sodium cycling schedules, carb loading protocols with glycogen timing, or visual condition tracking (the coach needs to see how the competitor looks at specific intervals, not just what they ate).

## The Customization Gap
The needed tool is a peak week protocol builder: (1) templates for common peak week approaches (water/sodium manipulation, carb front-load, carb back-load, no-peak), (2) hour-by-hour scheduling with water intake targets, sodium amounts, and meal macros, (3) configurable timing based on stage call time, (4) client-facing app showing current-hour targets and next-hour reminders, (5) visual condition check-in prompts with photo capture at scheduled intervals, and (6) adjustable parameters so the coach can modify the protocol mid-peak based on how the competitor is responding.

## Target Customer
Contest prep coaches running 5-15 competitors through peak weeks per year who currently design hour-by-hour protocols in spreadsheets.

## Impact If Solved
Eliminates the 3-5 hours of manual protocol design per competitor peak week, reduces peak week errors (wrong timing, wrong amounts) by 70-80%, and provides the client with a clear, hour-by-hour mobile guide rather than a confusing spreadsheet — directly improving competitive outcomes.
