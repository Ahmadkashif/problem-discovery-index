# MaximTrak F&I Menu Customized for RV-Specific Products

**Niche:** [[niches/rv-dealerships/rv-finance-extended-warranty/profile|RV F&I Product Sales]]
**Industry:** [[industries/rv-dealerships|RV Dealerships]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** MaximTrak and IDS F&I present menu-based product options but use automotive templates — RV-specific products (coach warranty, tire/wheel for 6-tire motorhomes, campground memberships) aren't properly categorized or explained.
**Tags:** #automation #quick-win #revenue-impact

## The Problem
F&I menu software presents products as line items with prices and monthly payment impacts. But RV-specific products require RV-specific explanations that the menu doesn't provide: "Extended service contract covers the coach systems (slide-outs, LP gas, plumbing, A/C) separately from the chassis warranty — without this, a $3,000 slide-out motor repair at month 37 is 100% out of pocket." An automotive-style menu lists "Extended Warranty: $2,800" without explaining the dual-warranty gap unique to RVs. The F&I manager must verbally explain every RV-specific nuance, and less experienced managers skip products they can't explain well.

## What Already Exists
MaximTrak and IDS F&I provide digital menu presentation with payment impact calculations, product descriptions, and e-contracting. They support RV products in their catalogs but present them with automotive-style descriptions that don't highlight RV-specific value propositions.

## The Customization Gap
RV-specific product presentation templates that include: visual explanations of dual-warranty gaps ("your Ford chassis warranty covers X, your Jayco coach warranty covers Y, this ESC fills the gap"), tire/wheel protection with RV-specific pricing context ("a single motorhome tire replacement costs $450-$700 vs. $150 for a car — this protection covers all 6 tires"), and campground membership ROI calculators ("at $45/night average campground fees, the Good Sam membership saves $900/year"). Each product card should include a 30-second video explainer the buyer can watch while the F&I manager prepares paperwork.

## Target Customer
RV F&I managers using MaximTrak or IDS F&I who currently achieve below-average product penetration because the standard menu templates don't effectively communicate RV-specific value.

## Impact If Solved
Increases F&I product penetration by 15-25% through better RV-specific product presentation, adding $400-$800 per deal in F&I revenue. Enables newer F&I managers to present products confidently without 2-3 years of RV-specific experience.
