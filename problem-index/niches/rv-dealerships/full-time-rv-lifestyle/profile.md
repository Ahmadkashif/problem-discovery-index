# Full-Time RV Lifestyle Buyers

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/rv-dealerships|RV Dealerships]]
**Category:** Underserved Audience

## Profile
**Market Size:** $5B
**Share of Parent Industry:** 11%
**Digital Adoption:** Medium — Full-timers are active in online communities (iRV2, Escapees, Reddit) and research extensively, but dealers don't cater to their specific requirements in the sales or service process.
**Target Buyer:** Sales and service teams targeting customers who will live in their RV full-time
**Automation Potential:** Low-Medium — Full-timer needs are highly individual and require consultative selling; however, the unique service and warranty needs can be systematized.

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
An estimated 1-1.5 million Americans live in their RV full-time, and this segment is growing 10-15% annually (driven by remote work, retirees, and lifestyle choices). Full-timers have radically different requirements than recreational users: residential-grade appliances (residential fridge vs. RV absorption fridge), better insulation and climate control (4-season capability), washer/dryer, reliable internet connectivity (cellular boosters, satellite), robust electrical systems (lithium batteries, larger solar), and commercial-grade components that withstand daily use rather than weekend use. They also domicile in specific states for tax/legal advantages and need service anywhere in the country — not just at their selling dealer.

## Current Tools & Gaps
RV dealers sell to full-timers but don't differentiate the sales process — a full-timer sees the same units and receives the same pitch as a weekend warrior. No dealer CRM tags or workflows distinguish full-time buyers. Service departments don't prioritize full-timers who need their home repaired faster than someone whose RV can wait in storage. The gap is recognizing full-timers as a distinct customer segment with different product needs, higher urgency in service, and dramatically higher lifetime value (they replace and upgrade more frequently).
