# Consignment Agreement Management Beyond Paper Contracts

**Niche:** [[niches/rv-dealerships/used-rv-consignment/profile|Used RV Consignment]]
**Industry:** [[industries/rv-dealerships|RV Dealerships]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** DocuSign handles digital signatures, but RV consignment agreements require state-specific legal terms, automated price reduction schedules, and integration with the DMS — a workflow no generic e-signature tool supports.
**Tags:** #compliance #automation #workflow-orchestration #quick-win

## The Problem
RV consignment agreements involve state-specific legal requirements (disclosure obligations, title holding procedures, commission structures, liability provisions), agreed-upon price reduction schedules (e.g., "reduce asking price by $1,000 every 30 days if unsold"), and consignor notification requirements when price changes are made. Dealers manage these agreements with paper contracts filed in folders, manually tracking price reduction dates on calendars, and calling consignors when reductions are due. With 20-50 consignment units, tracking 20-50 different agreement terms, price reduction schedules, and communication obligations becomes unmanageable.

## What Already Exists
DocuSign and PandaDoc handle digital contract creation and signature. IDS and Lightspeed DMS track consignment inventory with stock numbers and pricing. But neither platform manages the ongoing lifecycle of a consignment agreement — price reduction schedules, consignor notifications, agreement expiration dates, and state-specific compliance terms.

## The Customization Gap
A consignment agreement management module that: (1) generates state-compliant consignment contracts with all required disclosures, (2) tracks agreed-upon price reduction schedules and auto-triggers reductions in the DMS on the agreed dates, (3) sends automated notifications to consignors before each price reduction ("Your unit's asking price will be reduced from $35,000 to $33,500 on March 15 per our agreement — reply HOLD to pause the reduction"), and (4) tracks agreement expirations and auto-generates renewal or termination notices.

## Target Customer
Dealers managing 20+ consignment units who currently track agreement terms, price reduction schedules, and consignor communication manually.

## Impact If Solved
Eliminates 3-5 hours per week of manual consignment management, prevents state compliance violations from missed disclosures ($1,000-$10,000 per violation in some states), and reduces consignor disputes by providing a documented trail of all communications and price changes.
