## Why These Niches

Towable RVs and used consignment represent the dominant revenue segments with distinct operational challenges (towable outsells motorized 4:1, consignment creates unique pricing/legal complexities). First-time buyers and full-timers are underserved audiences whose purchase journeys differ radically from experienced RV buyers. Rural dealers and service departments lag in digital adoption despite representing significant market share. Seasonal inventory management and F&I product sales are the two most rule-driven, automatable workflows unique to RV retail.
