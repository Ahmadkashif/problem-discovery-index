# Affordable DMS for Sub-100 Unit RV Dealerships

**Niche:** [[niches/rv-dealerships/rural-rv-dealers/profile|Rural Single-Location Dealers]]
**Industry:** [[industries/rv-dealerships|RV Dealerships]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** IDS and Lightspeed EVO are excellent RV DMS platforms, but they cost $1,000-$3,000/month — unsustainable for a rural dealership doing $3M-$5M in annual revenue with 10-15% gross margins.
**Tags:** #quick-win #automation #data-integration #revenue-impact

## The Problem
A rural RV dealer needs the basic functions of a DMS: inventory management, deal desking (structuring F&I deals), trade-in appraisal tracking, service work orders, parts inventory, and accounting integration. IDS and Lightspeed EVO provide all of this with RV-specific features (floorplan tracking, consignment management, PDI checklists). But at $1,000-$3,000/month (plus $5,000-$15,000 implementation), the cost represents 2-4% of gross profit for a small rural dealer — a significant burden when competing with mega-dealers on already-thin margins.

## What Already Exists
IDS (Integrated Dealer Systems) and Lightspeed EVO are the two dominant RV-specific DMS platforms, together serving 80%+ of franchised RV dealers. CDK and Reynolds & Reynolds serve auto dealers but lack RV-specific features. QuickBooks handles basic accounting but not deal desking, service work orders, or inventory management for RV dealerships.

## The Customization Gap
An RV-specific DMS lite that provides the 5 essential functions (inventory, deal desking, service work orders, basic CRM, and QuickBooks integration) at a price point accessible to sub-100 unit dealers: $200-$500/month. It strips away enterprise features that small dealers never use (multi-location management, enterprise reporting, advanced analytics, manufacturer portal integration) while retaining the RV-specific core (unit specs, floorplan tracking, consignment agreements, trade-in appraisal, PDI checklists).

## Target Customer
Rural RV dealers with 30-100 units currently using QuickBooks + spreadsheets + paper because full DMS platforms are too expensive.

## Impact If Solved
Brings 3,000+ small RV dealers into a proper DMS ecosystem, improving deal accuracy by 15-20% (preventing deal structure errors that cost $500-$2,000 each), reducing administrative time by 30-40%, and enabling service department tracking that currently exists only on paper.
