# Simplified Inventory Listing Syndication for Small Dealers

**Niche:** [[niches/rv-dealerships/rural-rv-dealers/profile|Rural Single-Location Dealers]]
**Industry:** [[industries/rv-dealerships|RV Dealerships]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No affordable tool lets a small RV dealer photograph a unit with their phone, auto-populate specs from VIN decode, and syndicate the listing to RV Trader, Facebook, and their website in one workflow.
**Tags:** #automation #quick-win #ocr #revenue-impact

## The Problem
Listing an RV unit for sale online requires: 15-25 photos (exterior all angles, interior room by room, systems, tires, roof), spec entry (year, make, model, length, weight, floorplan, slides, features), pricing, and description writing. Then the listing must be posted to RV Trader ($200-$400/month subscription), Facebook Marketplace (manual posting), the dealer's website (manual upload or DMS integration), and Autotrader/Craigslist. For a rural dealer with 50 units and no marketing staff, this is 2-3 full days of work to list all inventory, plus ongoing updates as units are sold or prices change. Most rural dealers list 20-30% of their inventory online and leave the rest visible only to walk-in traffic.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
RV-specific listing tools exist (DealerSpike, InteractRV) but they're designed for metro dealerships with 200+ units and charge $500-$2,000/month — prohibitive for a small rural dealer. Automotive inventory syndication tools (Dealer.com, vAuto) don't handle RV-specific specs (floorplan, slide count, fresh water capacity, sleeping capacity). The RV-specific market is too small for the automotive tools, and the rural RV dealer market is too small for the RV tools.

## What to Build
A mobile-first listing app where the dealer walks around a unit, takes photos with their phone (the app guides which shots to take), scans the VIN (auto-populating year/make/model/specs from RVIA database), enters pricing and condition notes, and taps "publish." The app syndicates the listing to RV Trader, Facebook Marketplace, the dealer's website, and Autotrader simultaneously. Price changes and status updates (sold, pending, price reduced) propagate to all channels instantly. Pricing: $50-$150/month for up to 100 units.

## Target Customer
Rural RV dealers with 30-100 units who currently list fewer than 30% of their inventory online because the listing process is too time-consuming.

## Impact If Built
Enables 100% inventory listing in under 4 hours (versus 2-3 days manually), increasing online-sourced leads by 40-60%. For a rural dealer doing $4M/year in sales, capturing 5-10 additional online-sourced sales per year translates to $150K-$400K in incremental revenue.
