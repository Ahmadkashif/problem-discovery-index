## Why These Niches

Owner-operator trucking fragments along equipment type (dry van vs. reefer vs. flatbed), experience level (veteran operators vs. new authorities), demographic barriers (Spanish-speaking operators locked out of English-only tools), and operational maturity (tracking profitability vs. flying blind on cost). These 8 niches cover the two largest revenue segments (dry van and reefer), the two most digitally neglected (new authorities with no systems and rural operators with poor connectivity), the two most underserved populations (Spanish-speaking operators and military veterans), and the two highest-ROI automation targets (IFTA compliance and per-load P&L tracking). Excluded: flatbed/oversize (specialized and well-served by niche brokers), hazmat (distinct regulatory universe), and fleet operators with 5+ trucks (different business model).
