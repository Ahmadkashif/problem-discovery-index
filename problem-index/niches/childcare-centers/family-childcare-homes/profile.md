# Family Childcare Homes

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/childcare-centers|Childcare Centers]]
**Category:** Low Digitized

## Profile
**Market Size:** $8-12B
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~15-20%
**Digital Adoption:** Low — most family childcare providers use paper records, personal bank accounts for tuition collection, and text messages for parent communication; adoption of even basic childcare management software is under 20%
**Target Buyer:** Solo family childcare provider operating out of their home with 6-12 children
**Automation Potential:** Medium — the business is simple enough that technology should be minimal, but billing, tax documentation, USDA food program claims, and licensing compliance create disproportionate paperwork for a one-person operation

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Family childcare homes represent approximately 30% of all licensed childcare providers but operate in a fundamentally different mode from center-based care: a single provider (sometimes with one assistant) cares for 6-12 mixed-age children in their own home. The provider is simultaneously teacher, cook, business owner, bookkeeper, marketer, and compliance officer. Their business challenges are not operational complexity (like multi-site chains) but isolation, administrative overload, and economic fragility — the average family childcare provider earns $25,000-$35,000/year before expenses, with no benefits, no paid time off, and no backup when they are sick.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Brightwheel has begun targeting family childcare homes with a simplified tier, and Wonderschool provides business management tools for home-based providers. But adoption remains low because: (1) many providers are not tech-comfortable, (2) the cost of any software feels significant on $25,000-$35,000/year income, and (3) existing tools were designed for center-based workflows and have features irrelevant to home settings (multi-classroom management, staff scheduling) while missing features critical for home-based care (USDA food program meal tracking, mixed-age curriculum planning, home business tax deduction tracking).
