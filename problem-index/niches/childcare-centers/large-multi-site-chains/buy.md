# Procare/Brightwheel Multi-Site Aggregation Dashboard

**Niche:** [[niches/childcare-centers/large-multi-site-chains/profile|Large Multi-Site Childcare Chains]]
**Industry:** [[industries/childcare-centers|Childcare Centers]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Procare and brightwheel provide excellent single-site management, but chains running these platforms across 10-50 sites have no aggregated view — each site is a siloed instance with no cross-site reporting or benchmarking.
**Tags:** #data-integration #automation #quick-win #revenue-impact

## The Problem
A 15-site childcare chain uses brightwheel at each location. Each site's director sees their own enrollment, attendance, and billing data. The chain owner logs into 15 separate brightwheel accounts to understand chain-wide performance. There is no single view showing: total enrollment across all sites, enrollment trends by age group, revenue by site, parent satisfaction comparisons, or teacher turnover rates. The chain owner builds this view manually in a spreadsheet every month, a process that takes 8-12 hours and is outdated by the time it is complete.

## What Already Exists
Procare provides a "Procare Solutions" enterprise tier for multi-site operators. Brightwheel offers limited multi-site features. Both are evolving but lag far behind the multi-site management needs of chains with 10+ locations. Business intelligence tools (Tableau, Power BI) could aggregate the data but require custom data engineering.

## The Customization Gap
A lightweight aggregation layer that: (1) connects to Procare or brightwheel APIs across all sites; (2) normalizes data across sites (standardizing age group labels, classroom names, billing categories); (3) provides a chain-level dashboard with drill-down to individual sites showing: enrollment utilization by age group, revenue and AR aging, attendance patterns, teacher staffing levels vs. ratios, and waitlist depth; (4) generates automated weekly reports sent to regional management; and (5) supports cross-site benchmarking so the chain can identify best-performing and struggling sites.

## Target Customer
Owners and regional directors of 10-50 site childcare chains using Procare or brightwheel who currently spend 8-12 hours per month manually aggregating data from individual site instances.

## Impact If Solved
Monthly reporting time drops from 8-12 hours to 30 minutes, operational visibility shifts from monthly-retrospective to daily-real-time, and chain operators can identify underperforming sites weeks earlier — enabling timely interventions that prevent enrollment decline and revenue loss.
