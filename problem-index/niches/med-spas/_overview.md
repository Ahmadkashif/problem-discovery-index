## Why These Niches

Injectables and laser services together represent 60%+ of med spa revenue, making them the dominant market-share segments with distinct operational challenges (injectable inventory management vs. laser device utilization optimization). Body contouring and regenerative aesthetics are fast-growing but digitally neglected — practitioners track multi-session treatment protocols on paper or in spreadsheets. Male aesthetics is the fastest-growing demographic segment but med spa marketing, intake forms, and treatment protocols are all designed for female clients. Rural med spas face unique challenges around medical director oversight and limited referral networks. The two automation niches target the highest-ROI operational workflows: compliance documentation (critical given state-by-state medical supervision requirements) and client retention (where the difference between 2 and 4 annual visits per client doubles revenue).
