## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Med spas operate in a regulatory gray zone between traditional medicine and consumer beauty services. State-by-state rules govern which treatments can be performed by which credential level (RN, NP, PA, physician), what supervision is required, and what documentation standards apply. A treatment that an RN can perform independently in Florida requires direct physician supervision in California. Protocol compliance — ensuring each treatment is performed by appropriately credentialed staff with required documentation — is a daily operational challenge that most practices manage through institutional memory rather than systematic enforcement.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Some practices use shared Google Docs or binder-based protocol manuals. AestheticsPro and PatientNow include basic protocol templates but no compliance enforcement. No tool actively validates that the person performing a treatment has the required credential for that treatment in that state, that the required pre-treatment screening was documented, or that the post-treatment instructions were delivered.
