**Automation Potential:** Medium — Multi-session treatment protocols follow structured timelines and measurement checkpoints that are highly automatable.

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Body contouring (CoolSculpting, EmSculpt, truSculpt, BTL Vanquish) is fundamentally different from other med spa services because results develop over weeks to months after treatment, not immediately. A typical CoolSculpting protocol involves 1-3 sessions per treatment area, with results visible 2-3 months post-treatment. This creates a unique documentation and patient management challenge: the practice must track baseline measurements, schedule follow-up assessments at specific intervals, capture standardized progress photos, and manage patient expectations through a timeline where "nothing visible is happening" for 6-8 weeks after each session.

## Current Tools & Gaps
CoolSculpting (Allergan) has its own treatment planning software but it's device-specific and doesn't integrate with practice management. EmSculpt and truSculpt have basic treatment logging. No cross-device platform manages the full body contouring patient journey: consultation assessments, multi-area treatment plans, progress measurement tracking, standardized photography, and outcome comparison. Most practices track this on paper measurement cards or spreadsheets.
