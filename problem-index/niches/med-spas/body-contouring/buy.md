# Patient Photography System Adapted for Body Contouring Standards

**Niche:** [[niches/med-spas/body-contouring/profile|Body Contouring Practices]]
**Industry:** [[industries/med-spas|Med Spas]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Before/after photo systems designed for facial aesthetics don't address body contouring's unique requirements — full-body standardized angles, anatomical landmark consistency, and multi-month comparison timelines.
**Tags:** #cnn #pose-estimation #computer-vision #quick-win

## The Problem
Body contouring outcome documentation requires standardized full-body photography at consistent angles (front, side, 45-degree) with consistent lighting, patient positioning, and anatomical landmark framing — across sessions separated by weeks to months. Most med spas use an iPhone and hope for consistency. Lighting changes, angle variations, and patient positioning differences make before/after comparisons unreliable. Poor comparison photos undermine patient satisfaction ("it doesn't look different in the photos") and reduce referral marketing effectiveness.

## What Already Exists
Mirror, Canfield VECTRA, and PhotoMedex offer medical photography systems for dermatology and facial aesthetics. These are designed for close-up facial photography with standardized lighting pods. Touchmd and PatientNow include photo management features. None are optimized for full-body photography at multiple angles with body-specific standardization guides, and most facial systems cost $15,000-30,000 — overkill for body contouring documentation.

## The Customization Gap
The photography system needs body-specific features: full-body framing guides for standard angles, on-screen silhouette overlay to ensure consistent patient positioning between sessions, automated lighting quality checks, anatomical landmark annotation ("measure at this line across the navel"), and multi-month timeline comparison views that show 4-6 time points side-by-side. This could be built as a mobile app with computer vision guides rather than requiring dedicated hardware.

## Target Customer
Body contouring practices spending $5,000+/month on marketing but unable to produce compelling before/after galleries due to inconsistent photography.

## Impact If Solved
Produces marketing-quality before/after comparisons that increase consultation conversion by 15-25%, and provides patients with tangible visual evidence of progress during the 2-3 month result-development window.
