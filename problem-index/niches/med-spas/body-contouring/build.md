# Multi-Session Body Contouring Protocol & Outcome Tracker

**Niche:** [[niches/med-spas/body-contouring/profile|Body Contouring Practices]]
**Industry:** [[industries/med-spas|Med Spas]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No platform manages the full body contouring patient journey — from consultation through multi-session treatment to delayed outcome assessment — across different device types.
**Tags:** #cnn #regression #computer-vision #data-integration #revenue-impact

## The Problem
A body contouring patient may receive CoolSculpting on the abdomen (2 sessions, 4 weeks apart), EmSculpt on the buttocks (4 sessions, 2 weeks apart), and truSculpt on the flanks (1 session) — all in the same treatment plan. Each device has different session spacing, different measurement protocols, and different outcome timelines. The practice must track 5-8 treatment areas, each with its own measurement schedule and progress photography timeline. This multi-device, multi-area, multi-timeline coordination is done manually, leading to missed follow-ups, inconsistent measurements, and lost outcome data that could drive referrals.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
Device manufacturers build their own tracking tools (CoolConnect for CoolSculpting) but have no incentive to support competing devices. Practice management systems treat body contouring appointments like any other service — they don't model multi-session protocols with staggered timelines. The outcome tracking challenge (results develop 2-3 months post-treatment) doesn't fit standard "before/after at time of service" documentation models.

## What to Build
A body contouring protocol management platform that: (1) creates multi-area treatment plans with device-specific session spacing, (2) automatically schedules measurement and photography checkpoints at clinically appropriate intervals, (3) standardizes body measurements with anatomical landmark guidance, (4) captures progress photos with consistent framing/lighting prompts, (5) generates patient-facing progress reports with visual comparison, and (6) aggregates outcome data across patients to identify which protocols produce the best results by body type.

## Target Customer
Med spas generating $20,000+/month in body contouring revenue across 2+ device types, currently tracking patient progress on paper or in fragmented device-specific systems.

## Impact If Built
Improves treatment plan completion rates by 20-30% through automated follow-up scheduling, increases referral conversion by 25-35% through shareable outcome documentation, and provides clinical outcome data that informs protocol optimization.
