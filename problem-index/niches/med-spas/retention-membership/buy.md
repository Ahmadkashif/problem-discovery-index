# Membership Management Platform Adapted for Med Spa Treatment Bundles

**Niche:** [[niches/med-spas/retention-membership/profile|Client Retention & Membership Automation]]
**Industry:** [[industries/med-spas|Med Spas]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Subscription billing platforms handle recurring payments but don't manage med spa membership complexity — treatment credits, product bundles, tiered pricing, and utilization tracking.
**Tags:** #automation #data-integration #revenue-impact #workflow-orchestration

## The Problem
Med spa memberships are complex: a $199/month "Gold" membership might include one injectable treatment per quarter, two laser treatments per year, 20% off all additional services, and free skincare product samples. Tracking which services a member has used, what credits remain, and when they should be encouraged to book their included treatments requires spreadsheets and manual monitoring. When a member doesn't use their included treatments, the practice fails to reach out — leaving revenue on the table and reducing perceived membership value. When members cancel, there's no system to identify whether they were underutilizing (salvageable with engagement) or dissatisfied.

## What Already Exists
Membersy and Kleer handle membership billing for dental and med spa practices. Stripe and Chargebee manage subscription billing generally. Vagaro and Boulevard have built-in membership features. All handle recurring payment collection but none manage the treatment credit lifecycle: tracking usage, sending utilization reminders, alerting staff when a member is underusing their benefits, or generating membership ROI reports that show whether the membership drives incremental spending.

## The Customization Gap
The membership platform needs treatment-aware logic: (1) per-treatment credit tracking with automatic deduction when services are rendered, (2) utilization alerts when members haven't booked included treatments mid-cycle ("you have 1 injectable treatment remaining this quarter — book by March 31"), (3) tiered upsell automation (suggesting membership upgrade when a member consistently purchases add-on services), (4) cancellation risk scoring based on utilization patterns, and (5) membership profitability analysis showing margin per member tier.

## Target Customer
Med spas with 50+ membership clients generating $10,000+/month in recurring membership revenue, currently tracking utilization manually.

## Impact If Solved
Increases membership retention by 15-25% through utilization engagement, identifies underpriced tiers for restructuring, and reduces membership management administrative time by 60-70%.
