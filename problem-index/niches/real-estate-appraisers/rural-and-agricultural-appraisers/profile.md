# Rural & Agricultural Property Appraisers

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/real-estate-appraisers|Real Estate Appraisers]]
**Category:** Low Digitized

## Profile
**Market Size:** $600-900M
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~8-10%
**Digital Adoption:** Low — MLS coverage is sparse in rural areas, TOTAL used for report writing but comp data comes from personal records, county deed searches, and phone calls to other appraisers
**Target Buyer:** Rural or agricultural appraiser covering a multi-county territory, often the only licensed appraiser in a 50-100 mile radius
**Automation Potential:** Low-Medium — soil quality data integration, crop income analysis, and comparable sale assembly are partially automatable; property inspection in remote areas with acreage resists automation

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Rural and agricultural appraisers value properties where the land is the primary value driver — not the improvements. A 200-acre farm's value depends on soil type and productivity rating, irrigation access, crop yield history, USDA program enrollment (CRP, conservation easements), mineral rights, water rights, and proximity to grain elevators or processing facilities. These factors don't exist in suburban residential appraisal methodology. Comparable sales are scarce: a rural county may have 2-3 farm sales per year, and each involves different acreage, soil quality, and improvement mix. The appraiser covers a multi-county territory (driving 2-4 hours for inspections) and maintains a personal database of farm transactions going back decades because MLS coverage doesn't exist for agricultural properties.

## Current Tools & Gaps
TOTAL handles report formatting but has no agricultural property template. USDA NRCS Web Soil Survey provides soil data but doesn't integrate with appraisal workflow. USDA NASS provides crop yield data by county. Farm Credit System lenders have internal databases. No tool assembles soil productivity data, crop income analysis, and comparable farm sales into an integrated agricultural appraisal workflow.
