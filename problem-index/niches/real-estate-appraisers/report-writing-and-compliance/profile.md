**Target Buyer:** Any residential appraiser (workflow segment applicable across all assignment types)
**Automation Potential:** High — market commentary generation, USPAP compliance checks, narrative drafting, and report quality review are highly automatable with LLMs

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Report writing is the final phase of every appraisal — converting the appraiser's analysis into a USPAP-compliant document that communicates the valuation opinion with supporting evidence. For a lending appraisal, this includes: market conditions commentary (describing the market and trend direction), comp selection rationale (why these comps were chosen over alternatives), adjustment support narrative (how adjustments were derived), reconciliation statement (how the adjusted comp values were reconciled to a final opinion), and certifications and limiting conditions. These narrative sections take 20-40 minutes to write per report, and their quality directly affects CU scoring and lender acceptance.

## Current Tools & Gaps
TOTAL provides the form structure and data fields. The appraiser writes narrative sections manually or pastes from a personal template library (reusing boilerplate that may not reflect current market conditions). No tool generates context-aware narrative sections from the specific data in the current report — the market commentary should reflect the actual comps and market statistics in this report, not generic boilerplate.
