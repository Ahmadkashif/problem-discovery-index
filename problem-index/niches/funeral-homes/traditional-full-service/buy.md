# Modern Family-Facing Service Planner

**Niche:** [[niches/funeral-homes/traditional-full-service/profile|Traditional Full-Service Funerals]]
**Industry:** [[industries/funeral-homes|Funeral Homes]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Event planning platforms like Honeybook and wedding planners exist, but none are adapted for funeral service planning where families must make 30+ decisions in 48-72 hours while grieving.
**Tags:** #workflow-orchestration #recommendation #data-integration #quick-win

## The Problem
During the arrangement conference, families must choose a casket, vault, flowers, service music, readings, pallbearers, reception menu, obituary content, and dozens of other details — often within 48 hours of their loved one's death. The funeral director guides them through this verbally, writing selections on a paper form or entering them into legacy software the family never sees. Families leave overwhelmed, second-guess decisions, and call back with changes — each change requiring the director to re-coordinate vendors.

## What Already Exists
Honeybook, Aisle Planner, and Zola provide collaborative event planning with selection checklists, vendor management, and client-facing dashboards. These platforms are designed for weddings and corporate events. They don't handle the emotional sensitivity of funeral planning, the compressed timeline (days vs. months), the regulatory requirements (death certificates, permits), or the specific product catalog (caskets, vaults, urns) that funeral planning requires.

## The Customization Gap
The family-facing planner needs to present decisions in a guided, compassionate sequence — not as an overwhelming checklist. It should show casket/urn options with pricing (FTC Funeral Rule compliant), let families select music and readings from curated lists, collect pallbearer names and contact info, and display a timeline of events (visitation, service, procession, committal). Changes made by the family in the portal should auto-update the funeral home's case management system and trigger vendor re-coordination where needed.

## Target Customer
Family-owned funeral homes handling 100-250 cases/year that want to modernize the family experience without replacing their entire case management system.

## Impact If Solved
Reduces arrangement conference time by 20-30 minutes per case, cuts post-arrangement change requests by 40-50%, and differentiates the funeral home as modern and family-friendly in a market where consumer expectations are rising.
