# Multi-Vendor Coordination Hub

**Niche:** [[niches/funeral-homes/traditional-full-service/profile|Traditional Full-Service Funerals]]
**Industry:** [[industries/funeral-homes|Funeral Homes]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No tool coordinates the 8-15 vendors involved in a traditional funeral (cemetery, church, florist, caterer, newspaper, livery, musician) from a single case-linked dashboard.
**Tags:** #workflow-orchestration #ai-agent #automation #data-integration

## The Problem
A single traditional funeral requires coordinating with a cemetery (plot availability, opening/closing times), a church or venue (service scheduling), a florist (arrangement orders with delivery timing), a caterer (post-service reception), a newspaper (obituary placement), livery service (hearse, limousines), and often a musician, officiant, and monument company. The funeral director manages all of this via phone calls, with confirmation details tracked in notebooks or scattered email threads. One missed coordination — a hearse arriving 30 minutes late, a cemetery not ready — creates a cascading failure during the most emotionally vulnerable moment for a family.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
Funeral vendor ecosystems are hyperlocal — every funeral home works with a specific set of local vendors. A coordination platform would need to onboard vendors market by market, and most funeral vendors (local florists, small cemeteries) have no digital ordering or scheduling systems to integrate with. The market is too fragmented and low-tech on the vendor side for a horizontal platform to work. Building this requires starting with the funeral home side (tracking and confirming) and gradually digitizing vendor communication.

## What to Build
A case-linked vendor coordination dashboard where each funeral case shows all required vendors, their booking status, confirmation status, and timeline. The system sends standardized coordination requests to vendors (via SMS, email, or fax for legacy vendors), tracks confirmations, and flags any vendor that hasn't confirmed 24 hours before the service. Over time, frequently-used vendors can receive and confirm orders directly through the platform.

## Target Customer
Funeral directors at homes handling 150-300 cases/year who spend 2-3 hours per case on vendor phone calls and coordination — roughly 300-900 hours/year of phone-based vendor management.

## Impact If Built
Reduces vendor coordination time by 50-60% (1-1.5 hours saved per case), eliminates coordination failures that damage family trust and generate liability, and creates a case record that proves every vendor was confirmed.
