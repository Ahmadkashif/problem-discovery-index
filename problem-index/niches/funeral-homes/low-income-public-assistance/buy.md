# Benefit Claims & Reimbursement Tracker

**Niche:** [[niches/funeral-homes/low-income-public-assistance/profile|Low-Income & Public-Assistance Funerals]]
**Industry:** [[industries/funeral-homes|Funeral Homes]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Claims management tools like Availity and Change Healthcare exist for medical billing but none track funeral-specific government reimbursement claims across Medicaid burial benefits, county programs, FEMA, and VA.
**Tags:** #automation #data-integration #workflow-orchestration #compliance

## The Problem
A funeral home handling 30 public-assistance cases per year may have claims outstanding with 5-8 different government programs simultaneously, each with different payment timelines (30-120 days), documentation requirements, and follow-up processes. Tracking which claims have been submitted, which require additional documentation, which have been approved, and which are overdue is done in spreadsheets or notebooks. Claims that fall through the cracks represent $1,500-5,000 in unreimbursed services — money the funeral home has already spent providing the service.

## What Already Exists
Availity, Change Healthcare, and other revenue cycle management platforms handle medical insurance claims submission and tracking. QuickBooks and Xero track accounts receivable. But none understand the funeral-specific reimbursement landscape: Medicaid burial benefits have different processes per state, county indigent burial programs have their own application forms, FEMA funeral assistance requires specific COVID/disaster documentation, and VA burial benefits have their own portal. Each program is a separate claims universe.

## The Customization Gap
The tracker needs to model each government reimbursement program as a distinct workflow with its own documentation checklist, submission method (online portal, fax, mail), expected payment timeline, and follow-up protocol. It should show a dashboard of all outstanding claims across all programs, flag claims approaching deadlines, auto-generate follow-up correspondence for overdue payments, and reconcile received payments against outstanding claims. Integration with the funeral home's case management system links claims to specific cases.

## Target Customer
Funeral homes handling 20-50+ government-assisted cases per year, where $15,000-75,000 in annual reimbursements are at risk of being delayed, under-claimed, or lost entirely due to manual tracking.

## Impact If Solved
Recovers 15-25% of currently lost or delayed reimbursements ($3,000-15,000/year per funeral home), reduces claims tracking overhead from 3-5 hours per week to under 1 hour, and ensures no valid claim goes unsubmitted or unfollowed.
