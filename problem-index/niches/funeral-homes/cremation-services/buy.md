# Online Direct Cremation Arrangement Platform

**Niche:** [[niches/funeral-homes/cremation-services/profile|Cremation Services]]
**Industry:** [[industries/funeral-homes|Funeral Homes]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** E-commerce and service booking platforms exist but none handle the unique direct cremation workflow: legal authorization, death certificate data collection, and payment — all completed remotely by a grieving family within 24-48 hours.
**Tags:** #workflow-orchestration #automation #data-integration #revenue-impact

## The Problem
Direct cremation (no viewing, no service, cremation only) is the fastest-growing segment, driven by consumers who want simplicity and low cost. These families want to arrange everything online or by phone without visiting the funeral home. But the "arrangement" still requires: legal next-of-kin authorization to cremate, death certificate information (30+ data fields), payment, and decisions about remains disposition (urn selection, shipping vs. pickup, memorial service timing). Most cremation operators handle this through a series of phone calls and emailed PDFs — a clunky process that frustrates consumers expecting an online checkout experience.

## What Already Exists
Tulip Cremation and other "cremation disruptors" offer end-to-end online arrangement, but they're the cremation providers themselves — not tools for existing operators. FrontRunner and Passare offer web-facing arrangement modules, but they're add-ons to legacy systems with dated UX. Shopify and Squarespace can't handle the legal authorization and death certificate data collection workflow.

## The Customization Gap
The platform needs to guide a non-technical, emotionally distressed user through a legal and logistical process: verify next-of-kin status, collect death certificate information field by field (with inline help for unfamiliar questions like "father's birthplace"), present urn options with pricing, collect electronic authorization signatures that satisfy state law, process payment, and provide a clear timeline of next steps. All data must flow into the operator's case management system without re-entry.

## Target Customer
Cremation operators handling 100-400+ cases/year who are losing direct cremation families to online-first competitors because their own remote arrangement process feels disorganized and outdated.

## Impact If Solved
Increases direct cremation conversion rate by 25-35% for operators competing against online-first providers, reduces arrangement time from 2-3 phone calls over 2 days to a single 30-minute online session, and eliminates data re-entry from emailed PDFs into case management.
