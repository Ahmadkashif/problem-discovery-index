## Profile
**Market Size:** $8B
**Share of Parent Industry:** 18%
**Digital Adoption:** Medium-High — Large firms use Bullhorn and Epic-specific candidate databases, but credential verification and compliance tracking remain fragmented.
**Target Buyer:** VP of Talent or Healthcare IT staffing division leads at firms like Aya Healthcare, Medix, or regional health IT staffing shops.
**Automation Potential:** High — Credential verification, HIPAA training tracking, and Epic/Cerner certification matching follow strict rule-based workflows.

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Healthcare IT staffing requires matching candidates not just on technical skills but on specific EHR platform certifications (Epic, Cerner, MEDITECH), active HIPAA training credentials, and facility-specific onboarding requirements that vary by health system. A candidate who is "Epic-certified" in one module (Ambulatory) cannot be placed in a role requiring another (Inpatient). This granularity makes generic ATS skill-matching ineffective. The compliance overhead per placement — background checks, OIG exclusion list screening, immunization records, facility credentialing — is 3-5x that of standard IT staffing.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Bullhorn and JobDiva dominate the ATS layer but treat healthcare credentials as flat text fields rather than structured, expiring attributes. Firms supplement with manual spreadsheets to track which candidates have active Epic certifications in which modules, creating a dual-system workflow that breaks down at scale. No mainstream staffing platform integrates directly with health system credentialing portals or EHR vendor certification databases.
