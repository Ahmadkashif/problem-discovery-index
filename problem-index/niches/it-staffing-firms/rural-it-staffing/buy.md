# Lightweight ATS for Low-Volume Staffing

**Niche:** [[niches/it-staffing-firms/rural-it-staffing/profile|Rural & Small-Market IT Staffing]]
**Industry:** [[industries/it-staffing-firms|IT Staffing Firms]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Bullhorn and JobDiva are priced for firms making 50+ placements per month; rural IT staffing firms making 5-10 placements need the same workflow coverage at 10% of the cost.
**Tags:** #workflow-orchestration #quick-win #automation

## The Problem
Rural IT staffing firms need the core ATS workflow — candidate intake, job matching, submission tracking, client communication, and placement management — but cannot justify $99-$150/user/month for Bullhorn when their monthly placement volume generates $20K-$50K in gross revenue. These firms default to spreadsheets and email, losing candidates to disorganization and missing follow-ups. The result is a self-reinforcing cycle: low-tech operations limit growth, and limited growth prevents investing in better tools.

## What Already Exists
Bullhorn, JobDiva, and Ceipal serve enterprise staffing. Zoho Recruit and Freshteam offer lower-cost ATS options but are designed for direct employers, not staffing agencies — they lack features like client-side job management, submittal tracking, bill/pay rate tracking, and contractor compliance workflows. Staffing-specific platforms like Avionte have a lower price point but still assume metro-level placement volume.

## The Customization Gap
The gap is a staffing-specific ATS priced on a per-placement or transaction basis rather than per-seat, with workflows stripped to the essentials a 1-3 person staffing operation needs: candidate database with basic skill matching, job orders linked to specific clients, submittal tracking with client feedback, and simple contract/billing generation. Must include mobile-first design since rural recruiters often work from vehicles between client sites, not offices.

## Target Customer
IT staffing shop owners in non-metro markets running 1-5 person operations with 5-15 placements per month, currently managing everything in Google Sheets and Gmail.

## Impact If Solved
Enables 2-3x placement volume growth by eliminating the organizational bottleneck. At $15K average gross profit per placement, growing from 8 to 16 placements/month adds $120K/month in gross profit for a single firm.
