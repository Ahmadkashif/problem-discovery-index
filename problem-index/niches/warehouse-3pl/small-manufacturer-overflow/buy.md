# Simplified WMS for Small-Account 3PL Relationships

**Niche:** [[niches/warehouse-3pl/small-manufacturer-overflow/profile|Small Manufacturer Overflow Storage]]
**Industry:** [[industries/warehouse-3pl|Warehouse & 3PL]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** 3PL Central and Deposco are designed for high-velocity fulfillment operations, but small manufacturer overflow accounts generate 5-10 transactions per week, not 500 — the WMS overhead (configuration, client onboarding, per-transaction licensing) makes it uneconomical to manage low-volume accounts in the same system as high-volume fulfillment clients.
**Tags:** #automation #data-integration #quick-win #revenue-impact

## The Problem
A 3PL manages 50 small manufacturer overflow accounts alongside 10 large e-commerce fulfillment clients. The 10 large clients justify the WMS licensing cost ($2-5K/month for 3PL Central); the 50 small accounts each generate $1-5K/month in storage revenue but require the same WMS configuration (client setup, product master, receiving rules, billing configuration). Setting up a new overflow client in the WMS takes 2-4 hours of configuration work for an account that may generate 10 transactions/month. Many 3PLs manage small overflow accounts outside the WMS entirely — in spreadsheets — because the WMS setup overhead exceeds the account value.

## What Already Exists
3PL Central, Deposco, and Logiwa provide full-featured WMS platforms. These are designed for clients with 100+ daily transactions, not 10 weekly transactions. The per-client configuration cost is the same regardless of volume.

## The Customization Gap
A small-account WMS module needs: (1) rapid client onboarding (15 minutes, not 4 hours) with templated configuration for standard overflow use cases; (2) simplified inventory tracking (pallet-level, not piece-level) appropriate for overflow storage; (3) per-account billing calculation integrated with the 3PL's invoicing; (4) pricing appropriate for low-transaction accounts ($50-100/month, not $500+).

## Target Customer
3PLs managing 20+ small overflow accounts outside their primary WMS who want to bring these accounts into a managed system without the overhead of full WMS onboarding.

## Impact If Solved
Bringing 50 overflow accounts into a managed system reduces per-account administrative time by 2-3 hours/month (fewer email inquiries, automated billing). Total savings: 100-150 hours/month of account coordinator time — equivalent to a full-time position.
