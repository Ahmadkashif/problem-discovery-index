## Why These Niches

Termite and general residential represent the revenue backbone — together they generate over 60% of industry revenue and face distinct challenges (termite work is inspection-heavy and real-estate-transaction-linked; residential is recurring-subscription-based). Bed bug and wildlife control are digitally neglected specialties with complex workflows (heat treatment logistics, live animal handling regulations) that generic pest software ignores. Agricultural pest management and food safety pest services are underserved audiences with specialized regulatory requirements (EPA pesticide regulations, FDA food safety audits) not addressed by residential-focused platforms. Infestation diagnosis automation targets the industry's highest tacit-knowledge opportunity — experienced techs read environmental signs to identify pests without seeing them. Route optimization addresses the operational backbone of multi-tech operations where daily routing decisions drive profitability.
