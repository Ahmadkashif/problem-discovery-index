# Field Service Platform with Wildlife Regulations Engine

**Niche:** [[niches/pest-control/wildlife-control/profile|Wildlife & Animal Control]]
**Industry:** [[industries/pest-control|Pest Control]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Jobber and ServiceTitan handle scheduling and invoicing but know nothing about species-specific trapping regulations, release requirements, or permit tracking.
**Tags:** #compliance #automation #data-integration

## The Problem
Wildlife control operates under a complex regulatory framework that varies by state and species. In Texas, trapped raccoons must be euthanized (no relocation allowed). In California, trapped skunks must be released on-site. Some species require state permits per animal; others require annual licenses. Endangered species (certain bat species, migratory birds) have federal protections requiring specific handling protocols. Operators must know and follow these rules for every species in every jurisdiction they serve — a compliance failure can result in fines, license revocation, and criminal charges.

## What Already Exists
Field service platforms (Jobber, ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro) handle scheduling, dispatching, and invoicing for service businesses. Pest control-specific platforms (PestPac, FieldRoutes) focus on pesticide application record-keeping. Neither category includes a wildlife regulations engine — species-specific handling requirements, permit tracking, or state-by-state compliance rules.

## The Customization Gap
The platform needs a regulations database mapped to species and state: when a technician logs a capture (species, location), the system displays handling requirements (euthanize, relocate within X miles, release on-site), permit implications (does this capture count toward the annual quota?), and documentation requirements (photos, GPS coordinates, disposition). Permits should be tracked with expiration alerts and capture counts against limits.

## Target Customer
Wildlife control operators working across multiple counties or states who currently track regulations in their head or on printed reference sheets and risk compliance violations.

## Impact If Solved
Eliminates regulatory violations (fines of $500-$10,000 per incident), reduces onboarding time for new technicians (from months of memorizing rules to day-one compliance), and provides audit-ready documentation for license renewals.
