# Multi-Technique Job Pricing Calculator

**Niche:** [[niches/printing-shops/letterpress-specialty-printers/profile|Letterpress & Specialty Printers]]
**Industry:** [[industries/printing-shops|Printing Shops]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Standard print estimating tools price jobs by ink colors and substrate — but a letterpress wedding invitation combining letterpress (2 colors) + hot foil stamping (1 hit) + blind debossing + edge painting requires pricing each technique separately with its own setup cost, run cost, and time estimate, and no estimating tool handles multi-technique combinations.
**Tags:** #automation #revenue-impact #quick-win

## The Problem
A single specialty print piece might combine 2-4 techniques: letterpress printing (per-color plate cost + per-impression run cost), hot foil stamping (per-die cost + foil material cost + per-impression run cost), blind embossing or debossing (die cost + per-impression cost), edge painting (per-card hand application), and envelope addressing (calligraphy or digital addressing). The studio owner prices each technique component separately, estimates total production time, adds substrate cost, and calculates a per-piece price. This calculation takes 15-30 minutes and is prone to error — forgetting to include the foil die cost, underestimating edge painting time, or miscalculating the per-piece cost when quantity changes. Pricing errors directly erode margin on premium work where materials and labor are expensive.

## What Already Exists
Print estimating tools (EFI Pace, PrintSmith) price conventional commercial print jobs (offset or digital) by substrate, ink, and finishing. Craft business pricing tools (Craftybase, inFlow) track material costs for handmade goods. Neither handles the multi-technique pricing model specific to letterpress and specialty printing where each technique has its own cost structure.

## The Customization Gap
The pricing calculator must model each printing technique as a separate cost module (letterpress: plate cost + makeready time + per-impression cost by color; hot foil: die cost + foil material by coverage area + per-impression cost; edge painting: per-card time by paint type), combine multiple techniques for a single piece, calculate per-piece pricing at various quantities with appropriate quantity breaks per technique (letterpress has per-color setup regardless of quantity; edge painting scales linearly with quantity), and present the price to the customer broken down by technique so they can make informed add/remove decisions.

## Target Customer
Owner-operators of specialty print studios quoting 5+ multi-technique jobs per week. Approximately 1,500-2,500 US studios.

## Impact If Solved
Quoting time drops from 15-30 minutes to 3-5 minutes per job. Pricing errors decrease by 70-80%. Margin protection improves — studios stop accidentally under-pricing complex multi-technique work. Customers receive instant quotes instead of waiting 1-2 days, improving close rate.
