# Specialty Print Studio CRM with Wedding Timeline Management

**Niche:** [[niches/printing-shops/letterpress-specialty-printers/profile|Letterpress & Specialty Printers]]
**Industry:** [[industries/printing-shops|Printing Shops]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No CRM exists for specialty print studios that manages the multi-phase wedding stationery lifecycle (consultation → design → proofing → production → delivery across 4-6 print pieces over 6-12 months) — studios track client timelines in spreadsheets and miss production deadlines for 10-15% of orders.
**Tags:** #workflow-orchestration #revenue-impact #quick-win

## The Problem
A wedding stationery order is not a single transaction — it is a 6-12 month relationship with 4-6 deliverables: save-the-dates (8-10 months before wedding), invitations (6-8 months), RSVPs and details cards, day-of pieces (programs, menus, place cards), and thank-you cards (1-2 months after). Each piece requires design consultation, proofing (2-3 rounds), plate creation, and production. A busy studio manages 30-80 active wedding clients simultaneously, each at a different stage of their timeline. Without a system tracking which client needs which piece by when, studios rely on spreadsheets and memory — leading to missed production windows (save-the-dates needed in 2 weeks but not yet designed) and rushed jobs that compromise quality.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
The letterpress/specialty printing market is tiny (2,000-4,000 US studios) and operates at low technology adoption. CRM vendors target larger, higher-value markets. The wedding stationery lifecycle is niche within a niche — general wedding planning software (Aisle Planner, HoneyBook) manages vendor-client relationships but has no understanding of the print production timeline (plate lead time, drying time for edge painting, shipping deadlines for destination weddings).

## What to Build
A CRM designed for specialty print studios: client intake with wedding date and stationery package selection, automated timeline generation (working backward from wedding date to production and proofing deadlines for each piece), proofing workflow (upload proof → client review → revision tracking), production scheduling aligned with client timelines, and automated client communication (deadline reminders, proofing invitations, production status updates). Integration with QuickBooks for invoicing milestone billing (deposit → balance due per piece).

## Target Customer
Owner-operators of letterpress and specialty print studios producing 20+ wedding stationery orders per year. Approximately 1,500-2,500 US studios. Target price: $50-100/month.

## Impact If Built
Missed production deadlines decrease by 70-80%. Client communication time drops by 40-50% (automated reminders and status updates replace manual emails). Revenue per client increases 10-15% through systematic presentation of add-on pieces (day-of items, thank-yous) at the right timeline moment.
