## Why These Niches

The printing industry fragments along print technology (offset, digital, wide-format, letterpress, flexographic), customer type (commercial clients, nonprofits, packaging buyers), and operational function (estimating, production, finishing). Commercial offset and wide-format signage are the two largest revenue segments with distinct production challenges (offset demands color expertise and high makeready efficiency; wide-format demands substrate versatility and installation logistics). Small-run digital shops and letterpress printers are digitally neglected — too small for MIS and running on manual workflows. Nonprofit-serving shops and packaging/label printers are underserved by tools designed for general commercial print. Estimating and bindery are the two highest-ROI automation targets. Excluded: newspaper printing (declining rapidly), commercial web offset (dominated by a few large players), and 3D printing services (a distinct industry).
