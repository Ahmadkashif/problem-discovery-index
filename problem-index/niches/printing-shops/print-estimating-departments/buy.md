# Automated Standard Product Quotation

**Niche:** [[niches/printing-shops/print-estimating-departments/profile|Print Estimating Departments]]
**Industry:** [[industries/printing-shops|Printing Shops]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** MIS estimating modules require the estimator to configure every parameter for every job — but 60-70% of jobs at a typical commercial print shop are standard products (business cards, letterhead, envelopes, flyers, postcards) where the estimator's input is routine and could be automated from product templates and quantity.
**Tags:** #automation #quick-win #revenue-impact

## The Problem
A customer calls requesting "5,000 business cards, 2-sided full color on 14pt matte." The estimator opens the MIS, selects a press, assigns stock, configures impositions, calculates ink coverage, adds cutting, and generates a quote — a process that takes 5-10 minutes even though the shop runs this exact job type 20 times per week with trivial variation. The estimator's time is consumed by routine quotation that follows a predictable formula, leaving insufficient time for complex custom jobs that genuinely require expert judgment. At 10-30 RFQs per day with 60-70% being standard products, the estimator spends 3-5 hours on quotes that could be generated automatically.

## What Already Exists
MIS platforms (EFI Pace, PrintSmith, Avanti) have template-based estimating that pre-populates some parameters, reducing input time. Online print vendors (Vistaprint, 4Over) provide instant pricing for standard products. The MIS template approach still requires 3-5 minutes of estimator input per quote; the online vendor approach is for end consumers, not for a shop to use as its estimating tool.

## The Customization Gap
An automated quotation system for standard products must be configured with the shop's specific pricing: which press to assign for each product/quantity combination (the estimator knows that 500 business cards go on the digital press and 5,000 go on the offset press), which stock options are available with which price markups, which finishing operations are standard versus upcharge, and what margins to apply by customer tier. These rules encode the estimator's decision logic for routine jobs, freeing the estimator for custom work.

## Target Customer
Lead estimators at commercial print shops processing 15+ standard product quotes per day. Approximately 5,000-8,000 US commercial print shops.

## Impact If Solved
Standard product quote time drops from 5-10 minutes to under 1 minute. Estimator capacity for custom jobs increases by 3-5 hours per day. Quote turnaround to customers drops from hours to minutes for standard products, improving win rate.
