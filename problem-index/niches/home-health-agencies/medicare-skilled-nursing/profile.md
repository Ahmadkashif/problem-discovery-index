## Profile
**Market Size:** $55-60B

**Target Buyer:** Agency administrator / Director of Nursing
**Automation Potential:** High — OASIS documentation, visit scheduling, and patient risk stratification are all rule-heavy workflows amenable to ML augmentation

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Medicare-certified skilled nursing is the core of home health — registered nurses delivering episodic care under Medicare Part A, governed by CMS Conditions of Participation. The workflow revolves around 60-day certification periods, OASIS assessments at admission/recertification/discharge, PDGM payment groupings, and face-to-face encounter requirements from referring physicians. Unlike private-duty or Medicaid waiver care, this niche is visit-based (not shift-based), federally regulated (not state-regulated), and reimbursed per episode (not per hour).

## Current Tools & Gaps
WellSky and Homecare Homebase dominate, bundling scheduling, OASIS documentation, and billing into a single platform. Axxess serves mid-market agencies. All three platforms function as electronic versions of paper workflows — they capture data but don't act on it. No platform offers intelligent route optimization that accounts for visit priority and clinician skill mix, real-time re-scheduling when visits are missed, or predictive models that flag patients at risk of hospitalization before clinical signs become obvious.
