# Senior Move Lifecycle Management Platform

**Niche:** [[niches/moving-companies/senior-downsizing-specialists/profile|Senior Downsizing Specialists]]
**Industry:** [[industries/moving-companies|Moving Companies]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No tool manages the 2-6 month senior downsizing journey from initial consultation through sorting, donation, estate sale, and final move — it's all in the manager's head and a paper folder.
**Tags:** #workflow-orchestration #data-integration #ai-platform #revenue-impact

## The Problem
A senior downsizing project spans 2-6 months and involves: initial consultation with the senior and family, home walkthrough and inventory assessment, sorting sessions (keep/donate/sell/dispose), coordination with estate sale companies, donation pickups (Goodwill, Habitat for Humanity), junk removal scheduling, new-home measurement and furniture placement planning, the physical move, and post-move settling-in support. Each project has 15-25 distinct tasks spread across multiple service providers and family members. The project manager tracks everything in a paper folder, a personal calendar, and their memory. When they manage 8-12 concurrent projects, balls get dropped: the estate sale company wasn't booked in time, the donation pickup was scheduled for a day nobody was at the house, and the family hasn't made decisions on 200 items in the basement.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
The senior move management market (~2,000 specialist companies, plus general movers who do senior work) is too small for CRM vendors. The workflow is also unlike any standard CRM pipeline: it's project management with emotional labor, involving multiple stakeholders who may disagree (adult siblings fighting over who gets the grandfather clock). The service delivery model blends moving, organizing, counseling, and coordination — no single software category fits.

## What to Build
A project management platform designed for senior downsizing: client intake with family tree (who are the decision-makers, what's their contact info, who has power of attorney), multi-month timeline with milestone tracking (sorting complete, estate sale scheduled, donations coordinated, move date confirmed), task assignment to service providers (estate sale company, donation pickup, junk removal), inventory tracking with disposition status (keep/donate/sell/dispose for every major item), and family communication portal (shared progress updates, pending decisions, photo documentation of items needing decisions).

## Target Customer
Senior move managers and downsizing specialists running 5-15 concurrent projects who currently manage everything through paper folders and personal memory.

## Impact If Built
Reduces project management overhead from 5-8 hours/project to 2-3 hours through automated task tracking and communication. Enables managing 50% more concurrent projects without additional staff. Prevents dropped-ball incidents that cost $500-$2,000 each in rebooking fees, expedited services, and family dissatisfaction.
