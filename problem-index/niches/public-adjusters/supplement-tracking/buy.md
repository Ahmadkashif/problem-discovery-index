# Supplement Submission Workflow Tracker

**Niche:** [[niches/public-adjusters/supplement-tracking/profile|Carrier Negotiation & Supplement Tracking]]
**Industry:** [[industries/public-adjusters|Public Adjusters]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Project management and CRM tools exist, but none model the specific multi-round supplement negotiation lifecycle — submission, carrier acknowledgment, review period, counter-response, and resolution — with carrier-specific timeline expectations.
**Tags:** #workflow-orchestration #automation #compliance

## The Problem
A PA with 50 active claims has 50 separate carrier negotiation threads, each at a different stage: initial estimate submitted, awaiting carrier response, carrier response received and supplement in preparation, supplement submitted, awaiting carrier response to supplement, and so on for 2-4 rounds. Each thread has carrier-specific expectations — State Farm typically responds in 15-20 days, Citizens takes 45-60 days, USAA responds in 10 days. PAs track all of this mentally or in spreadsheets, inevitably losing track of claims that need follow-up and missing opportunities to escalate non-responsive carriers.

## What Already Exists
CRM tools (HubSpot, Salesforce) track customer interactions. PA-specific CRMs (ClaimWizard, Job Nimbus) track claim status at a high level (open, in negotiation, closed). Task management tools (Asana, Monday.com) track to-dos. None model the supplement negotiation lifecycle as a structured workflow with stage-specific deadlines, carrier-specific timeline expectations, and automated escalation triggers.

## The Customization Gap
The gap is a negotiation lifecycle tracker designed for supplement workflows: each claim has a negotiation timeline showing every submission, response, and counter-response with dates. The system knows carrier-specific response expectations and flags claims where the carrier is overdue. Auto-generates follow-up emails after the expected response window. Tracks negotiation outcomes by carrier to identify patterns — "State Farm accepts O&P 80% of the time but always disputes waste factors over 12%."

## Target Customer
PAs managing 30-80 active claims simultaneously with 2-4 supplement rounds per claim, spending 3-5 hours per week just tracking where each negotiation stands.

## Impact If Solved
Eliminates 3-5 hours per week of manual tracking and follow-up coordination. Reduces carrier response delays by 20-30% through systematic follow-up. Prevents revenue leakage from claims that stall without follow-up — estimated at 5-10% of total settlement value across a PA's book.
