# Multi-Unit Claim Coordination Dashboard

**Niche:** [[niches/public-adjusters/condo-hoa-claims/profile|Condo Association & HOA Claims]]
**Industry:** [[industries/public-adjusters|Public Adjusters]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Project management tools exist, but none handle the specific coordination requirements of a single loss event affecting 50-200 condo units with individual inspections, separate estimates, and coordinated carrier submissions.
**Tags:** #workflow-orchestration #data-integration #automation

## The Problem
A hurricane hits a 150-unit condo building. The PA must inspect each unit individually, document unit-specific damage, write unit-specific estimates, coordinate with each unit owner's personal carrier (there may be 15 different carriers across 150 units), and manage the master policy claim for common elements simultaneously. This creates a matrix of 150+ individual claims plus 1 master claim, each at different stages, each with different carrier contacts, and each requiring individual communication. PAs manage this in spreadsheets, losing track of which units have been inspected, which estimates are submitted, and which carriers have responded.

## What Already Exists
Project management tools (Monday.com, Asana, Basecamp) handle task tracking and team coordination. PA CRMs (ClaimWizard, Job Nimbus) manage individual claims. Neither supports the multi-unit-to-single-event relationship where one loss event generates 100+ linked claims that must be tracked individually but managed collectively — with aggregate reporting to the HOA board.

## The Customization Gap
The gap is a claim coordination layer designed for multi-unit events: a building-level view showing all unit claims on a floor plan, unit-by-unit status tracking (inspected/estimated/submitted/paid), carrier-by-carrier response monitoring, aggregate financial tracking (total estimated, total paid, total outstanding), and HOA board reporting. Must support mass operations — send all State Farm claims simultaneously, generate a board report showing claim progress across all units.

## Target Customer
PA firms handling condo and HOA claims where single events affect 20+ units, typically 5-15 events per year.

## Impact If Solved
Reduces per-unit administrative overhead by 50-60% on multi-unit claims. Prevents units from falling through the cracks — currently 5-10% of unit claims in large events are delayed or forgotten due to tracking failures. Enables the PA to manage 3-4 large multi-unit events simultaneously instead of 1-2.
