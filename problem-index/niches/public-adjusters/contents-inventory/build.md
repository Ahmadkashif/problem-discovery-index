# AI-Powered Contents Inventory Builder

**Niche:** [[niches/public-adjusters/contents-inventory/profile|Contents & Inventory Claims]]
**Industry:** [[industries/public-adjusters|Public Adjusters]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No product can take photos or video walkthroughs of damaged personal property and automatically generate a contents inventory with item identification, description, and quantity counts.
**Tags:** #cnn #object-detection #computer-vision #automation #revenue-impact

## The Problem
Building a contents inventory for a total-loss fire claim requires listing every item the policyholder owned — furniture, electronics, clothing, kitchenware, tools, personal items — with descriptions, quantities, and conditions. Policyholders are traumatized and can't remember everything they owned. Contents specialists walk through the debris or salvage, photograph everything, and manually create a line-by-line inventory. A 3-bedroom home produces 1,000-3,000 line items. At 2-3 minutes per item for identification, description, and entry, a single inventory takes 50-150 hours.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
The diversity of personal property items makes object detection extremely challenging — a contents inventory includes everything from a $2,000 sectional sofa to a $3 kitchen spatula. Damaged items are particularly hard to identify visually (a charred appliance, a water-swollen book). The accuracy bar is high because every item has a dollar value attached, and carriers scrutinize contents inventories for inflated or fabricated items.

## What to Build
A mobile app that captures video walkthroughs of damaged property and uses computer vision to identify visible items, estimate quantities, and generate a draft inventory. The contents specialist reviews, corrects, and supplements the AI-generated list rather than building from scratch. Handles both intact items (pre-loss video from security cameras or real estate listings) and damaged items (post-loss walkthrough). Integrates with replacement cost databases for automatic pricing.

## Target Customer
Contents specialists and PAs handling total-loss claims (fire, flood) with 500+ item inventories, averaging 2-4 claims per month.

## Impact If Built
Reduces contents inventory creation time by 50-70% (from 80 hours to 25-40 hours for a major loss). Improves inventory completeness — AI-assisted detection catches items the policyholder forgot, increasing average contents settlement by 10-20%.
