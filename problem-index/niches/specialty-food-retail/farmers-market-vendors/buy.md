# Pre-Order and Market Pickup Management

**Niche:** [[niches/specialty-food-retail/farmers-market-vendors/profile|Farmers Market Vendors]]
**Industry:** [[industries/specialty-food-retail|Specialty Food Retail]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** E-commerce pre-order tools exist, but none handle the farmers market pickup model — where customers order online for pickup at a specific market on a specific day, with inventory shared across multiple markets.
**Tags:** #automation #workflow-orchestration #data-integration #revenue-impact

## The Problem
Regular customers want to reserve popular items before market day — the sourdough that sells out by 10am, the specialty sausage with limited production. Vendors manage pre-orders through text messages, Instagram DMs, and email — individually confirming each request, tracking who's picking up at which market, and setting aside the right quantities. With 20-30 pre-orders across 3 markets, this coordination takes 1-2 hours per week and is error-prone: a vendor who accidentally sells a pre-ordered loaf to a walk-up customer has an angry regular at 10:30am.

## What Already Exists
Shopify and Square Online provide basic e-commerce with pickup options. Barn2Door offers farm-specific online stores with delivery and pickup. Google Forms and Jotform collect basic pre-orders. Instagram and Facebook messaging handle informal reservations.

## The Customization Gap
Existing tools assume a single pickup location (the store or farm) and don't handle the multi-market model: a customer orders 2 sourdough loaves for Saturday pickup at the downtown market, while another customer orders the same product for Sunday pickup at the suburban market. The pre-order system must allocate inventory across market-day pickups, cap pre-orders per market based on available supply, and generate a market-specific pickup list the vendor prints or views on their phone. Must integrate with the inventory allocation decision (pre-orders reduce available walk-up inventory at each market) and be simple enough for customers to use without creating an account.

## Target Customer
Farmers market vendors with popular items that sell out regularly, receiving 10+ informal pre-order requests per week via text and social media.

## Impact If Solved
Converts informal pre-order demand into guaranteed revenue — pre-orders typically represent 15-25% of market-day sales for popular vendors. Eliminates the 1-2 hours/week spent managing pre-orders via text. Reduces customer disappointment from sold-out items, improving retention of high-value regular customers who currently drive 40-60% of vendor revenue.
