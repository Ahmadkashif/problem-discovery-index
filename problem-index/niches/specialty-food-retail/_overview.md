## Why These Niches

Specialty food retail fragments by product category (cheese vs. meat vs. produce vs. wine), store format (full grocery vs. single-category specialist vs. market vendor), customer demographic (health-conscious vs. ethnic community vs. foodie/gourmet), and operational function (procurement, prepared foods, spoilage management). These 8 niches cover the two largest revenue segments (natural/organic grocers and craft butcher shops), the two most digitally underserved (artisan cheese shops with near-zero operational technology and ethnic markets serving immigrant communities with culturally specific supply chains), two underserved populations (farmers market vendors who need mobile POS + inventory but have no fixed infrastructure, and independent wine/spirits retailers whose compliance burden is uniquely heavy), and two high-ROI automation targets (prepared food production scheduling/labeling and small-vendor procurement coordination). Excluded: large natural chains (Whole Foods, Sprouts — structurally different), convenience stores, and mass-market grocery.
