# State-Specific Eligibility Rule Updates

**Niche:** [[niches/nonprofits-social-services/benefits-eligibility-screeners/profile|Benefits Eligibility Screeners]]
**Industry:** [[industries/nonprofits-social-services|Nonprofits Social Services]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Findhelp and BenefitsCheckUp maintain program directories but their eligibility rules are often 6-12 months behind actual state policy changes, causing navigators to screen clients against outdated thresholds.
**Tags:** #automation #compliance #data-integration #nlp #quick-win

## The Problem
Federal poverty guidelines update annually, but state eligibility thresholds for Medicaid, SNAP, TANF, LIHEAP, and other programs change on different schedules — some adjust quarterly, others change mid-year through legislative action or emergency provisions. Benefits screening tools that hardcode eligibility rules quickly become stale. Navigators using a tool with last year's income thresholds may incorrectly screen a family as ineligible for a program they actually qualify for, or refer them for a program whose rules have tightened.

## What Already Exists
Findhelp maintains a directory of 500,000+ programs with basic eligibility criteria. BenefitsCheckUp (NCOA) covers senior-specific programs. The Policy Surveillance Program tracks state policy changes. However, the pipeline from "state issues updated eligibility guidance" to "screening tool reflects the new threshold" takes 3-12 months across existing platforms, and there's no verification mechanism to confirm rule accuracy.

## The Customization Gap
The missing capability is a real-time eligibility rule monitoring and update system that tracks state policy changes (legislative actions, regulatory updates, emergency provisions, inflation adjustments), extracts updated eligibility criteria from policy documents, and pushes verified updates to screening tools within days rather than months. This requires NLP-based policy document monitoring, structured rule extraction, and a human-in-the-loop verification workflow with state-level policy experts.

## Target Customer
Organizations operating benefits screening programs in 5+ states who need current eligibility data and cannot afford to maintain 50-state policy monitoring teams.

## Impact If Solved
Reduces eligibility screening errors caused by outdated rules from 12% to under 2%, prevents incorrect denials that cost families $200-$500/month in missed benefits, and eliminates the 10-15 hours/month navigators spend manually checking state websites for rule changes.
