# HMIS-to-Funder Report Automation

**Niche:** [[niches/nonprofits-social-services/homelessness-continuum-of-care/profile|Homelessness Continuum of Care]]
**Industry:** [[industries/nonprofits-social-services|Nonprofits Social Services]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** HMIS platforms like Clarity and WellSky collect HUD-mandated data but cannot automatically generate the funder-specific reports each agency needs — APRs, CAPERs, and custom reports for state and local grants.
**Tags:** #automation #compliance #data-integration #quick-win

## The Problem
Homelessness agencies report to multiple funders — HUD (Annual Performance Report, CAPER), state government, local government, and private foundations — each with different reporting formats, metrics, and timelines. HMIS contains the underlying data, but extracting it into funder-specific formats requires 15-30 hours per report, run by an HMIS administrator who manually queries the database, cleans the data, and formats it in Excel. A typical agency submits 8-15 reports per year, consuming 200+ staff hours annually on reporting alone.

## What Already Exists
HMIS platforms include built-in HUD report generation (APR, CAPER), and Tableau/Power BI can visualize HMIS data. However, built-in reports cover only HUD-specific formats — the 60% of reports required by state, local, and private funders must be built manually. Business intelligence tools require technical expertise that most homelessness agencies don't have on staff.

## The Customization Gap
The missing layer is a report builder specifically designed for homelessness data that understands HMIS data models, common funder reporting templates, and the specific metrics each funder cares about (returns to homelessness, length of stay, exits to permanent housing, income changes). The tool needs to map HMIS fields to funder report fields, apply funder-specific business logic (some funders count program enrollments differently), and schedule recurring report generation.

## Target Customer
HMIS administrators at CoC lead agencies who spend 20+ hours per month generating reports for multiple funders across the CoC network.

## Impact If Solved
Reduces per-report generation time from 20 hours to 2 hours, frees 150+ staff hours per year per agency for direct service delivery, and improves report accuracy by eliminating manual data extraction errors that cause compliance findings.
