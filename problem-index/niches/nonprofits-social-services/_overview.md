## Why These Niches

Homelessness CoC and workforce development programs represent the largest federal funding streams in social services, commanding the most organizational capacity and compliance burden. Food banks and rural CAAs remain digitally neglected — food banks coordinate thousands of donation sources and distribution points using spreadsheets and phone trees, while rural CAAs serve multi-county areas with minimal IT infrastructure. Refugee resettlement, reentry services, and foster youth aging out are underserved populations where existing case management tools were designed for mainstream social services and miss critical workflow differences. Benefits eligibility screening is highly automatable because it involves matching client circumstances against complex but well-defined federal and state eligibility rules. Excluded: large national nonprofits (United Way, Salvation Army) with enterprise IT budgets, and advocacy-only organizations without direct service delivery.
