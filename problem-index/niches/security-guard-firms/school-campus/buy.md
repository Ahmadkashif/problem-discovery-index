# Lockdown Drill Performance Tracker

**Niche:** [[niches/security-guard-firms/school-campus/profile|School & Campus Security]]
**Industry:** [[industries/security-guard-firms|Security Guard Firms]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Schools conduct lockdown drills but have no systematic way to measure guard response time, protocol adherence, and improvement trends — drill reports are narrative-based, not data-driven.
**Tags:** #automation #compliance #data-integration

## The Problem
Schools conduct 2-4 lockdown/safety drills per year as required by state law. The security guard's performance during these drills is critical — they're often responsible for securing entry points, directing law enforcement, and monitoring cameras. But drill evaluation is informal: the principal walks the halls, the guard does their part, and someone writes a narrative summary ("drill went well, completed in 6 minutes"). There's no structured measurement of guard-specific performance: response time from alert to first action, entry points secured (and in what order), camera monitoring initiated, law enforcement staging area prepared.

## What Already Exists
Emergency drill tracking platforms (Navigate360, CrisisGo) document drill completion for state compliance reporting. Security training platforms track certification completion. Neither provides drill-specific performance measurement for security guards: timed response benchmarks, protocol step completion verification, or performance trending across drills.

## The Customization Gap
The platform needs a drill performance framework: define the guard's protocol steps for each drill type (lockdown, lockout, evacuation), time each step during the drill (via mobile app timestamps), compare to target benchmarks, and track improvement across drills. Post-drill debriefs should capture gaps identified, corrective actions, and responsible parties. The security firm can present drill performance trending to the school administration as evidence of continuous improvement.

## Target Customer
Security firms serving school districts that conduct 8+ drills per year across multiple schools and need documented evidence of guard performance improvement for contract justification.

## Impact If Solved
Transforms lockdown drills from compliance checkboxes into measurable performance improvement opportunities. Security firms demonstrating drill-over-drill improvement in guard response metrics differentiate themselves from competitors and justify premium pricing.
