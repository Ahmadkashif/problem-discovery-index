# Camera-Guard Integration for Construction Sites

**Niche:** [[niches/security-guard-firms/construction-site/profile|Construction Site Security]]
**Industry:** [[industries/security-guard-firms|Security Guard Firms]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Construction site cameras and guard patrols operate independently — camera alerts go to a remote monitoring center while the on-site guard walks past the same area unaware.
**Tags:** #computer-vision #object-detection #data-integration #automation

## The Problem
Many construction sites deploy both on-site guards and camera systems (MODS, Stealth Monitoring, Sensera). These systems don't talk to each other. The camera system detects motion on the north side of the site and alerts a remote monitoring center in another state. The on-site guard is on the south side, unaware. By the time the monitoring center calls the guard (if they even have the guard's number), the intruder is gone. The camera and guard are two separate security layers that never coordinate.

## What Already Exists
Construction site camera systems (MODS, Stealth Monitoring, Sensera) provide AI-powered motion detection and remote monitoring. Guard tour systems (TrackTik, Silvertrac) track on-site guard patrol patterns. These operate as completely independent systems with different vendors, different dashboards, and different alert channels.

## The Customization Gap
Integration needs to be bidirectional: camera alerts should route directly to the on-site guard's mobile device with location and camera snapshot ("motion detected at north fence, camera 7"). Guard patrol data should inform camera monitoring priorities (when the guard is patrolling area A, prioritize camera monitoring of areas B and C). Combined reporting should show the GC a unified security picture: patrol coverage + camera coverage = total site security posture.

## Target Customer
Security firms competing against camera-only solutions at construction sites, who need to demonstrate that integrated guard + camera coverage provides superior protection.

## Impact If Solved
Reduces incident response time from 5-15 minutes (guard discovers during next patrol) to 30-90 seconds (guard receives camera alert). Enables security firms to offer an integrated solution that justifies premium pricing over camera-only alternatives.
