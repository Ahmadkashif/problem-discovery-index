## Why These Niches

Commercial property and event security represent the revenue core — together they account for over 55% of contract security revenue and face distinct operational challenges (long-term site management vs. short-duration high-intensity staffing). Residential community patrol and construction site security are digitally neglected segments where guard activity reporting is still paper-based and client communication is ad hoc. Hospital and school security serve underserved audiences with unique regulatory requirements (HIPAA in healthcare, child safety protocols in education) that generic security software ignores. Guard scheduling and compliance automation target the two highest-cost operational pain points — labor scheduling is the single largest expense driver, and licensing compliance is the single largest regulatory risk.
