# Chef-Facing Protein Ordering and Spec Management Portal

**Niche:** [[niches/restaurant-suppliers/independent-protein-suppliers/profile|Independent Protein Suppliers]]
**Industry:** [[industries/restaurant-suppliers|Restaurant Suppliers]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No product lets a chef view their custom-cut protein specifications, see current market prices for their regular items, and place orders digitally — while preserving the relationship-based pricing and advisory service that defines independent protein supply.
**Tags:** #recommendation #data-integration #revenue-impact #quick-win

## The Problem
A chef ordering from an independent protein supplier calls or texts the sales rep every 2-3 days: "I need 20 NY strips cut at 14 oz, 30 lbs of ground chuck 80/20, 15 lbs of diced chicken thigh for the special, and whatever salmon looks good this week." The rep writes this down, calls or texts the office, and the order is entered into the ERP manually. The chef has no way to see: their own custom-cut specifications (are my strips 14 oz or 16 oz?), current market prices for their regular items, their order history, or what new products the supplier carries. Every order requires a phone call, and every phone call risks miscommunication — "14 ounce" heard as "40 ounce" costs the supplier a re-cut and the chef a delayed delivery.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
Protein ordering portals (BlueCart, Cut+Dry) exist but are designed for broadline ordering — they present a generic catalog and do not support custom-cut specifications (cut thickness, trim level, portion weight, packaging preference) that are unique to each chef-supplier relationship. Building a chef-facing portal for protein suppliers requires: (1) per-customer product catalogs with custom-cut specs, (2) real-time market-adjusted pricing (protein prices change daily), (3) order history with reorder shortcuts, and (4) a design that enhances the rep relationship rather than disintermediating it.

## What to Build
A mobile-first ordering portal for the chef that: (1) displays their personalized product catalog with saved custom-cut specs ("Tony's Pizzeria: 14 oz NY Strip, 3/4" thick, trimmed to 1/4" fat cap"), (2) shows current market prices with trend indicators, (3) enables quick reorder from previous orders with quantity adjustment, (4) supports voice-to-order for chefs who prefer calling ("order my usual proteins for Friday" gets transcribed and matched to saved specs), and (5) routes all orders through the sales rep for review and confirmation — the rep remains in the loop. The portal is branded for the supplier, not a marketplace.

## Target Customer
Owners and sales managers at independent protein suppliers serving 50+ restaurant accounts. Approximately 2,000-3,000 US protein suppliers at this scale. Price point: $200-$500/month.

## Impact If Built
Order processing time reduced from 15-20 minutes per order (phone call + manual entry) to 3-5 minutes (chef enters directly, office reviews). Custom-cut specification errors reduced by 70-80%, saving $500-$2,000/week in re-cuts and re-deliveries. Average order size increases 5-8% as the portal surfaces items the chef forgets to order by phone. Rep time freed from order-taking redirected to relationship-building and new account development.
