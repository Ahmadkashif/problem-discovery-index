# Call Recording-Integrated Skill Assessment

**Niche:** [[niches/corporate-training/sales-enablement-training/profile|Sales Enablement & Revenue Training]]
**Industry:** [[industries/corporate-training|Corporate Training]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Gong and Chorus.ai capture every sales call but don't connect conversation quality metrics to training modules — an enablement leader cannot see "this rep struggles with discovery questions, recommend Module 4."
**Tags:** #bert #text-classification #nlp #recommendation #data-integration

## The Problem
Conversation intelligence platforms record thousands of sales calls and generate metrics: talk-to-listen ratio, question frequency, competitor mentions, next steps set. But these metrics are disconnected from the training curriculum. An enablement leader cannot query "which reps demonstrate weak discovery questioning skills on calls, and which training module addresses that skill gap?" The call data and the training data live in separate systems with no mapping between conversation behaviors and training content.

## What Already Exists
Gong, Chorus.ai (now ZoomInfo), and Clari Copilot analyze sales calls and provide coaching insights. LMS platforms (Lessonly/Seismic Learning, SalesHood) track training completion. Call analysis platforms identify behavioral patterns; LMS platforms track content completion. Neither connects behavioral skill gaps to training prescriptions.

## The Customization Gap
A mapping layer between conversation intelligence metrics and training curriculum that: (1) defines a skill taxonomy linking call behaviors (discovery question depth, objection handling, multi-threading, closing technique) to specific training modules; (2) automatically scores each rep's calls against the skill taxonomy; (3) identifies per-rep skill gaps based on call analysis; and (4) recommends specific training modules to address each gap, creating a personalized learning path driven by actual call performance data.

## Target Customer
Sales enablement leaders at organizations using both a conversation intelligence platform and an LMS who want to close the loop between call coaching and training prescription.

## Impact If Solved
Reps receive targeted training based on observed skill gaps rather than one-size-fits-all curricula, reducing unnecessary training time by 30-40% while improving specific skill deficiencies 2x faster than generic course assignment.
