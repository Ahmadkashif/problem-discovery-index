## Why These Niches

Corporate training fragments along content domain (sales vs. compliance vs. technical skills), learner profile (desk-based vs. deskless vs. manufacturing floor), delivery format (in-person vs. virtual vs. asynchronous), and operational function (content creation vs. delivery vs. assessment vs. analytics). These 8 niches cover the two largest spending categories (sales enablement and compliance/mandatory training), two digitally neglected learner populations (frontline/deskless workers who lack computer access and DEI programs stuck in facilitated workshops with no measurement), two underserved audience segments (manufacturing floor workers needing hands-on skills and distributed teams with timezone and culture challenges), and two operational workflows with high automation ROI (employee onboarding and assessment/certification grading). Excluded: executive coaching (different delivery model), academic corporate partnerships (university territory), and conference/event-based training (events industry overlap).
