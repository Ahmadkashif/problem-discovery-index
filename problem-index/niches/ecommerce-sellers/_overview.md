## Why These Niches

E-commerce selling is not one market — it fragments by channel model (FBA aggregator vs. DTC brand vs. marketplace arbitrage), geographic scope (domestic vs. cross-border), product type (commodity vs. handmade vs. subscription), and operational function (advertising vs. fulfillment vs. returns). These 8 niches cover the two largest revenue concentrations (Amazon FBA portfolios and Shopify DTC), the two most digitally underserved segments (cross-border sellers navigating customs/VAT complexity and handmade sellers on Etsy/Faire with no operational tools), two specific populations poorly served by existing platforms (subscription box operators who need churn-specific tooling and resellers who need sourcing intelligence), and two high-ROI automation targets (PPC bid management and returns processing). Excluded: Amazon 1P vendors (structurally different), enterprise brands with in-house teams, and dropshippers (low margin, high churn segment).
