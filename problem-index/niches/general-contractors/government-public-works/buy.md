# Government Construction Compliance Platform

**Niche:** [[niches/general-contractors/government-public-works/profile|Government & Public Works Contractors]]
**Industry:** [[industries/general-contractors|General Contractors]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Construction accounting platforms like Sage handle job costing and payroll, but government contractors need a compliance layer on top — prevailing wage validation, certified payroll generation, DBE tracking, and contract-specific documentation requirements — that no accounting tool provides natively.
**Tags:** #large-language-models #feature-engineering #compliance #automation #data-integration #workflow-orchestration

## The Problem
Government construction contractors must simultaneously manage standard construction operations (scheduling, budgeting, sub coordination) AND a parallel compliance universe: certified payroll submissions, prevailing wage rate validation, DBE/MBE/WBE utilization reporting, OSHA compliance documentation specific to public works, apprenticeship ratio tracking, material Buy American requirements, and project-specific reporting to the contracting agency. Each requirement has its own reporting format, submission schedule, and penalty structure. The compliance workload is not optional or deferrable — miss a weekly certified payroll submission and the agency holds your payment.

## What Already Exists
Sage 300 CRE and Viewpoint Vista are the standard construction accounting platforms and can generate basic certified payroll reports. LCPtracker and Elations handle certified payroll submission to contracting agencies. B2Gnow tracks DBE compliance for agencies (not contractors). Each tool handles one slice of the compliance picture, but none provide an integrated compliance dashboard showing: all pending compliance deadlines across projects, status of each requirement, and risk flags for approaching violations.

## The Customization Gap
The missing layer is a compliance orchestration platform that sits on top of existing construction accounting and payroll systems and provides: (1) a unified compliance calendar across all government projects showing every deadline — certified payroll due dates, DBE utilization reports, apprenticeship ratio reports, material certifications; (2) automated compliance validation that checks payroll data against wage determinations before submission, verifies DBE spend percentages against contract goals before reports are due, and flags apprenticeship ratio violations before they trigger penalties; (3) agency-specific formatting — each contracting agency (federal, state DOT, municipal) has slightly different reporting templates, and the system must output in the correct format for each. This requires deep understanding of government construction regulations that general PM or accounting tools don't encode.

## Target Customer
Government construction contractors with 3+ simultaneous public works projects who maintain full-time or part-time compliance staff and use multiple disconnected tools for different compliance requirements.

## Impact If Solved
Consolidates compliance management from 4-6 disconnected tools and spreadsheets into one platform. Prevents compliance violations that result in payment holds (government agencies routinely hold $50K-$500K in progress payments pending compliance resolution). Reduces compliance staffing needs by 30-50%, freeing budget for field operations.
