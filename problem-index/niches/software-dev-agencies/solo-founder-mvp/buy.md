# Lightweight Proposal-to-Contract Pipeline for MVP Projects

**Niche:** [[niches/software-dev-agencies/solo-founder-mvp/profile|Solo-Founder MVP Agencies]]
**Industry:** [[industries/software-dev-agencies|Software Dev Agencies]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** PandaDoc and Proposify are designed for agencies selling $100K+ enterprise engagements with 20-page proposals; MVP agencies need a 2-page scope-and-price document that converts to a binding contract with e-signature in under 30 minutes.
**Tags:** #workflow-orchestration #automation #quick-win

## The Problem
A solo-founder MVP agency receives 3-5 inbound inquiries per week. Each inquiry requires: a discovery call (30 min), scope definition (1-2 hours), proposal writing (2-3 hours), client review and revision (1-2 rounds over 3-7 days), contract generation (copy proposal terms into a separate contract template), and e-signature execution. The entire pipeline takes 8-15 hours of agency principal time per deal, with a 40-50% close rate. For $25K-$50K engagements, the pre-sale cost ($1K-$2K in principal time) is disproportionate. Agency owners need a streamlined pipeline that moves from discovery call to signed contract in one day, not one week.

## What Already Exists
PandaDoc and Proposify offer proposal-to-contract pipelines with templates, e-signatures, and CRM integration. But they are designed for multi-page proposals with sections for company background, team bios, case studies, and detailed methodology — appropriate for $100K+ engagements but overkill for MVP projects. Their pricing ($49-$99/user/month) and workflow complexity are designed for sales teams, not solo agency owners handling 2-3 proposals per week. Simpler tools like Bonsai target freelancers but lack multi-developer team pricing and milestone-based payment structures.

## The Customization Gap
The adaptation requires: (1) a 1-2 page proposal template with feature list, timeline, fixed price, and payment milestones (not hourly estimates), (2) one-click conversion from proposal to binding contract (adding standard terms: IP assignment, warranty, scope change process), (3) milestone-based payment schedule tied to Stripe or PayPal invoicing, (4) a "scope change" amendment workflow (add a feature, update the price, get e-signature on the amendment), and (5) a client portal where the founder can view the approved scope, current progress, and upcoming milestones. All at $15-$25/month pricing appropriate for small agencies.

## Target Customer
2-8 person MVP agencies handling 2-4 concurrent projects, where the agency principal manages sales and delivery simultaneously.

## Impact If Solved
Reduces pre-sale time from 8-15 hours to 2-3 hours per deal. Shortens sales cycle from 1-2 weeks to 1-2 days. Increases close rate by 10-15% through faster response time. For an agency closing 20 projects/year at $30K average, saves 200+ hours of principal time and adds $90K-$135K in revenue from improved close rates.
