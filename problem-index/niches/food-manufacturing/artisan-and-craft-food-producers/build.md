# Artisan Food Production Management Platform

**Niche:** [[niches/food-manufacturing/artisan-and-craft-food-producers/profile|Artisan & Craft Food Producers]]
**Industry:** [[industries/food-manufacturing|Food Manufacturing]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No production management platform is designed for a 10-person cheese-making operation — where the founder needs batch tracking, ingredient traceability, HACCP documentation, nutrition labeling, and cost-per-batch visibility in a tool that takes 30 minutes to set up, not 30 days.
**Tags:** #automation #compliance #workflow-orchestration #quick-win

## The Problem
Artisan food producers manage production with paper batch records, handwritten HACCP logs, and mental arithmetic for costing. They must comply with FDA FSMA requirements (HACCP plans, allergen labeling, supplier verification), state health department regulations (facility licenses, production reports), and retailer requirements (certificates of insurance, product specifications, nutrition facts). Each of these compliance domains generates paperwork that the founder manages personally — stealing time from the production work that creates the product's value. Enterprise food manufacturing software (SAP, Infor, BatchMaster) costs $1,000-$5,000/month and requires implementation projects that small artisan producers can't afford or manage.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
The artisan food market is large in aggregate ($20-30B) but fragmented across thousands of micro-producers making different products (cheese, bread, chocolate, sauces, fermented foods). Building a platform that handles this product diversity requires a flexible batch record system that works for a cheese aging 6 months, a bread baked in 4 hours, and a hot sauce fermented for 3 weeks. The price sensitivity is extreme — these producers will pay $30-$75/month, not $500. Software companies that understand food manufacturing build for large plants; companies that build simple tools don't understand food regulatory requirements.

## What to Build
A mobile-first production management app for artisan food producers: batch record creation with recipe management (ingredients, quantities, process steps), ingredient lot tracking (scan supplier lot codes at receiving, link to batches), HACCP monitoring log (CCP readings captured on mobile with automatic limit verification), nutrition facts generation from recipe ingredients (using USDA nutrient database), and cost-per-batch calculation from ingredient costs and labor time. Self-service setup in under 1 hour. Price: $49-$79/month.

## Target Customer
Founders and production managers at artisan food companies with 5-50 employees and $200K-$5M annual revenue. Approximately 20,000-30,000 companies in the US.

## Impact If Built
Gives artisan producers their first digital production management tool — replacing paper batch records, handwritten HACCP logs, and spreadsheet costing. Ensures FDA compliance without requiring a food safety consultant ($5,000-$15,000/year savings). Provides per-batch cost visibility that enables pricing decisions. Reduces the founder's administrative burden by 5-10 hours/week, freeing time for the production and creative work that drives the business.
