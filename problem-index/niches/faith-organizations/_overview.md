## Why These Niches

Megachurches and small rural congregations together represent the vast majority of US religious attendance and giving — megachurches through concentrated resources and rural churches through sheer volume (250,000+ congregations under 100 members). Mosques and church-run preschools are digitally neglected: mosques lack faith-specific software designed for Islamic practices (zakat calculations, Ramadan scheduling, Friday khutbah management), while church preschools operate in a regulatory gray zone between ministry and licensed childcare with inadequate tools for either. Online worship and facility rental represent underserved use cases that exploded post-COVID but lack purpose-built platforms. Tithing/pledge management and volunteer scheduling are the highest-volume automatable workflows — every congregation does both, and most do them manually. Excluded: denominational headquarters (enterprise operations), parachurch organizations (different funding model), and seminary/theological education.
