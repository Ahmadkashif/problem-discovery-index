# Online Member Engagement Analytics

**Niche:** [[niches/faith-organizations/online-worship-communities/profile|Online Worship Communities]]
**Industry:** [[industries/faith-organizations|Faith Organizations]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** YouTube Analytics and Facebook Insights provide view counts and watch time but cannot measure spiritual engagement — distinguishing between someone who watched 3 minutes out of curiosity and someone who attended every Sunday for 6 months but has never connected with a small group.
**Tags:** #gradient-boosting #binary-classification #tabular-ml #data-integration #revenue-impact

## The Problem
Online campus pastors know how many people watched their stream (YouTube Analytics tells them) but have no idea who those people are, whether they're returning viewers, or what their engagement journey looks like. A church with 2,000 weekly online viewers cannot answer basic questions: How many are regulars vs. first-time visitors? Which regulars have been declining in engagement? Which viewers have progressed from watching to giving to joining a small group? Without this data, online pastors cannot do the relational work that drives retention — they're preaching to an invisible audience.

## What Already Exists
YouTube Analytics provides view counts, watch time, and demographics. Facebook Insights tracks group engagement. Planning Center People tracks known members. Google Analytics tracks website behavior. However, none of these tools connect the anonymous stream viewer to a known person, track engagement across platforms (YouTube + app + giving platform), or model the spiritual engagement journey from anonymous viewer to connected member.

## The Customization Gap
The missing layer is a cross-platform engagement aggregation tool that links identities across streaming platform (YouTube viewer ID), church app (logged-in user), giving platform (donor record), and small group system (group member) to build a unified engagement profile per person. The system tracks engagement progression (viewer → app user → giver → group member) and flags engagement drops (someone who gave monthly just missed 3 months). Reports are framed in church engagement language, not marketing funnel terminology.

## Target Customer
Online campus pastors at churches with 500+ weekly online viewers who want to move from "broadcast and hope" to intentional online community development.

## Impact If Solved
Enables proactive outreach to declining engagers (preventing 20-30% of online attendance attrition), identifies the 10-15% of viewers ready for deeper connection (small groups, serving, giving), and provides the engagement data to justify online campus budget to church leadership.
