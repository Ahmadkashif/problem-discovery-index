# Rental Contract & Insurance Automation

**Niche:** [[niches/faith-organizations/facility-rental-managers/profile|Facility Rental Managers]]
**Industry:** [[industries/faith-organizations|Faith Organizations]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Contract management tools like DocuSign handle digital signatures but don't provide church-specific rental agreement templates with liability clauses, insurance verification, security deposit management, and facility use rules tailored to shared sacred/community spaces.
**Tags:** #automation #compliance #quick-win #workflow-orchestration

## The Problem
Every facility rental requires a contract covering liability, insurance, security deposit, cancellation policy, prohibited activities, setup/teardown requirements, and facility use rules. Most churches use a Word document template (often drafted by a church member who happens to be a lawyer) that gets manually customized for each rental, printed, and signed in person. Insurance verification — confirming the renter has general liability coverage naming the church as additional insured — is done via phone call and faxed certificate. The process takes 2-3 hours per rental and creates compliance risk when contracts are incomplete or insurance lapses.

## What Already Exists
DocuSign and HelloSign handle digital contract signing. Tripleseat manages event venue contracts and BEOs. CAKE and EventBooking provide venue management for dedicated spaces. However, none provide church-specific contract templates that address unique concerns: use of sanctuary vs. fellowship hall (different rules), alcohol policies (denominationally specific), noise restrictions during worship services in adjacent spaces, and the church's right to cancel for emergency church programming.

## The Customization Gap
The missing product is a church-specific rental contract automation system with: pre-built contract templates covering common church rental scenarios (wedding, reception, community meeting, recurring group, filming), configurable clauses for denomination-specific policies (alcohol, dance, amplified music), automated insurance verification (COI upload with AI extraction of coverage amounts and named insured), digital security deposit management with auto-refund workflow, and e-signature for all parties.

## Target Customer
Church administrators managing 3+ facility rentals per month who currently spend 2-3 hours per rental on contract preparation and insurance verification.

## Impact If Solved
Reduces per-rental administrative time from 3 hours to 30 minutes, ensures 100% of rentals have verified insurance (currently 60-70% due to manual verification gaps), and eliminates the liability exposure from incomplete or outdated contract language.
