# Stewardship Campaign Automation

**Niche:** [[niches/faith-organizations/tithing-pledge-campaigns/profile|Tithing & Pledge Campaigns]]
**Industry:** [[industries/faith-organizations|Faith Organizations]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Email marketing tools like Mailchimp can send campaign communications but don't provide the full stewardship campaign workflow — paper-to-digital pledge card processing, pledge fulfillment tracking, and thank-you letter generation tied to specific campaign milestones.
**Tags:** #automation #workflow-orchestration #quick-win #revenue-impact

## The Problem
Most churches run an annual stewardship campaign (4-6 weeks) where members are invited to pledge their giving for the coming year. The campaign involves sermon series, testimony videos, personal letters from the pastor, pledge card distribution, pledge collection (still paper at 60% of churches), pledge data entry, acknowledgment letters, and ongoing pledge fulfillment tracking through the year. Each step is managed manually — the church secretary types pledge cards into a spreadsheet, the pastor hand-signs thank-you letters, and quarterly fulfillment reminders go out as mail-merged letters. A campaign for a 500-member church consumes 80-120 staff hours.

## What Already Exists
Mailchimp and Constant Contact handle email communications. Planning Center Giving tracks transactions. Church capital campaign consultants (Horizons Stewardship, RSI) provide strategic guidance. However, no tool provides the end-to-end campaign workflow: configurable campaign timeline with automated communications, pledge card OCR (scan paper cards and auto-populate the database), pledge-to-giving reconciliation, automated fulfillment reminders (with configurable frequency and tone), and campaign progress dashboards showing pledged vs. received in real time.

## The Customization Gap
The missing product is a stewardship campaign management tool with church-specific features: pledge card processing (paper scan → OCR → database entry), pledge fulfillment tracking per household with configurable reminder cadences (monthly email, quarterly letter), campaign thermometer dashboard for congregation-wide visibility, thank-you letter generation triggered by pledge milestones, and year-over-year campaign comparison reporting. Tone and language must be pastoral, not sales-oriented.

## Target Customer
Stewardship directors and church administrators running annual pledge campaigns at churches with 200+ giving units.

## Impact If Solved
Reduces campaign administration from 100 hours to 25 hours, increases pledge fulfillment rate from 82% to 91% through automated reminders, and generates 5-10% higher total campaign commitments by improving the pledge experience.
