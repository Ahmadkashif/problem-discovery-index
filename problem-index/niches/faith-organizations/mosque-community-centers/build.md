# Islamic Center Operations Platform

**Niche:** [[niches/faith-organizations/mosque-community-centers/profile|Mosque Community Centers]]
**Industry:** [[industries/faith-organizations|Faith Organizations]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No comprehensive management platform exists for US mosques that handles daily prayer time scheduling, Islamic calendar events, zakat tracking, multilingual communications, and community membership in a single tool.
**Tags:** #automation #workflow-orchestration #data-integration #ai-platform

## The Problem
A mosque administrator juggles 8-10 disconnected tools and manual processes: prayer time apps for daily salat schedule, WhatsApp for community announcements (in 2-3 languages), Square or cash boxes for donations, Google Sheets for membership, paper sign-ups for Quran classes, and email for facility rental inquiries. The cognitive load of managing these disparate systems falls on 1-2 volunteer administrators or a single underpaid staff member. There is no operational "hub" where everything comes together, and switching between tools for bilingual communities (Arabic/English, Urdu/English) adds another layer of complexity.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
The US mosque market (3,000 organizations) is small compared to the church market (300,000+), making it less attractive for venture-funded software companies. Cultural and linguistic diversity within the Muslim community means a one-size-fits-all platform is difficult — a Somali mosque in Minneapolis has different operational needs than an Arab mosque in Dearborn or a South Asian mosque in Houston. Previous attempts (Masjidal, My Masjid) have been underfunded and poorly maintained.

## What to Build
A mosque management platform with: automated daily prayer time display (calculated by location with Hanafi/Shafi'i method options), Islamic calendar integration (Ramadan, Eid, Hajj season), membership management with family-based records, bilingual communication (push notifications and SMS in English + community language), donation tracking with zakat/sadaqah categorization, class and event registration, and facility rental management. Mobile-first design for both administrators and community members.

## Target Customer
Board presidents and administrators at the 3,000+ US mosques and Islamic centers, particularly mid-sized mosques (200-1,000 regular attendees) that have outgrown WhatsApp but can't afford custom solutions. Priced at $50-$150/month.

## Impact If Built
Saves 15-20 hours per week of administrator time currently spent managing 8+ disconnected tools, increases community engagement by 30% through reliable multilingual communications, and improves donation tracking that recovers 10-15% of currently unrecorded cash contributions.
