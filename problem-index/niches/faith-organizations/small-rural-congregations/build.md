# One-Screen Church Dashboard

**Niche:** [[niches/faith-organizations/small-rural-congregations/profile|Small Rural Congregations]]
**Industry:** [[industries/faith-organizations|Faith Organizations]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No product exists that gives a small church pastor a single screen showing attendance trend, giving status, upcoming events, and prayer requests — everything a 50-member church needs to operate, without login credentials, training, or a monthly subscription over $10.
**Tags:** #quick-win #automation #workflow-orchestration

## The Problem
A bivocational pastor managing a 50-member rural church needs to know: Did giving cover this month's expenses? Who hasn't been to church in 3 weeks (and might need a visit)? What events are coming up? Who's on the prayer list? Currently, this information is scattered across a checkbook, a paper attendance roll, a bulletin board, and the pastor's memory. The pastor doesn't have time to learn church management software, and the volunteer secretary who updates the phone tree is 74 years old. Every existing church management tool assumes more complexity, more users, and more technical sophistication than this church has.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
Software companies target churches with budgets — a $72/month subscription times 250,000 small churches is a $200M market, but the sales cost per customer is prohibitive when the buyer doesn't have email. The user interface requirements are extreme: it must be simpler than Facebook (which many of these users already use), require no setup beyond "enter your church name," and work on a shared computer in the church office. Building simple software is harder than building complex software, and the unit economics don't attract venture-funded startups.

## What to Build
A single-page web app (no app store download required) that shows four panels: giving (total this month vs. budget, with a simple "record offering" button), attendance (this week's count, 3-month trend, names of members absent 3+ weeks), events (next 4 Sundays, any special events), and care (prayer requests, hospital visits needed, birthdays this week). Data entry happens on the same screen — tap a name to mark present, type a number to record offering. No menus, no settings, no configuration. Priced at $5-$10/month.

## Target Customer
Bivocational pastors and volunteer church secretaries at congregations with 20-100 members and no paid administrative staff. Total addressable market: 250,000+ congregations.

## Impact If Built
Gives 250,000 small churches their first digital operations tool, saving pastors 3-5 hours per week currently spent reconciling paper records, and enabling proactive pastoral care by surfacing attendance drops and care needs that currently go unnoticed.
