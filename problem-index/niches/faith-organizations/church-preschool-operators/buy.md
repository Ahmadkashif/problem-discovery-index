# Tuition Billing for Ministry-Priced Programs

**Niche:** [[niches/faith-organizations/church-preschool-operators/profile|Church Preschool Operators]]
**Industry:** [[industries/faith-organizations|Faith Organizations]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Childcare billing platforms like Brightwheel and Procare handle standard tuition but don't accommodate church-specific pricing models — member discounts, ministry scholarships, sliding-scale fees based on pastoral assessment, and tax-deductible tuition portions.
**Tags:** #automation #compliance #data-integration #quick-win

## The Problem
Church preschools typically charge below-market tuition as a ministry value proposition, supplemented by the church budget. Pricing structures are complex: church member families get 10-20% discounts, pastoral scholarships reduce or eliminate tuition for families in hardship, multi-child discounts stack differently than at commercial centers, and some churches structure a portion of tuition as a tax-deductible "church donation" to reduce family cost. Standard childcare billing platforms handle flat-rate or hourly billing but cannot model these ministry-specific pricing tiers without manual workarounds.

## What Already Exists
Brightwheel, HiMama, and Procare provide automated tuition billing with ACH processing, late fee management, and subsidy tracking (for state childcare assistance). QuickBooks can handle custom invoicing. However, none support the church-specific pricing logic: "The Smith family is a church member (10% discount) with a pastoral scholarship ($100/month credit) enrolled in 3-day program, and $50/month of their tuition is structured as a tax-deductible contribution."

## The Customization Gap
The missing capability is a tuition billing module that understands church-preschool pricing: configurable discount tiers (member, staff, multi-child, sibling), scholarship/aid management with pastoral override, split billing (tuition portion + donation portion with separate tax treatment), and integration with the church's giving system so the donation portion appears on the family's year-end giving statement. The system must also handle the church budget reconciliation — showing the preschool director how much tuition revenue covers vs. how much the church budget subsidizes.

## Target Customer
Preschool directors at church programs with 40+ enrolled children who currently manage tuition in QuickBooks or spreadsheets because childcare billing platforms can't handle their pricing model.

## Impact If Solved
Reduces tuition billing administration from 8 hours to 2 hours per month, eliminates billing errors that average $500-$1,000/month in under-collected tuition, and simplifies year-end tax reporting for families receiving split tuition/donation billing.
