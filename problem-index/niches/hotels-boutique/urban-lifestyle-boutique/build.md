# Competitive Rate Intelligence for Single-Property Independents

**Niche:** [[niches/hotels-boutique/urban-lifestyle-boutique/profile|Urban Lifestyle Boutiques]]
**Industry:** [[industries/hotels-boutique|Boutique Hotels]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No affordable product gives a single-property boutique GM real-time competitive rate positioning relative to their 15-30 direct competitors — the intelligence chain hotels get from IDeaS starts at $15K/year.
**Tags:** #gradient-boosting #time-series-forecasting #regression #tabular-ml #revenue-impact

## The Problem
An urban boutique GM checks Booking.com manually 2-3 times per week to see competitor rates — but this snapshot misses rate changes that happen hourly, does not show booking pace or availability trends, and provides no recommendation. Chain competitors reprice 3-5 times per day based on automated competitive intelligence. The boutique GM is bringing a knife to a gunfight: reacting to yesterday's market while chains are pricing to tomorrow's demand.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
Lighthouse (formerly OTA Insight) provides rate shopping at $100-$150/month per property, but it shows raw data — competitor rates by date — without recommendations. IDeaS and Duetto provide recommendations but at $15K-$20K/year with multi-property minimums. The gap is an affordable ($200-$500/month) rate intelligence tool that combines competitive data with property-specific demand signals to produce actionable rate recommendations for a single independent property.

## What to Build
A competitive intelligence dashboard that scrapes OTA rates for the property's comp set daily, combines with the property's PMS booking pace data, local event calendar, and historical patterns to produce rate recommendations per room type per night. Key output: "Your Standard King is priced at $189 tonight. Your comp set average is $215. Booking pace is 30% above baseline. Recommended rate: $225." The GM reviews and accepts with one click.

## Target Customer
GMs of 20-80 room urban boutique hotels in markets with 15+ competitive properties who currently set rates 2-3 times per week based on manual OTA checks.

## Impact If Built
Closing even half the rate gap between manual pricing and optimized pricing generates 8-15% RevPAR improvement — $100K-$400K annually for a 40-60 room urban property.
