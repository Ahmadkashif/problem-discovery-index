# Lightweight PMS for Sub-20 Room Properties

**Niche:** [[niches/hotels-boutique/rural-retreat-properties/profile|Rural Retreat Properties]]
**Industry:** [[industries/hotels-boutique|Boutique Hotels]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Cloudbeds and Mews are designed for 30+ room hotels with front desk staff — they are overpowered and overpriced for a 5-room B&B run by a couple who need a simple calendar, guest log, and breakfast count.
**Tags:** #automation #data-integration #quick-win #worker-facing

## The Problem
A 10-room rural B&B owner needs: a unified calendar showing all rooms across all channels (Airbnb, VRBO, direct), a guest record (dietary preferences, room preferences, past stays), a breakfast count for morning prep, and a simple nightly revenue summary. They do not need a front desk module, group booking management, rate plans by market segment, or housekeeping scheduling for a team they do not have. Cloudbeds ($100-$200/month) offers all this and more — but "more" means complexity that the owner-operator does not have time to learn.

## What Already Exists
Cloudbeds, Little Hotelier, and Lodgify offer PMS solutions for small properties. Airbnb's host dashboard provides calendar and messaging. Google Calendar is used by many B&B owners as a free alternative. But PMS products are designed for properties with staff and front desks — their onboarding requires configuring room types, rate plans, tax rules, and channel connections that take 10-20 hours to set up, overwhelming a couple running a B&B between guest check-ins and breakfast service.

## The Customization Gap
The tool must be radically simple: 15-minute setup, mobile-first (the owner runs the property from their phone, not a front desk computer), unified calendar with Airbnb/VRBO sync, a guest profile with notes (dietary, preferences, anniversaries), daily breakfast count (the B&B-specific feature no PMS includes), and a one-page daily dashboard showing arrivals, departures, and revenue. Everything else is noise.

## Target Customer
Owner-operators of 5-20 room B&Bs, lodges, and farmstays who currently manage reservations on Airbnb's dashboard or Google Calendar and have no guest records beyond Airbnb messaging history.

## Impact If Solved
A simple, affordable ($30-$50/month) PMS gives 20,000+ rural retreat owners their first unified property management tool — enabling the guest personalization (remembering dietary needs, room preferences) that is their primary competitive advantage.
