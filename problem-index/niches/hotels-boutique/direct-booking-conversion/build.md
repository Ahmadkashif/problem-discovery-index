# Independent Hotel Loyalty and Return-Guest Platform

**Niche:** [[niches/hotels-boutique/direct-booking-conversion/profile|Direct Booking Conversion Operations]]
**Industry:** [[industries/hotels-boutique|Boutique Hotels]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No product gives a single boutique hotel a loyalty program that competes with Marriott Bonvoy — capturing guest emails, incentivizing direct rebooking, and building the direct relationship that OTAs deliberately prevent.
**Tags:** #recommendation #automation #data-integration #revenue-impact

## The Problem
A boutique hotel's best potential direct bookers are past guests — people who already know and love the property. But OTA bookings do not provide guest email addresses (Booking.com controls the communication), and the hotel has no system to capture guest contact information, track stay history, or incentivize direct return bookings. The guest who stayed 3 times via Booking.com could have booked direct at 15% less cost to the hotel — but nobody asked.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
Chain loyalty programs work because of network effects (earn points at any Marriott worldwide). A single-property loyalty program has no network — the value proposition must be different: personal recognition, room preference memory, return-guest perks (complimentary upgrade, late checkout, welcome amenity), and a direct price guarantee. Building this requires guest data capture at check-in, a CRM that tracks preferences across stays, and an automated post-stay outreach sequence — capabilities that exist separately but have not been bundled for single-property independents.

## What to Build
A return-guest platform that captures email and preferences at check-in (tablet at front desk), builds a guest profile across stays, sends post-stay thank-you emails with a direct booking incentive (5-10% discount + guaranteed room preference), and manages a simple loyalty tier (3 stays = complimentary upgrade, 5 stays = complimentary night). Integration with the booking engine to recognize returning guests and auto-apply perks.

## Target Customer
Boutique hotel GMs with 20-35% repeat guest rates who have no guest email database and no system to incentivize direct rebooking.

## Impact If Built
Converting 20% of OTA repeat guests to direct booking saves $20K-$80K annually in commissions. Building a guest database of 2,000+ emails creates a direct marketing channel worth $50K-$150K in annual direct bookings within 2-3 years.
