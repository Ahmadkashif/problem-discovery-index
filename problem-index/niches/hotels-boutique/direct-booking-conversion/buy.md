# Google Hotel Ads for Single-Property Independents

**Niche:** [[niches/hotels-boutique/direct-booking-conversion/profile|Direct Booking Conversion Operations]]
**Industry:** [[industries/hotels-boutique|Boutique Hotels]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Google Hotel Ads is the most cost-effective direct booking channel (10-12% commission vs. 15-25% OTA), but setup and bid management require technical expertise and continuous optimization that single-property owners do not have.
**Tags:** #gradient-boosting #regression #tabular-ml #automation #revenue-impact

## The Problem
Google Hotel Ads places the hotel's direct rate alongside OTA prices in Google search and Maps — often the first touchpoint for a potential guest. For independents, this channel has lower commissions (10-12% on CPA model) than Booking.com (15-25%) and generates direct bookings with full guest data. But setting up Google Hotel Ads requires a Google-certified booking engine, ARI (Availability, Rate, Inventory) feed configuration, bid strategy selection, and ongoing bid optimization — technical work that requires a digital marketing specialist a boutique hotel does not have.

## What Already Exists
Triptease, Bookassist, and SHR offer Google Hotel Ads management as part of their booking engine packages. Google's own Hotel Center provides the setup interface. But these tools assume a revenue manager or digital marketer is available to set bid strategies, monitor performance, and adjust campaigns — skills and bandwidth that a boutique GM does not have.

## The Customization Gap
The tool must provide a fully managed Google Hotel Ads experience for single-property independents: automated setup (connect PMS, configure ARI feed), intelligent bid management (bid higher on dates with low occupancy, lower on dates already selling well), performance reporting in plain language ("Google Hotel Ads generated 12 direct bookings this month at $28 average cost per booking vs. $45 on Booking.com"), and automatic budget allocation based on ROI.

## Target Customer
Boutique hotel GMs who know Google Hotel Ads exists but have not set it up because the technical requirements and ongoing management exceed their capabilities.

## Impact If Solved
Google Hotel Ads typically generate 10-20% of a property's direct bookings within 6 months. At a lower cost-per-booking than OTAs, each booking shifted saves $15-$30 — $10K-$40K annually for a 40-room property.
