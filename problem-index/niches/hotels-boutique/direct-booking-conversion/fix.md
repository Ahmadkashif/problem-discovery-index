# Website Booking Abandonment Recovery

**Niche:** [[niches/hotels-boutique/direct-booking-conversion/profile|Direct Booking Conversion Operations]]
**Industry:** [[industries/hotels-boutique|Boutique Hotels]]
**Type:** Fix (Pain Point)
**One-liner:** 85-90% of visitors who start a booking on a boutique hotel's website abandon before completing it — most return to Booking.com where they feel safer, costing the hotel 15-25% commission on a booking it almost had.
**Tags:** #gradient-boosting #binary-classification #tabular-ml #revenue-impact #automation

## The Problem
A boutique hotel website gets 5,000 monthly visitors. 500 start a booking (search dates, view rates). Only 50 complete the booking — a 10-15% conversion rate on booking initiators. The remaining 450 leave, and 60-70% of them book the same hotel on Booking.com instead. The hotel pays $45 in commission for a guest who was already on their website. The abandonment happens because: the booking widget is slow, the guest is not sure if the rate is the best available, there is no price-match guarantee visible, and Booking.com has "only 2 rooms left!" urgency.

## Why It's Still Broken
E-commerce abandoned cart recovery is a mature practice (email/SMS follow-up, retargeting ads), but hotel booking abandonment recovery requires two capabilities boutique hotels lack: (1) email capture before the guest completes the booking (most booking widgets do not capture email until payment), and (2) retargeting infrastructure (pixel placement, ad campaign management). Triptease and The Hotels Network offer exit-intent overlays, but these are standalone tools that require setup and monitoring the boutique GM does not do.

## What a Fix Looks Like
An integrated booking abandonment recovery system: email capture at the date-search step (before checkout), exit-intent overlay with direct price guarantee ("Book direct — same price as Booking.com, plus free breakfast"), automated follow-up email within 1 hour with the specific dates and rate the guest searched, and retargeting ads showing the property for 7 days. All automated with no ongoing GM involvement.

## Who Feels the Pain
The owner who pays $80K/year in OTA commissions while watching their website analytics show 450 abandoned bookings per month — bookings that were inches from being commission-free.

## Impact If Fixed
Recovering 10-15% of abandoned bookings shifts 50-70 bookings per year from OTA to direct — saving $3K-$10K in commissions annually and building the guest email database for future direct marketing.
