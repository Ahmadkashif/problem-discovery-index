## Why These Niches

Commercial janitorial and residential recurring represent the two dominant revenue engines — together they account for over 70% of industry revenue and face distinct operational challenges (contract bidding vs. consumer scheduling). Post-construction and carpet/floor care are digitally neglected segments still running on paper work orders and phone scheduling despite having complex job costing needs. Green cleaning and medical facility cleaning serve underserved audiences with specialized compliance and certification requirements that generic cleaning software ignores. Franchise operations and quality inspection represent the highest automation ROI — franchise owners manage dozens of identical workflows across locations, and quality inspection is still done by walk-throughs that could be standardized and sensor-augmented.
