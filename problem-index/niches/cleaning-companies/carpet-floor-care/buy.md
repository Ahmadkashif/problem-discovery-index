# Job Costing for Multi-Service Floor Care

**Niche:** [[niches/cleaning-companies/carpet-floor-care/profile|Carpet & Floor Care Specialists]]
**Industry:** [[industries/cleaning-companies|Cleaning Companies]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Generic invoicing tools can't price a job that combines carpet extraction, tile grout cleaning, and hardwood refinishing with different rates per service per surface type.
**Tags:** #automation #data-integration #revenue-impact

## The Problem
A single floor care job might include carpet steam cleaning in bedrooms ($0.35/sq ft), tile and grout cleaning in bathrooms ($0.50/sq ft), hardwood buffing in living areas ($0.25/sq ft), plus add-ons like stain treatment ($25/spot), furniture moving ($15/piece), and Scotchgard application ($0.15/sq ft). Building an accurate quote requires measuring each surface area, identifying the material and service, applying the correct rate, and totaling with add-ons. Technicians do this on paper or calculator apps, making math errors that cost $50-$200 per job.

## What Already Exists
Invoicing tools like QuickBooks, FreshBooks, and Jobber create line-item invoices. But they don't have a room-by-room measurement interface, don't know floor care rate structures, and don't calculate surface area from room dimensions. The technician must manually calculate everything and type it in.

## The Customization Gap
The tool needs a room-by-room quoting workflow: select room type, enter dimensions, identify surface type, select services, and auto-calculate. It should store customer-specific rate cards (commercial clients get volume pricing), remember room measurements for recurring jobs, and integrate with scheduling to track actual vs. estimated time for profitability analysis.

## Target Customer
Floor care operators quoting 15+ jobs per week who lose revenue to pricing errors and spend 30-45 minutes per quote on manual calculations.

## Impact If Solved
Eliminates under-pricing errors (typically 5-10% revenue leakage) and cuts quoting time from 30 minutes to 5 minutes per job.
