# Healthcare-Adapted Training & Certification Platform

**Niche:** [[niches/cleaning-companies/medical-facility/profile|Medical Facility Cleaning]]
**Industry:** [[industries/cleaning-companies|Cleaning Companies]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Generic LMS platforms deliver training content but don't track healthcare-specific certifications, renewal deadlines, or state-by-state requirements.
**Tags:** #automation #compliance #worker-facing

## The Problem
Medical facility cleaning technicians need multiple certifications: OSHA Bloodborne Pathogens (annual renewal), facility-specific infection control orientation, EPA disinfectant application training, and sometimes state-specific certifications (e.g., California's IIPP requirements). Tracking which employees hold which certifications, when they expire, and which state requirements apply to each client site is a manual nightmare that typically lives in a spreadsheet maintained by the owner.

## What Already Exists
LMS platforms like TalentLMS, Trainual, and cleaning-specific options like KaiNexus handle content delivery and basic certification tracking. OSHA training providers offer online bloodborne pathogen courses. None integrate the full lifecycle: identify required certifications per client site, assign training to employees, track completion and expiration, auto-schedule renewals, and flag non-compliant employee-site assignments before they happen.

## The Customization Gap
The platform needs a regulatory rules engine that maps state + facility type to required certifications, then cross-references employee cert status against scheduled assignments. It should block scheduling a technician at a dental office if their bloodborne pathogen training expired last month. Renewal reminders need to go to both the employee and the dispatcher, with enough lead time to complete training before the deadline.

## Target Customer
Medical cleaning company owners managing 15+ technicians across multiple states who spend 3-5 hours per week tracking certifications and occasionally discover expired certifications after the fact.

## Impact If Solved
Eliminates compliance gaps that could result in contract termination or regulatory fines, and reduces certification management overhead from 5 hours/week to 30 minutes/week.
