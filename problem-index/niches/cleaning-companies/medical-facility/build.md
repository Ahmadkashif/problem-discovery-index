# Infection Control Compliance Dashboard

**Niche:** [[niches/cleaning-companies/medical-facility/profile|Medical Facility Cleaning]]
**Industry:** [[industries/cleaning-companies|Cleaning Companies]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No tool gives medical facility cleaning operators a real-time view of compliance status across all client sites with automated audit preparation.
**Tags:** #automation #compliance #data-integration #workflow-orchestration

## The Problem
Medical facility cleaning companies must prove compliance with infection control standards across every client site, every shift. This means documenting that EPA-registered disinfectants were used with correct contact times, high-touch surfaces were disinfected at required frequencies, biohazardous waste was handled properly, and staff had current bloodborne pathogen training. When a health department inspector arrives at a client's dental office and asks "when was this operatory last terminally cleaned?", the cleaning company needs to produce documentation instantly — but it's buried in paper logs at the site or in a binder at the office.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
Enterprise EVS platforms serve large hospital systems with dedicated IT departments and six-figure budgets. The thousands of small cleaning companies serving outpatient clinics operate on thin margins and need a $50-$200/month solution. Building for this segment requires understanding both the regulatory requirements (which vary by state and facility type) and the operational reality (2-3 person crews working nights with minimal supervision).

## What to Build
A mobile-first compliance platform where technicians check off protocol steps on their phone (disinfectant applied, contact time met, waste disposed), with timestamps and optional photo verification. The dashboard aggregates compliance data across all client sites, flags gaps (missed cleanings, expired training certificates, overdue equipment maintenance), and generates audit-ready reports for health department inspections or client reviews.

## Target Customer
Medical facility cleaning companies with 5+ healthcare client sites who spend 8-12 hours per month preparing compliance documentation and live in fear of inspection failures.

## Impact If Built
Reduces compliance documentation time by 75% and transforms audit preparation from a multi-day scramble into a one-click report, while reducing the risk of regulatory violations that could cost $5,000-$50,000 per incident.
