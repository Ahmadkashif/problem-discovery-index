# Janitorial-Specific CRM with Scope Tracking

**Niche:** [[niches/cleaning-companies/commercial-janitorial/profile|Commercial Janitorial Contracts]]
**Industry:** [[industries/cleaning-companies|Cleaning Companies]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Generic field-service CRMs track jobs, not multi-year contracts with evolving scope-of-work documents.
**Tags:** #data-integration #workflow-orchestration #automation

## The Problem
Commercial janitorial contracts evolve constantly — clients add floors, change cleaning frequencies, request special services (strip-and-wax, window cleaning). These scope changes happen via email and rarely make it back into the scheduling or billing system. Operators discover months later that they've been providing unreimbursed services.

## What Already Exists
Jobber, ServiceTitan, and Housecall Pro handle scheduling and invoicing for field service businesses. CleanTelligent focuses on inspection and quality tracking. None of these tools model a multi-year contract with versioned scope documents, change-order workflows, and automatic billing adjustments when scope changes.

## The Customization Gap
Janitorial contracts need a "living scope document" — a versioned, auditable record of what's included in the contract price. When a client emails "can you also do the break room on Fridays," the system should generate a change order, update the schedule, adjust the invoice, and notify the crew lead. This workflow doesn't exist in any generic CRM.

## Target Customer
Janitorial company ops managers handling 15+ active contracts who lose track of scope changes and under-bill by 5-10%.

## Impact If Solved
Eliminates scope creep revenue leakage — typically $2,000-$8,000 per building per year in unreimbursed services — and reduces client disputes over "what's included."
