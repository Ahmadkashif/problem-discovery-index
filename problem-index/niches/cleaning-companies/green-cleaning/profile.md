## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Green cleaning companies differentiate on chemical safety — they use plant-based, non-toxic, fragrance-free products and market to clients with allergies, chemical sensitivities, children, or environmental values. This creates unique operational challenges: green products cost 2-4x more than conventional chemicals, they require different application techniques (longer dwell times, manual agitation vs. chemical reaction), and the company must maintain certifications (Green Seal, EPA Safer Choice) that require ingredient tracking and documentation. Clients expect transparency about every product used in their home or office.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Standard cleaning business software ignores the green segment entirely. No platform tracks product ingredient lists against certification requirements, manages supplier relationships for eco-products, or generates the transparency reports that clients increasingly demand. Marketing tools don't understand the green cleaning value proposition or SEO landscape.
