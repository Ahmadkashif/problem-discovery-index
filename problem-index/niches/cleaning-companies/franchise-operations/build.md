# Multi-Territory Performance Analytics

**Niche:** [[niches/cleaning-companies/franchise-operations/profile|Franchise Operations Management]]
**Industry:** [[industries/cleaning-companies|Cleaning Companies]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No tool gives multi-unit cleaning franchise owners a single dashboard comparing revenue, margins, churn, and labor efficiency across all their territories.
**Tags:** #data-integration #automation #revenue-impact

## The Problem
A multi-unit franchise owner with 5 territories might use the franchisor's CRM for bookings, QuickBooks for accounting, Homebase for time tracking, and Google Sheets for reporting — separately for each territory. Comparing Territory A's customer lifetime value against Territory B's requires manually pulling data from 3-4 systems across 5 instances and building a spreadsheet. Most owners simply don't do it, which means underperforming territories go undiagnosed.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
Each franchise system (Molly Maid vs. Jan-Pro vs. MaidPro) uses different proprietary CRMs with different data structures and API availability. Building a cross-franchisor analytics platform requires integrating with a fragmented ecosystem where the franchisors have little incentive to enable third-party data access. The market is also fragmented — multi-unit operators are a small subset of all franchisees.

## What to Build
A cross-territory analytics platform that connects to common franchisor CRMs, accounting systems, and time-tracking tools via APIs and data imports. It normalizes data across territories into a unified dashboard showing: revenue per territory, per-clean profitability, customer acquisition cost, churn rate, labor utilization, and royalty-adjusted net income. Benchmarking features let owners compare their territories against each other and against franchisor-provided industry averages.

## Target Customer
Multi-unit cleaning franchise owners operating 3+ territories who currently have no cross-territory visibility and make resource allocation decisions on gut feel.

## Impact If Built
Identifying and fixing the bottom-performing territory in a 5-unit portfolio (from -2% margin to +8% margin) can add $30,000-$80,000 in annual profit.
