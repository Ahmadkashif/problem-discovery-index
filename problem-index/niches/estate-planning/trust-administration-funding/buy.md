# Trust Accounting with Institutional Coordination Workflow

**Niche:** [[niches/estate-planning/trust-administration-funding/profile|Trust Administration & Funding]]
**Industry:** [[industries/estate-planning|Estate Planning Law Firms]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Trust accounting software handles the math but not the coordination — the gap is connecting accounting entries to the institutional retitling and distribution workflows that generate them.
**Tags:** #workflow-orchestration #data-integration #automation #compliance

## The Problem
Trust administration requires two parallel workflows: the accounting (tracking income, expenses, distributions, and tax allocations across trust shares) and the institutional coordination (retitling assets, liquidating positions, transferring funds, distributing to beneficiaries). Trust accounting software handles the first but is completely disconnected from the second. The paralegal manages institutional coordination in a spreadsheet or task list alongside the accounting system, manually reconciling when an institution confirms a retitling that changes the asset inventory.

## What Already Exists
Fiduciary Accounting System (FAS) and Sitgo handle trust accounting — principal and income allocations, distribution calculations, Form 1041 tax reporting, and beneficiary accounting. These are solid accounting tools used by estate attorneys, corporate trustees, and professional fiduciaries.

## The Customization Gap
Trust accounting needs to be connected to institutional task management: when an asset is inventoried in the accounting system, it should automatically generate the retitling tasks for that institution. When a retitling is confirmed, the accounting should auto-update. When a distribution is calculated, the wire/check instructions should auto-generate. The gap is workflow integration between accounting and institutional coordination — turning two disconnected workflows into one.

## Target Customer
Estate administration paralegals and trust officers managing 10+ concurrent estates who currently maintain parallel tracking systems (accounting software + coordination spreadsheet).

## Impact If Solved
Eliminates dual data entry and reconciliation errors. Reduces estate administration calendar time by 20-30% by surfacing bottlenecks (which institutions haven't responded, which retitlings are blocking distributions).
