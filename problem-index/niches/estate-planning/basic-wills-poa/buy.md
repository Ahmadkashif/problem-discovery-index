# Client Acquisition Trigger System

**Niche:** [[niches/estate-planning/basic-wills-poa/profile|Basic Wills & POA]]
**Industry:** [[industries/estate-planning|Estate Planning]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Marketing automation exists for every industry — but no tool identifies and acts on the life events that trigger estate planning need (new baby, home purchase, marriage, retirement) to reach people at the exact moment they're receptive.
**Tags:** #logistic-regression #gradient-boosting #feature-engineering #evaluation-metrics #automation #revenue-impact

## The Problem
Most people know they need a will but never take action. The conversion trigger is almost always a life event: birth of a child, purchase of a home, marriage, divorce, retirement, death of a parent. Estate planning attorneys rely on referrals from financial advisors and word-of-mouth, which are unpredictable and unscalable. When someone does search for estate planning, they're equally likely to find LegalZoom as a local attorney. Attorneys have no systematic way to identify people experiencing trigger events in their service area and reach them with timely, relevant outreach.

## What Already Exists
General marketing automation platforms (HubSpot, Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign) handle email sequences, landing pages, and lead nurturing. Legal-specific marketing tools (Martindale, Avvo, FindLaw) generate leads through directory listings and SEO. Social media advertising platforms allow demographic and interest-based targeting. But none of these tools are built around life-event triggers specific to estate planning, and none connect the marketing pipeline to the estate planning intake workflow.

## The Customization Gap
The missing layer maps life-event signals to estate planning outreach campaigns. It integrates public records (home purchases, marriage licenses, birth announcements in some jurisdictions), financial advisor referral networks, and demographic data to identify individuals in the attorney's service area who recently experienced a trigger event. The system sends personalized, event-specific outreach: "Congratulations on your new home — here's why homeowners with mortgages need a will and power of attorney" rather than generic "do you have a will?" advertising. It tracks conversion from trigger event to consultation to signed plan, measuring which triggers and messages produce the highest-value clients. Estate-planning-specific compliance is critical: attorney advertising rules vary by state and restrict claims, guarantees, and solicitation methods.

## Target Customer
Solo and small-firm estate planning attorneys who currently spend $2,000-$5,000/month on generic legal marketing (Avvo listings, Google Ads) with poor conversion rates and no way to measure which marketing produces actual signed plans.

## Impact If Solved
Increases new client acquisition by 30-50% by reaching people during trigger events when conversion intent is highest. Reduces cost per acquisition from $300-$500 (generic legal marketing) to $100-$200 (targeted life-event outreach). Shifts attorneys from reactive referral dependence to proactive, measurable client development.
