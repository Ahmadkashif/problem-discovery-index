# Fractional HR for Startups

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/hr-consultants|HR Consultants]]
**Category:** High Market Share

## Profile
**Market Size:** $5-7B
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~22%
**Digital Adoption:** Medium-High — startups use modern HRIS (Rippling, Gusto, Deel) but the fractional HR leader must context-switch between 5-10 different platforms daily
**Target Buyer:** Fractional HR leader or People Operations consultant serving 3-8 seed-to-Series B startups simultaneously
**Automation Potential:** Medium-High — onboarding, policy creation, and compliance setup follow repeatable patterns but must be adapted to each startup's stage, culture, and investor requirements

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Fractional HR for startups is a rapidly growing segment driven by venture-backed companies that need HR infrastructure (offer letters, handbooks, benefits, compliance, stock option administration) but can't justify a full-time VP People until 50-100 employees. The fractional HR leader typically serves 3-8 startup clients simultaneously, spending 5-15 hours/week per client. Each startup uses different tools (Rippling vs. Gusto vs. Deel vs. Justworks), has different equity compensation structures, and operates in a fast-changing environment where headcount doubles in 6 months. The fractional HR leader must build HR infrastructure from scratch for each client — creating handbooks, setting up benefits, establishing performance review processes — while juggling the context-switching overhead of 5-8 concurrent HRIS environments.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Modern HRIS platforms (Rippling, Gusto, Deel, Justworks) are individually excellent but designed for dedicated in-house admins. The fractional HR leader manages 5-8 separate HRIS instances with no unified view. Carta handles equity administration but doesn't connect to HR workflows. SaaS management tools (Vendr, Cledara) track software spending but not HR operations. No platform serves the fractional HR leader's unique need: a meta-layer across multiple client HRIS systems that provides a unified task queue, compliance dashboard, and onboarding workflow.
