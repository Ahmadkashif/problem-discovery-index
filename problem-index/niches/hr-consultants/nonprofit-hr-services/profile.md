# Nonprofit HR Services

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/hr-consultants|HR Consultants]]
**Category:** Underserved Audience

## Profile
**Market Size:** $2-3B
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~8%
**Digital Adoption:** Low-Medium — nonprofits use basic HR tools but underspend on HR infrastructure, and the unique constraints of grant-funded positions create compliance requirements standard HR tools don't handle
**Target Buyer:** HR consultant specializing in nonprofits with 10-100 employees, serving executive directors and board-level HR committees
**Automation Potential:** Medium — nonprofit HR follows the same employment laws as for-profit, but grant compliance, board governance, and compensation constraints add layers that require domain expertise

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Nonprofit HR operates under constraints that for-profit HR doesn't face: compensation is benchmarked against sector norms (excessive pay triggers IRS intermediate sanctions), many positions are partially or fully grant-funded (requiring time allocation tracking and grant reporting), employee classifications interact with volunteer policies, board members have governance responsibilities over HR decisions but often lack HR expertise, and mission alignment creates unique retention and culture dynamics. The HR consultant serving nonprofits must navigate IRS Form 990 compensation reporting, grant-funded position management, and the politics of board-directed HR decisions. Standard HR consulting frameworks designed for for-profit companies miss these nonprofit-specific requirements.

## Current Tools & Gaps
BambooHR, Gusto, and Paychex serve nonprofits for basic HR functions, but none handle grant-funded position tracking (allocating an employee's time and salary across multiple grants with different cost centers and reporting requirements). Time tracking for grant allocation uses Toggl or spreadsheets. Compensation benchmarking uses GuideStar/Candid 990 data, but the consultant must manually pull and analyze comparable organizations. No integrated platform connects nonprofit HR, grant compliance, and board governance in a single workflow.
