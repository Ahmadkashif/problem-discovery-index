# Remote Workforce Compliance

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/hr-consultants|HR Consultants]]
**Category:** Underserved Audience

## Profile
**Market Size:** $3-5B (rapidly growing post-2020 as distributed workforces create new compliance complexity)
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~12%
**Digital Adoption:** Medium — remote companies use modern tools (Deel, Remote.com, Oyster) for international workers but lack solutions for domestic multi-state complexity
**Target Buyer:** HR consultant helping US companies manage compliance for employees working remotely across multiple states
**Automation Potential:** High — multi-state tax registration, wage law compliance, and benefits portability are rule-based problems that can be systematically tracked

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
The post-2020 shift to remote work created a compliance crisis for companies with employees scattered across states: every state where an employee works triggers income tax withholding obligations, unemployment insurance registration, workers' comp coverage, and compliance with that state's employment laws. A 50-person company with employees in 15 states has 15x the compliance burden of a single-state employer. HR consultants are the primary advisors helping SMBs navigate this complexity, but the guidance changes constantly as states revise nexus rules, adopt or withdraw reciprocity agreements, and create new remote worker tax safe harbors. This niche barely existed before 2020 and has no mature tooling.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Deel, Remote.com, and Oyster solve international employer-of-record challenges but don't address domestic multi-state compliance. Tilt365 and Mosey handle state registration but not ongoing compliance management. Payroll systems (Gusto, ADP, Paychex) register for state taxes but don't manage the ongoing compliance obligations triggered by having an employee in a new state (local posting requirements, state-specific handbook provisions, benefits portability, workers' comp classification). No tool provides the "you just hired someone in Massachusetts — here are the 23 things you must now do" workflow that HR consultants deliver manually.
