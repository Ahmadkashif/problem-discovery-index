# Benefits Renewal Processing

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/hr-consultants|HR Consultants]]
**Category:** Highly Automatable

## Profile
**Market Size:** $2-3B (embedded in consulting and brokerage fees)
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~8%
**Digital Adoption:** Low — benefits renewal involves manual data extraction from carrier spreadsheets, side-by-side plan comparison in Excel, and client presentation decks built from scratch each year
**Target Buyer:** HR consultant managing annual benefits renewals for 20-60 SMB clients
**Automation Potential:** High — renewal analysis follows the same workflow every year: extract carrier rates, compare to current and market alternatives, model employee cost impact, and present options

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Annual benefits renewal is a high-stakes, time-concentrated deliverable. Every client's health, dental, vision, life, disability, and retirement plans renew annually, and the HR consultant must: receive renewal quotes from carriers (arriving as PDFs and spreadsheets in inconsistent formats), analyze rate changes, compare against market alternatives, model employee contribution impacts under different plan designs, prepare a recommendation, present to the client's leadership, and administer the enrollment period. For a consultant managing 40 client renewals, this work concentrates into a 3-month window (typically September-December), creating an extreme workload spike. Each renewal analysis takes 8-20 hours of data extraction, comparison, and presentation preparation — work that follows the same pattern year after year.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Benefits brokerages use proprietary quoting tools (Zywave, Ease Marketplace, Employee Navigator Marketplace) that compare carrier rates but don't integrate with the consultant's client data, contribution modeling, or presentation workflow. Carrier rate sheets arrive as PDFs and Excel files with different column layouts, rate tier structures, and benefit descriptions. The consultant must manually normalize data from 3-5 carriers into a comparable format before analysis can begin. No tool automates the full renewal workflow from carrier quote ingestion to client presentation.
