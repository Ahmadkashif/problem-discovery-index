# Mineral/SixFifty with Multi-Client Portfolio Mapping

**Niche:** [[niches/hr-consultants/multi-state-compliance-advisory/profile|Multi-State Compliance Advisory]]
**Industry:** [[industries/hr-consultants|HR Consultants]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Mineral provides legal update analysis and SixFifty generates state-specific employment documents — but neither supports the multi-client consultant who needs to map updates across 50 clients and generate documents for each one, not just one company at a time.
**Tags:** #data-integration #workflow-orchestration #automation #compliance #quick-win

## The Problem
Mineral (formerly ThinkHR) provides excellent employment law analysis with attorney-written summaries of new laws, required employer actions, and compliance checklists. SixFifty automates the generation of employment agreements, offer letters, and handbook provisions with state-specific clauses. Both tools assume a single-company user: one company, one set of states, one headcount, one handbook. An HR consultant managing 50 clients must log into these tools 50 times, enter each client's parameters separately, and manually track which updates apply to which clients. The tools solve the legal analysis and document generation problems but not the portfolio management problem.

## What Already Exists
Mineral ($100-200/month per company) provides legal update feeds, compliance checklists, and live advisor access. SixFifty ($300-500/year per company for employment documents) generates state-specific employment agreements, handbooks, and policies. ComplianceHR ($200-400/month per company) provides compliance risk assessments. All priced and designed for a single company's HR department.

## The Customization Gap
The consultant needs a portfolio layer on top of these tools: (1) a single dashboard showing compliance status across all clients (who is compliant, who has pending actions, who has overdue requirements), (2) batch document generation — when a new state law requires a policy update, generate the updated policy for all 15 clients in that state, not one at a time, (3) client-specific compliance histories — what updates were applied, when, and by whom, creating an audit trail, and (4) consultant-level pricing ($500-1,000/month for the practice, not $200/month per client). The underlying legal analysis and document generation capabilities already exist — the gap is the multi-client orchestration layer.

## Target Customer
HR consultants currently subscribing to Mineral and/or SixFifty at a single-company level and manually managing the multi-client workflow through spreadsheets and email.

## Impact If Solved
Reduces multi-client compliance management overhead by 50-60%. Enables batch policy updates that currently take 1-2 hours per client to be completed across all affected clients in a single workflow. Provides audit trail documentation that protects the consultant against E&O claims.
