## Why These Niches

Insurance DRP and specialty restoration represent the two dominant revenue models in collision repair with fundamentally different workflows. Rural shops and PDR operators remain digitally neglected despite viable addressable markets. Rideshare drivers and commercial vehicle operators are underserved audiences with distinct urgency and documentation needs. Supplement negotiation and photo-based estimating are the two most rule-heavy, automatable workflows in the industry — both currently consuming hours of skilled labor on pattern-matching tasks.
