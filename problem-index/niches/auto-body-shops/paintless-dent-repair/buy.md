# Mobile Invoicing with Per-Dent Matrix Pricing

**Niche:** [[niches/auto-body-shops/paintless-dent-repair/profile|Paintless Dent Repair Operators]]
**Industry:** [[industries/auto-body-shops|Auto Body Shops]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Square Invoices and Wave handle generic line items, but PDR operators need per-dent matrix pricing by size/depth/panel with automatic calculation — not manual line-item entry.
**Tags:** #quick-win #automation #revenue-impact

## The Problem
PDR pricing follows a matrix: a dime-sized shallow dent on a door might be $50, while a quarter-sized deep dent on a roof might be $150. A single hail-damaged vehicle can have 30-80 dents across 8-12 panels, each requiring individual pricing. Using Square Invoices or Wave, the PDR operator must manually add each dent as a line item with the correct price — a process that takes 15-25 minutes per vehicle and is error-prone. Most operators skip the itemized approach entirely and quote a flat price, which often undersells or creates disputes when the customer expects an itemized breakdown.

## What Already Exists
Square Invoices, Wave, and FreshBooks handle generic invoicing with line items. Some PDR operators use spreadsheets with their pricing matrix built in. PDR industry-specific pricing guides (like the NAGS/Dent Matrix) provide standardized pricing, but no invoicing tool natively supports matrix-based pricing input.

## The Customization Gap
The invoicing tool needs a panel-map interface where the technician taps each panel, enters dent counts by size category, and the system automatically calculates pricing from the operator's stored rate matrix. The output is a professional invoice with itemized per-panel pricing, a vehicle diagram showing the dent map, before/after photos, and payment processing — all generated in under 5 minutes on a phone from the customer's driveway.

## Target Customer
PDR operators who invoice 10-30 vehicles per week and currently spend 15-25 minutes per invoice using generic tools or handwriting receipts.

## Impact If Solved
Reduces invoice creation time from 15-25 minutes to 3-5 minutes per vehicle, improves pricing accuracy by 10-15% through consistent matrix application, and professionalizes the customer experience — increasing referral rates by 20-30%.
