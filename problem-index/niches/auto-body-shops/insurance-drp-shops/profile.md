## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Direct Repair Program (DRP) shops have contractual relationships with insurance companies that guarantee referral volume in exchange for meeting performance KPIs: cycle time, customer satisfaction scores (CSI), supplement ratios, and severity-adjusted costs. A single DRP shop might participate in programs with State Farm, GEICO, Progressive, and Allstate — each with different KPI thresholds, reporting portals, and compliance requirements. Losing a DRP relationship means losing 15-30% of revenue overnight. The shop's survival depends on managing these insurer relationships as carefully as managing the actual repairs.

## Current Tools & Gaps
CCC ONE, Mitchell Cloud Estimating, and Audatex handle individual claim estimating per insurer requirements. But no tool aggregates DRP performance metrics across multiple insurer programs into a single dashboard. Shop owners log into 3-8 different insurer portals to track their KPIs, manually compile the data, and react when a metric is trending below threshold. The gap is a unified DRP performance management layer.
