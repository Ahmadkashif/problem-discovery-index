# Experienced Estimators Bottlenecked on Supplement Writing

**Niche:** [[niches/auto-body-shops/supplement-negotiation/profile|Supplement Negotiation Workflow]]
**Industry:** [[industries/auto-body-shops|Auto Body Shops]]
**Type:** Fix (Pain Point)
**One-liner:** Senior estimators spend 60-70% of their day writing and negotiating supplements rather than doing initial estimates or managing production — the shop's most expensive role doing the most repetitive work.
**Tags:** #llm #text-generation #nlp #worker-facing #automation

## The Problem
A senior estimator at a mid-size body shop ($2M+ revenue) spends 4-5 hours per day on supplement-related work: documenting hidden damage, writing supplement line items, citing OEM repair procedures, taking and organizing photos, submitting through insurer portals, and negotiating with adjusters on disputed items. This is the shop's highest-paid non-owner employee ($60K-$90K) doing work that is 70% repetitive documentation and 30% genuine expertise (damage identification and negotiation judgment). The shop can't hire a junior to do supplements because the documentation quality drives approval rates, and poor supplements cost more in delays than they save in salary.

## Why It's Still Broken
Supplement writing requires a specific combination of skills: estimating knowledge (what repair operations are needed), documentation skill (how to photograph and describe damage), insurer knowledge (what each carrier requires), and negotiation judgment (when to push back, when to accept). No single junior hire can do all four. The work can't be easily divided — the same person who identifies the damage needs to document and argue for it. The result is the senior estimator becoming an irreplaceable bottleneck.

## What a Fix Looks Like
An AI writing assistant that drafts supplement documentation from the estimator's inputs: the estimator identifies the damage and selects the repair operation; the system generates the line-item description, pulls the relevant OEM repair procedure citation, formats the photo documentation to insurer standards, and produces the complete supplement package for the estimator to review and submit. The estimator's role shifts from writing supplements to reviewing and approving AI-drafted supplements, tripling their throughput.

## Who Feels the Pain
Senior estimators working 50-55 hour weeks because supplement volume consumes their entire day, and shop owners who can't scale their supplement capacity without cloning their best estimator.

## Impact If Fixed
Triples the senior estimator's effective supplement throughput from 5-7 per day to 15-20 per day, enabling the shop to handle 30-50% more concurrent repairs without hiring additional estimating staff. Saves $40K-$60K per year in potential additional estimator salary.
