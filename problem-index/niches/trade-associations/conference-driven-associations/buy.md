# Sponsor Deliverable Fulfillment Tracker

**Niche:** [[niches/trade-associations/conference-driven-associations/profile|Conference-Driven Associations]]
**Industry:** [[industries/trade-associations|Trade Associations]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** CRM tools like Salesforce and HubSpot track sponsor sales and payments but cannot manage the 15-30 individual deliverables per sponsorship package — logo placement, speaking slots, attendee data, booth upgrades, email sends, social media posts — that must be fulfilled across multiple platforms.
**Tags:** #workflow-orchestration #automation #data-integration #revenue-impact

## The Problem
A $25,000 conference sponsorship package includes 15-30 deliverables: logo on website, logo in mobile app, logo on signage, complimentary registrations, speaking slot assignment, exhibit booth upgrade, attendee list delivery, pre-conference email send, social media mentions, and branded insert in attendee bags. Each deliverable is fulfilled by a different team member using a different tool — the web developer adds the logo to the website, the app administrator adds it to Whova, the signage coordinator sends it to the printer, and the exhibits manager upgrades the booth. Tracking which deliverables have been fulfilled for which sponsor happens in a spreadsheet that the sponsorship director updates manually. When a $25K sponsor's logo is missing from the mobile app on opening day, the association loses credibility and risks the renewal.

## What Already Exists
Salesforce and HubSpot track the sponsorship sales pipeline and payment status. SponsorMyEvent and SponsorPitch handle sponsorship sales outreach. However, none track post-sale deliverable fulfillment — the operational work of actually delivering what was promised. Conference teams use spreadsheet checklists with manual checkboxes, relying on each team member to report their deliverable status.

## The Customization Gap
The missing product is a sponsor deliverable management system that: defines each sponsorship tier's deliverables as a structured checklist, assigns each deliverable to a responsible team member with a deadline, tracks fulfillment status across all sponsors in a single dashboard, sends reminders when deliverables are approaching deadline, and generates a sponsor fulfillment report showing what was delivered (with screenshots/links). At renewal time, the sponsorship director can show each sponsor: "Here's everything we delivered on your $25K package."

## Target Customer
Sponsorship directors at associations managing 20-50 conference sponsors with $500K-$5M in total sponsorship revenue, currently tracking fulfillment in spreadsheets.

## Impact If Solved
Eliminates the 5-10 missed deliverables per conference that damage sponsor relationships, saves 10-15 hours of manual tracking per conference, and increases sponsor renewal rate by 15-20% through documented fulfillment that supports the value conversation.
