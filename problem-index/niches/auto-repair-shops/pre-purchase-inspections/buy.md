# DVI Inspection Templates Adapted for Pre-Purchase Assessment

**Niche:** [[niches/auto-repair-shops/pre-purchase-inspections/profile|Pre-Purchase Inspections]]
**Industry:** [[industries/auto-repair-shops|Auto Repair Shops]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** AutoVitals and Tekmetric DVI templates are designed for maintenance inspections, not buyer-focused pre-purchase assessments — the questions, scoring, and output format are wrong.
**Tags:** #quick-win #automation #workflow-orchestration

## The Problem
Shops repurpose their maintenance DVI templates for PPIs, but the workflows are fundamentally different. A maintenance DVI asks "does this need service?" while a PPI asks "what is the overall condition and remaining life?" A maintenance DVI flags a brake pad at 4mm as "needs replacement soon" — useful for an owner. A PPI buyer needs to know "brake pads have 40% life remaining, estimated replacement cost $350 in approximately 12,000 miles." The framing, scoring, and output structure need to be buyer-oriented, not maintenance-oriented.

## What Already Exists
AutoVitals and Tekmetric provide configurable DVI templates with photo/video capture, condition coding, and digital delivery. The template builder is flexible enough to create PPI-specific checklists, but no pre-built PPI template exists, and the default output format (red/yellow/green with technician notes) isn't structured for buyer consumption.

## The Customization Gap
The template needs PPI-specific inspection categories (structural/frame inspection, fluid condition assessment, electrical system health, tire/brake remaining life percentages, accident history indicators), buyer-facing language instead of technician jargon, and a scoring system that outputs an overall vehicle condition grade (A-F) rather than maintenance urgency colors. The output should be a polished PDF designed for the buyer to share with the seller, not a technician work order.

## Target Customer
Shops already using DVI tools (AutoVitals, Tekmetric) that want to offer PPIs as a distinct service with professional-grade output.

## Impact If Solved
Converts PPIs from a low-margin commodity service ($100, 30 minutes) to a premium professional service ($200-$300, 45 minutes) by differentiating the output quality. Reduces PPI report creation time from 20 minutes of manual write-up to 5 minutes of template completion.
