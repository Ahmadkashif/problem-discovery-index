# Bilingual Customer Communication Platform for Shops

**Niche:** [[niches/auto-repair-shops/spanish-speaking-vehicle-owners/profile|Spanish-Speaking Vehicle Owners]]
**Industry:** [[industries/auto-repair-shops|Auto Repair Shops]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Broadly's text-based customer communication works for English-speaking shops, but lacks automotive-specific Spanish messaging templates and bilingual thread management.
**Tags:** #llm #text-generation #nlp #quick-win #automation

## The Problem
Shops use platforms like Broadly, Podium, or Kimoby to send automated text updates ("Your vehicle is ready for pickup") and request reviews. These platforms operate in English only or offer generic Spanish translations that don't account for automotive context. A bilingual service advisor currently manages two parallel communication streams — texting some customers in English and others in Spanish manually — with no templates, no automation, and no consistency.

## What Already Exists
Broadly and Podium provide text-based customer communication with automated status updates, review requests, and appointment reminders. Kimoby offers automotive-specific messaging integration. All operate primarily in English with basic translation options that produce stilted, non-automotive Spanish.

## The Customization Gap
The platform needs automotive-specific Spanish message templates (appointment confirmations, repair status updates, estimate approvals, pickup notifications) written by native Spanish speakers with automotive knowledge. It also needs auto-detection of customer language preference (based on past interactions or phone number registration), so the service advisor doesn't have to manually select language for each message. Review request links should route to Spanish-language review flows.

## Target Customer
Shop owners using text-based customer communication platforms who serve a 25%+ Spanish-speaking customer base and currently handle Spanish communications manually.

## Impact If Solved
Eliminates 30-60 minutes per day of manual bilingual text management, increases Spanish-speaking customer engagement rates by 40-50%, and improves Google review volume from Hispanic customers by 25-35%.
