## Why These Niches

Painting contracting fragments along setting (interior vs. exterior vs. commercial vs. industrial), project complexity (residential repaint vs. protective coatings), business scale (solo painter vs. 20-person crew), and customer type (homeowners vs. property managers vs. facility managers). These 8 niches cover the two largest revenue segments (residential interior and exterior), the two most digitally neglected (commercial painting with after-hours scheduling complexity and specialty/industrial with coating specification requirements), the two most underserved by existing tools (solo painters who find PaintScout too complex and property management turn painters who need batch scheduling), and the two highest-ROI automation targets (surface area estimation from photos and AI color consultation). Excluded: automotive painting (distinct industry), marine/aerospace coatings (specialized manufacturing), and artistic/decorative painting (craft business, not contracting).
