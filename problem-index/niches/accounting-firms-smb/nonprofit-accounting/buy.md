# Nonprofit Audit Workflow with Grant Compliance Tracking

**Niche:** [[niches/accounting-firms-smb/nonprofit-accounting/profile|Nonprofit & Fund Accounting Specialists]]
**Industry:** [[industries/accounting-firms-smb|SMB Accounting Firms]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Audit workflow tools exist for commercial engagements but do not handle the nonprofit-specific requirements of single audits, grant compliance testing, and FASB ASC 958 disclosures.
**Tags:** #automation #compliance #data-integration #workflow-orchestration

## The Problem
Nonprofit audits under Uniform Guidance (for organizations spending $750K+ in federal awards) require specific compliance testing procedures that commercial audit workflows do not address: testing of each major federal program, identification of questioned costs, evaluation of internal controls over federal awards, and completion of the Data Collection Form for the Federal Audit Clearinghouse. CPA firms performing single audits use commercial audit software (Caseware, CCH ProSystem, Workiva) and manually add nonprofit-specific sections, creating inconsistent workpaper structures across engagements and increasing the risk of missing required procedures.

## What Already Exists
Caseware, CCH ProSystem Engagement, and Workiva provide general audit workflow management with templates, workpaper organization, and tick-mark documentation. These tools include basic nonprofit templates, but they are skeletal outlines rather than comprehensive procedure checklists tailored to the Uniform Guidance compliance requirements for each type of federal program.

## The Customization Gap
The tool needs embedded knowledge of Compliance Supplement requirements by CFDA number (now ALN), auto-generated testing procedures for each major program identified in the Schedule of Expenditures of Federal Awards, pre-built disclosure templates for FASB ASC 958 (net assets with and without donor restrictions, liquidity disclosures), and a Data Collection Form assistant that pre-populates from audit findings. The gap is between "generic audit workflow" and "nonprofit single audit workflow that knows what tests are required for a Head Start grant vs. a CDBG grant."

## Target Customer
Audit managers and partners at CPA firms performing 10-50 nonprofit audits annually, particularly those with single audit requirements under Uniform Guidance.

## Impact If Solved
Reduces single audit setup and procedure documentation time by 30-40% per engagement, saving 8-15 hours per audit. Decreases the risk of peer review findings from incomplete compliance testing procedures.
