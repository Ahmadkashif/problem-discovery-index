# Nonprofit & Fund Accounting Specialists

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/accounting-firms-smb|SMB Accounting Firms]]

## Profile
**Market Size:** $5-8B
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~5%
**Digital Adoption:** Low-Medium — most use QBO plus manual workarounds for fund accounting; purpose-built tools like Aplos and Araize exist but are narrowly adopted
**Target Buyer:** Partner or senior manager at a CPA firm specializing in nonprofit audits and 990 preparation
**Automation Potential:** Medium — fund accounting rules are complex but deterministic, and 990 preparation follows a predictable structure

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Nonprofit accounting is governed by FASB ASC 958, which requires tracking revenues and expenses by fund (unrestricted, temporarily restricted, permanently restricted), a concept that does not exist in standard for-profit accounting software. CPA firms serving nonprofits must maintain fund-level tracking, prepare Form 990 (which has a completely different structure than any business tax return), perform single audits for organizations receiving federal grants (under Uniform Guidance), and navigate state charity registration requirements that vary by state. The accounting staff needs expertise in both nonprofit GAAP and the specific reporting requirements of funders, government agencies, and state attorneys general. This specialization makes the niche underserved by generic accounting tools.

## Current Tools & Gaps
QBO Nonprofit edition offers basic fund-tracking via class/location tags, but it is a workaround, not a true fund accounting system. Aplos, Araize, and Sage Intacct (for larger nonprofits) provide proper fund accounting. On the CPA firm side, 990 preparation uses the same tax software (UltraTax, Lacerte) but the 990-specific modules are less mature than the 1040/1120 modules. The major gap is the bridge between the nonprofit's books and the CPA's audit/990 workflow: reconciling fund-level trial balances from heterogeneous client accounting systems into a standardized audit or 990 workpaper set is almost entirely manual.
