## Why These Niches

SMB accounting firms fragment along service mix (tax-heavy vs. bookkeeping-advisory), practice size (sole practitioner vs. multi-staff), client population (general vs. nonprofit vs. agricultural vs. Spanish-speaking), and operational function (payroll processing vs. document intake). These 8 niches cover the two largest revenue concentrations (seasonal tax prep and recurring bookkeeping/advisory), the two most digitally neglected segments (solo CPAs still running desktop software and nonprofit specialists using generic tools), two underserved client populations that existing software ignores (Spanish-language practices and rural ag accountants), and the two highest-ROI automation targets within firm operations (payroll processing and document intake). Excluded: large regional firms with 50+ employees (different buying behavior), forensic accounting (specialized niche), and government audit firms (distinct regulatory framework).
