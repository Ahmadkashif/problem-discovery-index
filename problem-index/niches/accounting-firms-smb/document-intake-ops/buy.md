# Multi-Channel Document Collection Aggregator

**Niche:** [[niches/accounting-firms-smb/document-intake-ops/profile|Client Document Intake & Processing Operations]]
**Industry:** [[industries/accounting-firms-smb|SMB Accounting Firms]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Client portals exist for secure document upload, but clients still send documents via email, text, and physical mail — no tool aggregates all channels into a single engagement-linked inbox.
**Tags:** #automation #data-integration #workflow-orchestration #quick-win

## The Problem
Despite having a client portal, CPA firms receive documents through at least 4 channels: the portal itself (20-30% of clients use it), email attachments (40-50%), text message photos (10-15%), and physical mail (10-20%). Staff must monitor all channels, download or scan documents from each, identify which client sent them, and file them in the correct engagement folder. During peak season, a firm processing 400 returns might receive 50-100 documents per day across these channels, and the administrative staff spends 2-3 hours daily just routing documents to the right place.

## What Already Exists
SafeSend, Liscio, Canopy Client Portal, and TaxDome provide upload portals with engagement-level folder structures. Email management tools (Outlook, Gmail) handle email. Scanning software (ScanSnap, Adobe Scan) digitizes physical mail. Each channel has a functional tool, but they are siloed.

## The Customization Gap
The missing piece is an aggregation layer that treats every inbound document the same regardless of channel: an email attachment from john.smith@gmail.com is auto-matched to the John Smith engagement and filed alongside his portal uploads and scanned mail. The tool needs email parsing (extract attachments, match sender to client), text/SMS integration (receive document photos via a dedicated firm number), physical mail scan integration (batch scan with auto-splitting), and a unified inbox where the staff sees all documents by client/engagement with their source channel tagged. Client matching must handle the common case where a spouse sends documents from a different email than the one on file.

## Target Customer
Administrative staff and office managers at 5-20 person CPA firms who currently spend 15-20 hours per week during tax season on manual document routing across multiple channels.

## Impact If Solved
Consolidates document routing into a single workflow, reducing daily document management time from 2-3 hours to 30-45 minutes and eliminating the risk of documents being lost in email inboxes or unfiled on someone's desktop.
