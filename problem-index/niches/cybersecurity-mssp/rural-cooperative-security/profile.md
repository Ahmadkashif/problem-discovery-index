# Rural Cooperative & Utility Security

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/cybersecurity-mssp|Cybersecurity MSSP]]
**Category:** Underserved Audience

## Profile
**Market Size:** $800M
**Share of Parent Industry:** 2.7%
**Digital Adoption:** Low — Rural electric cooperatives, water utilities, and telecom co-ops rely on legacy SCADA systems with minimal cybersecurity; most have no dedicated IT staff, let alone security monitoring.
**Target Buyer:** Regional MSSP owners in non-metro markets, and cooperative general managers who have received cybersecurity mandates from regulatory bodies (NERC CIP for electric, state public utility commissions for water).
**Automation Potential:** Medium-High — Cooperative networks are small and standardized, enabling template-based monitoring, but the OT component adds complexity.

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
There are 900+ rural electric cooperatives, 50,000+ community water systems, and hundreds of rural telecom cooperatives in the US. These organizations provide critical infrastructure to rural communities but operate with 5-50 employees and IT budgets under $100K/year. They run SCADA systems controlling power distribution, water treatment, and communications infrastructure — the same types of systems that nation-state actors have targeted at larger utilities (Oldsmar water treatment, Ukraine power grid). Regulatory mandates are increasing: NERC CIP for electric cooperatives, EPA/AWIA for water systems, and state-level cybersecurity requirements. These cooperatives cannot comply alone and cannot afford enterprise security services.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Cooperative service organizations like NISC (National Information Solutions Cooperative) provide IT services to electric cooperatives but focus on billing and operational technology, not cybersecurity. Some regional MSSPs serve individual cooperatives, but the market is fragmented — each cooperative independently seeks security services without shared procurement or standardization. No MSSP has built a cooperative-specific security offering that addresses the combination of IT security, OT/SCADA monitoring, and regulatory compliance at cooperative-affordable pricing.
