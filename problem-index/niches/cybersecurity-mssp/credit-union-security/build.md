# Credit Union Security Bundle for Sub-$5B Institutions

**Niche:** [[niches/cybersecurity-mssp/credit-union-security/profile|Credit Union & Community Bank Security]]
**Industry:** [[industries/cybersecurity-mssp|Cybersecurity MSSP]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No MSSP offers a pre-packaged, credit-union-priced security service that bundles SIEM monitoring, vulnerability management, NCUA compliance, and incident response for institutions with $50M-$5B in assets and no dedicated security staff.
**Tags:** #ai-platform #automation #compliance #revenue-impact

## The Problem
A $300M credit union with 15 employees and no security staff needs: 24/7 security monitoring, quarterly vulnerability scans, annual penetration testing, FFIEC CAT compliance documentation, incident response planning, phishing awareness training, and NCUA examination preparation. Enterprise MSSPs quote $50K-$100K/month for comprehensive services. The credit union's entire IT budget is $200K/year. They end up with piecemeal solutions: a basic firewall from their core processor, an annual penetration test from a local consultant, and no continuous monitoring. This leaves 5,000+ credit unions with inadequate security coverage, vulnerable to the ransomware and business email compromise attacks that have increasingly targeted small financial institutions.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
MSSPs optimize for enterprise clients where a single contract generates $500K+/year in revenue. Serving credit unions requires a different economic model: standardized service delivery (same monitoring stack across all clients), volume-based pricing (serving 100 credit unions at $3K/month each = $3.6M/year), and regulatory specialization (NCUA examiners ask different questions than bank regulators). Building this requires upfront investment in credit union-specific monitoring templates, compliance documentation, and core banking system integrations that cannot be repurposed for other verticals.

## What to Build
A standardized MSSP service package for credit unions that includes: (1) cloud SIEM monitoring with pre-built detection rules for credit union threat patterns (ACH fraud, wire fraud, online banking attacks, card skimming alerts), (2) automated vulnerability scanning with credit union network topology templates, (3) FFIEC CAT compliance documentation auto-generated from monitoring data, (4) NCUA examination preparation package (pre-populated responses to common examiner questions), (5) incident response retainer with credit union-specific playbooks, and (6) monthly security posture reports in board-ready format. Priced at $2K-$8K/month based on asset size.

## Target Customer
Credit unions with $50M-$5B in assets, no dedicated security staff, currently spending less than $50K/year on security, and facing increasing NCUA examiner scrutiny.

## Impact If Built
Provides enterprise-grade security monitoring to 5,000+ underserved credit unions at 5-10% of enterprise MSSP pricing. Reduces NCUA examination findings by 60-70% through continuous compliance evidence. At $5K/month average and 200 clients, generates $12M/year in recurring MSSP revenue.
