# Cornerstone Ambulatory Add-On for Large Animal Practices

**Niche:** [[niches/veterinary-practices/large-animal-equine/profile|Large Animal & Equine]]
**Industry:** [[industries/veterinary-practices|Veterinary Practices]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Large animal vets already own Cornerstone or Avimark but use only 20-30% of the features because the software assumes a fixed-clinic workflow — they need an ambulatory add-on that layers farm call scheduling, mobile documentation, and multi-animal invoicing onto their existing PMS.
**Tags:** #data-integration #workflow-orchestration #automation #worker-facing #quick-win

## The Problem
Many large animal and equine practices purchased Cornerstone or Avimark because it was the industry standard or came bundled with IDEXX diagnostics. But these PMS platforms are designed for companion animal clinics where patients arrive at scheduled appointment times in exam rooms. Large animal vets have attempted to adapt: they enter "Farm Call — Smith Ranch" as the appointment type, manually type mileage into notes, create workaround invoice templates for multi-animal visits, and maintain a separate spreadsheet for truck inventory. The result is a PMS that technically contains all the practice's data but in a format that makes it nearly impossible to generate accurate reports, track drug inventory, or bill consistently for travel.

## What Already Exists
Cornerstone's core scheduling, medical records, and invoicing modules work for any veterinary species. IDEXX offers a mobile companion app for Cornerstone, but it provides read-only access to records — you cannot enter exam findings, document procedures, or generate invoices from the field. Some practices supplement with generic tools: Google Maps for mileage, Square for field payments, and a paper logbook for truck pharmacy tracking.

## The Customization Gap
The gap is an ambulatory workflow layer that extends the existing PMS without replacing it. This add-on needs to: (1) provide a mobile-optimized interface for entering exam findings and procedures in the field, syncing to Cornerstone when connectivity is available, (2) integrate GPS-based route planning and automatic mileage calculation that populates the invoice travel fee line item, (3) support multi-animal visit structures — one farm call generating 3-12 individual patient records with shared travel charges and optional herd discount pricing, (4) manage truck pharmacy inventory as a mobile sub-location of the practice's main inventory, auto-deducting dispensed items and generating restock lists when the vet returns to the clinic. Sold as a monthly add-on ($79-129/month) to existing Cornerstone or Avimark licenses.

## Target Customer
Large animal and equine practices already running Cornerstone or Avimark who have adapted the software with workarounds but lose 30-60 minutes daily to manual mileage tracking, duplicate data entry, and multi-animal invoice assembly.

## Impact If Solved
Eliminates 30-60 minutes of daily administrative workarounds for ambulatory vets, ensures accurate travel fee billing (recovering $10K-20K/year in underbilled mileage), and provides real truck pharmacy inventory visibility that reduces drug waste and compliance risk.
