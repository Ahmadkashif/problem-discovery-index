# Cornerstone-Native Client Retention & Recall Suite

**Niche:** [[niches/veterinary-practices/companion-animal-general/profile|Companion Animal General Practice]]
**Industry:** [[industries/veterinary-practices|Veterinary Practices]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** IDEXX Cornerstone handles medical records and billing but its recall and client communication features are primitive — practices need a Cornerstone-native plugin that manages wellness recall sequences, vaccination reminders, and post-visit follow-ups without requiring a separate SaaS subscription.
**Tags:** #data-integration #workflow-orchestration #automation #quick-win

## The Problem
IDEXX Cornerstone is the dominant PMS in companion animal practice with roughly 30% market share, but its built-in client communication tools are limited to basic postcard-style recall reminders. Practice owners who want automated text reminders, two-way SMS communication, targeted wellness campaigns, and post-surgical follow-up sequences must purchase separate tools — PetDesk, Vet2Pet, or Demandforce — that run outside Cornerstone, require separate logins, and don't share patient context. A practice running Cornerstone plus PetDesk plus a separate email marketing tool is paying three vendors, managing three dashboards, and manually reconciling which clients received what communication.

## What Already Exists
PetDesk and Vet2Pet provide appointment reminders, two-way texting, and loyalty programs for veterinary practices. They integrate with Cornerstone at a surface level (pulling appointment data) but don't have deep access to medical records, treatment plans, or recall protocols. Cornerstone's own "Communications" module sends basic reminders but cannot segment by treatment history, species, breed, or vaccination status.

## The Customization Gap
The gap is depth of PMS integration, not new communication functionality. A Cornerstone-native recall suite needs to: (1) auto-generate recall sequences based on species-specific wellness protocols (puppy series vs. adult annual vs. senior biannual), (2) segment outreach by vaccination status, breed-specific screening schedules (heartworm test timing varies by region), and dental grade from last exam, (3) trigger post-visit follow-up messages based on diagnosis codes and prescribed medications, and (4) track which outreach channel (text, email, phone) each client responds to and auto-route future communication accordingly. This requires read access to Cornerstone's medical record, reminder, and billing tables — not just the appointment calendar. Sold as a single subscription ($99-149/month) that replaces PetDesk + email marketing + manual recall list pulling.

## Target Customer
Independent companion animal practices running Cornerstone who are either paying for 2-3 separate communication tools that don't talk to each other, or relying on Cornerstone's built-in reminders and losing 30-40% of recall patients to attrition.

## Impact If Solved
Eliminates the need for separate patient communication subscriptions (saving $200-400/month in tool consolidation), reduces front-desk staff time spent on manual recall list management by 4-6 hours per week, and increases recall compliance from the industry average of 55-60% to 75-80% by matching outreach channel and timing to individual client behavior.
