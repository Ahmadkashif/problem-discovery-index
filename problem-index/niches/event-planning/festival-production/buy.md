# Festival Crew Management Platform

**Niche:** [[niches/event-planning/festival-production/profile|Festival & Multi-Day Event Production]]
**Industry:** [[industries/event-planning|Event Planning]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Workforce management tools handle restaurant and retail shifts — they don't manage the multi-department, multi-day crew scheduling of a festival with 200+ crew across production, security, medical, and operations.
**Tags:** #automation #workflow-orchestration #worker-facing

## The Problem
A mid-size festival employs 200-500 crew members across 8-12 departments (stage production, sound/lighting, security, medical, vendor operations, parking/traffic, artist relations, volunteer coordination) for a 5-7 day period (including buildout and teardown). Crew scheduling must account for: department-specific shift structures (production crews work 12-hour shifts, security works 8-hour shifts), skills and certifications (rigging, forklift operation, EMT), meal breaks, camping assignments, and credential/access levels. This is managed on printed spreadsheets and radio.

## What Already Exists
Workforce management platforms (Deputy, When I Work, Homebase) handle shift scheduling for single-location businesses. Event staffing platforms (Liveforce, StaffConnect) handle event-day staffing for single-day events. Neither handles multi-day festival crew management with department-based hierarchies, multi-shift rotation, on-site camping logistics, and credential management for different access zones.

## The Customization Gap
The platform needs festival-specific features: multi-day shift calendars by department, credential management (all-access, stage-only, backstage, general), on-site check-in/check-out with GPS verification, department-head dashboards showing crew status, and integrated communication by department or role. It should handle the transition from buildout crew (smaller, skilled) to event crew (larger, mixed skills) to teardown crew automatically.

## Target Customer
Festival production managers coordinating 100+ crew members across multiple departments who currently manage schedules, credentials, and communications through printed documents and radio.

## Impact If Solved
Reduces crew management overhead by 50-60%, eliminates credential confusion (unauthorized access to restricted areas), and ensures proper shift coverage across all departments throughout multi-day operations.
