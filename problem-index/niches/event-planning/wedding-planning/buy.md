# Vendor Communication Hub for Wedding Planners

**Niche:** [[niches/event-planning/wedding-planning/profile|Wedding Planning]]
**Industry:** [[industries/event-planning|Event Planning]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Generic messaging platforms handle conversations but don't organize vendor communication by wedding, track confirmation status, or maintain a searchable record of agreed details.
**Tags:** #nlp #data-integration #automation #workflow-orchestration

## The Problem
A wedding planner managing 25 active weddings communicates with 15-25 vendors per wedding. That's 375-625 vendor relationships across every possible channel: email, text, Instagram DM, phone calls, and WhatsApp. When the florist texts "pink peonies confirmed for Sarah's wedding" and the caterer emails "can you confirm final count for the Johnson reception?" — the planner must mentally map each message to the right wedding, the right vendor, and the right decision. Details agreed via text don't make it into the planning document. Conversations from 4 months ago are unsearchable.

## What Already Exists
CRM platforms (Honeybook, Dubsado) manage client relationships and contracts. Messaging tools (Slack, WhatsApp, email clients) handle conversations. Planning platforms (Aisle Planner) manage timelines and documents. None aggregate all vendor conversations per wedding into a single searchable thread, extract confirmed details into a structured record, or track which vendors have confirmed their specifics and which haven't.

## The Customization Gap
The hub needs to aggregate vendor communication across channels (email, SMS, WhatsApp) and organize it by wedding. NLP should extract confirmed details from conversations ("pink peonies" → floral details confirmed) and flag outstanding items ("caterer hasn't confirmed dietary accommodations for Table 7"). A confirmation tracker shows the planner which vendors have finalized all details and which have outstanding items — across all active weddings simultaneously.

## Target Customer
Wedding planners managing 15+ simultaneous weddings who spend 2-3 hours daily searching through message histories and manually updating planning documents with details confirmed via scattered conversations.

## Impact If Solved
Saves 10-15 hours per week in message searching and manual detail tracking, prevents detail miscommunications (wrong flowers, wrong menu, wrong timeline) that cost $500-$5,000 to fix, and reduces the cognitive load of managing hundreds of vendor relationships.
