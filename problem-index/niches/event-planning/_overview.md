## Why These Niches

Corporate events and weddings are the two dominant revenue segments — together they represent over 60% of industry revenue with fundamentally different client dynamics (corporate procurement vs. emotional consumer purchase). Nonprofit and festival production are digitally neglected despite complex logistics because their budgets don't justify enterprise event platforms. Hybrid events and cultural community events are underserved audiences — hybrid demands production capabilities that most planners lack, while cultural events need multilingual, community-accessible tools that don't exist. Vendor coordination and budget reconciliation represent the highest automation ROI — event planners spend 40-60% of their time on vendor communication and financial tracking that follows predictable patterns.
