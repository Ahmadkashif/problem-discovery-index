# Event-Specific Vendor Portal

**Niche:** [[niches/event-planning/vendor-coordination/profile|Vendor Coordination Automation]]
**Industry:** [[industries/event-planning|Event Planning]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Project management tools let planners assign tasks to vendors, but vendors won't adopt yet another platform — they need a zero-login, event-specific page with their details and confirmation buttons.
**Tags:** #automation #workflow-orchestration #data-integration

## The Problem
Event planners have tried using project management tools (Asana, Monday.com, Aisle Planner) to coordinate vendors, but vendors won't create accounts, download apps, or learn new platforms. The florist who works with 50 different planners isn't going to maintain accounts on 50 platforms. So the planner maintains the project management tool for their own tracking while communicating with vendors via email and text — creating a dual-maintenance burden where every update must be entered in two places.

## What Already Exists
Project management platforms (Asana, Monday.com) allow external collaborators but require account creation. Wedding planning platforms (Aisle Planner) have vendor-facing features but are wedding-specific. Client portals in CRMs (Honeybook, Dubsado) face the same adoption problem — vendors won't log into the planner's proprietary system.

## The Customization Gap
The vendor portal needs zero friction: a unique URL per vendor per event (no login, no account, no app). The page shows only what that vendor needs: their specific event details, timeline, load-in instructions, and outstanding items. Confirmation is one-click ("I confirm these details"). Changes are highlighted since last visit. The vendor can submit questions or updates. The planner sees all vendor portal activity in a single dashboard — who's viewed their page, who's confirmed, who hasn't visited in 2 weeks.

## Target Customer
Event planners who've tried and failed to get vendors onto project management platforms and are back to email-based coordination with no visibility into vendor preparedness.

## Impact If Solved
Increases vendor confirmation rates from 60-70% (email-based) to 90-95% (portal-based), reduces "I didn't get that email" issues, and gives the planner real-time visibility into vendor preparedness without manual follow-up calls.
