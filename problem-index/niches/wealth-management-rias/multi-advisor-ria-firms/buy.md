# Firm-Wide Model Portfolio Governance Platform

**Niche:** [[niches/wealth-management-rias/multi-advisor-ria-firms/profile|Multi-Advisor Ensemble RIA Firms]]
**Industry:** [[industries/wealth-management-rias|Wealth Management RIAs]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Rebalancing tools like Tamarac and iRebal handle individual portfolio drift but don't enforce firm-wide investment policy consistency across multiple advisors with different styles.
**Tags:** #data-integration #compliance #automation #workflow-orchestration

## The Problem
Multi-advisor firms adopt model portfolios to standardize investment management, but individual advisors frequently deviate — overweighting a sector they favor, holding legacy client positions that don't fit the model, or applying different tax-loss harvesting thresholds. The CIO or investment committee cannot see these deviations in real time, creating compliance risk and inconsistent client outcomes across the firm.

## What Already Exists
Tamarac, iRebal, and Orion's rebalancing tools manage drift tolerance and trade execution at the account level. Riskalyze (now Nitrogen) scores portfolio risk alignment. These tools work well for individual account rebalancing but do not provide a firm-level view of "which advisors are consistently deviating from our approved models and by how much."

## The Customization Gap
The missing layer is a governance dashboard that aggregates model adherence metrics across all advisors, flags systematic deviations (not one-off exceptions), and provides the CIO with drill-down capability to see why an advisor's book looks different from the firm's approved models. This requires mapping each account to its assigned model, computing deviation metrics beyond simple drift, and surfacing patterns — "Advisor X consistently underweights international by 3% across all accounts."

## Target Customer
CIOs and managing partners at RIA firms with 5+ advisors and $500M+ AUM who need to maintain investment policy consistency without micromanaging each advisor's daily trades.

## Impact If Solved
Reduces investment policy violations from quarterly discovery (during audits) to real-time detection, improving SEC exam readiness and ensuring clients receive the investment approach they were promised during onboarding.
