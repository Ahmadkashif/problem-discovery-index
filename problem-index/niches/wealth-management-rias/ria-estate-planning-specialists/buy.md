# Estate Planning Collaboration Hub for Multi-Professional Teams

**Niche:** [[niches/wealth-management-rias/ria-estate-planning-specialists/profile|RIA Estate Planning Specialists]]
**Industry:** [[industries/wealth-management-rias|Wealth Management RIAs]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Project management tools like Asana and Monday.com exist but don't understand the estate planning workflow — trust funding timelines, attorney-advisor coordination, and document version tracking across professionals.
**Tags:** #workflow-orchestration #data-integration #compliance

## The Problem
Implementing an estate plan requires coordination among 3-5 professionals: the RIA advisor, an estate attorney, a CPA, an insurance specialist, and sometimes a trust company. Each professional uses their own systems and communication tools. The advisor is typically the quarterback but has no structured way to track action items across professionals, ensure trust documents are funded correctly, verify that beneficiary designations are updated across all accounts, and confirm that insurance policies are properly titled to the ILIT trustee.

## What Already Exists
Project management platforms (Asana, Monday.com, ClickUp) provide task tracking and team collaboration. Document management tools (NetDocuments, iManage) handle version control for legal documents. CRM systems track client communications. None are designed for the specific workflows of estate plan implementation.

## The Customization Gap
Estate planning implementation has a specific task taxonomy: trust formation, trust funding (re-titling assets from individual ownership to trust ownership across multiple custodians), beneficiary designation updates, insurance policy ownership transfers, power of attorney and healthcare directive execution, and ongoing trust administration. A vertical collaboration hub would provide: workflow templates for each estate planning strategy, cross-professional task assignment with role-based access, custodian-specific funding checklists, and a unified client timeline showing implementation progress visible to all professionals.

## Target Customer
Estate-planning-focused RIA advisors who coordinate with 3-5 external professionals per client engagement and currently track implementation via email threads and spreadsheets.

## Impact If Solved
Reduces estate plan implementation time from 3-6 months to 4-8 weeks, eliminates dropped tasks (unfunded trusts, outdated beneficiary designations) that can cost families millions in avoidable estate taxes, and provides the client with visibility into their plan's completion status.
