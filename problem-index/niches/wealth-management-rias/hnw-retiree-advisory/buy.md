# Retirement Income Planning Tool with Portfolio Integration

**Niche:** [[niches/wealth-management-rias/hnw-retiree-advisory/profile|HNW Retiree & Distribution-Phase Advisory]]
**Industry:** [[industries/wealth-management-rias|Wealth Management RIAs]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Income Lab and Timeline exist for distribution planning but don't integrate with portfolio management or CRM systems, forcing advisors to re-enter data and manually sync recommendations.
**Tags:** #data-integration #workflow-orchestration #automation #revenue-impact

## The Problem
Advisors run distribution analyses in standalone tools like Income Lab or Timeline, then manually transfer the recommended withdrawal amounts into their portfolio management system (Orion, Black Diamond) for execution. When market movements change the plan, the advisor must re-run the analysis, re-enter the updated figures, and re-communicate to the client — a cycle that happens quarterly for many retiree households. The result is stale plans and execution drift.

## What Already Exists
Income Lab provides Monte Carlo distribution analysis with guardrail-based spending rules. Timeline offers similar functionality with a different simulation methodology. Both produce PDF reports showing sustainable withdrawal rates and income projections. MoneyGuidePro and eMoney handle broader financial planning with retirement income modules built in.

## The Customization Gap
None of these tools have bidirectional integration with portfolio management platforms. When Income Lab says "withdraw $15,000 from the traditional IRA this quarter," the advisor must log into Orion, create a distribution request, send it to the custodian, and then update the planning tool with the new balance. The gap is a middleware layer that translates distribution plan outputs into portfolio management system actions, syncs actual withdrawals back to the plan, and triggers re-analysis when drift exceeds a threshold.

## Target Customer
Operations managers and lead advisors at RIAs managing 100+ retiree accounts who currently spend 3-5 hours per week manually syncing distribution plans with portfolio execution.

## Impact If Solved
Eliminates 3-5 hours per week of manual data transfer per advisor, reduces execution errors that result in incorrect withdrawal amounts, and ensures plans reflect actual (not projected) portfolio values — improving both operational efficiency and client outcomes.
