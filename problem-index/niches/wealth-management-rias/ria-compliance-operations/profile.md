# RIA Compliance & Regulatory Operations

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/wealth-management-rias|Wealth Management RIAs]]
**Category:** Highly Automatable

## Profile
**Market Size:** $2-3B services market
**Share of Parent Industry:** Embedded operational cost (~2-4% of RIA revenue goes to compliance)
**Digital Adoption:** Medium — SmartRIA, RIA in a Box, and NRS provide compliance management platforms, but most firms still assemble ADV amendments manually, conduct annual compliance reviews via spreadsheet checklists, and prepare for SEC exams with paper files
**Target Buyer:** Chief Compliance Officer or compliance consultant at a mid-size RIA (100-500 employees)
**Automation Potential:** High — compliance tasks follow regulatory rules with defined filing deadlines, required documentation, and standardized forms

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
RIA compliance is a rule-driven operational function with clear inputs (regulatory requirements, firm activities, client transactions) and clear outputs (filings, disclosures, documentation). Yet compliance staff spend the majority of their time on manual data gathering rather than substantive compliance analysis. An ADV amendment requires pulling employee data, AUM figures, fee schedules, and business description changes from multiple systems. An annual compliance review requires checking 150+ regulatory requirements against the firm's actual practices. SEC exam preparation requires assembling 3-5 years of documentation across email archives, trading systems, CRM, and filing systems. Each of these workflows is highly structured and ripe for automation.

## Current Tools & Gaps
SmartRIA and RIA in a Box provide compliance task calendars, policy templates, and ADV filing assistance. NRS offers outsourced compliance consulting. ComplianceStar and MyRIACompliance handle specific filing workflows. The gap is intelligent automation — pulling data from firm systems to pre-populate filings, automatically detecting compliance exceptions in daily activities, and generating exam-ready documentation without manual assembly.
