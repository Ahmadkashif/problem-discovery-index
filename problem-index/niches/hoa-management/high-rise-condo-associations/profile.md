# High-Rise Condo Associations

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/hoa-management|HOA Management]]
**Category:** High Market Share

## Profile
**Market Size:** $2-3B
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~20-25%
**Digital Adoption:** Medium — management platforms for finances, but building systems management (HVAC, elevator, fire suppression, plumbing risers) and reserve study compliance are often tracked separately or manually
**Target Buyer:** Condo association manager or building engineer at high-rise buildings with 50-500+ units
**Automation Potential:** Medium — building systems maintenance scheduling, reserve component tracking, and unit owner communication are automatable; physical building management resists automation

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
High-rise condo associations manage a single building with shared mechanical systems — elevators, HVAC plant, fire suppression, plumbing risers, roof membrane, facade, parking garage — that serve all units collectively. The management challenge is fundamentally different from subdivision HOAs: building systems failures affect all residents simultaneously (elevator breakdown, pipe burst on a riser affecting 20 floors), capital reserve requirements are massive ($2-5M+ for major building components with 15-30 year replacement cycles), and post-Surfside (2021 Champlain Towers collapse) structural inspection requirements have created new compliance obligations. Building engineering knowledge — understanding which maintenance items are critical vs. cosmetic — is tacit expertise that differentiates effective condo management.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Vantaca and AppFolio HOA handle financial management and work orders. Building systems maintenance is tracked in spreadsheets or facility management tools (eMaint, Hippo CMMS) designed for commercial buildings, not condos. Post-Surfside structural inspection compliance tracking is manual. No tool integrates building systems maintenance scheduling, reserve component lifecycle tracking, and structural inspection compliance in a single condo-specific platform.
