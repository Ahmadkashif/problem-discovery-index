# Age-Restricted Senior Communities

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/hoa-management|HOA Management]]
**Category:** Low Digitized

## Profile
**Market Size:** $1-1.5B
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~10-12%
**Digital Adoption:** Low — residents are predominantly 55+ with limited technology comfort, management communications default to paper mail and in-person meetings, online portals have low adoption rates
**Target Buyer:** Community manager at an age-restricted (55+) HOA or active adult community
**Automation Potential:** Low-Medium — paper-to-digital communication transition, activities programming, and accessibility compliance are partially automatable; the human relationship aspects of senior community management resist automation

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Age-restricted communities (55+ under the Housing for Older Persons Act) face unique management challenges: residents have limited technology adoption (30-40% don't use email, 60%+ prefer paper correspondence), ADA and Fair Housing Act age-restriction compliance requires ongoing verification (at least 80% of units must be occupied by residents 55+), activities and social programming is a core community expectation (not optional amenities like in a subdivision), and health-related emergencies are more frequent (requiring wellness check procedures, emergency contact management, and coordination with local EMS). The manager serves not just as a property administrator but as a community social coordinator for an aging population — a role that no HOA management platform is designed for.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Standard HOA management platforms assume digital-first communication (email, portals). Activities management tools (Eventbrite, SignUpGenius) aren't integrated with community management. Age-restriction compliance tracking (occupant age verification) is manual. Wellness check procedures are informal. No platform addresses the complete senior community management workflow: paper + digital communication, activities programming, age-restriction compliance, and resident wellness coordination.
