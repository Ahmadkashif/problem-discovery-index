# Turnkey HOA-in-a-Box for Volunteer Boards

**Niche:** [[niches/hoa-management/self-managed-small-hoas/profile|Self-Managed Small HOAs]]
**Industry:** [[industries/hoa-management|HOA Management]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No platform provides a complete, zero-training-required management system for volunteer HOA boards — assessment collection, budget tracking, meeting management, CC&R document storage, and violation tracking — at a price point affordable for a 20-unit community ($20-$50/month total, not per unit).
**Tags:** #automation #workflow-orchestration #quick-win

## The Problem
A volunteer board president at a 30-unit subdivision manages: $54,000/year in assessment collections (check processing, tracking who has paid, following up on late payments), vendor payments for landscaping and snow removal ($15,000-$25,000/year), an annual budget ($3,000-$5,000 in reserve contributions, operating expenses), quarterly board meetings (scheduling, agendas, minutes, Open Meeting Act compliance), CC&R enforcement (violation notices, hearing procedures), and resident communications. They do this with: a personal bank account (or an HOA account at a local bank with paper statements), Excel or handwritten ledger, email, and a filing cabinet. No existing tool serves this market at the right price point ($20-$50/month for the entire community) with zero onboarding complexity.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
Professional HOA management platforms charge per-unit pricing that is affordable for 200-unit communities but not for 20-unit ones. Building a $20-$50/month product for self-managed HOAs requires a fundamentally different unit economics model — extremely low cost-to-serve with no implementation support. The market (150,000-200,000 communities) is large but the revenue per customer ($240-$600/year) makes venture-funded approaches uneconomic. This is a high-volume, low-ARPU SaaS play that requires product-led growth — the volunteer board president must be able to set up the entire system in 30 minutes without talking to a salesperson.

## What to Build
A turnkey HOA platform that a volunteer board president sets up in 30 minutes: enter community name, unit count, assessment amount, and upload CC&Rs → the system generates: an online assessment payment page, a financial dashboard (who's paid, who's late, current balance), a meeting management module (agenda builder, minutes template, notice generator), a document library (CC&Rs, meeting minutes, vendor contracts), and a violation tracking log. No per-unit pricing — flat monthly fee at $20-$50 for communities under 100 units.

## Target Customer
Volunteer board presidents at self-managed HOAs with 10-100 units — approximately 150,000-200,000 communities nationally. Average contract value: $30-$50/month.

## Impact If Built
Assessment collection shifts from check processing (3-5 hours/month) to online payments (30 minutes/month). Board transition becomes seamless because all governance data is in the platform — the outgoing president transfers admin access instead of handing over a box of files. Board president burnout decreases because the 5-15 hours of monthly administrative work drops to 2-5 hours.
