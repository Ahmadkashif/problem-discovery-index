# Multi-State Remote Filer Specialists

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/tax-prep-firms|Tax Prep Firms]]
**Category:** High Market Share

## Profile
**Market Size:** ~$2.5B in preparation fees for multi-state individual and small business returns
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~18%
**Digital Adoption:** Medium — tax software handles multi-state calculations, but nexus determination, income allocation, and credit-for-taxes-paid logic across states are poorly automated and require significant manual research
**Target Buyer:** Senior preparer or firm principal handling high-complexity individual and small business returns
**Automation Potential:** Medium-High — nexus rules, income allocation methods, and reciprocity agreements follow deterministic state-specific logic but vary across 50 states

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Remote work has created a multi-state filing crisis: a worker living in New Jersey, employed by a New York company, who worked from a Florida vacation home for two months creates nexus in three states with three different income allocation methods, reciprocity agreements (or lack thereof), and credits for taxes paid to other states. Pre-2020, multi-state returns were primarily for traveling executives, multi-state business owners, and military families. Post-2020, they include anyone who worked remotely from a different state — and the rules are in flux as states update their nexus and withholding requirements.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Tax software (Drake, Lacerte, UltraTax) generates multi-state returns once the allocation is entered. But the preparer must manually determine: (1) which states have nexus based on the client's physical presence, employer location, and income sources; (2) how to allocate income across states (days worked in each state, source-based allocation, domicile vs. statutory residence rules); (3) whether reciprocity agreements exist between the states involved; (4) how credits for taxes paid to other states apply. This research and allocation calculation adds 30-60 minutes per multi-state return.
