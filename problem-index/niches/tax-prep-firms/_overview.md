## Why These Niches

Schedule C and multi-state returns represent the two fastest-growing complexity categories in individual tax preparation, driven by gig economy growth and remote work respectively. Spanish-language firms and seasonal storefronts are digitally underserved — both serve populations with lower digital literacy and rely on in-person interactions that mainstream tax tech does not optimize for. K-1 partnership specialists and military/expat preparers serve populations with genuinely different filing requirements that mainstream software handles poorly. Document intake and amendment processing are the two highest-volume repetitive workflows with the clearest automation ROI. Excluded: large CPA firms (enterprise buyers), corporate tax departments (different workflow), and H&R Block/Jackson Hewitt franchises (captive technology).
