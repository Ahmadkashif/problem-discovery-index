# Affordable SIS Tier for Sub-200 Student Schools

**Niche:** [[niches/k12-private-schools/single-campus-rural/profile|Single-Campus Rural Private Schools]]
**Industry:** [[industries/k12-private-schools|K-12 Private Schools]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** FACTS, Gradelink, and other SIS platforms exist but price their entry tier at $3K-$10K/year with feature sets designed for 300+ student schools — overkill for a 100-student operation.
**Tags:** #automation #data-integration #quick-win #worker-facing

## The Problem
When a rural school administrator evaluates SIS options, they find that even "affordable" options include features they do not need (multi-campus management, department-level reporting, advanced analytics) at price points that consume 3-5% of the school's total operating budget. The implementation process assumes dedicated IT staff and a multi-week onboarding timeline. The result: many small schools either stay on Google Sheets or adopt an SIS they cannot afford and do not fully use.

## What Already Exists
FACTS SIS (Blackbaud) offers the broadest market reach. Gradelink provides a more affordable option. Alma and SchoolMint serve the K-12 SIS market. Each has a minimum pricing tier that assumes a school of meaningful size.

## The Customization Gap
Sub-200-student schools need a dramatically simplified SIS: no multi-campus features, no complex scheduling engine (most have 5-8 teachers), no advanced analytics (they need basic enrollment counts and financial summaries), and no dedicated IT administrator for configuration. The setup must be completable by a non-technical person in under 4 hours. The platform must handle the combined principal/administrator role (one person doing admissions, billing, and academics) with a workflow designed for a single user, not departmental separation.

## Target Customer
School administrators at 50-200 student private schools who have evaluated FACTS or Gradelink and found them either too expensive or too complex for their needs.

## Impact If Solved
Brings SIS functionality to 3,000+ schools currently operating on spreadsheets, improving data accuracy, reducing administrator workload by 5-10 hours per week, and providing parents with a professional portal experience that supports enrollment retention.
