# IEP Tool Adaptation for Private LD School Context

**Niche:** [[niches/k12-private-schools/learning-difference-schools/profile|Learning Difference & Special Needs Schools]]
**Industry:** [[industries/k12-private-schools|K-12 Private Schools]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Public school IEP management tools (GoalBook, Branching Minds, SpedTrack) handle goal tracking and progress monitoring but assume IDEA compliance workflows that do not apply to private LD schools.
**Tags:** #data-integration #workflow-orchestration #automation #worker-facing

## The Problem
Private LD schools need goal tracking and progress monitoring similar to what IEP tools provide for public schools. But when they evaluate these tools, they find workflows designed for public school special education: CSE meeting scheduling, IDEA compliance documentation, transition planning forms, related service mandates, and state reporting requirements. These features are irrelevant to private LD schools and clutter the interface. More importantly, the goal structures assume public school grade-level standards as the baseline, while LD schools measure progress on individual trajectories.

## What Already Exists
GoalBook provides goal-setting aligned to standards. Branching Minds offers a learning support platform with intervention tracking. SpedTrack and EasyIEP manage the full IEP lifecycle for public schools. These tools are designed for public school special education departments, not private LD schools.

## The Customization Gap
A private LD school adaptation needs: (1) goal templates aligned to LD-specific curricula (Orton-Gillingham, Wilson, Lindamood-Bell) rather than Common Core; (2) progress tracking on individual growth trajectories without normative grade-level anchoring; (3) accommodation management without IDEA compliance overhead; (4) therapeutic service tracking integrated with academic goals (not separate "related services" in IDEA terminology); (5) parent-facing reports that explain progress in the school's pedagogical framework, not in IEP legal language.

## Target Customer
Learning specialist directors at private LD schools who have evaluated public school IEP tools and found them too IDEA-centric but still need structured goal tracking and progress monitoring.

## Impact If Solved
Provides the 60-70% of IEP tool functionality that LD schools actually need without the 30-40% of IDEA-specific overhead they do not. Reduces goal tracking setup time by 50% through LD-specific goal templates. Improves inter-staff coordination on shared students by providing a common platform for goal tracking across academic and therapeutic domains.
