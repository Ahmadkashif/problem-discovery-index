# Smart Tuition Mid-Year Adjustment Automation

**Niche:** [[niches/k12-private-schools/tuition-billing-aid-automation/profile|Tuition Billing & Financial Aid Optimization]]
**Industry:** [[industries/k12-private-schools|K-12 Private Schools]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Smart Tuition and FACTS billing handle standard payment plans, but mid-year changes (withdrawal refunds, additional aid, payment plan modifications, ancillary charge additions) require manual recalculation and system updates.
**Tags:** #automation #workflow-orchestration #data-integration #quick-win

## The Problem
A family withdraws mid-semester. The business office must calculate the prorated refund (based on the school's refund policy, which varies by withdrawal date), adjust the payment plan (cancel remaining installments, calculate any overpayment or balance due), process the financial aid adjustment (unused aid is returned to the pool), and update the billing system. This scenario — along with mid-year aid increases, payment plan restructuring for families experiencing hardship, and adding/removing ancillary charges — occurs 50-100 times per year at a typical school and takes 30-60 minutes per adjustment.

## What Already Exists
Smart Tuition and FACTS billing manage recurring tuition payments, payment plans, and basic invoicing. They handle standard billing scenarios well but require manual intervention for non-standard adjustments. QuickBooks and Xero handle accounting but are not integrated with the school's billing platform.

## The Customization Gap
School tuition adjustments involve policy-specific calculations that generic billing tools do not handle: prorated refunds based on a custom refund schedule (90% before September 15, 80% before October 15, etc.), cascading sibling discount recalculations when one child withdraws, financial aid clawback and reallocation logic, and ancillary charge prorating tied to the school calendar (not the billing calendar). Each school's refund policy and aid policy is different, requiring configurable business rules.

## Target Customer
Business office directors at schools processing 50+ mid-year billing adjustments per year who spend 25-50 hours annually on manual recalculations and system updates.

## Impact If Solved
Reduces per-adjustment processing time from 30-60 minutes to under 10 minutes. Eliminates calculation errors that trigger parent disputes (estimated 5-10% error rate on manual adjustments). Frees 20-40 hours per year of business office time for financial planning and family communication.
