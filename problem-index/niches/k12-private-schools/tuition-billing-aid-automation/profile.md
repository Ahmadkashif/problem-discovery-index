**Automation Potential:** High — tuition billing, financial aid awards, payment plan management, and collection follow-up are rule-based workflows with high volume and predictable logic

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Private school tuition billing is more complex than a simple invoice: each family has a unique rate calculated from base tuition minus financial aid (often a percentage negotiated during admissions), sibling discounts (cascading percentages for 2nd, 3rd, 4th children), payment plan selection (annual, semester, monthly, 10-month), mid-year adjustments (student withdrawal, family financial hardship, additional aid appeal), and ancillary charges (after-school programs, hot lunch, field trips, sports fees). The business office manages these calculations for every family, and any error triggers parent calls, trust erosion, and reconciliation overhead.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Smart Tuition and FACTS billing handle payment processing and plan management. But financial aid award modeling (how much aid to offer each family to maximize enrollment yield within a budget) is done in spreadsheets. Mid-year adjustments require manual recalculation and billing system updates. Collection on delinquent tuition is handled through manual email follow-up — there is no automated escalation workflow designed for the sensitive context of parent relationships in a school community.
