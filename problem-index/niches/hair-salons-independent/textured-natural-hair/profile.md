# Textured & Natural Hair Salons

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/hair-salons-independent|Independent Hair Salons]]
**Category:** Low Digitized

## Profile
**Market Size:** $4.2B
**Share of Parent Industry:** 7%
**Digital Adoption:** Low — Many operate through social media (Instagram DMs for booking), cash/Zelle payments, and no formal practice management.
**Target Buyer:** Salon owner specializing in locs, braids, twists, protective styles, and natural hair care
**Automation Potential:** Medium — Service time variability and pricing complexity make scheduling and pricing particularly automatable.

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Textured and natural hair services (braiding, locs, twists, silk press, protective styles) differ fundamentally from straight-hair salon services in three ways: (1) Service time varies enormously — a full head of box braids takes 4-8 hours depending on hair density, length, and braid size, versus 1-2 hours for a typical cut-and-color. (2) Pricing is inherently custom — the same service on two clients can take twice as long and require twice the product. (3) The product ecosystem is entirely different — textured hair products (edge controls, curl definers, loc gels) are not carried by mainstream salon distributors. Salon management tools designed around 1-hour appointment blocks, flat-rate pricing, and mainstream product catalogs fundamentally don't work.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Many textured hair stylists use Instagram for portfolio display and DMs for booking, Square or Cash App for payments, and no formal scheduling or client management system. Those who try salon platforms (Vagaro, Square Appointments) find the standard 30/60-minute appointment blocks unusable for 4-8 hour services, and flat-rate pricing models don't accommodate the per-client variability that defines textured hair work.
