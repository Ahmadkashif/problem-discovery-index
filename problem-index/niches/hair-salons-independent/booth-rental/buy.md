# Property Management Tool Adapted for Salon Booth Leasing

**Niche:** [[niches/hair-salons-independent/booth-rental/profile|Booth-Rental Salon Operators]]
**Industry:** [[industries/hair-salons-independent|Independent Hair Salons]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Property management platforms handle lease tracking and rent collection but are designed for apartments and offices — not for 8-station salon spaces with shared supply rooms, variable-rate leases, and weekly/monthly rent schedules.
**Tags:** #automation #data-integration #workflow-orchestration #quick-win

## The Problem
Booth-rental salon leases have unique characteristics that property management software doesn't handle well: weekly or bi-weekly rent cycles (not monthly), variable rates based on station location (window station costs more), bundled vs. unbundled services (some rents include supplies, others don't), and informal lease terms that change frequently. A salon owner who tries Buildium or Avail finds the setup designed for 12-month apartment leases with monthly payments — forcing workarounds for every salon-specific requirement.

## What Already Exists
Buildium, AppFolio, and Avail handle residential property management with lease tracking, rent collection, and maintenance requests. Stessa and Baselane offer simpler landlord tools. All model "units" as apartments or offices with standard monthly leases. None support: variable rent schedules (weekly, bi-weekly), station-level unit management within a single address, bundled service charges (supply room access, laundry, backbar products), or the informal lease modification style common in salon booth rental.

## The Customization Gap
The property management platform needs salon-specific adaptations: (1) units modeled as stations within a single commercial space, not separate addresses, (2) flexible rent schedules (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly) per renter, (3) add-on charges for shared services (supply room, laundry, product access), (4) informal lease modification support (rent changes with 30-day notice, not formal lease amendments), and (5) independent contractor compliance features (1099 tracking, ensuring lease structure maintains contractor status rather than creating employment risk).

## Target Customer
Booth-rental salon owners currently using Venmo and spreadsheets who want structured rent collection but find apartment-focused property management tools too rigid.

## Impact If Solved
Automates rent collection (saving 3-5 hours/month of chasing payments), provides a clear financial record for tax purposes, and ensures lease structures comply with independent contractor classification requirements.
