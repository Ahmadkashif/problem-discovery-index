## Why These Niches

Color services drive the majority of salon revenue and require the most specialized product inventory, formula management, and continuing education. Booth-rental operations represent the dominant independent salon business model but create unique management challenges around occupancy, common-area maintenance, and hybrid contractor/employee relationships. Textured hair salons and mobile stylists are the most digitally neglected segments — textured hair requires entirely different product lines, techniques, and service timing, while mobile stylists lack any purpose-built practice management tools. Aging clientele and male grooming represent underserved demographic segments with distinct service requirements. The two automation niches target the most error-prone manual workflows: retail product management and the complex payroll calculations inherent in commission/booth-rent hybrid salon models.
