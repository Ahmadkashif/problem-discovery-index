# Biomechanical Performance Tracking Dashboard

**Niche:** [[niches/chiropractic-practices/sports-performance/profile|Athlete & Sports Performance]]
**Industry:** [[industries/chiropractic-practices|Chiropractic Practices]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No platform tracks an athlete's biomechanical performance metrics over time, correlates them with chiropractic treatment interventions, and shows the measurable impact of spinal adjustments on sport-specific performance outcomes.
**Tags:** #linear-regression #regression #tabular-ml #data-integration

## The Problem
A sports chiropractor adjusts a golfer's thoracic spine and tells them "that should help your rotation." But neither the DC nor the athlete has objective data showing how thoracic rotation changed pre/post adjustment, how that change correlates with club head speed, or whether the improvement persists between visits. The DC's clinical value proposition — "I make you perform better" — is subjective and undocumented. Athletes who invest $200-$500/month in sports chiropractic want metrics showing ROI, and DCs who can provide them command premium pricing.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
Building this requires combining chiropractic clinical data (spinal findings, treatment rendered, segmental mobility assessments) with sport-specific performance data (range of motion, power output, movement quality scores) and showing the correlation over time. No existing platform bridges the chiropractic EHR and sports performance analytics — they're built by different companies for different audiences.

## What to Build
A performance tracking dashboard that ingests chiropractic assessment data (ROM, segmental mobility, functional movement scores) and sport-specific metrics (from wearables, force plates, coach assessments, or manual entry) and visualizes the athlete's performance trajectory. Shows the relationship between treatment interventions and performance metrics — "after your L4-L5 adjustment on March 3, your hip rotation improved 12 degrees and your squat depth improved 3 inches." Generates shareable progress reports athletes can show coaches, trainers, and teams.

## Target Customer
CCSP/DACBSP-certified sports chiropractors treating 20+ competitive athletes, charging premium cash-pay rates ($100-$250 per visit).

## Impact If Built
Enables data-driven clinical decision-making for sports DCs. Justifies premium pricing through documented performance outcomes. Creates shareable data that drives athlete referrals — the athlete shows their coach the performance improvement report, and the coach refers the entire team.
