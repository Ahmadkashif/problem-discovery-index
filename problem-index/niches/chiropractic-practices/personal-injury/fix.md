# Attorney Communication Black Hole

**Niche:** [[niches/chiropractic-practices/personal-injury/profile|Personal Injury / Auto Accident]]
**Industry:** [[industries/chiropractic-practices|Chiropractic Practices]]
**Type:** Fix (Pain Point)
**One-liner:** PI chiropractors send records and reports to attorneys and hear nothing for months — no case status updates, no settlement notifications, and no payment timeline — because there is no shared communication channel between DC offices and law firms.
**Tags:** #workflow-orchestration #data-integration #revenue-impact

## The Problem
After a PI patient reaches MMI and the DC submits their final report to the attorney, the case enters the attorney's negotiation/litigation pipeline. The DC has zero visibility into what happens next — is the demand package sent? Did the carrier make an offer? Is the case in mediation? Has it settled? The DC calls the attorney's office periodically, gets voicemail or a paralegal who says "it's in progress," and waits. When the case finally settles, the DC may receive a reduced payment with no explanation, or discover the case settled months ago and the attorney's office forgot to send the check. DC offices spend 5-10 hours per week calling attorney offices for status updates.

## Why It's Still Broken
Attorneys use case management systems (Clio, MyCase, Filevine) that track case status internally but provide no external visibility to treating providers. The attorney-provider relationship is transactional — the attorney refers the patient, the provider treats and sends records, the attorney settles and sends payment. There's no shared platform for status communication because the volume of providers per attorney (each case may have 5-10 treating providers) makes individual updates impractical.

## What a Fix Looks Like
A provider-attorney communication portal that gives the DC real-time visibility into case status milestones: demand sent, carrier response received, mediation scheduled, settlement reached, check issued. The attorney's case management system pushes milestone updates to the portal automatically — no manual communication required. The DC sees their entire portfolio of cases by attorney with current status, and receives automated alerts when milestones are reached. Reduces phone-tag to near-zero.

## Who Feels the Pain
DC office managers who spend 5-10 hours per week calling attorney offices for case status updates. DCs who discover that settled cases went unpaid because the attorney's office lost track of their lien.

## Impact If Fixed
Eliminates 5-10 hours per week of attorney follow-up calls (equivalent to $15K-$30K in annual staff time). Accelerates lien collection by 30-60 days through proactive milestone tracking. Prevents lien payment losses from cases where the attorney "forgot" to include the DC's lien in the settlement disbursement.
