# Medical-Legal Narrative Generator from Treatment Notes

**Niche:** [[niches/chiropractic-practices/personal-injury/profile|Personal Injury / Auto Accident]]
**Industry:** [[industries/chiropractic-practices|Chiropractic Practices]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No product transforms a chiropractor's SOAP notes and examination findings into a formatted medical-legal narrative report that attorneys can use in demand packages and litigation — currently a 4-8 hour manual writing task per case.
**Tags:** #llm #text-generation #nlp #revenue-impact

## The Problem
When a PI case approaches settlement, the attorney requests a final narrative report from the chiropractor documenting the injury mechanism, objective findings, diagnosis, treatment rendered, treatment outcomes, permanency assessment, and causation opinion. This report must be written in medical-legal language (not clinical shorthand), cite specific examination findings with dates, and withstand defense scrutiny. A chiropractor treating 50+ PI cases writes 50+ final reports per year, each taking 4-8 hours. Many DCs procrastinate on reports because the writing is tedious, delaying attorney settlements and their own lien payments.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
Medical-legal narrative generation requires understanding both the clinical documentation (SOAP notes with chiropractic-specific terminology — subluxation listings, motion palpation findings, orthopedic test results) and the legal requirements (causation language, permanency opinions, AMA Impairment Guide ratings). The output must be factually consistent with the treatment record — any discrepancy between the narrative and the notes will be exploited by defense attorneys. This is not generic medical report writing; it's chiropractic-specific, PI-specific, and jurisdiction-specific.

## What to Build
An LLM-powered report generator that ingests the patient's complete treatment record (initial exam, re-exams, SOAP notes, X-ray findings, orthopedic test results), and produces a formatted medical-legal narrative report with injury mechanism, objective findings summary with date citations, diagnosis with ICD-10 codes, treatment summary, outcomes, and causation opinion. The DC reviews, edits, and signs the report rather than writing from scratch. Templates for different jurisdictions and attorney preferences.

## Target Customer
PI chiropractors treating 30-100+ auto accident cases per year, each requiring a final narrative report for settlement.

## Impact If Built
Reduces report writing time from 6 hours to 1-1.5 hours per case. Accelerates settlement timelines by 30-60 days (reports submitted faster means attorneys send demand packages sooner). For a DC with 60 active PI cases, saves 270-300 hours per year in report writing and accelerates lien payment collection by $150K-$300K annually.
