# Chiropractic-Specific Membership Management Platform

**Niche:** [[niches/chiropractic-practices/cash-pay-wellness/profile|Cash-Pay Wellness & Maintenance]]
**Industry:** [[industries/chiropractic-practices|Chiropractic Practices]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Membership management platforms exist for gyms and wellness businesses, but none handle chiropractic-specific membership structures — visit-based plans with rollover, family bundles, add-on services (X-rays, therapy), and insurance coordination for patients with partial coverage.
**Tags:** #automation #data-integration #revenue-impact

## The Problem
Cash-pay practices sell memberships that include a set number of adjustments per month (e.g., 4 visits for $149), with options for family members, add-on services, and rollover of unused visits. Managing these memberships in generic billing software requires manual tracking of visit credits, family member linkages, rollover calculations, and hybrid billing for patients who have insurance for some services (X-rays, exams) but pay cash for adjustments. The front desk spends 30-45 minutes per day managing membership logistics.

## What Already Exists
Membership platforms (Hint Health, Practice Better, Stripe Billing) handle subscription billing. Gym management platforms (Mindbody, Zen Planner) handle visit-based memberships. Practice management systems (ChiroTouch, Jane App) handle scheduling and billing. None combines subscription billing with visit credit tracking, family membership linkages, add-on service management, and chiropractic-specific features like insurance coordination for covered services within a cash-pay membership model.

## The Customization Gap
The gap is a membership engine purpose-built for chiropractic: manages multiple plan tiers (individual, couple, family), tracks visit credits with rollover rules, applies add-on pricing for services beyond the plan (additional visits, decompression therapy, supplements), handles insurance coordination when applicable, and provides the DC with membership analytics (LTV by plan type, upgrade/downgrade patterns, optimal plan pricing). Must integrate with the practice management system for scheduling and with payment processors for recurring billing.

## Target Customer
Cash-pay chiropractic practices with 100+ membership patients, currently managing membership logistics through spreadsheets or generic billing tools.

## Impact If Solved
Eliminates 30-45 minutes per day of manual membership management (equivalent to $12K-$18K annually in staff time). Reduces billing errors that cause patient disputes and churn. Provides analytics that optimize plan pricing and structure, increasing average revenue per member by 10-15%.
