# Borrower Portal Adapted for Spanish-First ITIN Workflows

**Niche:** [[niches/mortgage-brokers/hispanic-immigrant-borrowers/profile|Hispanic & Immigrant Borrower Specialists]]
**Industry:** [[industries/mortgage-brokers|Mortgage Brokers]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Borrower-facing portals like Blend and SimpleNexus offer Spanish translations but don't support ITIN-based workflows, alternative document types, or the trust-building communication patterns that immigrant borrowers need.
**Tags:** #llm #text-generation #nlp #automation #worker-facing

## The Problem
ITIN borrowers interact with the mortgage process differently than SSN borrowers — they need to upload documents that standard portals don't recognize (foreign tax returns, consulate-issued IDs, letters from landlords on non-standard letterhead, remittance receipts), they need explanations of US mortgage concepts in culturally contextualized Spanish (not just translated English), and they need more hand-holding through a process that is unfamiliar and often intimidating due to immigration-related fears about financial documentation.

## What Already Exists
Blend offers a consumer-facing mortgage application portal with Spanish language support, document upload, and LOS integration. SimpleNexus (now nCino Mortgage) provides a mobile-first borrower portal. Both support standard 1003 application workflows and common document types (W-2, pay stubs, bank statements). Spanish translations exist but are mechanical — they translate the words without adapting the experience.

## The Customization Gap
The portal needs to accept ITIN as the primary identifier (not SSN), present document checklists specific to ITIN program requirements (which vary by lender), support upload of non-standard documents (rent receipts, utility bills for alternative credit, foreign income documents), and provide contextual guidance in natural Spanish — explaining concepts like "alternative credit" and "non-traditional documentation" in terms that resonate with borrowers navigating the US system for the first time. It also needs to assure borrowers about data privacy in a way that addresses immigration-related concerns explicitly.

## Target Customer
Bilingual brokerage owners who currently collect ITIN borrower documents via text message, WhatsApp, and in-person visits because no digital portal supports their workflow — spending 3-5 hours per file on document collection that takes 30 minutes for a conventional borrower.

## Impact If Solved
Reduces ITIN borrower document collection time from 3-5 hours to 45-60 minutes. Improves borrower experience and reduces drop-off — currently estimated at 35-40% of ITIN applicants abandon during document collection due to confusion or fear. Enables the brokerage to serve more ITIN borrowers without proportional staffing increases.
