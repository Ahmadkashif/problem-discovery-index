# All-in-One Shop Management for Micro Fabricators

**Niche:** [[niches/metal-fabrication/small-custom-job-shops/profile|Small Custom Job Shops (<20 Employees)]]
**Industry:** [[industries/metal-fabrication|Metal Fabrication]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No shop management platform is designed for a 5-person fabrication shop where the owner needs to quote a job, schedule it, track materials, invoice the customer, and manage the books — all from a phone while standing in the shop — without the complexity of a full ERP system.
**Tags:** #automation #workflow-orchestration #quick-win #worker-facing

## The Problem
A small fab shop owner runs every business function simultaneously: answering quote requests, estimating jobs, ordering material, scheduling production on the whiteboard, fabricating alongside the crew, invoicing on completion, and managing accounts in QuickBooks. No tool integrates these functions at the simplicity and price point appropriate for a 5-person shop. ERP systems (JobBOSS, E2 Shop System) cost $200-$500/month and require hours of data entry that a working owner doesn't have. Generic project management tools (Trello, Asana) don't understand fabrication workflows. The result: the owner manages everything in their head, on paper scraps, and in text message threads — a system that works until the shop gets busy enough that things start falling through the cracks.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
The micro fab shop market is enormous in number (~50,000 shops in the US) but tiny in per-customer revenue — these shops will pay $30-$75/month for software, not $300-$500/month. Building for this price point requires extreme simplicity (no implementation cost, no training required) and high-volume adoption. Software companies that understand manufacturing build for larger operations; consumer software companies that build simple tools don't understand manufacturing workflows. The product sits at an intersection of domain expertise and UX simplicity that neither camp has addressed.

## What to Build
A mobile-first shop management app: quote a job in 3 minutes (select material type and thickness, enter linear weld feet and piece count, apply shop rate — app calculates price), schedule the job (drag to calendar slot), track materials (simple material inventory with auto-deduct on job completion), generate an invoice on completion (one tap from the completed job), and see a monthly P&L showing job profitability. No BOM configuration, no routing setup, no work center capacity planning — just the essentials for a small custom shop. Price: $49/month flat rate.

## Target Customer
Owner-operators of fabrication shops with 3-20 employees and $500K-$5M annual revenue. Approximately 50,000 shops in the US.

## Impact If Built
Gives 50,000 micro fabricators their first digital business management tool. Surfaces per-job profitability that owners currently can't see — identifying which job types and customers are profitable and which are not. Reduces administrative overhead (quoting, invoicing, material tracking) from 5-10 hours/week to 1-2 hours/week, freeing the owner for revenue-generating shop work.
