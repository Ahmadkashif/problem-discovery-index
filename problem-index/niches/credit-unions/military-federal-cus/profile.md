# Military & Federal Employee CUs

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/credit-unions|Credit Unions]]
**Category:** Underserved Audience

## Profile
**Market Size:** $200-300B in combined assets across ~150 military/federal employee CUs (including Navy Federal, PenFed, USAA FCU, and hundreds of smaller base/agency CUs)
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~10% of assets
**Digital Adoption:** Medium-High — Large military CUs (Navy Federal, PenFed) are technology leaders; but small base-level CUs ($50-500M) serving specific installations or agencies lag significantly; the member population is digitally native and expects fintech-quality experiences
**Target Buyer:** VP of Member Services or Digital Banking Manager
**Automation Potential:** High — PCS (Permanent Change of Station) financial planning, military-specific lending (VA loans, deployment protections), and transient member management follow predictable patterns

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Military and federal employee CUs serve a fundamentally transient member base — active duty military members relocate every 2-3 years through PCS orders, creating unique financial challenges (dual housing costs, moving expenses, deployment income changes) and operational challenges (branch relationships reset constantly). Military members have specific financial products (VA home loans, SCRA protections, deployment savings programs) and regulations (Military Lending Act caps on rates to service members) that standard CU platforms don't model. Smaller base-level CUs compete directly with Navy Federal and USAA for the same member population but with a fraction of the technology budget.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Large military CUs have custom-built or enterprise platforms. Small base-level CUs use standard CU core platforms (Symitar, KeyStone) that don't model military-specific lending products, PCS financial workflows, or deployment-related account management. VA loan origination requires specialized processing that standard CU LOS platforms handle poorly. No CU platform automatically detects PCS events from member transaction patterns (allotment changes, new-location ATM usage) to trigger proactive outreach.
