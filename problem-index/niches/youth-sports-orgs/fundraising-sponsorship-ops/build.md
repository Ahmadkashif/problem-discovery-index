# Youth Sports Sponsor Management Platform

**Niche:** [[niches/youth-sports-orgs/fundraising-sponsorship-ops/profile|Fundraising & Sponsorship Operations]]
**Industry:** [[industries/youth-sports-orgs|Youth Sports Organizations]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No platform manages youth sports sponsorships end-to-end — from prospect outreach and proposal generation to contract signing, deliverable fulfillment tracking, and renewal pipeline management — forcing volunteer treasurers to manage $20K-50K in sponsor relationships through email and handshakes.
**Tags:** #automation #workflow-orchestration #data-integration #revenue-impact #ai-platform

## The Problem
A travel team or booster club typically has 5-20 local business sponsors paying $250-5,000 each for banner placement at games, logo on jerseys, social media mentions, and newsletter features. The sponsorship lifecycle is entirely manual: a volunteer parent solicits sponsors using a homemade proposal PDF, negotiates terms verbally, collects payment via check, promises specific deliverables (your banner at every home game, monthly social media post), and then may or may not fulfill those deliverables. There's no tracking of which sponsors got which deliverables, no proof of fulfillment to share with the sponsor at renewal time, and no organized renewal pipeline. 30-40% of sponsors don't renew — partly because nobody asked, partly because they can't verify they received what they paid for.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
Nonprofit donor management platforms (Bloomerang, DonorPerfect) handle donations but not sponsorship deliverables. CRM platforms (HubSpot, Salesforce) handle pipeline management but are overkill for a volunteer running 15 sponsor accounts. Youth sports platforms (TeamSnap, SportsEngine) focus on registration and team management, not revenue operations. The sponsor management workflow is too small for enterprise tools and too complex for spreadsheets — a gap that nobody has targeted.

## What to Build
A sponsor management platform for youth sports that: (1) provides professional sponsorship proposal templates with tier options and pricing, (2) tracks sponsor contracts with deliverable checklists (banner displayed, logo on jersey, social media post dates), (3) collects deliverable fulfillment evidence (photos of banner at games, screenshots of social posts), (4) generates sponsor impact reports at end-of-season showing what they received with photo evidence, (5) manages a renewal pipeline with automated outreach 60/30 days before season start, and (6) tracks total sponsorship revenue and per-sponsor ROI.

## Target Customer
Team treasurers and booster club presidents managing $10K-50K in annual sponsorship revenue who want to professionalize sponsor relationships and increase renewal rates.

## Impact If Built
Increases sponsor renewal rates from 60-70% to 85-90% through documented fulfillment and proactive outreach, worth $5K-20K in preserved annual sponsor revenue per team — while reducing treasurer time on sponsor management from 5-8 hours/month to 1-2 hours/month.
