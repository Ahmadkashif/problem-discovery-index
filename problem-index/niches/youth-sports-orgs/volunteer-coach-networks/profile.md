# Volunteer Coach Networks

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/youth-sports-orgs|Youth Sports Organizations]]
**Category:** Underserved Audience

## Profile
**Market Size:** $1.5B
**Share of Parent Industry:** 8%
**Digital Adoption:** Low — Volunteer coaches receive a team roster via email, download a generic practice plan PDF (maybe), and are left to figure out everything else on their own — from practice drills to game strategy to parent communication.
**Target Buyer:** League Admin / Volunteer Coordinator
**Automation Potential:** Medium — Practice plan generation, background check coordination, training delivery, and communication templates are automatable; the mentorship and community-building aspects require human facilitation.

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Volunteer coaches are the backbone of youth recreational sports — 2.5+ million parents volunteer to coach in the US annually. Most have no coaching education, limited sport-specific knowledge, and zero tools beyond a paper roster and a whistle. Leagues recruit volunteer coaches with desperation ("anyone willing to coach?") and provide minimal support: a background check, a roster, and sometimes a one-day clinic. The volunteer coach is then expected to plan practices, teach skills, manage games, communicate with parents, handle conflicts, and keep kids safe — with no training, no resources, and no ongoing support. The result is wildly inconsistent coaching quality, coach burnout (average tenure: 1.5 seasons), and negative youth sports experiences driven by undertrained coaches.

## Current Tools & Gaps
TeamSnap and GameChanger provide team communication and scorekeeping. YouTube has unlimited coaching drill videos with no curation. NAYS (National Alliance for Youth Sports) and the Positive Coaching Alliance offer online training courses. But no tool helps the actual week-to-week work: "I have practice Thursday with 14 eight-year-olds and 90 minutes — what do I do?" The gap is between coaching education (generic knowledge) and coaching operations (specific, actionable plans for tonight's practice).
