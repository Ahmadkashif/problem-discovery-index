# Multi-Sport Complexes

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/youth-sports-orgs|Youth Sports Organizations]]
**Category:** Low Digitized

## Profile
**Market Size:** $3B
**Share of Parent Industry:** 15%
**Digital Adoption:** Medium — Booking systems exist for individual fields, but cross-facility scheduling, tournament hosting operations, utilization analytics, and maintenance coordination across 10-20 fields/courts are still managed on whiteboards and in one person's head.
**Target Buyer:** Facility Manager / Complex Owner
**Automation Potential:** High — Field allocation, tournament bracket scheduling to fields, maintenance rotation, and utilization-based pricing are all algorithmically solvable but currently done manually.

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Multi-sport complexes (SportsPlex facilities, tournament parks, indoor sports centers) manage 10-20+ fields, courts, or rinks under one roof or campus. They serve multiple sports simultaneously, host tournaments on weekends, rent to leagues on weeknights, and run their own programming on remaining time slots. The scheduling complexity is massive: a 16-field soccer complex hosting a weekend tournament with 80 teams needs every game assigned to a specific field at a specific time, with rest periods, age-group separation, warm-up windows, and contingency plans for weather delays. This scheduling is typically done by one person in Excel, taking 20-40 hours for a major tournament.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Facility scheduling platforms (eSoft Planner, Upper Hand, Dash) handle individual field/court booking. Tournament bracket software (TourneyMachine, Tourney Central) generates brackets. But no tool bridges the gap: assigning bracket games to physical fields/courts with time slots, accounting for field dimensions (not all fields are the same size), surface conditions (field rotation to prevent damage), age-group clustering (U8 games should be on adjacent fields for parent convenience), and real-time rescheduling when weather delays shift the entire tournament. This field-to-game assignment is the most time-consuming task in tournament hosting.
