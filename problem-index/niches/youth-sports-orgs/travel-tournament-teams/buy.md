# Per-Family Financial Ledger for Team Accounts

**Niche:** [[niches/youth-sports-orgs/travel-tournament-teams/profile|Travel Tournament Teams]]
**Industry:** [[industries/youth-sports-orgs|Youth Sports Organizations]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** QuickBooks tracks team income and expenses, but can't show each family their individual balance — factoring in team fees, tournament costs, fundraising credits, uniform charges, and payment plan installments — without hours of manual reconciliation.
**Tags:** #data-integration #automation #revenue-impact #quick-win

## The Problem
Travel teams handle complex per-family financial accounts: each family owes a base season fee ($2,000-5,000), plus per-tournament entry fees (which vary by tournament and may not apply to all families), uniform costs, private lesson fees, and miscellaneous charges. Against these charges, families earn fundraising credits, sponsor allocations, and may be on installment payment plans. The team treasurer (a volunteer parent) tracks all of this in a spreadsheet, manually reconciling Venmo/Zelle payments against charges, calculating each family's current balance, and chasing past-due amounts. A team of 15 families with 25 tournaments has 375+ individual financial transactions per season.

## What Already Exists
QuickBooks and Wave handle general accounting. TeamSnap includes basic payment collection. LeagueApps and SportsEngine process registration payments. But none maintain a per-family ledger that shows: "The Smith family owes $4,200 for the season, has paid $2,800, earned $350 in fundraising credits, and has a remaining balance of $1,050 due in 3 installments." This per-family view requires a ledger that tracks charges by type, credits by source, and payments by method — a vertical accounting problem that general tools don't solve.

## The Customization Gap
The needed adaptation is a team financial management layer: (1) per-family ledger accounts with charge categories (season fee, tournament fees, uniforms, gear), (2) credit tracking by source (fundraising, sponsorship, scholarship), (3) payment plan management with installment schedules and auto-reminders, (4) online payment collection with auto-reconciliation against the family ledger, (5) family-facing balance portal where parents can see their current balance and upcoming charges, and (6) treasurer dashboard showing total team financial position and per-family status.

## Target Customer
Team treasurers managing $30K-100K+ in annual team finances across 12-20 families who currently maintain manual spreadsheets and chase payments via text message.

## Impact If Solved
Reduces treasurer workload from 5-10 hours/month to 1-2 hours/month, eliminates the 10-15% of collections that go unrecovered due to lost records or uncomfortable follow-up, and prevents the financial disputes between families and the organization that are the #1 source of team drama.
