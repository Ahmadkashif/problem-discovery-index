## Why These Niches

Electrical contracting is not one market — it fragments along project type (service vs. new construction vs. maintenance), voltage class (120V residential vs. 480V industrial), setting (residential vs. commercial vs. industrial), and business function (field operations vs. estimating vs. compliance). These 8 niches cover the full span: the two largest revenue segments (residential service and commercial new construction), the two most digitally neglected (industrial maintenance and solar/EV integration), the two most underserved by existing tools (small/rural electricians and multi-family property management), and the two highest-ROI automation targets (panel inspection diagnostics and estimating/job costing). Excluded: utility-scale power distribution (a distinct industry), low-voltage/data cabling (specialized sub-segment), and fire alarm systems (governed by separate NFPA standards and licensing).
