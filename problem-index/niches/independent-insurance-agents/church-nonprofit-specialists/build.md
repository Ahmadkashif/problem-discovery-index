# Faith-Based Risk Exposure Assessment Engine

**Niche:** [[niches/independent-insurance-agents/church-nonprofit-specialists/profile|Church & Nonprofit Specialists]]
**Industry:** [[industries/independent-insurance-agents|Independent Insurance Agents]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No tool systematically identifies the unique risk exposures of a church or nonprofit based on its specific activities — youth programs, mission trips, food banks, daycare, counseling — and maps them to required coverage endorsements.
**Tags:** #bayesian-network #multiclass-classification #tabular-ml #compliance #revenue-impact

## The Problem
A church that runs a youth overnight camp, operates a food pantry, hosts AA meetings, and sends mission teams to Central America has dramatically different risk exposures than a church that holds Sunday services only. The agent must manually identify each activity, assess its liability implications, and ensure the policy includes appropriate endorsements (abuse/molestation for youth programs, hired/non-owned auto for mission trips, professional liability for counseling). Missing an exposure means the organization is uninsured for a major risk category, and the agent faces E&O liability.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
The faith-based insurance market is small and dominated by a handful of specialty carriers whose underwriting expertise is not digitized. Risk exposure assessment for churches requires knowledge of ecclesiastical governance structures, volunteer management practices, and activity-specific liability that does not exist in any standard commercial risk assessment framework. The market size has not justified standalone tool development.

## What to Build
An interactive risk assessment tool that takes the organization's profile (denomination, size, programs, facilities, staffing model, volunteer count, annual budget) and outputs a comprehensive exposure map with recommended coverage endorsements, sublimits, and carrier-specific product matches. The tool flags high-risk activities that require supplemental applications and identifies coverage gaps in existing policies.

## Target Customer
Producers building a faith-based book of business who need to compete with specialty brokers by demonstrating comprehensive risk understanding, even on unfamiliar denomination or program types.

## Impact If Built
Reduces risk assessment time from 2-3 hours to 30 minutes per account, catches 2-3 missed exposures per church that would otherwise leave the organization uninsured, and enables generalist agents to enter the faith-based niche with confidence.
