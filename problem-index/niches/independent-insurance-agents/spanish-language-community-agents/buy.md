# Bilingual Client Portal and Document Generation

**Niche:** [[niches/independent-insurance-agents/spanish-language-community-agents/profile|Spanish-Language Community Agents]]
**Industry:** [[industries/independent-insurance-agents|Independent Insurance Agents]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Client portals like Applied CSR24 exist for document sharing and self-service, but none offer a Spanish-language interface or generate Spanish-language coverage summaries and proposals.
**Tags:** #llm #text-generation #nlp #automation #worker-facing

## The Problem
Agents send clients coverage proposals, renewal summaries, certificate explanations, and billing notices through client portals — all in English. For Spanish-speaking clients, the agent must either manually translate these documents (time-consuming and inconsistent) or send English documents the client cannot fully understand. The result is increased phone calls for explanation, higher error rates on coverage selections, and a diminished client experience.

## What Already Exists
Applied CSR24, Vertafore Client Portal, and HawkSoft's client portal provide digital document sharing, payment processing, and basic self-service. Generic translation services (Google Translate, DeepL) can translate text but do not understand insurance terminology or maintain the formatting of insurance documents.

## The Customization Gap
Insurance documents contain domain-specific terminology (endorsement, declarations page, aggregate limit, per-occurrence) that generic translation tools handle poorly. A proper bilingual portal must translate not just words but concepts — rendering "named insured" as "asegurado nombrado" with a tooltip explaining what it means. Coverage proposals must be generated in the client's preferred language from structured policy data, not translated from a pre-formatted English PDF. The portal must also handle Spanish-language text input for claims reporting and service requests.

## Target Customer
Bilingual agency owners whose client base is 50%+ Spanish-speaking and who currently spend 5-10 hours per week on manual document translation and explanation calls.

## Impact If Solved
Eliminates 5-10 hours per week of manual translation labor, reduces explanation-related phone calls by 40-50%, and improves client retention in a market segment where trust and comprehension are the primary drivers of loyalty.
