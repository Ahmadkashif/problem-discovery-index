# Unified Senior Client Dashboard Across Medicare and P&C

**Niche:** [[niches/independent-insurance-agents/senior-medicare-specialists/profile|Senior & Medicare Supplement Specialists]]
**Industry:** [[industries/independent-insurance-agents|Independent Insurance Agents]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No product provides a single view of a senior client's Medicare plan, P&C policies, life insurance, and ancillary coverage — agents toggle between 3-4 disconnected systems to see the full picture.
**Tags:** #data-integration #workflow-orchestration #recommendation #revenue-impact

## The Problem
A Medicare-focused agent who also writes P&C must log into a Medicare CRM (SunFire, MedicarePro) to see the client's health plan, the AMS (Applied Epic, HawkSoft) to see their home and auto policies, and a separate life insurance portal to see final expense or long-term care coverage. When a 72-year-old client calls with a question, the agent cannot see their full insurance picture without switching between systems. Cross-sell opportunities (a Medicare client who has no umbrella policy, or a P&C client turning 65 who needs Medicare guidance) are invisible.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
Medicare and P&C are regulated by different bodies (CMS vs. state DOIs) with different data standards, compliance requirements, and enrollment processes. The tech vendors in each space have no incentive to integrate with the other — SunFire does not connect to Applied Epic, and Applied Epic does not track Medicare enrollment data. Building a unified layer requires expertise in both regulatory domains and API access to systems that were not designed to share data.

## What to Build
A client-centric dashboard that aggregates Medicare enrollment data (plan type, carrier, premium, drug coverage), P&C policy data (home, auto, umbrella), and life/ancillary coverage into a single view per client household. The dashboard flags cross-sell opportunities (turning-65 clients in the P&C book, Medicare clients without umbrella coverage), surfaces upcoming renewal dates across all product lines, and tracks compliance documentation for each product type.

## Target Customer
Agency principals running dual Medicare/P&C books with 500+ senior client households, currently managing the relationship across 3-4 disconnected systems.

## Impact If Built
Increases cross-sell revenue by 20-30% by surfacing opportunities that are currently invisible. Reduces per-client service time by 40% by eliminating system-switching during calls. Strengthens client retention by enabling holistic insurance reviews that demonstrate the agent's value across all coverage types.
