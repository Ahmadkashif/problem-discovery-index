# Autonomous Digital-First Small-Balance Collection Engine

**Niche:** [[niches/collections-agencies/small-balance-portfolio/profile|Small-Balance Portfolio Specialists]]
**Industry:** [[industries/collections-agencies|Collections Agencies]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No white-label product enables independent agencies to run fully automated small-balance collection campaigns via SMS, email, and self-service portals without agent involvement.
**Tags:** #reinforcement-learning #gradient-boosting #binary-classification #tabular-ml #ai-agent #automation

## The Problem
A portfolio of 50,000 accounts at $300 average balance contains $15M in face value. Traditional agent-driven collection at $15-$20 per productive contact hour would cost more than the expected recovery. These portfolios can only be profitable through automated digital outreach: sequenced SMS and email messages with escalating urgency, personalized settlement offers, and frictionless online payment — all without a human agent touching the account. But independent agencies lack the technology to build these automated campaigns.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
TrueAccord (now TransUnion) pioneered digital-first collections but operates as a first-party solution, not a white-label platform for independent agencies. Building an autonomous collection engine requires Reg F-compliant SMS/email sequencing, real-time settlement offer optimization, self-service dispute handling, and payment processing — each of which has regulatory nuances (opt-out handling, frequency limits, required disclosures) that are expensive to implement correctly. The compliance risk has deterred tech-first approaches in an industry built on phone-based collection.

## What to Build
A platform that automates the entire small-balance collection lifecycle: (1) debtor contact via Reg F-compliant SMS, email, and IVR sequences; (2) ML-optimized contact timing and channel selection per debtor; (3) dynamic settlement offers based on account age, balance, and debtor engagement signals; (4) self-service payment portal with installment plan options; (5) automated dispute intake and resolution workflow. The agency configures campaign rules and monitors results; no individual account requires agent attention unless escalated.

## Target Customer
Owner-operators and portfolio buyers purchasing small-balance portfolios at 1-3 cents on the dollar who currently cannot profitably work these accounts with traditional agent-driven methods.

## Impact If Built
Makes previously unworkable small-balance portfolios profitable by reducing per-account collection cost from $5-$10 (agent-driven) to $0.50-$1.00 (fully automated). Enables independent agencies to compete with TrueAccord and other digital-first collectors for portfolio purchases. Potential to double recovery rates on small-balance portfolios from 5-8% to 12-18%.
