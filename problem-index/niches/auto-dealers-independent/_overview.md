## Why These Niches

Independent auto dealing fragments along financing model (cash vs. BHPH vs. subprime), geography (metro vs. rural), customer demographic (prime vs. subprime vs. first-time vs. Spanish-speaking), and operational function (acquisition, reconditioning, titling). These 8 niches cover the two largest revenue segments (BHPH dealers and wholesale flippers who represent nearly half of independent dealer volume), the two most digitally neglected (rural dealers and independents attempting CPO programs without franchise infrastructure), two underserved buyer populations (Spanish-speaking markets and first-time/subprime buyers who need fundamentally different sales and financing workflows), and two high-ROI automation targets (reconditioning workflow and title/registration processing). Excluded: franchise dealers (structurally different), luxury/exotic used car dealers (small niche), and online-only dealers like Carvana (not independent).
