# Permit Compliance Tracking

## Profile
**Market Size:** $3-5B (embedded in consulting and client operational costs)
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~8%
**Digital Adoption:** Low-Medium — compliance calendars are spreadsheets, permit conditions are tracked in PDFs and memory, and deadline management is Outlook reminders
**Target Buyer:** Compliance manager at an industrial client with 10-50 environmental permits, or environmental consulting firm managing compliance for 20-100 client facilities
**Automation Potential:** High — permit compliance is entirely rule-based: specific actions (sampling, reporting, inspections) must occur at specific intervals, and the rules are written in the permit itself

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Environmental permit compliance is an ongoing obligation — unlike assessment and remediation (which are project-based), compliance monitoring runs indefinitely. An industrial facility may hold 5-20 environmental permits (NPDES wastewater discharge, Clean Air Act Title V, RCRA hazardous waste, stormwater, etc.), each containing 50-200 specific conditions (sampling frequencies, reporting deadlines, discharge limits, operational requirements). Environmental consultants manage compliance for clients as a recurring service, tracking thousands of permit conditions across dozens of facilities. A single missed condition — a late DMR (Discharge Monitoring Report), a skipped stack test, an unreported exceedance — can trigger enforcement action with penalties of $25,000-100,000 per day of violation.

## Current Tools & Gaps
RegAction, Intelex, and Enablon provide enterprise compliance management for large corporations with dedicated EHS departments. These platforms cost $50,000-200,000/year and require dedicated implementation. For mid-size consulting firms and their SMB clients, compliance tracking is spreadsheets: permit conditions extracted manually into an Excel calendar, with Outlook reminders for deadlines. No affordable platform ingests actual permit documents, extracts conditions, and generates a compliance calendar automatically.
