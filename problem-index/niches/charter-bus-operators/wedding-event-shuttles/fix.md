# Day-of Guest Communication and Shuttle Tracking

**Niche:** [[niches/charter-bus-operators/wedding-event-shuttles/profile|Wedding & Event Shuttles]]
**Industry:** [[industries/charter-bus-operators|Charter Bus Operators]]
**Type:** Fix (Pain Point)
**One-liner:** Wedding guests have no idea when or where their shuttle is, leading to missed pickups, confused crowds at hotel lobbies, and panicked calls to the wedding planner.
**Tags:** #automation #worker-facing #quick-win

## The Problem
On the day of a wedding, 100-300 guests need to get from hotels to venues and back, but they receive shuttle information only through a paper insert in the invitation or a line on the wedding website. When shuttles run even 5 minutes late, guests congregate in hotel lobbies calling the front desk, the wedding planner, and each other. The bus driver has no way to communicate ETA to waiting guests. The wedding planner fields 10-20 "where is the shuttle?" calls during an event that should require zero transportation-related attention. When a shuttle arrives and guests are scattered across the hotel bar, pool, and rooms, the driver waits 10-15 minutes for boarding, cascading delays across the entire schedule.

## Why It's Still Broken
Charter operators have no guest-facing communication channel — they interact with the event planner or coordinator, not with individual passengers. Building a guest notification system requires collecting guest phone numbers (which the operator does not have), integrating with the bus's GPS position, and presenting a consumer-grade UX for a one-time use case. Small operators lack the technical capability, and the per-event economics (one wedding = one day of use) do not justify custom development.

## What a Fix Looks Like
A lightweight SMS/web-based shuttle tracker that the wedding planner activates with a shared link distributed to guests. Guests see real-time bus position and ETA on a branded event page. Automated text notifications fire 10 minutes before each pickup ("Your shuttle to the reception departs from Hotel A lobby in 10 minutes"). The driver's tablet shows a boarding countdown. No app download required — just a URL.

## Who Feels the Pain
Wedding planners who field constant shuttle inquiries on event day, bus drivers who wait for scattered guests and fall behind schedule, and wedding couples who experience transportation as the one logistical element they cannot control.

## Impact If Fixed
Reduces guest wait confusion by 80%, eliminates 10-20 distraction calls to the wedding planner per event, and cuts boarding delays by 50% through proactive notifications — directly improving the operator's on-time performance and review scores.
