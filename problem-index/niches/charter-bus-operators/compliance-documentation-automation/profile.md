# DOT/FMCSA Compliance Documentation

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/charter-bus-operators|Charter Bus Operators]]
**Category:** Highly Automatable

## Profile
**Market Size:** $300-500M (compliance services and software)
**Share of Parent Industry:** Cross-cutting regulatory burden affecting all operators
**Digital Adoption:** Low — Most operators track drug testing, vehicle inspections, driver qualifications, and annual filings in paper folders and filing cabinets
**Target Buyer:** Safety/compliance officer / owner-operator / operations manager
**Automation Potential:** High — Compliance requirements are rule-based with fixed deadlines, defined document types, and binary pass/fail outcomes — ideal for automated tracking and alerts

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Charter bus operators face a dense regulatory environment: FMCSA requires driver qualification files (medical certificates, CDL verification, MVR checks, drug/alcohol testing records), vehicle inspection reports (daily pre-trip, annual DOT inspections), HOS compliance documentation, and biennial registration updates. A single missing drug test result or expired medical certificate during a DOT audit can result in an out-of-service order that grounds the entire fleet. Small operators (1-15 buses) typically have no dedicated compliance person — the owner or operations manager handles compliance alongside every other responsibility, and documents are scattered across email, filing cabinets, and multiple vendor portals (drug testing labs, CDL verification services, inspection shops).

## Current Tools & Gaps
J.J. Keller offers DOT compliance tools (DataSense, Encompass) used widely in trucking, but these are designed for freight carriers and do not address charter-specific requirements (passenger carrier pre-trip inspections, bus-specific annual inspection items, charter-specific operating authority documentation). ELD providers (Samsara, Motive) handle HOS tracking but not the broader compliance file management. No tool provides a charter-operator-specific compliance dashboard that tracks every required document, deadline, and filing across drivers, vehicles, and operating authority.
