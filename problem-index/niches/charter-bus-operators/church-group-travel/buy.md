# Group Payment Collection for Charter Trips

**Niche:** [[niches/charter-bus-operators/church-group-travel/profile|Church & Faith Group Travel]]
**Industry:** [[industries/charter-bus-operators|Charter Bus Operators]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Generic group payment tools exist but none integrate with charter booking workflows or handle the church-specific dynamics of partial payments, scholarship subsidies, and family bundling.
**Tags:** #automation #data-integration #quick-win

## The Problem
Church trip coordinators collect $30-$80 per person from 20-55 travelers, often through a mix of cash, checks, Venmo, and Zelle. Tracking who has paid, who owes a balance, and whether the total collected covers the charter cost is done on paper or in a personal spreadsheet. When the charter operator requires a 50% deposit 30 days before the trip, the coordinator is often short because 10-15 members have not yet paid. The coordinator personally floats the difference or the trip is at risk. Some churches offer scholarships for youth or low-income members, adding another tracking layer.

## What Already Exists
Group payment platforms (Splitwise, PayRequest, Cheddar Up) handle splitting costs among groups. Church giving platforms (Tithe.ly, Pushpay) process donations and event registrations. Eventbrite handles event ticketing with payment. None of these connect payment status to charter booking confirmation, trip roster management, or the scholarship/subsidy tracking that churches need.

## The Customization Gap
Church charter payments require: per-person pricing with family bundle discounts (a family of 4 pays $200 instead of $280), scholarship flags that reduce individual cost without revealing the subsidy to other members, integration with the charter deposit schedule (automated reminders when the group total approaches the deposit threshold), and the ability to issue refunds for no-shows against the operator's cancellation policy. The tool must also accommodate members who insist on paying by cash or check — tracking offline payments alongside digital ones.

## Target Customer
Church administrators and trip coordinators managing charter payments for groups of 20-55 travelers, and charter operators who want to ensure deposit deadlines are met without chasing the coordinator.

## Impact If Solved
Reduces payment collection from a 4-6 week ordeal to a 1-2 week automated process, ensures charter deposits are met on time (currently late 40% of the time), and eliminates the personal financial risk coordinators take when fronting deposits.
