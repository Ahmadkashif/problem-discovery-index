# Ultra-Lightweight Community Acupuncture Practice OS

**Niche:** [[niches/acupuncture-practices/solo-community-acupuncture/profile|Solo Community Acupuncture Practitioners]]
**Industry:** [[industries/acupuncture-practices|Acupuncture Practices]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No sub-$50/month all-in-one system exists for community acupuncture's unique model of sliding-scale pricing, group rooms, and minimal charting.
**Tags:** #automation #workflow-orchestration #quick-win #worker-facing

## The Problem
Solo community acupuncturists treat 30-50 patients per day at $15-40/session in group rooms. Their workflow — patient arrives, picks a recliner, gets needled, rests, pays on the way out — is fundamentally different from private-practice acupuncture. Every existing acupuncture EHR is built for the private-room, 1-hour-appointment, insurance-billing model. These tools cost $150-400/month and require per-patient charting workflows that take longer than the treatment itself in a community setting.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
The community acupuncture market is small (~3,000-5,000 practitioners in the US) and price-sensitive (median income $50,000-70,000). SaaS companies targeting healthcare practices expect $200+/month ARPU. The community model's low per-visit revenue makes it unattractive for vendors optimizing for insurance-billing clinics. Building for this market requires a fundamentally different business model — likely $20-40/month with high volume.

## What to Build
A mobile-first practice OS with: (1) group-room scheduling showing recliner availability by time slot, (2) sliding-scale payment processing with auto-applied rates based on patient self-selection, (3) one-tap clinical documentation (point selection, chief complaint, outcome rating), (4) patient visit history and basic outcome tracking, and (5) end-of-day financial reconciliation. Total interaction time per patient under 60 seconds for the practitioner.

## Target Customer
Solo community acupuncturists treating 150+ patients/week who currently manage everything on paper and free tools.

## Impact If Built
Saves 6-10 hours/week of administrative time per practitioner, enables outcome tracking that was previously impossible, and provides financial visibility that most community acupuncturists lack entirely.
