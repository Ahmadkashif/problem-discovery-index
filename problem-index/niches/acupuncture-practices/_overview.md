## Why These Niches

Insurance-billing clinics and integrative groups dominate revenue but face wildly inconsistent payer rules across states. Solo community acupuncture and TCM herbalists remain almost entirely paper-based, representing the largest digitization gap. Veterans acupuncture is growing rapidly via VA expansion but providers struggle with federal billing requirements, while rural practitioners face geographic isolation and limited referral networks. The two automation niches target the highest-volume manual workflows — claims processing and intake documentation — where error rates directly erode already-thin margins.
