# Client Reporting and ROI Attribution

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/marketing-agencies-smb|SMB Marketing Agencies]]
**Category:** Highly Automatable

## Profile
**Market Size:** $5-8B (embedded cost across all agencies)
**Share of Parent Industry:** Embedded function — represents 25-35% of account manager time across the industry
**Digital Adoption:** Medium — reporting platforms exist but still require significant manual assembly to produce client-ready deliverables with narrative context
**Target Buyer:** Account manager or agency owner spending 30-40% of time on monthly client reporting
**Automation Potential:** High — data aggregation, chart generation, and metric calculation are deterministic; narrative generation is now feasible with LLMs

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Client reporting is the single largest non-billable time sink for marketing agencies. Every client expects a monthly performance report, but the report is not just a data dump — it must tell a story: what happened, why, and what the agency recommends next. Account managers spend 2-4 hours per client per month assembling reports: pulling data from 3-5 platforms (Google Ads, Meta, GA4, email platform, CRM), creating visualizations, writing narrative commentary that explains performance in the client's business context, and formatting everything for presentation. For an agency with 30 clients, this is 60-120 hours per month — equivalent to 1-2 full-time employees dedicated entirely to reporting.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Agency Analytics, Whatagraph, and Google Looker Studio aggregate data from multiple sources and produce dashboards and PDF reports. These tools handle data aggregation and chart generation well but produce metric-heavy reports without narrative context. The client receives "CTR: 2.3%, up 0.4pp" but not "CTR improved because we refreshed ad creative on March 5, and the new headline is outperforming the previous version by 35%." The narrative — the part that demonstrates the agency's strategic value — is still written manually for every client every month.
