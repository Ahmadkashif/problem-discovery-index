# Integrated Print-Digital Campaign Management for Local Markets

**Niche:** [[niches/marketing-agencies-smb/rural-small-market-agencies/profile|Rural & Small-Market Agencies]]
**Industry:** [[industries/marketing-agencies-smb|SMB Marketing Agencies]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Digital campaign management tools ignore print, radio, and local sponsorship channels that still drive significant results in small markets — agencies need to manage the full media mix in one place.
**Tags:** #data-integration #workflow-orchestration #automation #quick-win

## The Problem
In small markets, a local business's marketing mix often includes newspaper ads, radio spots, community event sponsorships, direct mail, chamber of commerce listings, and high school sports program ads alongside Google Ads and Facebook. The agency managing this mix uses one set of tools for digital (Google Ads, Meta, analytics) and a completely separate process for traditional media: calling the newspaper for rate cards, sending artwork via email, tracking insertion orders in spreadsheets, and reconciling print invoices manually. The total campaign picture — "how much did we spend across all channels and what results did each produce?" — does not exist in any single view.

## What Already Exists
Google Ads and Meta manage digital campaigns. Marketing project management tools (Monday.com, Asana) track tasks. Media buying platforms (Strata, SQAD) serve large agency media departments. Some local media companies offer self-serve portals for ad placement. None of these connect print media buying to digital campaign management in a single workflow.

## The Customization Gap
The tool needs a unified campaign view that includes both digital and traditional channels: track newspaper ad placements, radio spot schedules, direct mail drops, and event sponsorships alongside Google Ads and Meta campaigns. For each channel, capture spend, estimated reach (newspaper circulation, radio listenership, event attendance), and any measurable response (coupon codes, tracking phone numbers, QR codes). Aggregate into a total campaign dashboard that shows spend by channel, estimated reach by channel, and cost-per-thousand-reached across the full media mix. This enables the agency to demonstrate the value of the full plan, not just the digital portion.

## Target Customer
Small-market agency owners managing the full media mix (print + digital) for 15-40 local business clients who currently track traditional media in spreadsheets and digital media in platform dashboards.

## Impact If Solved
Provides a unified view of campaign performance that justifies the agency's full retainer (not just the digital portion), reduces traditional media management time by 40-50%, and enables data-driven reallocation between print and digital channels based on comparative reach and response.
