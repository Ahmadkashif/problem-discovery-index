# Simplified Marketing Dashboard for First-Time Business Owners

**Niche:** [[niches/marketing-agencies-smb/diverse-owned-business-agencies/profile|Minority and Women-Owned Business Marketing Agencies]]
**Industry:** [[industries/marketing-agencies-smb|SMB Marketing Agencies]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Marketing reporting dashboards exist but show 30+ metrics that overwhelm first-time business owners — they need 5 numbers with plain-language explanations of what each means for their business.
**Tags:** #llm #text-generation #nlp #worker-facing #quick-win

## The Problem
An agency sends a monthly report to a first-time restaurant owner showing impressions, clicks, CTR, CPC, CPM, ROAS, conversion rate, bounce rate, sessions, and page views. The owner looks at it for 30 seconds, does not understand any of it, and decides "marketing isn't working" — or worse, decides "I'll trust whatever the agency says because I can't understand this." Neither outcome is healthy for the relationship. The owner needs to know: How many people found my restaurant this month? How many of them took an action (called, made a reservation, got directions)? How does that compare to last month? Is my investment paying off? That is 4-5 numbers, not 30.

## What Already Exists
Agency Analytics, Whatagraph, and Google Looker Studio produce marketing performance dashboards and reports. These are powerful visualization tools with dozens of widgets, metrics, and comparison views. They are designed for marketing professionals, not business owners.

## The Customization Gap
The tool needs a "client-simple" mode that presents 4-6 key metrics with: plain-language labels ("People who found you" not "Total impressions"), directional indicators with context ("Up 15% from last month — your Google listing is showing up for more searches"), a narrative summary explaining what happened and why in 3-4 sentences that a non-marketer can understand, and a single "health score" that answers the fundamental question: "Is my marketing working?" The agency configures which metrics to show per client based on their service and the client's sophistication level.

## Target Customer
Account managers at agencies serving 20-50 early-stage or non-technical business owners who need a reporting format that their clients actually read and understand.

## Impact If Solved
Increases client engagement with reports from 10-20% (current open and read rate for detailed reports) to 70-80% (for simplified, actionable reports), reduces "is this working?" calls by 50-60%, and improves retention by 15-25% by giving clients confidence that they understand what they are paying for.
