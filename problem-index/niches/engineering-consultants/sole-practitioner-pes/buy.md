# Lightweight Time Tracking for Solo Engineers

**Niche:** [[niches/engineering-consultants/sole-practitioner-pes/profile|Sole Practitioner PEs]]
**Industry:** [[industries/engineering-consultants|Engineering Consultants]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Time tracking apps abound, but none are designed for the engineering consultant who needs to log time against project phases (schematic design, design development, construction administration) and link entries to fee proposals for real-time budget visibility.
**Tags:** #data-integration #automation #quick-win #revenue-impact

## The Problem
Solo PEs bill on fixed fees but work on hourly labor. Without accurate time tracking by project phase, they cannot know whether a project is profitable until it is over. Generic time tracking tools (Toggl, Harvest, Clockify) let users log hours against "projects" but don't understand engineering project phases, don't map to fee proposal line items, and don't provide budget-vs-actual visibility in engineering terms. The result: 40% of solo PEs don't track time at all, and those who do track it in spreadsheets that are never reconciled against proposals.

## What Already Exists
Toggl Track ($10/month), Harvest ($11/month), and Clockify (free) are well-built time tracking tools with timer-based and manual entry, project categorization, and basic reporting. They serve freelancers and agencies well. Deltek Vantagepoint and Ajera provide engineering-specific time tracking but at $300+/month and with configuration complexity designed for 20+ person firms.

## The Customization Gap
Engineering consultants need time tracking that speaks their language: project phases (SD, DD, CD, CA), discipline codes (structural, civil, MEP), and fee structure types (fixed fee, hourly NTE, T&M). When a PE logs 3 hours on "Project 1234 - Construction Administration," the tool should immediately show "CA phase: 18 of 20 budgeted hours used — 90% consumed with 3 months of construction remaining." This requires importing the fee proposal structure as the budget template and mapping time entries to proposal line items. The customization is not complex — it's a data model extension and a pre-built import from common proposal formats — but no lightweight time tracking tool has built it.

## Target Customer
Solo PEs and micro-firm engineers currently using spreadsheets or no time tracking at all, who need budget visibility but refuse to pay for or configure enterprise tools.

## Impact If Solved
Gives solo PEs real-time budget visibility for the first time, enabling them to file change orders before projects go over budget. PEs who track time against budgets report 8-12% higher effective billing rates because they catch scope creep early.
