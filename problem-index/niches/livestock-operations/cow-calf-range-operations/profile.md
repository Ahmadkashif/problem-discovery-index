# Cow-Calf Range Operations

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/livestock-operations|Livestock Operations]]
**Category:** High Market Share

## Profile
**Market Size:** $35-45B
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~35%
**Digital Adoption:** Low — most cow-calf operations under 500 head rely on paper tally books, mental note-keeping, and generational knowledge; fewer than 15% use any herd management software
**Target Buyer:** Ranch Owner / Operator managing 100-2,000 head cow-calf pairs on range or pasture
**Automation Potential:** Medium — calving management, pasture rotation, and breeding decisions are automatable but require overcoming infrastructure limitations on remote rangeland

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Cow-calf operations are the entry point of the beef supply chain, maintaining breeding herds on rangeland and selling weaned calves at 6-8 months of age. These operations are overwhelmingly family-owned, multi-generational, and geographically dispersed across millions of acres of rangeland in the western US and Great Plains. The operator is simultaneously the herdsman, range manager, fence builder, equipment mechanic, and business manager. Profit margins are razor-thin — $50-150 per cow per year on good years, with total dependence on calf prices set at auction. The critical tacit knowledge is in reading individual animal condition across a herd scattered over thousands of acres: knowing which cows to cull, which heifers to retain, when to move cattle to fresh pasture, and which calves are falling behind. Cellular connectivity is absent on 40-60% of rangeland, making cloud-dependent tools impractical.

## Current Tools & Gaps
CattleMax, Herdsman, and CattlePro offer herd recordkeeping but require manual data entry that ranch operators rarely maintain consistently. Walk-over weighing systems (Tru-Test, Gallagher) exist but require $15,000-30,000 investment and only work at choke points. No tool integrates pasture forage assessment, herd health tracking, and breeding management into a single offline-capable platform designed for the range environment.
