# Lightweight Mobile Booking System

**Niche:** [[niches/tattoo-studios/rural-small-town-studios/profile|Rural & Small-Town Studios]]
**Industry:** [[industries/tattoo-studios|Tattoo Studios]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Booking tools like Vagaro and Square Appointments exist but are overbuilt and overpriced for solo rural artists who just need a shareable link that lets clients pick a date and pay a deposit.
**Tags:** #quick-win #automation #workflow-orchestration

## The Problem
Rural tattoo artists manage their schedule through phone calls, Facebook messages, and text threads. Double-bookings happen because the artist forgot to check one channel. Clients call during sessions, disrupting workflow. After-hours inquiries go unanswered until the next day. The artist spends 30-60 minutes daily just managing communication about scheduling — time that could be spent tattooing.

## What Already Exists
Vagaro ($25-85/month), Square Appointments ($0-69/month), Booksy ($30/month), and Acuity ($16-46/month) all provide online booking. These tools work well for multi-artist urban salons but are feature-heavy for a solo rural artist: they require configuring services, staff schedules, and integrations the artist doesn't need. The learning curve and monthly cost deter adoption — most rural artists try one, find it confusing, and go back to phone calls.

## The Customization Gap
The ideal tool for this market is radically simple: a single booking link (shareable via Facebook, text, or business card QR code) with the artist's available dates, a deposit collection via Venmo/CashApp/card, and automatic confirmation texts. No service menu, no staff management, no email marketing — just a calendar and a payment link. Setup should take under 5 minutes. Cost should be under $15/month or a small per-booking fee.

## Target Customer
Solo tattoo artists doing 8-15 appointments per week who currently manage scheduling via phone/text and lose 2-3 bookings per month to unreturned calls and scheduling confusion.

## Impact If Solved
Eliminates 30-60 minutes of daily scheduling overhead, reduces double-bookings and no-shows by 40-50% through deposits and confirmations, and recovers 2-3 lost bookings per month worth $400-900.
