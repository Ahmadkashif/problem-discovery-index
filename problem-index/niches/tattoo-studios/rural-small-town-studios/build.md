# Radius Marketing Tool for Rural Artists

**Niche:** [[niches/tattoo-studios/rural-small-town-studios/profile|Rural & Small-Town Studios]]
**Industry:** [[industries/tattoo-studios|Tattoo Studios]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No marketing tool helps rural tattoo artists target potential clients across a 30-60 mile radius spanning multiple small towns, where traditional local advertising and Instagram discovery don't work.
**Tags:** #recommendation #binary-classification #tabular-ml #revenue-impact

## The Problem
A rural tattoo artist in a town of 8,000 people has saturated their immediate local market — everyone who wants a tattoo knows about them. Growth requires reaching clients in surrounding towns 20-60 miles away, but Instagram's algorithm doesn't do geographic targeting for small accounts, Facebook ads require sophistication most solo artists lack, and Google Maps only surfaces them when someone searches "tattoo near me" (which assumes the client already decided to get a tattoo). There's no tool designed for hyper-local radius marketing for service businesses in low-density areas.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
The rural small business marketing problem exists across many industries, but the economics don't attract platform builders: low average revenue per user ($30-80/month willingness to pay), fragmented geography, and users with minimal digital literacy. Generic tools like Mailchimp or Google Ads are too complex. The opportunity is in simplicity — a tool so easy a cash-register-only business can use it.

## What to Build
A mobile app that auto-generates targeted Facebook/Instagram ads for surrounding zip codes, creates "guest spot" announcements when the artist travels to nearby towns, and builds an email/SMS list from previous clients for seasonal promotions. The key differentiator is zero-configuration geographic targeting: the artist enters their location, the system identifies population centers within a configurable radius, and runs micro-campaigns ($5-15/day) targeting those areas.

## Target Customer
Solo artists or 2-person shops in rural areas doing 8-15 tattoos per week who have plateaued on local word-of-mouth and want to grow without learning digital marketing.

## Impact If Built
Expands effective client radius from 10-15 miles (word of mouth) to 40-60 miles (targeted digital), adding 3-5 new clients per month worth $1,500-3,000 in incremental revenue.
