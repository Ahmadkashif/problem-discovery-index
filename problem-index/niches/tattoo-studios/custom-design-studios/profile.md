# Custom Design Studios

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/tattoo-studios|Tattoo Studios]]
**Category:** High Market Share

## Profile
**Market Size:** $2.1B
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~42%
**Digital Adoption:** Medium — Most use Instagram for portfolios and DMs for consultations, but design iteration and pricing remain manual.
**Target Buyer:** Studio owner or lead artist managing a multi-artist custom shop
**Automation Potential:** Medium — Design mockup generation and client communication are automatable, but artistic judgment is not.

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Custom design studios derive the majority of their revenue from bespoke pieces that require one or more consultation sessions, reference gathering, and iterative sketching before any ink touches skin. Unlike walk-in shops, these studios manage multi-week client pipelines where a single large piece may span 3-6 sessions at $150-300/hour. The workflow is fundamentally different: it's project-based rather than transactional, with significant upfront creative labor that may never convert to paid work if the client ghosts after seeing the design.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Artists use Instagram as a portfolio and lead generation tool, with DMs serving as the primary consultation channel. Some studios use Square or Vagaro for appointments, but none of these tools handle design versioning, reference image organization, or deposit-to-session tracking. The gap is a unified client pipeline tool that connects initial inquiry through design approval to session scheduling and payment.
