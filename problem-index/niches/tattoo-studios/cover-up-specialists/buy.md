# Before/After Portfolio System

**Niche:** [[niches/tattoo-studios/cover-up-specialists/profile|Cover-up Specialists]]
**Industry:** [[industries/tattoo-studios|Tattoo Studios]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Generic portfolio platforms like Squarespace and Instagram display finished work but don't pair before/after images with cover-up context — what was covered, how, and why it worked.
**Tags:** #data-integration #recommendation #multimodal #revenue-impact

## The Problem
Cover-up clients need to see examples of completed work on similar existing tattoos to trust that their piece is coverable. But cover-up portfolios on Instagram are just finished-piece photos — the "before" image, the constraints involved, and the technique used are buried in captions or omitted entirely. Clients can't search for "cover-up of a faded black tribal on a forearm" and find relevant examples, so they can't self-qualify before reaching out.

## What Already Exists
Squarespace, Wix, and Instagram serve as portfolio platforms for tattoo artists. TattooDo and Inkstinct offer tattoo-specific discovery platforms. However, none support structured before/after pairing with metadata (original tattoo characteristics, cover-up technique, sessions required, laser pre-treatment needed). The portfolio is flat — just images — with no searchable taxonomy of cover-up scenarios.

## The Customization Gap
The portfolio system needs structured metadata per project: before photo, existing tattoo characteristics (age, colors, size, location), cover-up approach (technique, sessions, laser), and after photos at multiple stages. Clients should be able to filter by their situation ("faded color piece on upper arm") and see comparable completed work. This turns a portfolio from a marketing tool into a pre-consultation qualification tool.

## Target Customer
Cover-up specialists who spend 3-5 hours per week on consultations that don't convert because the client's expectations were misaligned — a structured portfolio reduces these wasted consultations.

## Impact If Solved
Increases consultation conversion rate from 50-60% to 75-85% by pre-qualifying clients, reduces artist consultation time by 30%, and builds a searchable case library that compounds in value over time.
