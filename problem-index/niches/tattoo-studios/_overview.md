## Why These Niches

Custom design and walk-in flash represent the two dominant revenue models in tattooing — together they account for roughly 70% of studio revenue — making them essential high-market-share segments. Cover-up work and cosmetic/medical tattooing are growing specializations that remain almost entirely paper-based or Instagram-DM-based, with zero purpose-built digital tools. Culturally-specific artists and rural studios are systematically underserved by platforms designed around urban, Western tattoo culture. Appointment management and consent/aftercare compliance were selected as automation targets because no-show rates run 15-25% and health department paperwork is still largely paper forms, both problems with clear rule-based solutions.
