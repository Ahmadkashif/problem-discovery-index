# Digital Flash Display with Dynamic Pricing

**Niche:** [[niches/tattoo-studios/walk-in-flash-shops/profile|Walk-in Flash Shops]]
**Industry:** [[industries/tattoo-studios|Tattoo Studios]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No product lets flash shops display designs on screens with real-time pricing that adjusts based on artist availability, queue depth, and time of day.
**Tags:** #recommendation #reinforcement-learning #tabular-ml #revenue-impact

## The Problem
Walk-in flash shops display designs on printed sheets taped to walls, with static pricing that doesn't reflect demand. During peak hours (Friday/Saturday evenings, tourist season), shops leave money on the table with flat pricing. During slow periods, there's no mechanism to discount or promote specific designs to drive volume. Artists sit idle while printed flash sheets gather dust — the shop has no data on which designs get selected, which get ignored, and how pricing affects conversion.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
Tattoo shops are small businesses with minimal tech budgets, and the market is too fragmented to attract venture-backed startups. The concept of dynamic pricing feels foreign to an industry built on personal relationships and artistic integrity. Building this requires hardware (displays), software (pricing engine), and domain credibility — a combination that hasn't aligned for any builder yet.

## What to Build
A tablet or TV-based flash display system where clients browse designs by style, size, and price. Pricing adjusts in real time based on configurable rules: artist chair availability, current queue depth, day of week, and historical conversion data. The system tracks which designs are viewed, selected, and tattooed, giving shop owners data to retire underperforming designs and commission new ones that match demand patterns.

## Target Customer
High-volume walk-in shops doing 15-30+ tattoos per day, typically in tourist or nightlife areas, where a 10-15% revenue increase from dynamic pricing would add $3,000-8,000/month.

## Impact If Built
Increases average revenue per client by 10-20% through demand-based pricing, reduces client indecision time by presenting curated options, and gives shop owners their first data-driven view of flash design performance.
