# AppFolio/Buildium Simplified for Small Portfolios

**Niche:** [[niches/property-management/self-managing-landlords/profile|Self-Managing Landlords]]
**Industry:** [[industries/property-management|Property Management]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** AppFolio and Buildium are excellent property management platforms with 90% of features that small landlords will never use — a simplified tier with pre-configured defaults, 5-minute onboarding, and pricing at $5-10/property/month would capture the 10M+ landlord market these platforms currently can't serve.
**Tags:** #automation #quick-win #data-integration

## The Problem
A landlord with 3 properties tries AppFolio: they see a dashboard with 25 menu items, a setup wizard asking about CAM calculations and owner draws, and a pricing structure with per-unit minimums designed for 50+ unit portfolios. They quit within 10 minutes. The same landlord tries Buildium: $52/month minimum for features they'll use 5% of. The software is too much — too many features, too much setup, too high a price floor. These platforms were built for professional property managers, not for a dentist who owns 3 rental properties and manages them on weekends.

## What Already Exists
AppFolio and Buildium handle every property management workflow: screening, leasing, rent collection, maintenance, accounting, owner reporting, vendor management, and portfolio analytics. The underlying functionality is excellent. The barrier is complexity and pricing, not capability.

## The Customization Gap
A "Landlord Lite" tier that: (1) pre-configures all settings with sensible defaults — no setup wizard, no CAM configuration, no owner draw setup (the landlord IS the owner); (2) shows only 5 screens (tenants, rent, maintenance, money, leases) rather than the full 25-menu professional interface; (3) prices at $5-10/property/month with no minimum — 3 properties = $15-30/month, affordable for a part-time landlord; (4) onboards in 5 minutes — enter property address, rent amount, and tenant name, and the system is operational; and (5) provides an upgrade path — when the landlord grows to 15-20 properties and wants full PM features, they upgrade to the professional tier with all their data intact.

## Target Customer
AppFolio and Buildium seeking to capture the massive small-landlord market (10M+ potential users at $5-10/property/month = $600M-1.2B TAM). Landlords who have tried and abandoned professional PM software due to complexity.

## Impact If Solved
Captures a market 100x larger (by user count) than the professional PM market these platforms currently serve. Creates a natural upgrade funnel — landlords who grow their portfolios migrate to the full-featured tier. Generates $50-100M in incremental ARR from a market segment currently served by free tools with limited functionality.
