# Vacation & Short-Term Rental

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/property-management|Property Management]]
**Category:** Low Digitized

## Profile
**Market Size:** $8-10B
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~10%
**Digital Adoption:** Medium-High — PriceLabs, Beyond Pricing, and Wheelhouse handle dynamic pricing. Guesty, Hostaway, and Lodgify handle multi-platform listing management. But cleaning coordination, quality verification, and guest communication across multiple platforms remain fragmented.
**Target Buyer:** STR management company managing 20-200+ units across Airbnb, VRBO, and Booking.com
**Automation Potential:** High — dynamic pricing is a solved problem (PriceLabs), but multi-platform listing optimization, cleaning crew coordination, and quality consistency between turns are automation opportunities

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Short-term rental management combines hospitality operations with property management at the unit level. Each property is listed on 2-4 platforms (Airbnb, VRBO, Booking.com, direct website), each requiring separate listing management, calendar synchronization, pricing updates, and guest communication. The operational tempo is intense: a high-performing STR turns every 2-3 days, requiring cleaning, inspection, and restocking between guests. A 50-unit STR management company processes 500-800 turnovers per month, each with a 3-5 hour window between checkout and check-in. The margin depends entirely on occupancy and rate optimization — the same unit generating $150/night at 65% occupancy produces $2,925/month, while optimized pricing at $180/night and 80% occupancy produces $4,320/month.

## Current Tools & Gaps
PriceLabs and Beyond Pricing handle dynamic pricing using market demand signals. Guesty and Hostaway handle multi-platform listing and calendar synchronization. But cleaning crew scheduling, turnover quality verification, and consumable restocking are managed through text message groups with cleaning teams. No tool closes the loop between checkout, cleaning, quality verification, and guest-ready confirmation.
