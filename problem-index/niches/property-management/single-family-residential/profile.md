**Market Size:** $25-28B

**Target Buyer:** SFR property management company owner managing 100-2,000 scattered-site single-family rental homes
**Automation Potential:** High — maintenance route optimization across scattered sites is a solvable logistics problem, owner communication templating is an LLM opportunity, and tenant retention prediction is a classification task

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Single-family residential property management is a scattered-site business: properties are distributed across a city or metro area, each owned by a different investor with different expectations, requiring individual attention rather than the economies of scale that multifamily provides. A PM company managing 500 SFR homes has 500 different owners to communicate with, 500 different property conditions to monitor, and maintenance requests that require dispatching vendors to addresses spread across a 30-mile radius. The operational complexity per unit is 2-3x higher than multifamily because there's no on-site staff — every maintenance visit, inspection, and showing requires travel. Owner communication is the #1 source of PM company overhead: each owner wants personalized updates about their specific property, and the communication cadence varies by owner personality (some want monthly reports, others call weekly). The PM company's profitability depends on managing this communication burden without spending more time per owner than their 8-12% management fee justifies.

## Current Tools & Gaps
AppFolio handles accounting, rent collection, maintenance requests, and owner portals. Propertyware serves the SFR segment specifically. Neither optimizes the scattered-site logistics that define SFR management: maintenance vendor routing across dispersed properties, drive-by inspection scheduling, and showing coordination for vacant units.
