# Standard PM Software with Affordable Housing Compliance

**Niche:** [[niches/property-management/affordable-housing-section8/profile|Affordable Housing & Section 8]]
**Industry:** [[industries/property-management|Property Management]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** AppFolio dominates SMB property management but has no affordable housing compliance module — property managers using AppFolio for their market-rate units must switch to Yardi Affordable or maintain parallel spreadsheets for their Section 8 and LIHTC units, creating operational fragmentation.
**Tags:** #data-integration #compliance #automation #workflow-orchestration #quick-win

## The Problem
Many property management companies manage a mix of market-rate and affordable housing units. They use AppFolio for market-rate operations (accounting, maintenance, leasing) but AppFolio doesn't handle: LIHTC income certification and recertification, Section 8 HQS inspection tracking, HAP payment reconciliation, or affordable housing regulatory reporting. These companies either: (1) switch entirely to Yardi Affordable (expensive and complex for their market-rate operations), (2) run AppFolio for market-rate and Yardi for affordable (double the cost and double the systems), or (3) manage affordable compliance in Excel alongside AppFolio (fragmented and error-prone).

## What Already Exists
AppFolio handles accounting, rent collection, maintenance, and tenant management. Yardi Affordable Housing handles LIHTC compliance, Section 8 processing, and HUD reporting. Neither serves the mixed-portfolio PM company well.

## The Customization Gap
An affordable housing compliance module for AppFolio that: (1) adds income certification fields to tenant records — household income, household size, income limit compliance, certification date, recertification due date; (2) tracks Section 8 voucher details per unit — PHA, HAP amount, tenant portion, voucher expiration, HQS inspection schedule; (3) generates recertification workflows — 120 days before anniversary, initiate document collection from tenant, verify income, complete certification form, file in compliance record; (4) produces HUD-required reports (Form 50059 for Section 8, LIHTC annual owner certification) from AppFolio data; and (5) tracks compliance file documentation per unit with completeness scoring. This enables mixed-portfolio PMs to use one platform for all their properties.

## Target Customer
AppFolio users managing a mix of market-rate and affordable housing who currently use parallel systems for affordable compliance. AppFolio seeking to capture the affordable housing PM market ($8-10B).

## Impact If Solved
Eliminates the dual-platform burden for mixed-portfolio PMs, saving $500-2,000/month in redundant software costs. Reduces compliance errors caused by data fragmentation between market-rate and affordable systems. Enables AppFolio to capture affordable housing market share from Yardi. Could be offered as an AppFolio add-on module at $5-10/affordable-unit/month.
