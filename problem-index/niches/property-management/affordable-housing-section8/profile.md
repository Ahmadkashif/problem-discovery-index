# Affordable Housing & Section 8

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/property-management|Property Management]]
**Category:** Underserved

## Profile
**Market Size:** $8-10B
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~10%
**Digital Adoption:** Low-Medium — standard PM platforms handle accounting but don't know HQS inspection requirements, HAP payment processing, or LIHTC income certification rules. Compliance is managed through manual processes, paper files, and annual panic.
**Target Buyer:** Affordable housing property manager or compliance officer managing Section 8/HCV, LIHTC, or public housing units
**Automation Potential:** High — income recertification is a structured rules-based process, HQS inspection preparation follows a known checklist, and compliance tracking is a calendar and documentation management problem

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Affordable housing property management layers regulatory compliance on top of standard PM operations. Section 8 Housing Choice Voucher (HCV) properties must pass Housing Quality Standards (HQS) inspections, process Housing Assistance Payment (HAP) contracts with local Public Housing Authorities (PHAs), and comply with tenant income verification requirements. Low Income Housing Tax Credit (LIHTC) properties must certify and annually recertify tenant income eligibility, maintain detailed compliance files per unit, and report to state housing finance agencies. Failure to comply results in: HAP payment suspension (Section 8), tax credit recapture (LIHTC — potentially millions in penalties), or federal funding loss (public housing). The compliance burden is significant: LIHTC properties require annual income recertification for every tenant, with specific documentation requirements (tax returns, pay stubs, bank statements, asset verification) that must be collected, verified, and filed within regulatory timelines.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Yardi Affordable Housing and RealPage Onesite handle LIHTC compliance tracking and HUD reporting. AppFolio does not serve affordable housing well. But even Yardi's affordable module requires extensive manual work: the compliance officer must manually track recertification deadlines, collect documentation from tenants, verify income calculations, and prepare inspection files. No tool automates the tenant document collection process or pre-screens compliance files for errors before the audit.
