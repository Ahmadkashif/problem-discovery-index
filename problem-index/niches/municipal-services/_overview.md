## Why These Niches

Municipal services span thousands of local governments with wildly different budgets, populations, and technology maturity. Water/sewer and parks represent the two largest budget categories and highest citizen-interaction volume — the revenue centers of municipal operations. Code enforcement and small-town public works are operationally critical but still run on paper forms, spreadsheets, and phone calls in most jurisdictions. ADA accessibility and rural permitting serve populations that commercial software vendors routinely ignore because deal sizes are small. Utility billing and seasonal workforce management are high-volume, rule-heavy processes where automation can eliminate tens of thousands of manual hours annually. Excluded: fire/EMS, police, and transit, which have their own mature vendor ecosystems.
