## Why These Niches

Catering fragments along event type (corporate vs. wedding vs. social), production model (commissary kitchen vs. on-site), and business function (sales vs. production vs. staffing). These 8 niches cover the two largest revenue segments (corporate and wedding, together representing 65% of the market), the two most digitally neglected (small social caterers and mobile production operations that exist entirely on paper), the two most underserved populations (multi-event kitchens and dietary-complex events that no tool handles well), and the two highest-ROI automation targets (the proposal-to-invoice pipeline and temp crew management, both rule-heavy and currently manual). Excluded: institutional catering (hospitals, schools — different contract model), food truck catering (covered under food trucks), and restaurant private dining (a different workflow).
