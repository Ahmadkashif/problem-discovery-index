# Instagram-to-Booking Lead Capture

**Niche:** [[niches/catering-companies/small-social-event-caterers/profile|Small Social Event Caterers]]
**Industry:** [[industries/catering-companies|Catering Companies]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** ManyChat and generic chatbot tools automate Instagram DM responses but do not capture catering-specific event details (date, guest count, dietary restrictions, venue) or route them into a booking pipeline.
**Tags:** #llm #text-classification #nlp #automation #quick-win

## The Problem
Small social caterers get 60-80% of inquiries through Instagram DMs. A typical inquiry is: "Hi! We're having my daughter's quinceanera on March 15, about 80 guests, looking for Mexican food. What are your prices?" The caterer responds manually, often hours later between prep and service, and the conversation bounces back and forth 5-10 times before reaching a quote. By then, 30% of prospects have booked someone else. There is no system to capture the event details from the DM, qualify the lead, and start the booking process automatically.

## What Already Exists
ManyChat and Chatfuel automate Instagram DM responses with rule-based flows. HoneyBook offers a "contact form" that can be linked from Instagram. But ManyChat's flows are generic (designed for e-commerce product questions, not event inquiries), and HoneyBook's form requires the prospect to leave Instagram and fill out a web form — adding friction that drops conversion rates.

## The Customization Gap
The tool must parse natural-language catering inquiries from Instagram DMs, extract structured event details (date, guest count, cuisine type, dietary needs, venue, budget range), auto-respond with availability confirmation and pricing guidance, and route qualified leads into the caterer's booking pipeline with pre-populated event details — all without requiring the prospect to leave Instagram.

## Target Customer
Small social caterers who receive 20+ Instagram DM inquiries per month and currently respond manually, losing 20-30% of leads to slow response time.

## Impact If Solved
Reducing response time from 4-6 hours to under 5 minutes and capturing structured event details increases booking conversion by 15-25% — adding $10K-$30K in annual revenue for a typical micro-caterer.
