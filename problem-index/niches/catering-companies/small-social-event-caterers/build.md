# All-in-One Micro-Caterer Business Platform

**Niche:** [[niches/catering-companies/small-social-event-caterers/profile|Small Social Event Caterers]]
**Industry:** [[industries/catering-companies|Catering Companies]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No product provides a mobile-first, affordable business platform for 1-5 person catering operations — combining lead tracking, quoting, contracts, recipe scaling, prep lists, and payment collection in one tool.
**Tags:** #automation #workflow-orchestration #data-integration #ai-platform #quick-win

## The Problem
A small social caterer running 6-10 events/month manages their entire business from their phone — DM inquiries on Instagram, quote conversations via text, contracts as PDF attachments, payments on Venmo, recipes in Notes app, and prep lists on paper. At 3 events/month this works. At 8 events/month, the owner loses track of which inquiries were quoted, double-books weekends, forgets to collect final payments, and has no idea whether they are making money. There is no single tool that handles the complete micro-caterer workflow without requiring enterprise-level setup.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
The per-customer revenue potential ($20-$50/month for a $100K caterer) is too low for enterprise catering software companies. Consumer-facing tools (HoneyBook, Dubsado) serve the quoting/contract layer but ignore food production. The market is fragmented, informal, and hard to reach through traditional SaaS marketing — these operators do not attend trade shows or read industry publications.

## What to Build
A mobile-first platform ($20-$40/month) that handles: lead inbox (aggregating DMs, texts, and form submissions), quote builder with per-head pricing and auto-calculated food costs, digital contract with e-signature, deposit and final payment collection (Stripe/Venmo), recipe scaling from a personal recipe library, auto-generated prep lists and shopping lists per event, and a simple post-event P&L showing actual profit. The key is radical simplicity — setup in 15 minutes, usable on a phone between prep and service.

## Target Customer
Home-based or commissary-based caterers doing 4-12 events/month, $50K-$200K in annual revenue, who currently run their business entirely through their phone's native apps.

## Impact If Built
Giving 50,000+ micro-caterers their first real business infrastructure prevents the revenue plateau that forces talented operators to either stay small or burn out — enabling growth from $80K to $200K+ revenue while maintaining profitability visibility.
