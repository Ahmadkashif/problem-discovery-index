# Wedding-Specific Production Planning Pipeline

**Niche:** [[niches/catering-companies/wedding-catering-operations/profile|Wedding Catering Specialists]]
**Industry:** [[industries/catering-companies|Catering Companies]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No product manages the 6-12 month wedding catering production pipeline — from tasting menu selection through scaled recipes, timeline coordination, and day-of execution checklists.
**Tags:** #workflow-orchestration #automation #data-integration #ai-platform

## The Problem
A wedding caterer managing 80 weddings/year has 30-50 weddings in various stages of planning simultaneously. Each requires tracking tasting selections, guest count updates, venue-specific logistics (kitchen access, plating space, power availability), dietary restriction matrices, rental orders, and day-of timelines coordinating with 5-8 other vendors. This project management happens across email threads, Google Sheets, printed BEOs, and the event coordinator's memory. Critical details (the bride's cousin is deathly allergic to tree nuts; the venue oven only reaches 450F) live in email and are discoverable only by searching.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
Wedding catering sits between wedding planning software (focused on the couple, not the caterer's production) and general catering management (focused on event-day execution, not the 6-month planning pipeline). The production planning is a project management problem specific to caterers — vendors like Monday.com or Asana could theoretically be adapted, but they lack the domain-specific templates, BEO formats, and vendor coordination workflows that make them usable without extensive customization.

## What to Build
A wedding production management system that creates a structured project for each wedding at booking: tasting selection tracking, automatic BEO generation from tasting choices scaled to guest count, venue logistics checklists (populated from a venue database), dietary restriction matrix that cascades through recipes, day-of timeline builder with vendor coordination, and progress tracking across all active weddings with alerts for upcoming milestones.

## Target Customer
Wedding caterers managing 50+ weddings/year who currently track production planning across 5+ disconnected tools and lose critical details in email threads.

## Impact If Built
Reducing production planning time by 3-5 hours per wedding saves 150-250 hours annually. Preventing a single allergen mistake or venue logistics failure avoids $10K-$50K in liability exposure and referral-network damage.
