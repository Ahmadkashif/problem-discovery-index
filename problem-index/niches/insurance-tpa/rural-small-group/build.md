# Micro-TPA Platform for Small Rural Employers

**Niche:** [[niches/insurance-tpa/rural-small-group/profile|Rural Small-Group Plans]]
**Industry:** [[industries/insurance-tpa|Insurance TPA]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No TPA platform exists that can profitably administer self-insured health plans for employers with 25-100 employees at a PEPM price point competitive with level-funded carrier products.
**Tags:** #automation #workflow-orchestration #revenue-impact

## The Problem
Self-insurance saves employers 10-20% versus fully-insured premiums by eliminating carrier profit margins and state premium taxes. But TPA setup costs ($10K-$25K) and ongoing administration fees ($30-$50 PEPM) make self-insurance uneconomical for groups under 100 employees. Small rural employers — farms, manufacturers, local governments, healthcare facilities — are stuck paying fully-insured rates or accepting level-funded products with no plan customization or claims transparency.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
Traditional TPA platforms (HealthEdge, QNXT) require $500K+ implementations and per-client configuration. The unit economics don't work at small-group scale with traditional technology stacks. Building a platform that automates plan setup, claims adjudication, compliance, and member services to the point where PEPM costs drop below $20 requires ground-up architecture — not a feature bolted onto an enterprise claims system.

## What to Build
A cloud-native micro-TPA platform with templated plan designs (10-15 pre-configured SPD variants covering 90% of small-group needs), automated claims adjudication via rules engine, self-service member portal with ID cards and EOBs, and automated compliance (ACA reporting, non-discrimination testing, ERISA filings). Broker onboards a new group in 2 hours instead of 2 weeks. Target PEPM of $12-$18.

## Target Customer
Benefits brokers serving rural small employers (25-100 employees) who currently place fully-insured or level-funded coverage because no TPA can serve the group economically.

## Impact If Built
Brings self-insurance savings (10-20% of premium) to 500,000+ small rural employers currently priced out of the market. A 50-employee group spending $600K annually on fully-insured premiums saves $60K-$120K per year.
