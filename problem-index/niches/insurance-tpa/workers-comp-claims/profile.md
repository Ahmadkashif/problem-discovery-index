## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Workers' comp claims processing is the single largest revenue segment for TPAs, driven by complex state-by-state regulatory requirements and the need for medical management coordination. Unlike group health, workers' comp involves return-to-work programs, vocational rehabilitation, and fraud detection — each requiring specialized workflows. The niche is distinct because claims examiners must simultaneously manage medical treatment authorization, indemnity calculations, and legal exposure across 50 different state jurisdictions with different benefit schedules.

## Current Tools & Gaps
CorVel, Sedgwick, and Broadspire dominate the large-employer segment with end-to-end platforms. Mid-market TPAs (50-500 employees) often use older systems like Claim Center or custom-built Access databases. The gap is in intelligent triage — determining which claims need senior examiner attention versus auto-processing, and predicting which claims will develop into litigation before it happens.
