# Automated Cleaning Verification System

**Niche:** [[niches/gyms-independent/24hr-unstaffed-gyms/profile|24-Hour Unstaffed Gyms]]
**Industry:** [[industries/gyms-independent|Independent Gyms]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Cleaning management platforms like Janitorial Manager and CleanTelligent track task completion for commercial offices, but don't verify gym-specific cleaning standards — equipment wipe-downs, bathroom restocking, floor mopping in free-weight areas — for facilities where the owner is never present during cleaning.
**Tags:** #computer-vision #automation #compliance #quick-win

## The Problem
24-hour gyms contract cleaning services (typically 1-2 hours daily, often overnight). The owner pays $800-2,000/month but has no way to verify that cleaning was actually completed to standard. Did the cleaner wipe down all cardio machines? Restock paper towels? Mop the rubber flooring in the weight area? Clean the bathrooms? The owner discovers missed cleaning through member complaints the next day or by reviewing hours of overnight camera footage. Some cleaners check in, do a cursory job, and leave — the owner can verify arrival/departure time but not work quality.

## What Already Exists
Janitorial Manager and CleanTelligent provide cleaning task checklists and GPS check-in verification for commercial cleaning companies. Swept and OrangeQC offer mobile inspection tools. These platforms verify that a cleaner was present and checked off tasks, but don't independently verify completion quality. For office buildings, a supervisor walks the floor; for an unstaffed gym, there's nobody to inspect. The verification gap is the independent quality check.

## The Customization Gap
The needed adaptation is cleaning verification using the gym's existing cameras: (1) a cleaning checklist specific to gym environments (equipment wipe-down, bathroom restock, floor mopping zones, trash removal, towel station refresh), (2) camera-based verification that compares pre-cleaning and post-cleaning snapshots of key zones, (3) automated scoring of cleaning completion (restocked stations are visually full, wiped equipment is visibly clean, floors show mop patterns), and (4) instant owner notification when cleaning falls below threshold with timestamped evidence for contractor accountability.

## Target Customer
Owners of 24-hour gyms paying $800-2,000/month for cleaning services with no way to verify work quality beyond next-morning walkthroughs.

## Impact If Solved
Ensures consistent cleaning quality that eliminates 60-70% of facility-condition complaints (the #1 reason members cite for canceling at budget gyms), provides evidence-based leverage in cleaning contractor negotiations, and saves 20-30 minutes/day of owner inspection time.
