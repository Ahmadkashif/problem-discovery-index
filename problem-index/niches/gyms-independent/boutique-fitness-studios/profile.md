# Boutique Fitness Studios

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/gyms-independent|Independent Gyms]]
**Category:** High Market Share

## Profile
**Market Size:** $12B
**Share of Parent Industry:** 30%
**Digital Adoption:** Medium-High — Most boutique studios use Mindbody or Mariana Tek for booking, but client retention analytics, marketing automation, and revenue optimization remain largely manual.
**Target Buyer:** Studio Owner / Manager
**Automation Potential:** Medium — Class booking and payments are digitized, but client engagement scoring, instructor performance analytics, and dynamic pricing are manual or nonexistent.

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Boutique fitness studios (cycling, barre, Pilates, HIIT, yoga studios charging $25-40/class or $150-300/month) are the premium segment of independent fitness. They compete on experience, community, and instructor quality — not equipment or floor space. The business model is fundamentally different from traditional gyms: revenue per member is 3-5x higher, but so is churn sensitivity. A member who misses two weeks is at high risk of canceling. Studios live and die on retention, yet most lack any systematic way to identify at-risk members, measure instructor impact on retention, or optimize class schedules for revenue rather than just popularity.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Mindbody dominates booking and payments for boutique studios. Mariana Tek (acquired by Xponential) and Momence serve the same market. ClassPass fills empty spots but at deep discounts. The gap is the analytics and intelligence layer: Mindbody has the data (booking patterns, attendance frequency, purchase history) but its reporting is basic — no churn prediction, no instructor-level retention analytics, no dynamic pricing recommendations. Studios that want these insights export CSVs to spreadsheets.
