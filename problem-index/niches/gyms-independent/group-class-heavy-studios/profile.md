# Group-Class-Heavy Studios

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/gyms-independent|Independent Gyms]]
**Category:** Low Digitized

## Profile
**Market Size:** $3.5B
**Share of Parent Industry:** 9%
**Digital Adoption:** Low-Medium — Class booking may be digital (Mindbody, Momence), but instructor scheduling, sub management, playlist licensing, and class capacity optimization are handled through group texts, personal favors, and intuition.
**Target Buyer:** Studio Owner / Lead Instructor
**Automation Potential:** High — Instructor scheduling, substitute management, waitlist optimization, and capacity planning follow predictable patterns but are currently managed through personal communication and manual tracking.

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Group-class-heavy studios (those where 70%+ of revenue comes from group classes rather than open gym or personal training) have a distinct operational challenge: managing a roster of 5-15 independent-contractor instructors across 30-60 weekly class slots. The studio's product quality depends entirely on having the right instructor in the right class at the right time — and the entire instructor coordination system runs on group texts, personal relationships, and last-minute scrambling. When an instructor cancels, the studio owner sends a group text asking for subs at 5 AM. Waitlist management, capacity optimization, and schedule planning are equally informal.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Mindbody and Momence handle member-facing class booking. When I Work and Homebase handle employee scheduling. But instructor management for group fitness is neither — instructors are typically independent contractors (not employees), their availability changes weekly, sub requests have complex qualification requirements (not every instructor can teach every format), and the studio needs to balance instructor preferences with member demand. No platform handles the full instructor operations lifecycle: availability collection, schedule generation, sub management, format qualification tracking, and performance analytics.
