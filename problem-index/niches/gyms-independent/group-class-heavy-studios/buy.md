# Instructor Scheduling for Independent Contractor Models

**Niche:** [[niches/gyms-independent/group-class-heavy-studios/profile|Group-Class-Heavy Studios]]
**Industry:** [[industries/gyms-independent|Independent Gyms]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** When I Work and Deputy handle employee shift scheduling, but group fitness instructors are independent contractors with format-specific certifications, variable weekly availability, and per-class pay — a scheduling model these platforms don't support.
**Tags:** #automation #workflow-orchestration #data-integration #quick-win

## The Problem
Group fitness studios schedule 30-60 classes per week across 5-15 instructors who are independent contractors. Each instructor teaches specific formats (yoga, cycling, HIIT, barre), has availability that changes weekly, prefers certain time slots, and is paid per class at rates that vary by format and seniority. Building the weekly schedule is a complex constraint-satisfaction problem that studio owners solve manually in spreadsheets, spending 3-5 hours per week juggling instructor availability, format requirements, member demand, and room/equipment availability. Changes cascade: moving one class means checking 3 instructors' availability and notifying 20 booked members.

## What Already Exists
When I Work, Deputy, and Homebase handle shift scheduling for hourly employees with fixed roles. Mindbody and Momence handle class scheduling from the member-booking side but don't help build the instructor schedule — they display whatever the owner manually enters. SubSync and a few fitness-specific tools handle sub management but not primary schedule creation. The gap is that employee scheduling tools assume fungible workers (any barista can work any shift), while group fitness scheduling requires matching format-certified instructors to format-specific time slots with variable pay rates.

## The Customization Gap
The needed adaptation is a constraint-aware scheduling engine for independent contractor instructors: (1) instructor profiles with format certifications and weekly availability windows, (2) schedule generation that auto-assigns instructors to classes based on certification, availability, and preferences, (3) per-class pay calculation with format-specific rates, (4) automatic member notification when schedule changes occur, (5) integration with Mindbody/Momence to publish the finalized schedule to the booking platform, and (6) contractor payment tracking (1099, not W-2). This is a vertical scheduling template, not a new platform.

## Target Customer
Studio owners who spend 3-5 hours weekly building class schedules by hand and want to reduce scheduling from a creative puzzle to a review-and-approve workflow.

## Impact If Solved
Reduces schedule creation from 3-5 hours/week to 30-60 minutes, eliminates 80% of scheduling conflicts that cause last-minute changes, and provides data for optimizing the schedule based on attendance patterns — worth $5K-10K annually in saved owner time plus improved member experience.
