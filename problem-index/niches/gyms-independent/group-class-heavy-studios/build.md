# Instructor Substitute Marketplace and Auto-Fill

**Niche:** [[niches/gyms-independent/group-class-heavy-studios/profile|Group-Class-Heavy Studios]]
**Industry:** [[industries/gyms-independent|Independent Gyms]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No platform automatically finds qualified substitute instructors for group fitness classes — studio owners resort to desperate 5 AM group texts and personal favors, risking class cancellations that cost $200-500 each in lost revenue.
**Tags:** #recommendation #automation #workflow-orchestration #ai-agent #quick-win

## The Problem
When a group fitness instructor calls in sick (which happens 2-4 times per month per instructor), the studio owner enters crisis mode: they send a group text to every instructor they know, post on social media, call personal contacts, and sometimes end up teaching the class themselves regardless of their qualification in that format. The process takes 30-90 minutes of frantic communication, and still results in class cancellations 20-30% of the time. Each canceled class costs $200-500 in lost bookings and membership dissatisfaction. The sub-finding process is the single most stressful recurring operational task for group-class studio owners.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
Sub management requires matching on multiple dimensions simultaneously: format certification (can they teach HIIT?), availability (can they get there by 5:30 PM?), location (are they within 20 minutes?), and relationship (does this studio accept instructors from the broader community or only their roster?). Existing platforms either serve employees (When I Work's shift-swap feature) or full-time workers (staffing agencies). The independent-contractor, gig-style nature of group fitness instruction falls between these models. Building a sub marketplace also requires network effects — it only works if enough instructors in a metro area are registered.

## What to Build
A marketplace and auto-fill system that: (1) maintains instructor profiles with format certifications, availability windows, location, and preferred studios, (2) receives sub requests from studios with class details (format, time, location, pay rate), (3) auto-matches to qualified, available instructors and sends push notifications in priority order, (4) handles acceptance, confirmation, and studio notification in a single workflow, and (5) tracks sub performance ratings for quality control. The studio posts a sub request; the system fills it in minutes, not hours.

## Target Customer
Owners of group-class studios running 30+ classes per week with 5-15 instructors who experience 5-15 sub needs per month.

## Impact If Built
Reduces sub-finding time from 30-90 minutes to under 5 minutes per request, eliminates 70-80% of class cancellations due to instructor unavailability, and recovers $15K-40K annually per studio in prevented cancellation revenue loss.
