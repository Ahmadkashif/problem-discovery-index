# Equipment Warranty and Service Record Loss

**Niche:** [[niches/gyms-independent/equipment-maintenance-ops/profile|Equipment Maintenance Operations]]
**Industry:** [[industries/gyms-independent|Independent Gyms]]
**Type:** Fix (Pain Point)
**One-liner:** Gym owners lose $5K-15K annually in warranty claims they could have made because purchase receipts, warranty documents, and service records are scattered across email inboxes, filing cabinets, and the previous owner's memory.
**Tags:** #automation #data-integration #compliance #quick-win

## The Problem
A typical independent gym has $100K-500K in equipment from 3-5 different manufacturers, purchased over 5-15 years. Warranty terms vary: Life Fitness offers 3-year parts on commercial equipment, Precor offers 5-year frame warranties, and used equipment has whatever warranty the reseller provides (often none). When a machine breaks, the owner's first question is "is this under warranty?" — and the answer requires finding the original purchase receipt, identifying the warranty terms, and confirming that maintenance requirements (some warranties require proof of preventive maintenance) have been met. In practice, owners can't find the paperwork 40-60% of the time and pay for repairs that should have been covered.

## Why It's Still Broken
Equipment purchases happen infrequently (a gym buys equipment every 1-3 years), so there's no habitual workflow for warranty documentation. Receipts arrive as paper invoices, email PDFs, and credit card statements. Warranty registration is done on each manufacturer's separate website (or not done at all). Service records from third-party technicians arrive as handwritten invoices. When the gym changes ownership (common — independent gyms turn over every 5-7 years), the documentation chain breaks completely. No single system designed for gym owners aggregates purchase, warranty, and service data.

## What a Fix Looks Like
A digital equipment vault that: (1) stores purchase receipts (photo/scan/email forward), warranty documents, and serial numbers in one searchable database, (2) auto-extracts warranty terms and expiration dates from uploaded documents using OCR, (3) links service records to specific machines, (4) alerts the owner when a machine under warranty develops an issue that should be a warranty claim, and (5) generates warranty claim packages with all required documentation (purchase proof, service history, issue description) ready to submit to the manufacturer.

## Who Feels the Pain
Gym owners who pay out-of-pocket for equipment repairs that should be warranty-covered, and who spend hours searching email and file cabinets when a machine breaks.

## Impact If Fixed
Recovers $5K-15K annually in warranty claims that would otherwise be missed, and reduces the time spent searching for equipment documentation from 30-60 minutes per incident to under 2 minutes.
