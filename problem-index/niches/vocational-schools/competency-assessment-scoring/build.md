# Mobile-First Hands-On Assessment Capture

**Niche:** [[niches/vocational-schools/competency-assessment-scoring/profile|Competency Assessment & Scoring]]
**Industry:** [[industries/vocational-schools|Vocational Schools]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** A tablet app that lets instructors score student hands-on work in 30 seconds — tap rubric dimensions, snap a photo of the work product, add a voice note — replacing paper rubrics that take 3-5 minutes and produce no digital record.
**Tags:** #automation #worker-facing #computer-vision #quick-win

## The Problem
Vocational instructors assess 15-25 students per class on hands-on tasks. Each assessment requires completing a multi-dimension rubric (safety compliance, technique, quality of finished work, time management), recording scores, and providing feedback. On paper, this takes 3-5 minutes per student — consuming 45-125 minutes of a 3-hour class on documentation alone. Many instructors skip the documentation, recording only pass/fail in their head and filling in rubrics later from memory. The result: unreliable assessment data, no photo evidence of student work, and no longitudinal skill development record.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
Ed-tech companies focus on scalable products — standardized testing, LMS platforms, adaptive learning — not niche tools for in-person observation-based assessment. The shop floor environment is hostile to technology: dust, noise, heat, welding flash, and instructors wearing gloves. Any tool must work one-handed, with thick gloves, in poor lighting, and be faster than paper. This is an industrial UX challenge as much as a software challenge, which typical ed-tech teams don't have experience designing for.

## What to Build
A ruggedized tablet app (or ruggedized-case iPad) with an interface designed for shop-floor conditions: large touch targets for gloved fingers, trade-specific rubric templates pre-loaded (welding: bead appearance, penetration, undercut, porosity, safety compliance), one-tap scoring per dimension, automatic camera capture of work product linked to the assessment record, and optional voice-to-text notes. Assessments sync to a cloud dashboard showing student progression by competency dimension over time. The entire assessment flow from "start" to "submitted" takes under 30 seconds.

## Target Customer
Program directors and lead instructors at vocational schools with 50+ students in hands-on trades programs who are losing 20-40% of class time to paper-based assessment documentation.

## Impact If Built
Reduces per-student assessment time from 3-5 minutes to 30 seconds — recovering 30-90 minutes per class for actual instruction. Creates the first digital longitudinal competency record for vocational students, supporting accreditation evidence, employer verification, and student self-tracking. Generates photo evidence libraries that anchor assessment standards and reduce inter-rater inconsistency.
