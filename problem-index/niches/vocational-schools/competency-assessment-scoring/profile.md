# Competency Assessment & Scoring

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/vocational-schools|Vocational Schools]]
**Category:** Highly Automatable

## Profile
**Market Size:** $500M-800M (embedded operational cost)
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~4% (but represents 20-30% of instructor time)
**Digital Adoption:** Low — Assessments are paper rubrics or instructor verbal evaluations; no digital capture of performance evidence; grading criteria vary by instructor with no calibration mechanism
**Target Buyer:** Lead instructor, program director, or academic dean
**Automation Potential:** High — Hands-on competency assessment follows structured rubric dimensions that can be digitized, standardized, and partially automated through computer vision and structured scoring workflows

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Competency assessment in vocational education is fundamentally different from academic testing — it evaluates physical performance, not knowledge recall. A student demonstrates competency by performing a task (welding a joint, wiring a circuit, plumbing a fixture) while an instructor observes and evaluates multiple quality dimensions simultaneously. This observation-based assessment is the most instructor-time-intensive activity in vocational programs and the most subjective — the same work product receives different scores from different instructors. Digitizing and partially automating this process is the single highest-ROI workflow improvement available in vocational education.

## Current Tools & Gaps
NCCER provides standardized performance profile testing for construction trades but still requires human evaluators. AWS certification testing uses human inspectors with standardized visual acceptance criteria. No digital tool captures the assessment event — the instructor's observation, the work product's visual quality, the student's technique — in a structured format. LMS quiz modules test theory knowledge but cannot evaluate hands-on performance. The gap is total: there is no digital competency assessment tool designed for vocational hands-on evaluation.
