# Pediatric Medical Transport

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/non-emergency-medical-transport|Non-Emergency Medical Transport]]
**Category:** Low Digitized

## Profile
**Market Size:** $800M-1.2B
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~8-10%
**Digital Adoption:** Low — Pediatric transport coordination happens through phone calls between parents, children's hospitals, and NEMT providers; no digital platform addresses the unique safety and consent requirements for transporting minors
**Target Buyer:** Pediatric NEMT operator / children's hospital transport coordinator / Medicaid MCO care manager
**Automation Potential:** Medium — Safety requirements (car seat types, escort verification, parent consent) add manual verification steps that are difficult to automate without purpose-built workflows

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Transporting minors for medical appointments introduces regulatory, safety, and coordination complexities that do not exist in adult NEMT. Every trip requires: age-appropriate child restraint systems (rear-facing car seats, forward-facing seats, booster seats, depending on age/weight), verification that a parent or authorized guardian has consented to the transport, confirmation that an escort (parent or authorized adult) is present or that the minor qualifies for unescorted transport under state Medicaid rules, and driver background checks that meet state requirements for transporting children (often stricter than standard CDL requirements). Pediatric patients also include medically complex children with ventilators, feeding tubes, or seizure risks — requiring drivers trained in pediatric medical equipment handling.

## Current Tools & Gaps
NEMT dispatch platforms do not differentiate pediatric trips beyond a basic "minor" flag. No system verifies car seat compatibility with vehicle type, tracks parent/guardian consent per trip, or validates driver pediatric training credentials. Children's hospitals coordinate transportation through social workers using phone calls and faxes. The entire pediatric NEMT workflow is managed through manual checklists and personal verification calls.
