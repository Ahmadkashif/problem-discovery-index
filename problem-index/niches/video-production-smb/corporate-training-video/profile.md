## Profile
**Market Size:** $4B
**Share of Parent Industry:** 29%

**Target Buyer:** Production Company Owner / Director
**Automation Potential:** High — Script formatting, accessibility captioning, multi-language versioning, and LMS integration are repetitive and rule-driven.

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Corporate training video production companies create onboarding, compliance, safety, product training, and leadership development videos for enterprise clients. Projects range from $5K talking-head videos to $200K multi-module e-learning series. The workflow is distinct from creative production: scripts require subject matter expert (SME) review and legal/compliance approval before shooting; videos must be captioned for ADA compliance; content often needs updating annually (compliance regulations change); and delivery targets an LMS (Learning Management System), not social media or broadcast. The client relationship is long-term — a training video company may produce 20–50 videos per year for a single corporate client across multiple departments.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Premiere Pro and After Effects handle production. Frame.io handles internal review. But the training-specific workflow — SME script review, compliance approval, version control across annual updates, accessibility captioning, SCORM packaging for LMS delivery, and multi-language versioning — is managed with email and shared drives. No tool connects the production pipeline to the LMS delivery pipeline, forcing the production company to manually export, format, caption, package, and upload each completed video to the client's learning platform.
