# Client-Facing Video Delivery and Review Portal

**Niche:** [[niches/video-production-smb/wedding-videography-studios/profile|Wedding Videography Studios]]
**Industry:** [[industries/video-production-smb|Video Production SMB]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Frame.io handles professional video review, but a wedding studio needs a client-facing portal where couples preview, request revisions, and download their final videos — not a pro editing collaboration tool.
**Tags:** #automation #workflow-orchestration #quick-win #worker-facing

## The Problem
When a wedding video is ready for review, the videographer sends a private Vimeo or Google Drive link via email. The couple watches, texts feedback ("can you make the speech shorter?" "can you add my aunt at 3:42?"), and the videographer translates these informal messages into editing tasks. Final delivery is another link — often a large file download that the couple's WiFi can't handle. There's no branded experience, no structured feedback mechanism, no persistent gallery where the couple can return years later to re-download their videos. When a couple asks for a copy 3 years later, the videographer searches their external hard drives hoping the project files still exist.

## What Already Exists
Frame.io provides professional video review with timestamped comments, version tracking, and approval workflows — designed for production teams and agency clients. Pixieset and ShootProof provide beautiful delivery galleries for photographers. Vimeo and YouTube handle video hosting and sharing. But Frame.io is too complex and expensive ($15–$49/user/month) for couple-facing review. Pixieset doesn't handle video. Vimeo doesn't provide a branded gallery experience or structured revision workflow. No tool combines: branded video gallery, client-friendly review with simple feedback capture, revision tracking, and permanent delivery in a package designed for wedding studios.

## The Customization Gap
A wedding video delivery portal needs to: (1) present videos in a beautiful, branded gallery (studio logo, couple's names, wedding date) that feels like a gift, not a file share, (2) allow timestamped feedback without requiring the couple to create an account or learn a tool ("tap the screen and type your note"), (3) track revision requests and show the couple the status of each (received, in progress, complete), (4) deliver final videos in multiple formats (4K master, social media clips, mobile-optimized) with reliable download for large files, (5) host videos permanently so couples can re-download years later without contacting the studio, and (6) integrate with HoneyBook/Dubsado for final payment collection upon delivery. Price: $20–$50/month per studio.

## Target Customer
Wedding videography studios delivering 20–60 wedding videos per year who want a professional delivery experience without the overhead of Frame.io.

## Impact If Solved
Eliminates 2–3 hours per wedding of revision management (translating text messages into editing tasks, sending multiple download links). Creates a referral-generating delivery experience that couples share with friends — the delivery gallery becomes a marketing tool. Reduces re-delivery requests (3–5% of clients request re-downloads annually) to zero through permanent hosting.
