## Why These Niches

SMB video production spans wildly different markets united only by the equipment used. Wedding videography and corporate training represent the largest revenue segments with the most clients and the most repetitive operational challenges. Real estate media and social media content studios operate in digitally underserved spaces — high-volume, fast-turnaround work that has outgrown artisanal workflows but hasn't attracted purpose-built tools. Legal video and nonprofit documentary serve specialized audiences with unique compliance, ethical, and funding constraints ignored by general production tools. Drone aerial production and multicamera livestreaming are the most equipment-intensive and workflow-heavy segments, where automation can eliminate the most manual operational overhead. Excluded: film/TV production (different scale and union structure) and in-house corporate video teams (different buyer dynamics).
