# End-to-End Real Estate Media Operations Platform

**Niche:** [[niches/video-production-smb/real-estate-media-producers/profile|Real Estate Media Producers]]
**Industry:** [[industries/video-production-smb|Video Production SMB]]
**Type:** Build (Greenfield Opportunity)
**One-liner:** No platform manages the full real estate media workflow — agent booking, photographer dispatch, shoot tracking, automated photo/video processing, QC, delivery, and invoicing — in a single system.
**Tags:** #workflow-orchestration #automation #data-integration #ai-platform #quick-win

## The Problem
A real estate media company doing 50 shoots per week operates like an uncoordinated relay race: the agent texts to book, the scheduler (the owner's spouse, usually) enters it into Google Calendar, the photographer gets a text with the address, shoots the property, uploads raw files to Dropbox, the editor downloads and processes them, uploads finished media to a delivery folder, emails the agent a link, and the bookkeeper sends an invoice. Each handoff is manual, untracked, and failure-prone. When the agent calls asking "where are my photos?" the owner checks 4 different systems to determine whether the shoot happened, whether files were uploaded, whether editing is complete, or whether delivery was sent.

## Why Nobody Has Built This
Real estate photography companies are typically 1–10 person operations with $200K–$2M in revenue — individually too small for enterprise workflow platforms. The operational model varies by market: some companies employ W-2 photographers, others use 1099 contractors; some do their own editing, others outsource to offshore editors; some deliver via branded portal, others via Dropbox. Building a flexible platform that accommodates these variations while remaining simple enough for a non-technical owner is a design challenge. The few tools that exist (TourFactory, HomeDiary) focus on the delivery/marketing side, not the operations side.

## What to Build
A SaaS operations platform with: (1) agent-facing booking portal (select services, enter address, choose time slot from available windows), (2) automated photographer dispatch based on location, availability, and qualification (some shoots require drone-licensed pilots), (3) shoot tracking with check-in/check-out and raw file upload confirmation, (4) automated photo processing pipeline (HDR merge, sky replacement, color correction) with QC preview before delivery, (5) branded delivery gallery where agents view, download, and approve media, (6) auto-invoicing triggered by delivery confirmation, and (7) a dashboard showing all shoots in pipeline with status. The platform replaces 6 separate tools (calendar, text messages, Dropbox, email, invoicing software, spreadsheet tracker) with one system.

## Target Customer
Real estate media company owners processing 20+ shoots per week who are ready to scale from "I manage everything" to "the system manages everything."

## Impact If Built
Enables a 3-person company to scale from 30 shoots/week to 60 shoots/week without adding operational staff. Reduces turnaround time from 48 hours to 24 hours through automated processing and delivery triggers. Eliminates the 5% of deliveries that are late or missed due to communication failures, preventing agent churn worth $10K–$30K in annual revenue.
