# Wholesale Order Management for Micro-Roasters

**Niche:** [[niches/coffee-shops-independent/specialty-roaster-cafes/profile|Specialty Roaster-Cafes]]
**Industry:** [[industries/coffee-shops-independent|Independent Coffee Shops]]
**Type:** Buy & Customize (Vertical Adaptation)
**One-liner:** Generic B2B order management platforms exist but none handle the unique fulfillment cadence of freshly roasted coffee with resting windows and shelf-life constraints.
**Tags:** #automation #workflow-orchestration #data-integration #revenue-impact

## The Problem
A roaster-cafe with 15-40 wholesale accounts (restaurants, offices, grocery stores) manages orders through email, text messages, and a spreadsheet. Each account has different ordering cadences (weekly, biweekly, on-demand), different blend preferences, and different delivery windows. Fulfillment requires coordinating roast schedules with delivery routes — you can't ship beans the day they're roasted (they need to rest), and you can't ship them 10 days later (they're stale). This creates a 3-7 day fulfillment window that no generic order management system understands.

## What Already Exists
Shopify, WooCommerce, and general B2B platforms (Handshake, NuORDER) handle wholesale ordering but treat coffee like any shelf-stable product. They don't model roast-date freshness windows, resting times, or the constraint that wholesale orders must be batched with the roasting schedule to avoid waste. QuickBooks Commerce can track orders but has no production-planning integration.

## The Customization Gap
The system needs to understand that a wholesale order placed Monday for 20 lbs of Ethiopian single-origin requires roasting by Tuesday, resting until Thursday, and delivery by Friday — and that this order should be batched with the Tuesday retail roast of the same origin to optimize batch size. Date-driven freshness constraints, production batching logic, and roaster capacity limits are domain-specific rules that generic platforms ignore entirely.

## Target Customer
Specialty roaster-cafes with 10+ wholesale accounts generating $50K-$300K in annual wholesale revenue alongside their retail operation.

## Impact If Solved
Eliminates 3-5 hours per week of manual order coordination and reduces fulfillment errors (wrong blend, stale beans, missed delivery) that cost 5-10% of wholesale revenue annually through account churn.
