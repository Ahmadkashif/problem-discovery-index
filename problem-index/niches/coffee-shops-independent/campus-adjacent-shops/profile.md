# Campus-Adjacent College Town Shops

**Parent Industry:** [[industries/coffee-shops-independent|Independent Coffee Shops]]
**Category:** Low Digitized

## Profile
**Market Size:** $3-5B
**Share of Parent Industry:** ~8-10%
**Digital Adoption:** Low — Most run basic Square POS with no loyalty, no demand modeling, and no awareness of academic calendar impacts on revenue
**Target Buyer:** Owner serving a highly seasonal student population with wildly variable demand
**Automation Potential:** Medium — Academic calendar integration and seasonal demand modeling are straightforward but no one has built them

## What Makes This a Distinct Niche
Campus-adjacent shops experience demand volatility that no other coffee niche faces: revenue drops 40-60% during summer break, spikes 30% during finals week, and shifts hour-by-hour as class schedules change each semester. The customer base turns over every 4 years (graduation), making retention strategies fundamentally different from neighborhood cafes where regulars stay for decades. Payment preferences skew heavily toward mobile and campus card systems. The owner must plan inventory, staffing, and cash flow around an academic calendar that varies by institution and isn't modeled by any coffee-shop tool.

## Current Tools & Gaps
Square and Toast are the standard POS choices, but neither integrates with university academic calendars to forecast demand shifts. Student loyalty tools like Boost and LevelUp have largely pivoted to enterprise. Campus card payment systems (Transact, CBORD) require separate hardware and reconciliation. No tool helps these owners plan for the 60% summer revenue cliff or the finals-week surge.
