# Employee Handbook Generation and Maintenance

**Industry:** [[hr-consultants|HR Consultants]]
**Type:** Low Impact (Customisation Opportunity)
**One-liner:** Handbook templates exist everywhere, but none produce a state-compliant, client-specific, living document that updates itself when laws change.
**Tags:** #large-language-models #transformers #attention-mechanisms #word-embeddings #transfer-learning #evaluation-metrics #compliance

## The Problem
HR consultants create and maintain employee handbooks for every client — a task that consumes 6-15 hours per new client and 3-5 hours per annual update cycle. Each handbook must reflect the client's specific state(s) of operation, industry-specific regulations (DOT drug testing for transportation, HIPAA for healthcare), company size thresholds triggering specific policies, and the client's cultural preferences around PTO structure, remote work, and disciplinary procedures. Consultants typically start from a master template and manually edit sections, but the number of jurisdiction-dependent clauses (at-will employment disclaimers, meal break requirements, harassment training mandates) makes every handbook a custom legal document.

## What Already Exists
SHRM provides handbook templates with state-specific modules. SixFifty offers an interview-driven handbook builder that asks questions and generates jurisdiction-aware drafts. Rocket Lawyer and LegalZoom provide basic templates. Blissbook and AirMason handle formatting and digital distribution.

## The Customisation Gap
Existing tools generate a one-time snapshot but don't maintain the handbook as a living document — when California updates its meal break rules or New York expands its paid family leave, the consultant must manually identify which client handbooks are affected and which sections need revision. None of the existing tools integrate with the consultant's client registry to batch-update across 40 handbooks simultaneously. Industry-specific policy language (e.g., tip pooling rules for restaurant clients, safety equipment requirements for construction clients) is absent from generic builders. The tone and structure customization is surface-level — real handbooks need to reflect whether the client is a 12-person startup or a 200-person manufacturing firm with union considerations.

## Impact If Solved
Cuts handbook creation from 10+ hours to 1-2 hours of review per client, and transforms annual updates from a dreaded multi-week project into an automated batch process with consultant sign-off.
