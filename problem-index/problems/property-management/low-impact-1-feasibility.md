# Feasibility — Maintenance Request Triage and Vendor Dispatch Automation

**Industry:** [[property-management|Property Management]]
**Problem:** [[problems/property-management/low-impact-1|Maintenance Request Triage and Vendor Dispatch Automation]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Generic ticketing and workflow tools exist but don't model the property management-specific urgency criteria — habitability requirements, emergency vs. non-emergency classification under fair housing, and the contractor dispatch priorities that experienced property managers apply by judgment.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Combine property records, lease/maintenance data, and simple decision-support into the existing broker/manager stack.

## Open Source Data
Property records and permitting are often open; lease, rent roll, and maintenance histories are private.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
