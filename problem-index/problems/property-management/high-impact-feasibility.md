# Feasibility — Tenant Retention Prediction and Proactive Renewal Outreach

**Industry:** [[property-management|Property Management]]
**Problem:** [[problems/property-management/high-impact|Tenant Retention Prediction and Proactive Renewal Outreach]]
**Problem type:** High Impact

**Problem summary:** Property managers who rely on tenant renewal decisions made at the lease expiration date — with no early warning of which tenants are at risk of non-renewal — get a 90-day renewal probability score for every upcoming lease expiration, enabling proactive engagement that reduces vacancy turnover costs.

## Solution Approach
Stand up a focused pilot that integrates current systems, captures the missing signals, and adds analytics/ML once the data path is stable. Combine property records, lease/maintenance data, and simple decision-support into the existing broker/manager stack.

## Open Source Data
Property records and permitting are often open; lease, rent roll, and maintenance histories are private.

## Worth Solving?
High — core outcome/financial impact justifies investment if change management is handled.
