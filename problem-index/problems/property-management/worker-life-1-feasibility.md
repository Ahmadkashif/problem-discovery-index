# Feasibility — Tenant Communication Drafting and Response Automation

**Industry:** [[property-management|Property Management]]
**Problem:** [[problems/property-management/worker-life-1|Tenant Communication Drafting and Response Automation]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** Property managers who receive 50-100 emails and texts per day from tenants — questions about lease terms, maintenance status, payment plans, renewal inquiries — and spend 3-5 hours per day drafting individual responses get an AI drafting assistant that generates appropriate responses from the tenant's message and the property record, requiring only review and send.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Combine property records, lease/maintenance data, and simple decision-support into the existing broker/manager stack.

## Open Source Data
Property records and permitting are often open; lease, rent roll, and maintenance histories are private.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
