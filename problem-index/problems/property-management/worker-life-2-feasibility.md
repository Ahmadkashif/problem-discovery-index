# Feasibility — Move-Out Inspection Report Generation and Security Deposit Accounting

**Industry:** [[property-management|Property Management]]
**Problem:** [[problems/property-management/worker-life-2|Move-Out Inspection Report Generation and Security Deposit Accounting]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** Property managers who conduct move-out inspections, photograph damage, and then spend 45-60 minutes writing the itemized damage report and security deposit accounting letter — the document that must satisfy state security deposit law requirements — get the report generated from their inspection photos and damage notes.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Combine property records, lease/maintenance data, and simple decision-support into the existing broker/manager stack.

## Open Source Data
Property records and permitting are often open; lease, rent roll, and maintenance histories are private.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
