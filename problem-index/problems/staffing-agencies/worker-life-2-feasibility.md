# Feasibility — Onboarding Coordinator Document Chase

**Industry:** [[staffing-agencies|Staffing Agencies]]
**Problem:** [[problems/staffing-agencies/worker-life-2|Onboarding Coordinator Document Chase]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** Onboarding coordinators spend their entire week chasing I-9 documents, W-4 forms, background check authorizations, drug test appointments, and client-specific paperwork across 30–80 new starts — texting, calling, and emailing the same people repeatedly while the recruiter and client both pressure them to get workers on-site faster.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Tie into CRM/PM/billing, codify playbooks/templates, and automate alerts with clear human approval points.

## Open Source Data
Only benchmark reports are public; meaningful signals live in each firm's CRM, PM, and billing data.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
