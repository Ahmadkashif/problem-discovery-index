# Feasibility — Recruiter Call/Outreach Volume Pressure

**Industry:** [[staffing-agencies|Staffing Agencies]]
**Problem:** [[problems/staffing-agencies/worker-life-1|Recruiter Call/Outreach Volume Pressure]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** Recruiters make 60–100 cold calls per day to candidates who mostly don't answer, leave voicemails that are never returned, and face constant rejection — leading to burnout rates above 50% annually in an industry where the average recruiter tenure is 18 months.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Tie into CRM/PM/billing, codify playbooks/templates, and automate alerts with clear human approval points.

## Open Source Data
Only benchmark reports are public; meaningful signals live in each firm's CRM, PM, and billing data.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
