# Feasibility — Recruiter-to-Requisition Semantic Match Optimization

**Industry:** [[staffing-agencies|Staffing Agencies]]
**Problem:** [[problems/staffing-agencies/high-impact|Recruiter-to-Requisition Semantic Match Optimization]]
**Problem type:** High Impact

**Problem summary:** Recruiters stop burning 4+ hours daily on keyword searches that miss 80% of viable candidates because the ATS can finally understand that "forklift operator with 3 years warehouse experience" and "material handler — distribution center, certified reach truck" describe the same person.

## Solution Approach
Stand up a focused pilot that integrates current systems, captures the missing signals, and adds analytics/ML once the data path is stable. Tie into CRM/PM/billing, codify playbooks/templates, and automate alerts with clear human approval points.

## Open Source Data
Only benchmark reports are public; meaningful signals live in each firm's CRM, PM, and billing data.

## Worth Solving?
High — core outcome/financial impact justifies investment if change management is handled.
