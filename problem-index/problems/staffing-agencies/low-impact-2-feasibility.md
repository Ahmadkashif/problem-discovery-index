# Feasibility — Job Order Margin Estimation at Quote Time

**Industry:** [[staffing-agencies|Staffing Agencies]]
**Problem:** [[problems/staffing-agencies/low-impact-2|Job Order Margin Estimation at Quote Time]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** When an account manager quotes a bill rate to a client on the phone, they're guessing at the true margin because the real cost — burden rate, workers' comp class code, SUTA rate by state, ACA obligations, and client-specific payment terms — lives in five different systems and one senior person's head.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Tie into CRM/PM/billing, codify playbooks/templates, and automate alerts with clear human approval points.

## Open Source Data
Only benchmark reports are public; meaningful signals live in each firm's CRM, PM, and billing data.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
