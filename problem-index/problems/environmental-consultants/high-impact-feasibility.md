# Feasibility — Phase I/II ESA Report Generation and Regulatory Research Automation

**Industry:** [[environmental-consultants|Environmental Consultants]]
**Problem:** [[problems/environmental-consultants/high-impact|Phase I/II ESA Report Generation and Regulatory Research Automation]]
**Problem type:** High Impact

**Problem summary:** Environmental Site Assessment turnaround drops from 3-4 weeks to 3-4 days by automating the regulatory database research and boilerplate report assembly that consumes 70% of project hours.

## Solution Approach
Stand up a focused pilot that integrates current systems, captures the missing signals, and adds analytics/ML once the data path is stable. Tie into CRM/PM/billing, codify playbooks/templates, and automate alerts with clear human approval points.

## Open Source Data
Only benchmark reports are public; meaningful signals live in each firm's CRM, PM, and billing data.

## Worth Solving?
High — core outcome/financial impact justifies investment if change management is handled.
