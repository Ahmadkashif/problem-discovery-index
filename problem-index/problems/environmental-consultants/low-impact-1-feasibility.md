# Feasibility — Field Data Collection and Lab Result Integration

**Industry:** [[environmental-consultants|Environmental Consultants]]
**Problem:** [[problems/environmental-consultants/low-impact-1|Field Data Collection and Lab Result Integration]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Generic field data apps exist but none handle environmental-specific chain-of-custody workflows, lab analytical result import with automatic regulatory limit comparison, or exceedance flagging tied to site-specific cleanup standards.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Tie into CRM/PM/billing, codify playbooks/templates, and automate alerts with clear human approval points.

## Open Source Data
Only benchmark reports are public; meaningful signals live in each firm's CRM, PM, and billing data.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
