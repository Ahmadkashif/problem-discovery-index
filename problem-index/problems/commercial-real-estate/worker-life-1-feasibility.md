# Feasibility — Offering Memorandum Generation from Property Data

**Industry:** [[commercial-real-estate|Commercial Real Estate]]
**Problem:** [[problems/commercial-real-estate/worker-life-1|Offering Memorandum Generation from Property Data]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** CRE brokers who spend 15-25 hours assembling an investment property offering memorandum — compiling financial summaries, market comparables, property description, and investment highlights into a formatted PDF — get the OM generated from the property data inputs in 2-3 hours of review rather than 15-25 hours of assembly.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Combine property records, lease/maintenance data, and simple decision-support into the existing broker/manager stack.

## Open Source Data
Property records and permitting are often open; lease, rent roll, and maintenance histories are private.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
