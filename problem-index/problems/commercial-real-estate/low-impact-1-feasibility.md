# Feasibility — Lease Abstract Extraction and Rent Roll Analysis

**Industry:** [[commercial-real-estate|Commercial Real Estate]]
**Problem:** [[problems/commercial-real-estate/low-impact-1|Lease Abstract Extraction and Rent Roll Analysis]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Generic document parsing tools can extract text from PDF leases but don't understand CRE-specific lease structure — rent escalations, CAM reconciliation, co-tenancy clauses, options to renew, and termination provisions — the fields that determine the economic risk and value of a commercial property's rent roll.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Combine property records, lease/maintenance data, and simple decision-support into the existing broker/manager stack.

## Open Source Data
Property records and permitting are often open; lease, rent roll, and maintenance histories are private.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
