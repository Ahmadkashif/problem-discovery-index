# Feasibility — Pipeline and Client Relationship Intelligence for Brokers

**Industry:** [[commercial-real-estate|Commercial Real Estate]]
**Problem:** [[problems/commercial-real-estate/worker-life-2|Pipeline and Client Relationship Intelligence for Brokers]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** CRE brokers who manage 20-40 active client and deal relationships across 12-24 month deal cycles — without a CRM discipline — and routinely lose deals because they didn't follow up at the right moment get an automated relationship intelligence layer that surfaces which clients need attention this week and why.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Combine property records, lease/maintenance data, and simple decision-support into the existing broker/manager stack.

## Open Source Data
Property records and permitting are often open; lease, rent roll, and maintenance histories are private.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
