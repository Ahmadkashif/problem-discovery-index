# Feasibility — Tenant Prospect Matching and Market Expansion Prediction

**Industry:** [[commercial-real-estate|Commercial Real Estate]]
**Problem:** [[problems/commercial-real-estate/low-impact-2|Tenant Prospect Matching and Market Expansion Prediction]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Generic business data platforms can identify companies that may be looking for space, but the CRE-specific signal — which companies are experiencing the employment growth, lease expiration timing, and industry expansion patterns that indicate an active space search — requires a CRE-specific model built on occupier behavior data.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Combine property records, lease/maintenance data, and simple decision-support into the existing broker/manager stack.

## Open Source Data
Property records and permitting are often open; lease, rent roll, and maintenance histories are private.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
