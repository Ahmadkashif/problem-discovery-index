# Feasibility — Commercial Property Valuation and Cap Rate Intelligence from Transaction Data

**Industry:** [[commercial-real-estate|Commercial Real Estate]]
**Problem:** [[problems/commercial-real-estate/high-impact|Commercial Property Valuation and Cap Rate Intelligence from Transaction Data]]
**Problem type:** High Impact

**Problem summary:** Small-to-mid-sized CRE brokers who rely on expensive CoStar subscriptions and manual comp searches to value properties and identify pricing signals — information that institutional brokers access through sophisticated analytics platforms — get an affordable, automated comparable transaction analysis and valuation model calibrated to their specific submarket and asset type focus.

## Solution Approach
Stand up a focused pilot that integrates current systems, captures the missing signals, and adds analytics/ML once the data path is stable. Combine property records, lease/maintenance data, and simple decision-support into the existing broker/manager stack.

## Open Source Data
Property records and permitting are often open; lease, rent roll, and maintenance histories are private.

## Worth Solving?
High — core outcome/financial impact justifies investment if change management is handled.
