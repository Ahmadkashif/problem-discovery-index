# Feasibility — Service Manager Timesheet and SLA Reporting Drudgery

**Industry:** [[it-managed-services|IT Managed Services]]
**Problem:** [[problems/it-managed-services/worker-life-2|Service Manager Timesheet and SLA Reporting Drudgery]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** Service managers spend 4-6 hours every week wrestling PSA data into client-facing SLA reports and internal utilization dashboards, time that should go to coaching technicians and improving service delivery.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Tie into CRM/PM/billing, codify playbooks/templates, and automate alerts with clear human approval points.

## Open Source Data
Only benchmark reports are public; meaningful signals live in each firm's CRM, PM, and billing data.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
