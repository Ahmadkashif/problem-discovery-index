# Feasibility — Estate Administration Coordination and Task Tracking

**Industry:** [[estate-planning|Estate Planning]]
**Problem:** [[problems/estate-planning/worker-life-2|Estate Administration Coordination and Task Tracking]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** Estate administration paralegals who manually track 40-60 open tasks per estate — contacting financial institutions, recording assets, filing tax returns, distributing to beneficiaries — across multiple simultaneous administrations get a structured workflow system that surfaces the right task at the right time and tracks every correspondence automatically.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Integrate with case management, add retrieval over statutes/precedents, and ship automation with human oversight.

## Open Source Data
Public statutes and filings are open; matter notes, client data, and outcomes are proprietary, so training/grounding must use firm data.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
