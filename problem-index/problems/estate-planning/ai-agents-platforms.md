## 1. Proactive Client Relationship Intelligence Platform
#ai-platform #tabular-ml #revenue-impact

**Concept:** A client monitoring and relationship management platform purpose-built for estate planning firms — continuously monitoring the existing client base for plan update triggers (life events, tax law changes, plan age), generating prioritized re-engagement recommendations, and automating outreach with personalized, attorney-reviewed communications. Transforms the firm's dormant client file into an active recurring revenue relationship.
**Inputs:** Case management system client data feed, public records monitoring APIs (vital records, marriage/divorce records, business registry changes), IRS and state revenue department regulatory monitoring feeds, annual client wellness questionnaire responses
**Outputs / Actions:** Weekly prioritized re-engagement list with specific trigger reason and recommended engagement type; auto-drafted outreach email for attorney review and send; tax law change impact report with affected client list; annual plan review invitation campaign; client lifetime value analysis by plan type and engagement history
**Why now:** Public records APIs now provide programmatic access to vital records in most states. LLMs generate personalized, attorney-voice outreach communications reliably. Estate planning firms are leaving substantial revenue on the table from dormant client relationships — the problem is systematic, and the technology to address it now exists.
**Market:** 30,000+ estate planning attorneys in the US. Boutique firms with 200-1,000 active client files are the target — they have the dormant relationship problem most acutely and can't solve it without automation. Platform pricing at $300-800/month per firm has obvious ROI against even 2-3 additional update engagements per month.

## 2. Conversational Estate Planning Intake Agent
#ai-agent #llm #nlp

**Concept:** A client-facing conversational agent that conducts the structured data-gathering phase of an estate planning intake — walking the client through their family structure, asset inventory, and planning objectives in a guided conversation before the attorney meeting — and produces a structured intake brief that populates directly into the document assembly system.
**Inputs:** Client-initiated conversation via secure web portal or app, structured question trees for different family/asset configurations, legal knowledge base for explaining estate planning concepts in plain language
**Outputs / Actions:** Structured intake brief: family structure map, asset inventory by category, beneficiary designations on non-probate assets, planning objectives, questions and concerns for attorney discussion; direct population of document assembly software (Wealth Counsel, HotDocs) fields from intake data; attorney meeting preparation brief identifying key planning decisions for the meeting
**Why now:** LLM-powered conversational data collection is now reliable enough for structured intake tasks. The estate planning intake is ideally suited to a guided conversational format — it is a known question set with conditional logic, not an open-ended advisory discussion. The AI handles the information gathering; the attorney handles the strategy.
**Market:** Estate planning attorneys who currently spend 60-90 minutes of meeting time gathering information before they can do any planning. A tool that makes the intake meeting more efficient — so the attorney focuses on strategy rather than fact-gathering — has immediate value to every estate planning practice.

## 3. Estate Administration Workflow Agent
#ai-agent #automation #workflow-orchestration

**Concept:** An autonomous workflow agent that manages the estate administration process from opening to closing — generating the complete task checklist, tracking correspondence with financial institutions, generating required notices to beneficiaries and creditors, monitoring tax filing deadlines, and surfacing the daily priority work list for the administering paralegal.
**Inputs:** Estate intake information (decedent, asset inventory, beneficiary list, executor identity), jurisdiction (state probate rules), case management system integration for task logging
**Outputs / Actions:** Auto-generated administration task checklist with statutory deadlines; financial institution correspondence templates pre-populated for each institution; automated deadline reminders at configurable advance intervals; beneficiary notification letters (Notice of Administration, inventory notice, distribution notice) generated from templates; executor status report generated monthly; escalation alerts for overdue tasks or approaching deadlines
**Why now:** Estate administration tasks are highly templated — the same sequence of steps applies to most administrations, with jurisdiction-specific variations. An agent that knows the sequence, knows the deadlines, and generates the correspondence reduces the paralegal's role from coordinator to exception manager. LLM document generation handles the correspondence templates; workflow automation handles the task sequencing.
**Market:** Estate planning firms that also handle estate administration — which is most of them. Administration generates 30-40% of total firm revenue at many boutique estate planning practices. An agent that increases capacity per paralegal by 30-40% directly expands margin.

## 4. Estate Tax Analysis and Planning Scenario Agent
#ai-agent #tabular-ml #llm #revenue-impact

**Concept:** An AI planning assistant for the estate attorney that — given a client's asset structure and family situation — runs multiple estate and gift tax scenarios (gifting strategies, GRAT structures, SLAT trusts, charitable planning combinations) and produces a comparative analysis showing projected estate tax liability and net-to-heirs for each scenario, in language the attorney can present directly to the client.
**Inputs:** Client asset inventory with values and basis, family structure (ages of beneficiaries), applicable exclusion amount (current + sunset scenario), state estate tax threshold, income and liquidity needs, charitable intentions
**Outputs / Actions:** Side-by-side tax scenario comparison (do nothing vs. annual gifting vs. GRAT series vs. SLAT vs. charitable trust options); projected estate tax at death under each scenario; net-to-heirs calculation; recommended strategy with primary rationale; client-ready narrative summary the attorney can use in the planning meeting
**Why now:** Estate tax modeling is fundamentally arithmetic on structured inputs — an area where AI augments the attorney's analytical capacity without requiring legal judgment in the output. The 2025 TCJA sunset makes estate tax planning urgency extremely high for wealthy clients, making this a particularly timely product.
**Market:** Estate planning attorneys advising clients with taxable estates (currently >$13M federal; lower thresholds in 18 states). Tax planning analysis is a premium engagement — attorneys charge $3,000-10,000 for comprehensive tax planning. A tool that reduces the analysis preparation from 4-6 hours to 30-minute review justifies premium pricing at significant volume.
