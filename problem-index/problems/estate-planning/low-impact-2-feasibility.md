# Feasibility — Asset Inventory Aggregation for Trust Funding Verification

**Industry:** [[estate-planning|Estate Planning]]
**Problem:** [[problems/estate-planning/low-impact-2|Asset Inventory Aggregation for Trust Funding Verification]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Financial account aggregation tools exist for wealth management but aren't purpose-built for the estate planning use case — where the goal is confirming that revocable trust funding is complete and that all non-probate beneficiary designations align with the client's overall plan.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Integrate with case management, add retrieval over statutes/precedents, and ship automation with human oversight.

## Open Source Data
Public statutes and filings are open; matter notes, client data, and outcomes are proprietary, so training/grounding must use firm data.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
