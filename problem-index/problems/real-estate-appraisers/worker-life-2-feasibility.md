# Feasibility — Market Commentary and Neighborhood Analysis Generation

**Industry:** [[real-estate-appraisers|Real Estate Appraisers]]
**Problem:** [[problems/real-estate-appraisers/worker-life-2|Market Commentary and Neighborhood Analysis Generation]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** Appraisers who write the same neighborhood and market conditions commentary for every appraisal in their coverage area — the required USPAP sections describing supply/demand balance, marketing time, price trends, and neighborhood characteristics — get that section auto-generated from the current MLS data for the subject's submarket, updated to reflect current market conditions.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Combine property records, lease/maintenance data, and simple decision-support into the existing broker/manager stack.

## Open Source Data
Property records and permitting are often open; lease, rent roll, and maintenance histories are private.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
