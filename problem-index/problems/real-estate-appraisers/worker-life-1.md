# Floor Plan Sketch Generation from Exterior Measurements

**Industry:** [[real-estate-appraisers|Real Estate Appraisers]]
**Type:** Worker Life Changing
**One-liner:** Appraisers who measure a house's exterior dimensions during the field inspection — tape measure or laser measure, walking the perimeter — and then spend 20-30 minutes in Apex Sketch recreating the floor plan from their field notes get that floor plan auto-generated from photos of the property and exterior measurements, ready to review and submit.
**Tags:** #computer-vision #automation #worker-facing #multimodal

## The Problem

Gross Living Area (GLA) calculation requires an accurate floor plan sketch showing all exterior dimensions, room configurations, and level-by-level area breakdown. Appraisers manually measure exterior dimensions during the site inspection, record them on paper, and then reproduce the floor plan in Apex Sketch or similar software back at the office or in their car. This drafting step takes 20-30 minutes for a simple rectangular ranch; 45-60 minutes for a multi-story or complex footprint.

The sketching task is purely mechanical — translating measurements into a scaled drawing. It adds no analytical value to the appraisal but consumes a significant fraction of the total time per assignment.

## Why It Matters to the Worker

For an appraiser doing 4-5 field inspections per day, sketch preparation consumes 80-150 minutes of post-field time — the single largest time block in report preparation. Reducing this to a 5-minute review would recover 1-2 hours per day. At the current AMC fee levels of $250-$350 per report, time recovery directly determines income per hour.

## What a Solution Looks Like

A mobile app where the appraiser: inputs exterior measurements during the walkthrough (voice input or keyboard, room by room); photographs each elevation; marks the number of stories and any step-backs or complex footprint features via a quick tap interface. The app generates a preliminary sketch in Apex Sketch-compatible format for review and correction. Alternatively, LiDAR-equipped phones (iPhone 12 Pro+) can estimate exterior dimensions directly — a combination of LiDAR measurement + room photo interpretation could eliminate the tape measure step entirely for many property types.

## Impact If Solved

Returns 1-2 hours per day for a full-time appraiser. At 4-5 assignments per day, this adds 1-2 additional assignments per week at equivalent income, or enables the same number of assignments with significantly reduced work hours — the primary quality-of-life improvement requested by residential appraisers.
