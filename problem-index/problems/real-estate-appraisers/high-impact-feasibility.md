# Feasibility — Market-Calibrated Comparable Adjustment Development from Matched-Pair Sales

**Industry:** [[real-estate-appraisers|Real Estate Appraisers]]
**Problem:** [[problems/real-estate-appraisers/high-impact|Market-Calibrated Comparable Adjustment Development from Matched-Pair Sales]]
**Problem type:** High Impact

**Problem summary:** Residential appraisers who develop supportable dollar adjustments for property differences (bedroom count, bathroom count, garage, condition, location) through subjective market experience and matched-pair analysis — a process that takes experienced appraisers years to calibrate and junior appraisers routinely get wrong — get a market-calibrated adjustment model derived from the local MLS sales data that reflects how buyers in that specific market actually price each property attribute.

## Solution Approach
Stand up a focused pilot that integrates current systems, captures the missing signals, and adds analytics/ML once the data path is stable. Combine property records, lease/maintenance data, and simple decision-support into the existing broker/manager stack.

## Open Source Data
Property records and permitting are often open; lease, rent roll, and maintenance histories are private.

## Worth Solving?
High — core outcome/financial impact justifies investment if change management is handled.
