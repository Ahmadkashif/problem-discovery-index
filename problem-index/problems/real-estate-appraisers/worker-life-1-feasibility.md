# Feasibility — Floor Plan Sketch Generation from Exterior Measurements

**Industry:** [[real-estate-appraisers|Real Estate Appraisers]]
**Problem:** [[problems/real-estate-appraisers/worker-life-1|Floor Plan Sketch Generation from Exterior Measurements]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** Appraisers who measure a house's exterior dimensions during the field inspection — tape measure or laser measure, walking the perimeter — and then spend 20-30 minutes in Apex Sketch recreating the floor plan from their field notes get that floor plan auto-generated from photos of the property and exterior measurements, ready to review and submit.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Combine property records, lease/maintenance data, and simple decision-support into the existing broker/manager stack.

## Open Source Data
Property records and permitting are often open; lease, rent roll, and maintenance histories are private.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
