# Feasibility — Comparable Sale Selection Assistance

**Industry:** [[real-estate-appraisers|Real Estate Appraisers]]
**Problem:** [[problems/real-estate-appraisers/low-impact-1|Comparable Sale Selection Assistance]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** MLS search tools let appraisers filter and sort sales but don't rank comparables by overall similarity to the subject property — the multi-attribute similarity judgment that experienced appraisers make intuitively to select the three best comparables from 20 candidates.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Combine property records, lease/maintenance data, and simple decision-support into the existing broker/manager stack.

## Open Source Data
Property records and permitting are often open; lease, rent roll, and maintenance histories are private.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
