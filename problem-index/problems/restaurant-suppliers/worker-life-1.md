# Sales Rep Route Planning and Visit Prioritization

**Industry:** [[restaurant-suppliers|Restaurant Suppliers]]
**Type:** Worker Life Changing
**One-liner:** Reps get back 5-8 hours per week currently spent driving to accounts that don't need a visit while missing the ones that do.
**Tags:** #gradient-boosting #decision-trees #feature-engineering #cross-validation #evaluation-metrics #optimization-fundamentals #gradient-descent #worker-facing

## The Problem
A typical restaurant supply sales rep drives 200-300 miles per week visiting 15-25 accounts across a metro area. Every Monday morning, they decide which accounts to visit based on habit, personal relationship, and whoever called last week. There is no data-driven input: a rep may drive 45 minutes to visit a loyal $2,000/week account that would have ordered anyway, while skipping a $5,000/week account showing early signs of reduced ordering. Route planning is done mentally or with a basic Google Maps multi-stop — there is no integration with account health, order recency, or margin data.

## Why It Matters to the Worker
Sales reps in food distribution are typically paid base + commission, earning $55K-$85K depending on territory. Their income is directly tied to account retention and growth, but they have no tools to help them allocate their most constrained resource: windshield time. A rep who spends Tuesday driving to three accounts that didn't need a visit has permanently lost the opportunity to visit the two accounts that were about to churn. The mental load of juggling 100+ accounts with no system leads to chronic anxiety about "the account I forgot" and burnout from 10-hour days that feel unproductive.

## What a Solution Looks Like
A mobile app that generates a prioritized daily visit list each morning, combining account churn risk scores, days since last visit, open order issues, margin opportunity (accounts buying only 2 of 5 potential categories), and geographic clustering. The rep sees: "Visit these 6 accounts today in this order. Tony's Pizzeria is flagged — protein orders dropped 35% this month. Bella Cucina hasn't been visited in 22 days and has a $1,200 credit issue." The rep can override any recommendation but starts each day with a data-informed plan instead of a blank windshield.

## Impact If Solved
Reps reclaim 5-8 hours per week in wasted drive time, convert that to 3-5 additional high-value visits, and reduce the mental burden of account prioritization. At the territory level, this translates to 10-15% better account retention and $50K-$100K in incremental annual revenue per rep from better-targeted visits.
