# Feasibility — AI-Assisted Hail and Wind Damage Assessment from Roof Inspection Photos

**Industry:** [[roofing-contractors|Roofing Contractors]]
**Problem:** [[problems/roofing-contractors/high-impact|AI-Assisted Hail and Wind Damage Assessment from Roof Inspection Photos]]
**Problem type:** High Impact

**Problem summary:** Experienced roofing inspectors who identify storm damage by reading hail impact patterns, granule loss, and wind lift signatures on shingles — pattern recognition built over thousands of roof inspections — get that visual expertise replicated by a computer vision model that classifies damage from photos, enabling junior inspectors to produce adjuster-quality assessments on the first visit.

## Solution Approach
Stand up a focused pilot that integrates current systems, captures the missing signals, and adds analytics/ML once the data path is stable. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
High — core outcome/financial impact justifies investment if change management is handled.
