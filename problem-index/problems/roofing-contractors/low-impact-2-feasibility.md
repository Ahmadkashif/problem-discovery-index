# Feasibility — Material Takeoff and Waste Factor Calculation from Aerial Measurement Reports

**Industry:** [[roofing-contractors|Roofing Contractors]]
**Problem:** [[problems/roofing-contractors/low-impact-2|Material Takeoff and Waste Factor Calculation from Aerial Measurement Reports]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** EagleView and Nearmap provide accurate roof geometry measurements, but converting those measurements into a materials order with correct waste factors for each plane, hip, ridge, and valley configuration — accounting for the contractor's specific installation method and shingle product — requires a roofing-specific calculation layer that general estimating tools don't provide.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
