# Feasibility — Job Photo Organization and Labeling Automation

**Industry:** [[roofing-contractors|Roofing Contractors]]
**Problem:** [[problems/roofing-contractors/worker-life-1|Job Photo Organization and Labeling Automation]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** Roofing crews who take 80–150 photos per job across inspection, installation progress, and completion documentation — and then spend 20-30 minutes organizing them into labeled folders by phase and roof zone — get that organization done automatically from the photo metadata and visual content.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
