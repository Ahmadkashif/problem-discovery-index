# Feasibility — Lead Qualification Scoring for Long Solar Sales Cycles

**Industry:** [[solar-installers|Solar Installers]]
**Problem:** [[problems/solar-installers/low-impact-1|Lead Qualification Scoring for Long Solar Sales Cycles]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Generic CRM lead scoring tools exist but don't model the solar-specific signals that predict whether a lead will close — homeownership, roof age, south-facing roof area, current electricity bill, shade conditions, local utility rate trajectory, and the customer's decision timeline for a $20,000 purchase.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
