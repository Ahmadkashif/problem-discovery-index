# Feasibility — Site Survey Automation and Structural Assessment from Photos

**Industry:** [[solar-installers|Solar Installers]]
**Problem:** [[problems/solar-installers/worker-life-1|Site Survey Automation and Structural Assessment from Photos]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** Solar site surveyors who photograph roof conditions, measure setbacks, document electrical panel specs, and assess structural suitability in a 45-60 minute site visit — then spend another 30-45 minutes writing up the survey report — get the survey report auto-generated from their photos and measurements, ready for the design team before the surveyor drives away.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
