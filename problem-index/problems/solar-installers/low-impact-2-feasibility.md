# Feasibility — Interconnection Queue Wait Time Prediction by Utility Territory

**Industry:** [[solar-installers|Solar Installers]]
**Problem:** [[problems/solar-installers/low-impact-2|Interconnection Queue Wait Time Prediction by Utility Territory]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Generic project timeline tools don't model the utility interconnection queue — the primary source of timeline uncertainty in solar projects — which varies by utility, queue backlog, and project size in ways that only firms with extensive local utility experience can estimate accurately.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
