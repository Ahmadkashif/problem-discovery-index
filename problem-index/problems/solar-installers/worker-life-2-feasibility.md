# Feasibility — System Performance Anomaly Detection and Proactive Customer Communication

**Industry:** [[solar-installers|Solar Installers]]
**Problem:** [[problems/solar-installers/worker-life-2|System Performance Anomaly Detection and Proactive Customer Communication]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** Solar installers whose monitoring platforms (SolarEdge, Enphase) show hundreds of installed systems but have no automated alert system for underperforming systems — meaning customers notice the underperformance months before the installer does — get proactive anomaly alerts and auto-drafted customer communication before the customer calls to complain.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
