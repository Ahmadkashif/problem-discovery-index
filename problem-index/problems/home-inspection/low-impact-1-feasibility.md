# Feasibility — Automated Report Narrative Generation from Photo and Voice Notes

**Industry:** [[home-inspection|Home Inspection]]
**Problem:** [[problems/home-inspection/low-impact-1|Automated Report Narrative Generation from Photo and Voice Notes]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Generic voice-to-text and LLM writing tools exist but don't understand InterNACHI/ASHI report standards, the distinction between deficient and safety-hazard severity levels, or the specific language patterns that real estate agents and buyers expect in a professional inspection report.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
