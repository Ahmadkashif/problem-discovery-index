# Feasibility — Defect Detection and Severity Classification from Inspection Photos

**Industry:** [[home-inspection|Home Inspection]]
**Problem:** [[problems/home-inspection/high-impact|Defect Detection and Severity Classification from Inspection Photos]]
**Problem type:** High Impact

**Problem summary:** Experienced home inspectors who identify defects across 12+ home systems by reading subtle visual evidence — hairline cracks in foundation walls, double-tapped breakers in panels, improper flashing patterns on roofs — get that multi-system visual expertise augmented by a computer vision model that flags potential defects in their inspection photos, reducing the miss rate for non-obvious defects.

## Solution Approach
Stand up a focused pilot that integrates current systems, captures the missing signals, and adds analytics/ML once the data path is stable. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
High — core outcome/financial impact justifies investment if change management is handled.
