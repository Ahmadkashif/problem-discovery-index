# Feasibility — Irrigation System Fault Identification from Zone-by-Zone Walk

**Industry:** [[landscaping|Landscaping]]
**Problem:** [[problems/landscaping/worker-life-1|Irrigation System Fault Identification from Zone-by-Zone Walk]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** Irrigation service technicians who diagnose system faults by running each zone and observing head coverage, pressure, and surface patterns — a diagnosis that takes 45-60 minutes on an unfamiliar system — get a guided fault identification workflow that captures observations zone-by-zone and returns a structured repair list and parts list before they leave the walkthrough.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
