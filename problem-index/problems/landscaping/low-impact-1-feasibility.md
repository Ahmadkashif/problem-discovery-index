# Feasibility — Route Optimization for Multi-Stop Lawn Care Days

**Industry:** [[landscaping|Landscaping]]
**Problem:** [[problems/landscaping/low-impact-1|Route Optimization for Multi-Stop Lawn Care Days]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Generic route optimization tools (Google Maps, routific) solve the traveling salesman problem for stop sequences but don't understand lawn care-specific constraints: crew productivity by property size, gate access requirements, scheduled vs. on-demand stops, mowing day restrictions by HOA rule, and the crew truck's equipment configuration affecting which properties it can service.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
