# Feasibility — Guest Inquiry Response and Automated Messaging

**Industry:** [[short-term-rentals|Short-Term Rentals]]
**Problem:** [[problems/short-term-rentals/worker-life-2|Guest Inquiry Response and Automated Messaging]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** STR property managers who respond to 30-80 guest messages per day — pre-booking inquiries, check-in instructions, mid-stay questions, checkout reminders — and spend 1-2 hours daily on routine guest communication get that communication handled automatically, with only escalations and unusual requests requiring human response.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Combine property records, lease/maintenance data, and simple decision-support into the existing broker/manager stack.

## Open Source Data
Property records and permitting are often open; lease, rent roll, and maintenance histories are private.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
