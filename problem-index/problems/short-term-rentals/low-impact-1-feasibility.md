# Feasibility — Guest Screening and Problem Booking Detection

**Industry:** [[short-term-rentals|Short-Term Rentals]]
**Problem:** [[problems/short-term-rentals/low-impact-1|Guest Screening and Problem Booking Detection]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Generic identity verification tools confirm who a guest is, but don't predict which bookings are likely to result in parties, property damage, or rule violations — the behavioral signals in the booking pattern and communication that experienced hosts recognize before check-in.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Combine property records, lease/maintenance data, and simple decision-support into the existing broker/manager stack.

## Open Source Data
Property records and permitting are often open; lease, rent roll, and maintenance histories are private.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
