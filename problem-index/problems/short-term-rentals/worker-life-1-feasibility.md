# Feasibility — Turnover Coordination and Cleaner Dispatch Automation

**Industry:** [[short-term-rentals|Short-Term Rentals]]
**Problem:** [[problems/short-term-rentals/worker-life-1|Turnover Coordination and Cleaner Dispatch Automation]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** STR property managers who manually text cleaners every morning with that day's checkout/checkin schedule — a task that takes 20-40 minutes of coordination per day across a multi-property portfolio — get automated turnover dispatch based on the booking calendar, with linen logistics and issue reporting built in.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Combine property records, lease/maintenance data, and simple decision-support into the existing broker/manager stack.

## Open Source Data
Property records and permitting are often open; lease, rent roll, and maintenance histories are private.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
