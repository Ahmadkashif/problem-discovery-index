# Feasibility — Listing Quality Optimization and Review Score Prediction

**Industry:** [[short-term-rentals|Short-Term Rentals]]
**Problem:** [[problems/short-term-rentals/low-impact-2|Listing Quality Optimization and Review Score Prediction]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Generic copywriting tools can improve listing descriptions, but don't understand the specific Airbnb/Vrbo search ranking signals, the photo ordering and quality standards that affect conversion, or the relationship between listing attributes and the specific review scores (cleanliness, location, value) that determine visibility in the platform's search algorithm.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Combine property records, lease/maintenance data, and simple decision-support into the existing broker/manager stack.

## Open Source Data
Property records and permitting are often open; lease, rent roll, and maintenance histories are private.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
