# Feasibility — Dynamic Pricing Optimization Beyond Rule-Based Algorithms

**Industry:** [[short-term-rentals|Short-Term Rentals]]
**Problem:** [[problems/short-term-rentals/high-impact|Dynamic Pricing Optimization Beyond Rule-Based Algorithms]]
**Problem type:** High Impact

**Problem summary:** STR operators using PriceLabs or Beyond Pricing who know their algorithmic tool is leaving money on the table during local events, holidays, and micro-demand shifts — but lack the time or data to consistently override it manually — get a reinforcement learning pricing agent that learns from each property's booking history and local demand signals to outperform static rule-based dynamic pricing.

## Solution Approach
Stand up a focused pilot that integrates current systems, captures the missing signals, and adds analytics/ML once the data path is stable. Combine property records, lease/maintenance data, and simple decision-support into the existing broker/manager stack.

## Open Source Data
Property records and permitting are often open; lease, rent roll, and maintenance histories are private.

## Worth Solving?
High — core outcome/financial impact justifies investment if change management is handled.
