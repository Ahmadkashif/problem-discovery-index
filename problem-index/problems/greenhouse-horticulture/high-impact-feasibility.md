# Feasibility — Greenhouse Climate Control Optimization for Energy and Crop Quality

**Industry:** [[greenhouse-horticulture|Greenhouse Horticulture]]
**Problem:** [[problems/greenhouse-horticulture/high-impact|Greenhouse Climate Control Optimization for Energy and Crop Quality]]
**Problem type:** High Impact

**Problem summary:** A reinforcement learning system that continuously adjusts greenhouse climate setpoints to minimize energy cost while maximizing crop quality and yield.

## Solution Approach
Stand up a focused pilot that integrates current systems, captures the missing signals, and adds analytics/ML once the data path is stable. Blend field/plant logs with weather/soil inputs and deliver simple decision-support that works offline.

## Open Source Data
NOAA/USDA weather and agronomic data are open; field logs, inputs, and yield histories come from growers/processors.

## Worth Solving?
High — core outcome/financial impact justifies investment if change management is handled.
