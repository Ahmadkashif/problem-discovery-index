# Feasibility — Crop Scheduling and Space Planning for Multi-Crop Greenhouses

**Industry:** [[greenhouse-horticulture|Greenhouse Horticulture]]
**Problem:** [[problems/greenhouse-horticulture/low-impact-1|Crop Scheduling and Space Planning for Multi-Crop Greenhouses]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Generic project management tools cannot model greenhouse-specific constraints like bench space allocation, light zone requirements, propagation-to-finish transitions, and temperature compartment sharing.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Blend field/plant logs with weather/soil inputs and deliver simple decision-support that works offline.

## Open Source Data
NOAA/USDA weather and agronomic data are open; field logs, inputs, and yield histories come from growers/processors.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
