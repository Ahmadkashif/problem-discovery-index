# Feasibility — Chart of Accounts Standardization Across Client Base

**Industry:** [[accounting-firms-smb|SMB Accounting Firms]]
**Problem:** [[problems/accounting-firms-smb/low-impact-1|Chart of Accounts Standardization Across Client Base]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Firms can finally benchmark clients against industry peers and deliver advisory insights without spending hours manually mapping each client's idiosyncratic chart of accounts.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Tie into CRM/PM/billing, codify playbooks/templates, and automate alerts with clear human approval points.

## Open Source Data
Only benchmark reports are public; meaningful signals live in each firm's CRM, PM, and billing data.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
