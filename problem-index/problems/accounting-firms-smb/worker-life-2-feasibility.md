# Feasibility — Bookkeeper Transaction Categorization Tedium

**Industry:** [[accounting-firms-smb|SMB Accounting Firms]]
**Problem:** [[problems/accounting-firms-smb/worker-life-2|Bookkeeper Transaction Categorization Tedium]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** Bookkeepers stop spending 3-4 hours per client per month on rote transaction categorization and shift to exception handling and client communication.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Tie into CRM/PM/billing, codify playbooks/templates, and automate alerts with clear human approval points.

## Open Source Data
Only benchmark reports are public; meaningful signals live in each firm's CRM, PM, and billing data.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
