# Feasibility — Campaign Performance Attribution Across Channels for SMB Clients

**Industry:** [[marketing-agencies-smb|SMB Marketing Agencies]]
**Problem:** [[problems/marketing-agencies-smb/high-impact|Campaign Performance Attribution Across Channels for SMB Clients]]
**Problem type:** High Impact

**Problem summary:** Agencies can finally prove to a $10K/month client exactly which channel mix drove their conversions, ending the "is this working?" conversation that causes 30% of churn.

## Solution Approach
Stand up a focused pilot that integrates current systems, captures the missing signals, and adds analytics/ML once the data path is stable. Tie into CRM/PM/billing, codify playbooks/templates, and automate alerts with clear human approval points.

## Open Source Data
Only benchmark reports are public; meaningful signals live in each firm's CRM, PM, and billing data.

## Worth Solving?
High — core outcome/financial impact justifies investment if change management is handled.
