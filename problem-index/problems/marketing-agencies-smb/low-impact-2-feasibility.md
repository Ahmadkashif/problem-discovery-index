# Feasibility — Project Scoping and SOW Generation from Client Briefs

**Industry:** [[marketing-agencies-smb|SMB Marketing Agencies]]
**Problem:** [[problems/marketing-agencies-smb/low-impact-2|Project Scoping and SOW Generation from Client Briefs]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Generic PM tools track tasks once scoped, but can't estimate that a "rebrand package" for a 3-location dental practice will take 47 hours across design, copy, and web — so agencies underbid by 20-30% and absorb the margin hit.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Tie into CRM/PM/billing, codify playbooks/templates, and automate alerts with clear human approval points.

## Open Source Data
Only benchmark reports are public; meaningful signals live in each firm's CRM, PM, and billing data.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
