# Feasibility — Automated Client Status Updates and Communication Management

**Industry:** [[immigration-law|Immigration Law Firms]]
**Problem:** [[problems/immigration-law/worker-life-1|Automated Client Status Updates and Communication Management]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** Immigration paralegals who spend 2-3 hours per day responding to "what's the status of my case?" calls and emails — from clients who are often dealing with work authorization gaps, visa expirations, and family separation anxieties — get that time back while clients actually receive more frequent and more informative updates.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Integrate with case management, add retrieval over statutes/precedents, and ship automation with human oversight.

## Open Source Data
Public statutes and filings are open; matter notes, client data, and outcomes are proprietary, so training/grounding must use firm data.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
