# Feasibility — Refrigerant Recovery and Charge Tracking Compliance

**Industry:** [[hvac-contractors|HVAC Contractors]]
**Problem:** [[problems/hvac-contractors/worker-life-2|Refrigerant Recovery and Charge Tracking Compliance]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** HVAC technicians handling refrigerants who are required by EPA Section 608 to log every refrigerant recovery and charge event — recording equipment identifier, refrigerant type, amount recovered/added, and technician certification number — get that logging done automatically from the service call data, eliminating a compliance documentation task that takes 5-10 minutes per call and is frequently done incorrectly.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
