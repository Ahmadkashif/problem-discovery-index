# Feasibility — Refrigerant System Diagnosis from Manifold Gauge Readings

**Industry:** [[hvac-contractors|HVAC Contractors]]
**Problem:** [[problems/hvac-contractors/high-impact|Refrigerant System Diagnosis from Manifold Gauge Readings]]
**Problem type:** High Impact

**Problem summary:** Experienced HVAC technicians who diagnose refrigerant system faults by reading the interplay of suction pressure, discharge pressure, superheat, and subcooling measurements — a multi-variable pattern recognition skill that takes 5-8 years to develop — get that diagnostic reasoning replicated by an ML model that interprets the gauge readings and returns a ranked differential diagnosis before the junior tech makes a costly misdiagnosis.

## Solution Approach
Stand up a focused pilot that integrates current systems, captures the missing signals, and adds analytics/ML once the data path is stable. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
High — core outcome/financial impact justifies investment if change management is handled.
