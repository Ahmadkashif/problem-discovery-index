# Feasibility — Load Calculation Assistant for Equipment Sizing

**Industry:** [[hvac-contractors|HVAC Contractors]]
**Problem:** [[problems/hvac-contractors/worker-life-1|Load Calculation Assistant for Equipment Sizing]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** HVAC technicians sizing replacement equipment who should perform a Manual J load calculation — the ACCA standard for determining correct system tonnage — but instead use rule-of-thumb methods (400-500 sq ft per ton) that produce oversized equipment, poor dehumidification, and comfort complaints get a mobile load calculation tool that produces a defensible Manual J result from house characteristics in 10 minutes.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
