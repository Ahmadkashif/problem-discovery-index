# Feasibility — Delivery Driver Customer Dispute Resolution

**Industry:** [[food-distributors|Food Distributors]]
**Problem:** [[problems/food-distributors/worker-life-2|Delivery Driver Customer Dispute Resolution]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** Drivers get a mobile documentation tool that captures delivery condition evidence instantly, so they stop being the scapegoat for quality disputes they did not cause.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that captures chain-of-custody evidence at drop-off (photos, temperature probe or logger data, signatures, timestamps) and syncs it to TMS/CRM so disputes can be resolved quickly. Keep the workflow offline-capable with minimal taps for drivers.

## Open Source Data
Open map/weather data can annotate delivery conditions, but dispute defense depends on proprietary photos, temperature telemetry, and delivery events collected by the distributor.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
