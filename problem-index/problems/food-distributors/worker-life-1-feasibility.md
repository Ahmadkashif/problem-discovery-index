# Feasibility — Warehouse Selector Productivity Pressure in Cold Environments

**Industry:** [[food-distributors|Food Distributors]]
**Problem:** [[problems/food-distributors/worker-life-1|Warehouse Selector Productivity Pressure in Cold Environments]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** Selectors get intelligent pick-path routing and realistic productivity targets that account for cold exposure, so they stop choosing between their joints and their paycheck.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that sets realistic pick targets based on zone temperature, slotting, travel distance, and historical pick times, then suggests ergonomic pick paths. Integrate WMS and labor management data, give selectors offline-friendly mobile guidance, and surface safety exceptions to supervisors.

## Open Source Data
Meaningful data is proprietary: WMS pick history, slotting, travel distances, temperature logs, and labor standards; open data is not required beyond basic mapping for travel estimates.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
