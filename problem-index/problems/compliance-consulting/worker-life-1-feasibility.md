# Feasibility — Regulatory Monitoring and Change Digest

**Industry:** [[compliance-consulting|Compliance Consulting]]
**Problem:** [[problems/compliance-consulting/worker-life-1|Regulatory Monitoring and Change Digest]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** Compliance consultants who spend 5-10 hours per week reading Federal Register notices, agency guidance updates, and industry association newsletters — trying to stay current across 4-6 regulatory frameworks — get a daily digest that surfaces only the changes relevant to their specific client portfolio, with a plain-language summary of each change's significance.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Integrate with case management, add retrieval over statutes/precedents, and ship automation with human oversight.

## Open Source Data
Public statutes and filings are open; matter notes, client data, and outcomes are proprietary, so training/grounding must use firm data.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
