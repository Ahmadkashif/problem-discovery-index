# Feasibility — Address Intelligence and Access Note Capture for Drivers

**Industry:** [[last-mile-delivery|Last-Mile Delivery]]
**Problem:** [[problems/last-mile-delivery/worker-life-1|Address Intelligence and Access Note Capture for Drivers]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** Delivery drivers who waste 2-4 minutes per problem address searching for apartment unit doors, intercom codes, or back entrance routes — knowledge that experienced drivers on that route have but new drivers lack — get that address-specific knowledge surfaced in their routing app before they arrive.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Ingest TMS/route/telematics feeds, surface exceptions, and add optimization that operators can override.

## Open Source Data
Maps/traffic and some GTFS feeds are open; telematics, carrier SLAs, and stop-level telemetry are private.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
