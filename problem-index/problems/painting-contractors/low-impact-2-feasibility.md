# Feasibility — Color Matching and Paint Selection Assistant

**Industry:** [[painting-contractors|Painting Contractors]]
**Problem:** [[problems/painting-contractors/low-impact-2|Color Matching and Paint Selection Assistant]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Paint manufacturer color matching apps exist but don't integrate the contractor's preferred product line, the substrate requirements, the sheen specification for the application context, and the customer's stated color mood into a recommendation that the estimator can present and order immediately.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
