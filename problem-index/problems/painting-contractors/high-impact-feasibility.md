# Feasibility — Surface Condition Assessment and Paint System Specification from Inspection Photos

**Industry:** [[painting-contractors|Painting Contractors]]
**Problem:** [[problems/painting-contractors/high-impact|Surface Condition Assessment and Paint System Specification from Inspection Photos]]
**Problem type:** High Impact

**Problem summary:** Experienced painters who assess surface condition — reading peeling paint patterns, substrate moisture, chalking, adhesion failure, and substrate type — to determine the correct prep method and paint system before applying any product get that diagnostic reasoning replicated by a computer vision model, preventing paint failures caused by incorrect surface assessment.

## Solution Approach
Stand up a focused pilot that integrates current systems, captures the missing signals, and adds analytics/ML once the data path is stable. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
High — core outcome/financial impact justifies investment if change management is handled.
