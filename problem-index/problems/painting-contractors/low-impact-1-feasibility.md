# Feasibility — Surface Area Measurement and Material Estimation from Photos

**Industry:** [[painting-contractors|Painting Contractors]]
**Problem:** [[problems/painting-contractors/low-impact-1|Surface Area Measurement and Material Estimation from Photos]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Generic area measurement tools exist but don't handle the painting-specific deductions (windows, doors, trim vs. field), substrate-specific spread rate adjustments, and two-coat vs. one-coat calculations that determine accurate material quantities for a residential painting quote.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
