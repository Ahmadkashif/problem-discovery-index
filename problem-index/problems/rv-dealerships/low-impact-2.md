# Seasonal Service Campaign Management

**Industry:** [[rv-dealerships|RV Dealerships]]
**Type:** Low Impact (Customisation Opportunity)
**One-liner:** CRM tools can send marketing emails, but they can't time winterization/de-winterization campaigns to match each customer's specific usage pattern, geography, and vehicle needs.
**Tags:** #gradient-boosting #time-series-forecasting #decision-trees #feature-engineering #cross-validation #evaluation-metrics #exponential-smoothing

## The Problem
RV dealerships face extreme seasonal service demand: spring de-winterization and fall winterization create 4-6 week backlogs that overwhelm service departments. Most dealerships blast generic reminders to their entire customer base, creating a first-come stampede rather than a managed flow. Customers who winterize late risk freeze damage ($2,000-$10,000 in repairs). Dealerships who can't spread the demand lose customers to mobile winterization services.

## What Already Exists
DMS-integrated CRM tools (DealerSocket, VinSolutions adapted for RV) send scheduled email campaigns. Service scheduling tools allow online booking. Neither optimizes the timing of outreach based on customer-specific factors or manages demand flow.

## The Customisation Gap
A seasonal campaign system for RV needs to: (1) predict optimal winterization/de-winterization timing for each customer based on their geographic location, usage patterns, and vehicle type, (2) stagger outreach to smooth demand across 6-8 weeks rather than creating a 2-week spike, (3) offer dynamic scheduling incentives (early-bird pricing, off-peak discounts) calibrated to capacity, and (4) generate vehicle-specific service recommendations based on the unit's age, systems, and prior service history.

## Impact If Solved
Smooths seasonal demand spikes by 40-60%, reducing overtime and improving service quality. Prevents $50K-$200K in annual freeze damage claims by reaching late-winterizing customers earlier. Increases seasonal service capture rate by 15-20% through personalized, well-timed outreach.
