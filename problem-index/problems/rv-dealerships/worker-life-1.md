# Service Tech Multi-System Complexity

**Industry:** [[rv-dealerships|RV Dealerships]]
**Type:** Worker Life Changing
**One-liner:** RV technicians stop drowning in repair manuals for systems they see once a year and start having instant access to the specific procedure for this brand's water heater or that manufacturer's slide-out mechanism.
**Tags:** #llm #text-generation #nlp #worker-facing

## The Problem
An RV service technician must be competent across automotive systems (engine, drivetrain, brakes), residential plumbing (freshwater, gray water, black water, water heater), residential electrical (12V DC, 120V AC, solar, inverter, converter), LP gas systems (furnace, stove, water heater, refrigerator), and appliance repair (refrigerator, microwave, washer/dryer, air conditioner). Each RV manufacturer uses different component brands and configurations. A tech might see a Dometic refrigerator once, a Norcold the next, and an Everchill after that — each with different repair procedures. The cognitive load is immense, and repair information is scattered across dozens of manufacturer websites, forums, and paper manuals.

## Why It Matters to the Worker
RV techs report the highest frustration with "documentation hunting" — spending 20-40% of their time finding the right repair procedure for the specific component in the specific unit. This time is often unbillable under flat-rate pay, creating the same financial pressure as auto repair but with 5x the system complexity. The result is high burnout and a severe shortage of qualified RV technicians (industry estimates a 10,000+ tech shortfall).

## What a Solution Looks Like
An AI-powered technical reference that takes the unit's VIN or model and the symptom description, then retrieves the specific repair procedure for the specific component in that specific RV — from the manufacturer's documentation, TSBs, and community knowledge. The system answers questions like "how do I reset the Dometic CCC-2 thermostat in a 2019 Grand Design Solitude 310GK" with step-by-step procedures and diagrams.

## Impact If Solved
Reduces documentation hunting time from 20-40% to under 5% of a tech's day. Enables automotive-background techs to competently service residential RV systems with AI-guided assistance. Addresses the technician shortage by lowering the expertise barrier for new hires.
