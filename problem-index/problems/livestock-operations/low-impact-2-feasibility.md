# Feasibility — Feed Ration Formulation for Small Operations

**Industry:** [[livestock-operations|Livestock Operations]]
**Problem:** [[problems/livestock-operations/low-impact-2|Feed Ration Formulation for Small Operations]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Enterprise ration-balancing software like AMTS.Cattle, NDS Professional, and PC Dart are designed for nutritionists managing 50,000+ head feedlots with lab-analyzed feed ingredients, but the 700,000+ cow-calf and small backgrounder operations under 500 head need simplified ration optimization that works with locally available feedstuffs, regional forage test results, and the economic constraint of buying feed in pickup-truck quantities rather than rail-car loads.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Blend field/plant logs with weather/soil inputs and deliver simple decision-support that works offline.

## Open Source Data
NOAA/USDA weather and agronomic data are open; field logs, inputs, and yield histories come from growers/processors.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
