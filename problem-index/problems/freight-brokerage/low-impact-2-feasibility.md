# Feasibility — Shipper Demand Forecasting for Capacity Pre-Procurement

**Industry:** [[freight-brokerage|Freight Brokerage]]
**Problem:** [[problems/freight-brokerage/low-impact-2|Shipper Demand Forecasting for Capacity Pre-Procurement]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Generic demand forecasting tools project volume but don't understand freight-specific demand drivers — shipper inventory cycles, seasonal produce patterns, retail inventory push ahead of holiday seasons — that allow a broker to pre-position carrier capacity before a demand spike.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Ingest TMS/route/telematics feeds, surface exceptions, and add optimization that operators can override.

## Open Source Data
Maps/traffic and some GTFS feeds are open; telematics, carrier SLAs, and stop-level telemetry are private.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
