# Feasibility — Permit Requirement Identification by Job Type and Jurisdiction

**Industry:** [[plumbing-contractors|Plumbing Contractors]]
**Problem:** [[problems/plumbing-contractors/low-impact-2|Permit Requirement Identification by Job Type and Jurisdiction]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Generic permit databases exist but don't resolve the specific question a plumber faces: does this specific job type in this specific municipality require a permit, and if so, what inspections are required and what is the current fee schedule?

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
