# Feasibility — AI-Assisted Sewer Camera Inspection and Pipe Condition Assessment

**Industry:** [[plumbing-contractors|Plumbing Contractors]]
**Problem:** [[problems/plumbing-contractors/high-impact|AI-Assisted Sewer Camera Inspection and Pipe Condition Assessment]]
**Problem type:** High Impact

**Problem summary:** A drain technician who runs a sewer camera inspection gets a frame-by-frame defect analysis — root intrusion, pipe offset, cracking, bellying, grease buildup — in the time it takes to pull the camera back out, replacing 20-30 minutes of expert video review with 2 minutes of AI report generation.

## Solution Approach
Stand up a focused pilot that integrates current systems, captures the missing signals, and adds analytics/ML once the data path is stable. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
High — core outcome/financial impact justifies investment if change management is handled.
