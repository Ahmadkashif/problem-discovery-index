# Feasibility — PHMSA Compliance Documentation and Audit Trail Automation

**Industry:** [[utility-contractors|Utility Contractors]]
**Problem:** [[problems/utility-contractors/low-impact-2|PHMSA Compliance Documentation and Audit Trail Automation]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Generic document management systems store PHMSA records but don't understand the federal pipeline safety regulation structure — which records must be retained for which assets, what inspection frequencies are required by regulation, and when a compliance gap creates reportable incident risk.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
