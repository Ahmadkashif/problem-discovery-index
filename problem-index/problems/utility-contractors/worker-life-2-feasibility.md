# Feasibility — Gas Leak Indicator Pattern Recognition for Field Survey Teams

**Industry:** [[utility-contractors|Utility Contractors]]
**Problem:** [[problems/utility-contractors/worker-life-2|Gas Leak Indicator Pattern Recognition for Field Survey Teams]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** Gas distribution survey teams performing leak detection patrols who must identify gas migration indicators from above-grade visual evidence — dead/stressed vegetation patterns, pavement cracking from subsurface gas accumulation, unusual frost patterns — get a photo-assisted field tool that flags potential leak indicators from survey photos for follow-up with combustible gas indicator equipment.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
