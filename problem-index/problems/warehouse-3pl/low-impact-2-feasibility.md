# Feasibility — Labor Planning and Staffing Optimization by Shift

**Industry:** [[warehouse-3pl|Warehouse / 3PL]]
**Problem:** [[problems/warehouse-3pl/low-impact-2|Labor Planning and Staffing Optimization by Shift]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Generic workforce management tools project labor need from historical averages, but don't model the specific inbound volume forecast, pick wave profile, and productivity variation by day-of-week and season that determines how many pickers, packers, and receivers each shift actually needs.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Ingest TMS/route/telematics feeds, surface exceptions, and add optimization that operators can override.

## Open Source Data
Maps/traffic and some GTFS feeds are open; telematics, carrier SLAs, and stop-level telemetry are private.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
