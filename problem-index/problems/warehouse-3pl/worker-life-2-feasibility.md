# Feasibility — Receiving Inspection and Damage Documentation Automation

**Industry:** [[warehouse-3pl|Warehouse / 3PL]]
**Problem:** [[problems/warehouse-3pl/worker-life-2|Receiving Inspection and Damage Documentation Automation]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** Receiving dock workers who document inbound shipment damage — photographing damaged cartons, counting short shipments, noting carrier damage signatures — and then manually enter exception reports in the WMS get that documentation automated from their photos, with carrier claims drafted automatically.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Ingest TMS/route/telematics feeds, surface exceptions, and add optimization that operators can override.

## Open Source Data
Maps/traffic and some GTFS feeds are open; telematics, carrier SLAs, and stop-level telemetry are private.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
