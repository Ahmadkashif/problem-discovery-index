# Feasibility — Inventory Discrepancy Detection and Cycle Count Targeting

**Industry:** [[warehouse-3pl|Warehouse / 3PL]]
**Problem:** [[problems/warehouse-3pl/low-impact-1|Inventory Discrepancy Detection and Cycle Count Targeting]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Generic inventory management systems track theoretical inventory levels, but don't identify which specific locations have the highest probability of physical-vs-system discrepancy — enabling targeted cycle counts that concentrate counting effort where errors are most likely.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Ingest TMS/route/telematics feeds, surface exceptions, and add optimization that operators can override.

## Open Source Data
Maps/traffic and some GTFS feeds are open; telematics, carrier SLAs, and stop-level telemetry are private.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
