# Feasibility — AI-Assisted Preliminary Cost Estimation from Architectural Drawings

**Industry:** [[general-contractors|General Contractors]]
**Problem:** [[problems/general-contractors/high-impact|AI-Assisted Preliminary Cost Estimation from Architectural Drawings]]
**Problem type:** High Impact

**Problem summary:** An experienced estimator who can eyeball a floor plan and get within 10-15% of final cost before any itemized takeoff — a judgment built from hundreds of projects — gets that capability embedded in a model that any estimator on the team can invoke in 5 minutes instead of 3 days of preparation.

## Solution Approach
Stand up a focused pilot that integrates current systems, captures the missing signals, and adds analytics/ML once the data path is stable. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
High — core outcome/financial impact justifies investment if change management is handled.
