# Feasibility — Subcontractor Performance Scoring and Bid Analysis

**Industry:** [[general-contractors|General Contractors]]
**Problem:** [[problems/general-contractors/low-impact-1|Subcontractor Performance Scoring and Bid Analysis]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Generic vendor management tools track contact information and certifications, but subcontractor performance in construction requires scoring based on field-specific outcomes — schedule compliance, change order rates, quality defect rates, and response time — that no generic vendor management tool is designed to capture.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
