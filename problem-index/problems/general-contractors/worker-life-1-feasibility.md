# Feasibility — Job Site Daily Progress Reporting from Photos

**Industry:** [[general-contractors|General Contractors]]
**Problem:** [[problems/general-contractors/worker-life-1|Job Site Daily Progress Reporting from Photos]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** Superintendents who spend 30-45 minutes at the end of each shift writing daily field reports — documenting weather, crew counts, work completed, and issues encountered — get that report generated automatically from the photos they're already taking throughout the day.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
