# Feasibility — Change Order Documentation Automation from Field Observations

**Industry:** [[general-contractors|General Contractors]]
**Problem:** [[problems/general-contractors/low-impact-2|Change Order Documentation Automation from Field Observations]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Generic voice-to-text and note-taking tools can transcribe field observations, but they can't structure them into formal change order requests with scope description, cost impact, schedule impact, and the owner/architect notification language that protects the GC's claim rights under the contract.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
