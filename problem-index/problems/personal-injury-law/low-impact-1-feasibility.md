# Feasibility — Demand Letter Generation and Settlement Negotiation Preparation

**Industry:** [[personal-injury-law|Personal Injury Law]]
**Problem:** [[problems/personal-injury-law/low-impact-1|Demand Letter Generation and Settlement Negotiation Preparation]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** General LLM tools can draft professional letters but can't produce demand letters that incorporate PI-specific damages formulas, jurisdiction-specific caps, insurer-specific negotiation dynamics, and the persuasive structure that has historically worked against specific adjusters.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Integrate with case management, add retrieval over statutes/precedents, and ship automation with human oversight.

## Open Source Data
Public statutes and filings are open; matter notes, client data, and outcomes are proprietary, so training/grounding must use firm data.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
