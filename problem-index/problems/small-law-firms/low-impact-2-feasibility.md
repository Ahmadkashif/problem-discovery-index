# Feasibility — Client Communication Drafting and Matter Status Updates

**Industry:** [[small-law-firms|Small Law Firms]]
**Problem:** [[problems/small-law-firms/low-impact-2|Client Communication Drafting and Matter Status Updates]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** General LLM tools can draft professional emails, but client communications from a law firm need to be drafted in the attorney's specific voice, reflect the matter's current posture and pending actions, and comply with professional responsibility rules about what can and cannot be communicated to a client.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Integrate with case management, add retrieval over statutes/precedents, and ship automation with human oversight.

## Open Source Data
Public statutes and filings are open; matter notes, client data, and outcomes are proprietary, so training/grounding must use firm data.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
