# Feasibility — Billing Reconciliation and Accounts Receivable Management

**Industry:** [[small-law-firms|Small Law Firms]]
**Problem:** [[problems/small-law-firms/worker-life-2|Billing Reconciliation and Accounts Receivable Management]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** Solo attorneys and small firm office managers who manually review unbilled time, send invoices, and follow up on overdue accounts — spending 4-8 hours per month on billing administration that generates its own awkwardness in client relationships — get an automated billing cycle that handles invoicing, follow-up, and AR aging without them having to initiate each step.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Integrate with case management, add retrieval over statutes/precedents, and ship automation with human oversight.

## Open Source Data
Public statutes and filings are open; matter notes, client data, and outcomes are proprietary, so training/grounding must use firm data.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
