# Feasibility — Electrical Fault Diagnosis Assistant

**Industry:** [[electrical-contractors|Electrical Contractors]]
**Problem:** [[problems/electrical-contractors/worker-life-1|Electrical Fault Diagnosis Assistant]]
**Problem type:** Worker Life

**Problem summary:** An apprentice or journeyman electrician standing in front of an intermittent electrical problem — flickering lights, a GFCI that keeps tripping, a breaker that trips under specific loads — gets a structured diagnostic checklist that surfaces the most likely causes based on the symptom pattern, instead of guessing or waiting for the master to come over.

## Solution Approach
Ship a lightweight assistant that removes repetitive steps for frontline staff, with fast feedback loops and clear handoff to humans. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
High for workers — improves retention and quality; commercial ROI is moderate but defensible as part of the platform.
