# Feasibility — Labor Hour Estimation from Project Scope

**Industry:** [[electrical-contractors|Electrical Contractors]]
**Problem:** [[problems/electrical-contractors/low-impact-2|Labor Hour Estimation from Project Scope]]
**Problem type:** Low Impact

**Problem summary:** Generic estimating tools handle material takeoffs but not the labor productivity rates that are specific to installation type, building construction type, access difficulty, and local crew experience — the variables an experienced electrical estimator encodes from years of project data.

## Solution Approach
Adapt a horizontal tool with industry-specific templates, rules, and integrations so teams stop working around generic software. Deliver mobile-first jobsite workflows with photo/checklist capture and scheduling tied to field realities.

## Open Source Data
Building codes, weather, and some permit data are open; job cost, photos, and schedule data are shop-specific.

## Worth Solving?
Medium — worthwhile as a customization/efficiency lever once a clear sponsor and data owner are identified.
