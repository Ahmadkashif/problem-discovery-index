# Feasibility — Delinquency Risk Scoring and Early Intervention Routing

**Industry:** [[hoa-management|HOA Management]]
**Problem:** [[problems/hoa-management/high-impact|Delinquency Risk Scoring and Early Intervention Routing]]
**Problem type:** High Impact

**Problem summary:** HOA managers who wait until an account is 60-90 days past due before escalating — by which time collections costs are high and lien filing is the only remaining lever — get an early warning score that identifies accounts likely to become chronically delinquent from the first missed payment, enabling proactive payment plan outreach before the situation escalates.

## Solution Approach
Stand up a focused pilot that pulls assessment billing, payment histories, prior delinquency outcomes, and communication logs from the HOA accounting platform, then scores new delinquencies and routes early outreach tasks. Keep managers in the loop with override controls and lien escalation rules by jurisdiction.

## Open Source Data
Open data adds little beyond county ownership records; the signals come from the HOA’s own assessment schedules, homeowner payment histories, prior lien outcomes, and communication/collection notes, all of which are private.

## Worth Solving?
High — core outcome/financial impact justifies investment if change management is handled.
