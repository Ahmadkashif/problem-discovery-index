# Med Spas

## Profile
**Category:** Healthcare / Aesthetics
**Market Size:** ~$15B US med spa market, fastest-growing segment of aesthetics at 12-15% annual growth, ~8,000 med spas operating
**Tech Maturity:** Medium — Aesthetic Record, Zenoti, Boulevard, and PatientNow provide practice management with photo documentation and treatment tracking. Before/after photo systems exist but lack standardized analysis. Treatment recommendation is entirely provider-dependent with no decision support
**Workforce:** Injectors (Nurse Practitioners, Physician Assistants, Registered Nurses), aestheticians, medical directors (supervising physicians, often part-time), patient coordinators/consultants, marketing staff, front desk

## Key Pain Themes
Treatment recommendation is the core revenue driver and the most skill-dependent function — an experienced injector looks at a face and sees the interplay of volume loss, muscle dynamics, skin quality, and bone structure that determines which combination of toxins, fillers, lasers, and skin treatments will achieve the patient's aesthetic goals. New injectors default to the treatments they know best rather than the treatments the patient needs, leading to suboptimal outcomes and lower per-patient revenue. Injectable inventory management is uniquely complex: Botox vials must be used within 24 hours of reconstitution, filler syringes are partially used and tracked per patient for future sessions, and expiration dates on high-cost biologics (a single vial of Sculptra costs $400+) create waste pressure. Regulatory compliance varies dramatically by state — some states require the medical director to be on-site during treatments, others allow remote supervision; scope of practice for NPs, PAs, and RNs differs; HIPAA applies to before/after photos stored in marketing systems.

## Current Tech Landscape
Aesthetic Record is the leading med spa-specific platform with photo documentation, consent management, and treatment tracking. Zenoti provides enterprise-level spa management. Boulevard handles scheduling and payments with a luxury-brand experience. PatientNow offers comprehensive practice management. Cherry, PatientFi, and Alle provide patient financing and loyalty programs. No platform provides treatment recommendation intelligence — connecting facial assessment to optimal treatment combinations based on the patient's goals, anatomy, and outcome data from similar patients.
